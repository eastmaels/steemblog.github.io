模型车|MySmartphonePhotography

在Costco看到有卖模型车，想买辆老爷车的模型做摆设，但是没找到喜欢的款式。

勇敢挑了一辆蓝色的车。（他最喜欢蓝色）


模型的车门和前后车厢可以打开。小部件也做的很逼真。孩子很喜欢。


另外还拿了两个模型车，打算日后作为孩子表现好的奖励。

Rules of monthly #MySmartphonePhotography Contest 1.Please put #MySmartphonePhotography in your titles and tags. 2.It must be original work and at least 2 shots. 3.Brief introduction about your photography. 4.Once a month, from the first day of every month to the end of the month (EST).

The First Place will get 2 SBD, the second place will get 1 SBD and the third place will get 0.5 SBD. Welcome to the contest！
