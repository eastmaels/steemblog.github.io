Shopkick App - reward you for visiting stores and scanning specific items

Shopkick App

reward you for visiting stores and scanning specific items

Screenshots

Hunter’s comment

The basic idea is that it’s an app you use when you’re shopping in a store or a mall to collect kicks (points), browse specials, and earn real-money rewards, including gift certificates.

Ways to earn kicks(points):

  • Walk-in: Earn kicks at the entrance when you walk into select partner stores
  • Scan: Hunt for select products in-store and earn kicks when you scan barcodes using in-app scanner
  • Purchase with linked card: Earn kicks for every dollar you spend at select partner stores when you shop with a linked credit card
  • Receipts: Purchase select items and submit your receipt through the app to earn kicks
  • Visit online stores: Earn kicks by visiting select online partners through shopkick
  • View online products: Check out select online partner offers through the app to earn kicks
  • Make online purchases: Earn kicks for every dollar you spend when you visit online partners through shopkick
  • Watch videos: Earn kicks and discover new products when you watch in-app videos

Use the kicks to redeem wide range of gift cards. Minimum kicks to redeem a gift card is 500 kicks, which give you $2 in gift card.

Link

https://www.shopkick.com/

blockrize card - the first credit card with rewards in Bitcoin and Ethereum

blockrize card

the first credit card with rewards in Bitcoin and Ethereum

Screenshots

Hunter’s comment

This is the first credit card that offers rewards in Ethereum and Bitcoin.

Holders of this credit card, will earn 1% back in cryptocurrency on every purchase.
Besides the 1% cashback, this card also offers :

  • 0 fees on cryptocurrency rewards
  • Top security for your cryptocurrency
  • Track what you’ve spent and the rewards earned

On their homepage they state that in 2017 the card offering the equivalent of:

  • 6.9% cash back rate for Bitcoin rewards
  • 16.3% cash back rate for Ethereum rewards

There are some problems with this credit card:

  • This card didn’t exist in 2017 and still doesn’t exist.
  • The high return rates are due to massive gains both cryptocurrencies made in 2017, and there is no guarantee the rate will be that high once it’s released.
  • No mention of fees, issuing bank or payment network

If you find this credit card interesting to you, you can sign up for their waitlist and get early access.

Anyway, this is a interesting credit card to folllow.

Link

https://www.blockrize.com/

如何从火币交易所提现steem到steemit？

有新人问怎么从火币上提现steem到steemit上。
搜索了一圈没有找到答案，所以自己试着转了一下。
基本操作和币安提现是一样的。教程 | 如何把steemit里的steem转到币安

步骤如下：

  • 首先登入: www.huobi.pro
  • 右上角点击 资产->充币&提币
  • 在列表里找到Steem，点击”提币“

  • 提现地址：填写你的steemit id
  • 地址标签：随便填写
  • 数量：你要提现steem的数量
  • 点击”提币“

好了，交易已经提交，等待steemit id ”huobi-withdrawal” 给你转steem。

最后说一下火币提币的费用，每笔提现需要0.5 steem.
如果有什么疑问，欢迎留言提问。
