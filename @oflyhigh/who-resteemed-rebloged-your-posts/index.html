Who resteemed/rebloged your posts? / 谁转发了你的文章？

We often see replies like this：

Nice post, upvoted and resteemed.

我们或许时常看到这样的回复：

好文章，投票并转发了。

Most of the replies like this come from robots, We don’t have to care.
But，Do you care who resteemed your posts?

大多数类似这样的回复来自机器人，我们无需关心，但是你是否关心都谁转发了你的帖子呢？

One of my friends said that： “Who resteemed your posts is your truly fan!”, I don’t think so, but the more people resteemed, the more popular the post is.

我的一个朋友说：“转发才是真爱粉。”，我不这样认为，但是转发的越多，说明这个帖子越受欢迎。

So I made a small tool to check who resteemed my posts.

所以我弄个了小工具来检查谁转发了我的帖子。

The URL & Interface

http://steemit.serviceuptime.net/check_resteem.php


Just input the account name you want to check, then press Return or click Check Button, you will get the results.

这个工具用起来非常简单啦，输入你要查看的用户名，然后回车或者点Check 按钮，就会得到结果了。

The Results Filed

The tool will read out 20 entries from user’s blog, and listed them in table, and give a list who resteemed the posts.

这个工具读取用户的近20条博客条目，并列出谁转发了对应文章。

For example, the part of the results for oflyhigh

以上是oflyhigh的部分测试结果。

You can click the user to reach the user’s blog page at steemit.

可以点击用户名直达用户的steemit博客主页。
