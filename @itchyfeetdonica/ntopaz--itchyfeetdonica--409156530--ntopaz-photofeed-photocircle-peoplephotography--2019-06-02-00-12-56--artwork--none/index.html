Hey everyone, it’s Children’s Day today in many parts of the world, and I’m sharing a photo of 2 kids that I captured. It’s my entry to the People Photography contest by @worldcapture and the @ntopaz Black and White photography contest hosted by @axeman.

These 2 sisters lived in the Amazon jungle of Ecuador. It was a very hot and humid afternoon. Under the scorching sun, they were playing on an open field in front of their hut. Without any fancy toys, the older sister was entertaining the younger one by dragging her in a self-made “cart”. She’s also taking care of her younger sister’s shoes.
