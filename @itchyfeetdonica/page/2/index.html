I saw these famous lines by Mark Twain printed on a wall of a travel agency in the famous travel destination in Croatia - Dubrovnik.

I like the design in this corner, and a fun fact: I just arrived there by hitchhiking from Montenegro. I missed the last connecting bus that day since the schedule changed when summer just finished. So I didn’t catch the trade winds in my sails, but caught a kind truck driver who was willing to give me a lift, after standing 3 hours in the scorching sun with a sign. It was a fun experience.

Actually, why do I ramble so much? Trying to say that the agency did a good job in marketing? Trying to convey some sort of silly profound message about life? Or trying to argue this single-picture post is not “low effort” because it’s a photo that I captured after going through lots of hardships?

I have no idea. Feel free to call it shitpost if you must.

Today I’m sharing a photo I took at Hastings Point, an attractive coastal settlement in the Tweed Shire of New South Wales, Australia.

It’s one of our first stops on the road trip during the extended Christmas and New Year holiday, when it was the hottest time in this part of Straya. We enjoyed the sea and stayed one night at this venue among other caravans and tents.

This small hamlet is surrounded by a large national park, and it’s nestled between the vast ocean and a delicate estuary. Offering a wide range of choices for various water sports, this area is popular among locals as well as visitors.

##
Greetings everyone! I’m sharing with you some minimal style photos I took right after sunrise. It was at around 7am in Erg Chebbi, the large seas of dunes formed by wind-blown sand in Morocco.

The sand in the Sahara Desert here looked more orangish than in the other dunes I’ve seen before. The heavy storm of the previous night also contributed to the deeper colours. I just couldn’t have enough of the gorgeous curves and shades formed by nature. Hope you would enjoy them as I did! =)

The head image is my entry for the #TravelPhotoContest by @lightcaptured. Don’t hesitate to join the fun if you also love travel and photography!

##
今天分享几张撒哈拉沙漠的简洁风沙丘照片，拍于早上七点左右，日出之后。
这儿的沙子呈现橙黄色，比我以前在其他沙漠地带看到的沙丘颜色要深，跟前一晚下的暴雨应该也有关系。
看着这美妙的光影、色调和线条，我又一次被大自然的造化所深深折服。
