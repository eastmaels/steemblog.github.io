Greetings everyone! I had a wonderful afternoon last Sunday shooting photos for a newborn and his families. ##
The @portraitcontest’s theme of this week is Emotion. I believe the emotion is pretty obvious in this photo with the parents and their newborn baby. Love and joy are in the air! It was set up with some cushions and carpets in the living room, and I used natural light coming in through the large window. A lovely family, isn’t it?



上周日应邀去给一个新生儿和他家人拍几组照片。在客厅的落地窗旁，用几条毯子和一个大靠垫布了下景，还准备了一些绒毛玩具等小道具。

##
不知道我应该说图片会说话，还是眼神会说话，总之，不用我多说，相信你也能很容易就感觉到这对父母满满的喜悦和爱。可爱的新生命，爱情的结晶，温馨的时刻，暖暖的心。:)
