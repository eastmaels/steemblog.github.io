Greetings everyone! It’s time to announce the winners of my【Cross-culture Challenge】! 😃

I’ve enjoyed reading through all the posts and comments, and I’m glad to see this challenge has inspired some Steemians to think about and to compare the different cultures they have experienced, and many of the entry posts have stimulated discussions on relevant topics. I’d like to thank all the participants and supporters! 👍 And a huge shout out to @partiko! Without you guys, this challenge wouldn’t have been so fun! 💪

Since there have been so many amazing entries, it’s really tough for me to pick winners, but the decision has to be made! In order to showcase and to award more participants, I’ll add another 8 honorable mentions besides the first 8 winners. 👏📣 So here we go! Let’s give a shout out to these awesome cross-culture ambassadors:
