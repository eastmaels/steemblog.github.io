Hello everyone! You might have heard of the Notre Dame Cathedral fire? Here I’m sharing some images of Norte-Dame de Paris through my lens. The first photo is my entry to Qurator’s Photo Friday competition. Since I have lived in Belgium, Paris is just a few hours away. I was fortunate to have visited this famous cathedral in its city centre a couple of times. The photos below were taken by me in 2013 and 2014. 2013 happened to be the 850th anniversary of Norte-Dame.

Actually I have traveled to most regions of France, but I just realized I have only done 1 post about this neighbor 🇫🇷 since I joined Steem in 2017. I guess we sometimes take the familiar for granted, right? If it’s not this devastating blaze in Paris, I might have left the Notre-Dame photos behind in my drive for even longer. Hopefully the rebuilding would start soon and go well.



在比利时搬过好几次家，但不管是法语区还是荷语区，都离巴黎不远，所以去过不少次，也有幸目睹了几次巴黎圣母院大火前的风采。刚才意识到在这里发了200多篇文，只有一篇是关于法国的，还不是巴黎，可能这个邻国去多了反而司空见惯了？如果不是这次的火灾，那些几年前拍的照片也连同巴黎的记忆一起尘封了吧。今天就来分享一些我镜头中圣母院的昔日光影，其中2013年去的那次正好是它的850周年建成纪念。烧毁的原物一去不复返，但愿重建尽快开始、顺利完成。
