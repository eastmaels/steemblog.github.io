#299 Flat Lay (4) - Desk | 平铺俯拍（四）

Here is an over-the-head shot of my desk, with a cup of coffee, a sketchbook, a pencil, a laptop and a notebook (tip ;p). I took this photo last year in the office in Queensland, Australia. It’s my entry for @ntopaz’s Still Life Bodegon contest by @acostaeladio.

The definition of Bodegón from their page:

Bodegon, a still life photograph, is an artistic composition of several inanimate objects. Still life photographs can be represented with immobile natural materials, such as flowers, seeds, fruits and shells. Or use everyday elements made by man, such as kitchen utensils, work tools or ornaments, which is creating a boom among photographers. Crayons, pencils, paints and 3D objects can be used to create good artistic drawings manually. The design software is also used as an artistic tool to create a good still life.

##

Mention the model of the camera or technique for the manual production or design process (include the step-by-step process).

So I arranged these objects on the awesome orange standing desk, and then stood on the chair and shot the picture with my iPhone 7. That’s it! :)

这些照片都是我用平铺俯拍方式拍的，以前提过这种平铺俯拍片近几年在Ins上和各图库网站比较流行，是很多用户搜索配图的素材。常见的有一桌吃的或办公用品、一地护肤品或化妆品、一地图的旅行用品之类。

题图用来参加@ntopaz的静物摄影比赛，是我在办公桌边拍的。过程很简单：把桌上的办公用品和咖啡进行排列，然后用手机朝下俯拍。
