##
Greetings everyone! I had a wonderful afternoon last Sunday shooting photos for a newborn and his families. It’s loaded with fun, and pictures too of course… :) There are too many sweet photos that I would love to share, and let me start by joining the @portraitcontest Week 86, and @ntopaz’s Black and White Photography Contest. Thank you @jasonrussell and @axeman for hosting and/or judging these fun contests!

##
Here’s the original photo before cropped by ntopaz:

##
The @portraitcontest’s theme of this week is Emotion. I believe the emotion is pretty obvious in this photo with the parents and their newborn baby. Love and joy are in the air! It was set up with some cushions and carpets in the living room, and I used natural light coming in through the large window. A lovely family, isn’t it?



上周日应邀去给一个新生儿和他家人拍几组照片。在客厅的落地窗旁，用几条毯子和一个大靠垫布了下景，还准备了一些绒毛玩具等小道具。

##
西北欧的初夏时分，阳光明媚却不刺眼，透过玻璃照射进来，作为自然光源很合适。后来也去后花园里拍了几组室外人像。忙了一下午，虽然有点累，但非常开心可以分享他们一家人的幸福和快乐。

##
不知道我应该说图片会说话，还是眼神会说话，总之，不用我多说，相信你也能很容易就感觉到这对父母满满的喜悦和爱。可爱的新生命，爱情的结晶，温馨的时刻，暖暖的心。:)
