How are you doing my Steemit friends? Enjoying your holiday with families and friends? Christmas is definitely in the air! 🎄🎉 And I just made a Christmas card for @Partiko, after I got to know the design contest from @rosatravels’ post.

##
圣诞快乐~ ！！今天分享的是我绘制Partiko圣诞贺卡的过程。因为喜欢画画和手工，就没有用电脑软件，而是手绘加一点点小刀工做的卡片。希望你们喜欢，欢迎留言交流~
比利时这边已经沉浸在一片浓厚的节日气氛中了，各地的圣诞市场非常热闹。不知道你那里有没有过节？ :)

##
Partiko is an awesome mobile app for Steemit developed by @crypto.talk and his team. It’s been widely known among CN community since a few months ago, and it’s getting more and more popular after SteemFest 3. If you haven’t started using it, give it a try at https://partiko.app. I’m sure you will love it!

Now have a look at how I made the Christmas card~ I really “made” a card - I mean a handmade paper card, not a design with software. :) I just love DIY and drawing by hands, so I came up with this idea for the Partiko contest. Hope you will like it! 😃

##
Here’s the breakdown:

##

  • Step 1 ✏
    Sketch the design with a pencil - Partiko for you as a gift from Santa’s stocking! And at the back, Partiko’s got a Christmas hat in this cold winter :) :

##

  • Step 2 🔪
    Finish the greetings with silver and gold glitter gel pens, and cut out the Partiko logo (this is some fun you can only get from handmade card! :D):

##

  • Step 3 🎨
    Start coloring with brushes and metallic watercolor paint. I opted for the metallic paint as they look more festive:

##

  • Step 4 ✨
    I limited the colors to silver, gold, red (the holidayish colors) and blue (Partikoish color ;D):
