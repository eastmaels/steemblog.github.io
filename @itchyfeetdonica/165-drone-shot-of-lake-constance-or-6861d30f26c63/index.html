#165 Drone Shot of Lake Constance ✈ ⛵ | 无人机航拍中欧大湖

How’s it going my fellow Steemians? It’s Thursday again, a day for images from Europe! :) So I’m sharing a shot by a drone (DJI Mavic Pro) above Lake Constance in Europe. I was at the sandy shore flying the little friend for a video of the lake region, and clicked this picture during its flight.

It’s my entry for the #TravelPhotoContest by @lightcaptured, a great photographer on Steemit. I’ve seen his cool posts by curating for Steemitworldmap, and now I’m very excited to find his brand new contest that combines travel and photography. I guess this is not only my passion, but also lots and lots of other Steemians’, right? Check out the contest and join the travel photo fun!

##

##
Lake Constance is known as Bodensee in German language. It is a huge lake that borders Austria, Germany and Switzerland. When I look at the seemingly endless lake surface from a certain angle, I feel it’s a vast sea, just like Lake Baikal and Lake Titicaca I visited before.

Since I used to live and am living now in Belgium which boarders Germany, I frequently passed this lake during road trips, or stayed a few days at a town by the riverside. In summer, it’s a popular destination for water sports like sailing, windsurfing and swimming. It’s also relaxing to just sit by the river, reading, chatting or having a picnic. You can also pedal your bike around the entire lake on a cycle path for about 270km. I only tried driving but I believe it’s a great way to see the lake and the sites nearby. Will you give it a try?

I would recommend the following places to visit along the way:

Wollmatinger Ried: a nature reserve to protect the rich flora and fauna;
Lindau: the largest town on the Bavarian shores;
Konstanz: boasts a beautiful old town and a historic harbor;
Mainau: a very touristic but amazing flower island;
Reichenau: Lake Constance’s largest island that was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site and preserves the traces of the Benedictine monastery;
Pfahlbaumuseum Unteruhldingen: an open-air museum displaying the German-style stilt houses;
Allgäu upland: a picturesque mountain region between Lake Constance and the Lech valley.

大家好~ 今天分享的是用DJI无人机Mavic Pro在康斯坦茨湖上空俯拍的照片。我当时在湖边的沙滩上拍这个湖区的全景视频，小机机飞行途中看到几个角度还不错就也拍了几张照片。

这个大湖在德国、奥地利和瑞士交界处，面积大得常常让我产生错觉是在看海，就像去过的俄罗斯贝加尔湖和南美的滴滴咔咔湖一样，湖水清澈、一望无际、辽阔悠远。夏天的时候，很多人来这里玩水上运动，在湖边野餐、看书、跟家人朋友散散步聊聊天。或者，什么都不做，什么都不想，坐在湖边发发呆看看“海”，也自有一番舒心惬意~。
