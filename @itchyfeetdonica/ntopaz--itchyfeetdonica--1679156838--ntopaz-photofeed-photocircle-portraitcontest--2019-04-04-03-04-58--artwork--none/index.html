#228 My Entry for Portrait Photography Contest 📷🎵 | 2个姑娘1首歌

##
I took this photo in a primitive tribe in the Omo Valley of Ethiopia, not far from the border with Kenya. The living environment was pretty harsh there. Like the other girls in the tribe, these 2 sisters decorated themselves with bright-colored beads.

Eyes speak a thousand words, and there are even 2 pairs of eyes in the picture. ;) Plus a picture is worth a thousand words. So here we go, 3000 words! Enough for today.

##
跟上篇一样，参加人像摄影活动，拍于埃塞俄比亚南部一原始部落。

突然想到下面这首十几年前听到的歌，跟照片没关系，只是都有两个姑娘。很喜欢The Ditty Bops这个组合，每首歌都百听不厌。可惜几年前就不活跃了。

P.S. 刚看有评论说到“自由飘荡的灵魂”，一想莫非还真有点关系：唱这歌的一对女同无视世俗偏见，最终结了婚； 而这部落的两姐妹也是活在现代社会之外。大概都算勇敢？
