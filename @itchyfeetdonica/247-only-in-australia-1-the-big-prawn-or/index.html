Also, I’m traveling now without access to most of my past camera photos, yet the pictures of this Big Prawn taken with my phone are lying there still (no pun intended). So here they are - a dedication to my lovely memories in Straya 🇦🇺, this amazing country that I will for sure go back one day. :)

##

##
The statue is in Ballina, a town located in New South Wales, serving as a gateway to the popular site Byron Bay. Built in 1989 and reopened in 2013 with a makeover, the Big Prawn is another of Australia’s iconic big things. There are also Big Pineapple, Big Banana, Big Peach… but I didn’t stop to take decent pictures of them.

This Big Prawn is situated adjacent to Bunnings Warehouse on River Street and have attracted road trippers like us.
