##
Greetings everyone! Here’s my entry for the Art of Love contest hosted by @ntopaz and @livvu. I have been following both for some time. @ntopaz is an awesome platform for artists, and @livvu is an awesome artist. ;) I mean it. Check out @ntopaz’s website for fun art contests, and check out @livvu’s page for her unique paintings and sculptures that ooze passionate love and touching stories.

My photo here was shot in Machu Picchu, yes, the world wonder where I took so many photos and videos but haven’t yet done a single post about. At first I was a bit afraid to visit this super famous place, but after I did, I was totally in awe. The llamas wandering around freely all over the site are part of the magic. I feel this picture fits into the LOVE theme. Love between the couple; love (or peace ;p) between the llamas; love of us for the nature, for the ancient civilization and for traveling ❤, and of course… my love for photography (or for clicking pictures since I’m far from a professional photographer). :)
