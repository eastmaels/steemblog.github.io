The journey of life is not perfect, you just need to enjoy the process.

人生的旅途没有完美，享受过程即可。

Yesterday, I noticed that the 2nd Drawing Challenge held by @helene had begun. But I missed the last contest. This time, I want to have a try. Aimed at the first three places, the process counts most. This is the theme—flowers, and the red rose comes to my mind first. For one thing, my favorite flower is the red rose. For another, the red color makes my life full of vitality. I am not good at painting, previously studied watercolor in the Children’s Palace for only 2 years, and now I’ d like to share my painting with you(^ __ ^).

昨天看到 @helene姐举办的绘画挑战赛第二期，上次没赶上，这次想来试试，冲着前三去的，重在参与✌。这一期的主题是花，我立马想到的是红玫瑰，一是因为我最喜欢的花当属玫瑰，二是火红的颜色让我的生活充满灿烂的气息。画画不是我的特长，以前在少年宫学过两年的水彩画，现在就给大家展示一下我的作品 (^__^) 。

Materials 准备材料

Two gel ink pens (0.5mm, one is black and the other is red) & a blank piece of paper


两支0.5mm中性笔（一黑一红）、一张空白的纸。

The Process 过程图

Step1
Draw a rough outline of the figure, starting from the petals. PS:Using a pencil to draw this outline is a better choice and you can modify it at any time.

用笔画出大概的轮廓，从花瓣开始。（注：用铅笔画草图是更完美，还可以随时修改。）

https://steemitimages.com/DQmXEVWf9sjCx25vDRF11ZNdY4aBgoKpbXzTcXpCecFzfpL/IMG_2757.JPG





Step 2 Draw the leaves and stems of flowers.

画出花的叶子和茎。

https://steemitimages.com/DQmdLzs7YGSMtDxxPi8KuQqPDo6Jd1HAC4WNNpEQZjL6fbw/IMG_2762.JPG



https://steemitimages.com/DQmaojnvC6SHUogN7CWkHUhaYmu8fYd1kTYLC2bsoEqriEa/IMG_2759.JPG


Step 3 Widen the outline, which makes it more layered.
把轮廓描粗，这样看上去更有层次感。

https://steemitimages.com/DQmQTRNfmG7c9eH6vKRPi1mJ5r4cYSSdguviQbZNYsnNWUC/IMG_2758.JPG


Step 4 Color the petals of the rose back and forth with a red pen, and then this painting may look more gorgeous. This technique was taught by my teacher when in the Children’s Palace.
给玫瑰花着色，用红笔沿着一个方向来回涂抹，这样效果更好。（以前学画画的时候，老师教的。）

https://steemitimages.com/DQmWeMeXC8TcngXs6zfpCqf7e6ovjobfWB4pDCYkzKYMKko/IMG_2756.JPG



https://steemitimages.com/DQmTrHNZJhCkugLnAMTgu7vvWoe9RQ5uBTVEBaompWQ4f57/IMG_2760.JPG


https://steemitimages.com/DQmaSChRcNo3CrSUFZnXQQs5WFCj6zLw7fgQjKz8V68hiQT/IMG_2763.JPG


### Finished! 作品完成！❤

**
Thanks @helene for holding this contest. It makes me regain my previous interest.This time, it is too hasty to draw this painting and I don’t have enough tools. I think some of you may notice that the leaves of this rose have no color. I just want to keep it, like the imperfectly perfect Venus.
感谢 @helene 姐的这个比赛，让又重拾了以前小时候的兴趣，这次画得太草率了，工具也不全。大家可能注意到了我的玫瑰花的叶子没有颜色，我想暂且留着，就像断臂的维纳斯一样，不完美的完美。
