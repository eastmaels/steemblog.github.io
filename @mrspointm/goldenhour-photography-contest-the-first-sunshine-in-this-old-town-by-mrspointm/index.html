Hi, steemit friends!💙

Happy Sunday! Here is my entry for Goldenhour Photography Contest hosted by @juliank. If you have an interest, you can click here to know more.

今天星期天，祝愿大家有美好的一天。这是我参加的由 @juliank 举办的Steemit 摄影参赛的照片，主题是黄金时刻。如果大家感兴趣的话，可以点击这里。

Theme: Goldenhour Photography

Location: Yunnan, China

Camera used: iPhone 6

This photo is taken in the old town of Lijiang. I remember that day, I woke up early and walked around the town. When I saw the sunshine, I felt it amazing. There were few people in the daytime, but at night, the streets were always crowded with people, singing, dancing and shopping.

这张照片是在丽江古城拍的，我还记得那天我很早就醒了，沿着古城散步，无意中看到这缕阳光。白天，丽江古城很冷清，没什么人。到了晚上，却正是它最热闹的时候，到处都是唱歌，跳舞，购物的人们，酒吧一条街好不热闹。
