Follow me to explore delicacies - Kao Master 跟着我一起去探索美食 - 烤匠

Hi, steemit friends!💙

These days I ‘ve been busy visiting my relatives and friends, so I hardly get my private time. I feel it a long time that I haven’t upvoted and read my dear steemians’ posts. Until now, only one free day have been left. Now, here is where I went this noon. As can be seen from the photo above, you may know I ‘m in a restaruant and enjoying my lunch. It’s called Kao Master.

这些天我忙着走亲访友，几乎没有自己的私人时间，更别提欣赏大家的文章了。直到现在，我的假期余额完全不足，仅剩短短的一天了。从照片上可以看出，我正在一家名叫烤匠的餐厅享用午餐。

Let me show you around the restaurant 我们先来看看餐厅环境

When we reached there, it was already 13:00. This place were always crowded with people. Immediately I got into it, I felt warm and comfortable. I like the decorations here and Victoria’s Secret show was being played on TV.

当我们到烤匠的时候已经是下午一点了，但这个地方仍然满是顾客。我一进店就觉得很暖和。餐厅的卡通装饰特别讨人喜欢，电视里反复播放着维密秀。

Menu 菜单

I think the food here has high quanlity and a reasonable price. Besides grilled fish, other barbecue tatses great.

这家食物性价比很高，烤匠除了烤鱼特色外，其他碳烤也是一绝。

Grilled fish with green pepper 青椒烤鱼

When orderring, you can choose three kinds of vegetables or meat in grilled fish. For me, potatoes, enoki mushroom and pork luncheon meat are necessary.

点单的时候，可以自由选择三样配菜。对我而言，土豆，金针菇和午餐肉是必须的。

Cheers! Starbucks is a nice choice as a suitable drink for fish. If you have a bad stomach, do not have a try.

干杯！星巴克是一个不错的选择，配着烤鱼一起享用。如果你胃不好的话，还是不要一起尝试了。

Grilled eggplant and chicken wings 烤茄子和鸡翅

Eggplant tastes so-so but chicken wings are the most delicious wings I have ever eaten. I also prepare a video about this restaurant. If you have an interest, you can check @mrpoinp’s latest post.

茄子的味道很一般，但鸡翅真的是我吃到过最好吃的。我还准备了一个小视频，如果大家有兴趣，可以看看 @mrpointp的最新帖子观看。
