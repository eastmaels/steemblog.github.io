The journey of life is not perfect, you just need to enjoy the process.

人生的旅途没有完美，享受过程即可。

The 3rd Drawing Challenge held by @helene has begun.This time, the theme of this contest is fruit, and I also want to say that the process counts most. But I still want to win, for that day is just my birthday. Without hesitation, I choose to draw a painting of strawberries. There are two reasons— one is that strawberry is definitely my favorite fruit and the other is that every year, I want to buy a strawberry cake to celebrate my birthday but I am always told that it is not seasonal fruit. So, this strawberry painting is considered as my birthday gift! Now I’ d like to share my painting with you(^ __ ^).

@helene姐举办的绘画挑战赛第三期拉开了序幕，这次主题是水果，还是那句话重在参与✌，不过还是想获奖，因为那天刚好是我的生日: P。这次没有犹豫，直接选择了草莓。原因有两个，一个肯定是我最爱的水果，二是我每年生日想要订草莓蛋糕，却总被告知这不是当季水果，只好作罢。所以，这个草莓画就当是给自己的生日礼物吧！现在就给大家展示一下我的作品 (^__^) 。

Materials 准备材料

Colored pencils, a pencil (9B) and a sketch book

彩铅笔，9B铅笔和速写本

The Process 过程图

Step1
Draw a rough outline of the figure, starting from the strawberries to the leaves.

用铅笔画出大概的轮廓，从草莓开始，再到叶子。

.jpg)

Step 2 Color the strawberries back and forth and then this painting may look more gorgeous.

给草莓着色，沿着一个方向来回涂抹，这样效果更好。

Step 3 Color the leaves with the same method.

用同样的方法给叶子上色。

Finished! 作品完成！❤


Hope you have a good day!

希望大家有美好的一天。
