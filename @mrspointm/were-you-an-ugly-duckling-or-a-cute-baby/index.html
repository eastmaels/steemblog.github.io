Hi, steemit friends!💙

First, I want to say thanks to @herlife for nominating me in the childhood photo challenge. For the question in the title, I personally think @mrpointp can give the right answer. But I still think I am cute!😊

首先，感谢 @herlife 点名我参加儿时照片挑战照。关于标题上面的问题，我个人认为应该由 @mrpointp作答。但我还是觉得我是可爱的，谁家小孩不可爱啊？😊

Let’s begin!🌸🌸🌸

This is a photo of my family. In the past, we used to take photos in camera and get the film developed, which is a good way to record our life.

这是我的爸妈和我的合影。过去总是喜欢用相机拍下身边的美好，然后把照片洗出来，这样的方式来记录生活我觉得是极好的。

This photo was taken when I was 5 years old, which I think is one of the most precious pictures, for the woman in it is my favorite teacher in kindergarten. At that time, I thought of her as my goddess and admire her so much.

这张照片是我五岁的时候，读幼儿园去野炊拍下的。这是我个人珍藏的一张照片，因为她是我幼儿园最喜欢的老师。那时候，我觉得她好美，现在来讲的话就是女神。

If you have an interest, you can join us and here are the rules.
如果大家有兴趣的话，不妨加入我们。以下是挑战规则：

share a few photos of when you were younger than 10 years old
do you think you were an ugly duckling back then or a cute baby?
use the tag #uglyduckling in your post
nominate 5 friends to join in
if you haven’t received a nomination yet, but really want to join, come on now, don’t wait!

分享几张你10岁以下的照片
你觉得当年你是丑小鸭还是可爱宝贝？
帖子用 #uglyduckling 标签
提名5位或更多朋友参加
如果你还没收到提名，又很想分享儿时的可爱照，不要再等，马上来吧！
