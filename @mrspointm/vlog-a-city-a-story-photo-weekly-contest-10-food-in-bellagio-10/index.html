Hi, steemit friends!💙

It was a shopping day for me and I wouldn’t miss this great opportunity to enjoy delicacy! After comparing a few restaurants recommended by colleagues, @mrpointp and I decided to try a Taiwanese-style called Bellagio named after Lukang in Changhua county, Taiwan. I had wanted to eat Taiwanese food for a long time and now I also plan to travel to Taiwan this year, so I experienced the “taste” of Taiwan today.

今天去成都购物，当然不能错过这个享用美食的大好机会！筛选了几家同事的推荐餐厅，我们决定今天去尝一尝一家名叫鹿港小镇的具有台湾风味的中餐厅，餐厅的名字正是来源于台湾彰化的鹿港镇。早就想去吃一次台湾菜，也有打算今年到台湾旅行，所以今天干脆就去先体验一下台湾的“味道”。

First of all, I want to introduce the decorations of this restaurant, which has brought me the first impression of being simple, fresh and clean. It looks like a cafe. I really like its table and the plaid pattern on the table makes me feel at home. It may because There is an open space in the center of the restaurant with three tables and I guess this is for those who wish to enjoy the “daylight meal.”

首先为大家介绍一下这家餐厅的装潢，鹿港小镇带给我的第一印象就是“简约、清新、干净”，看起来像是一家咖啡馆。很喜欢这家店的餐桌，格子的图案让我感觉到一种在家里吃饭的感觉，可能是因为通常餐厅里的餐桌都比较普通，在装饰上没有结合过多的个人想法。餐厅中央有一个露天的空间，摆了三张桌子，我猜这是为愿意享用“日光餐”的客人准备的。

Now it’s time to I introduce the main courses that we ordered. I focus on three of them and they are also considered as the entries for the contest held by @aaronli.

接下来就开始介绍今天的“重头戏”了，主要介绍三道菜，同时也作为城市故事摄影的参赛作品。

1. San Pei Chicken 三杯鸡

This dish is the most delicious dish I’ve ordered here! San Pei Chicken tastes a bit like coke chicken wings, but also a bit like Leshan’s local food sweet-skined duck. Although not spicy, yet with its unique flavor, even the garlic tastes also good.

这道菜是我在这里点的所有菜中最美味的！三杯鸡的味道有点像可乐鸡翅，又有点像乐山的甜皮鸭，虽然不辣，但具有其独特的香味，就连大蒜也非常美味。

2. Pork & String Beans with Vegetable 榄菜肉碎

This one is thought of as a kind of homemade food. why? Because it is a bit like cowpea with minced meat, but the meat is not so salty, and tastes good with rice.

榄菜肉碎算得上是一道非常家常的菜，为什么这样说呢，因为它有点像烂肉豇豆，但烂肉豇豆没有那么咸，并且非常下饭。

3. Purple Sweet Potato Bun 紫薯玫瑰花

To be honest, the reason for ordering this snack is its appearance, lol… However, it turns out I have made the right choice. Pick up a “rose” and dip with salad. How wonderful it is!

说实话，点这道小吃的原因是因为它的外表，哈哈。不过它的味道确实也没让我失望，夹起一块“玫瑰花”蘸一下配好的料，很棒的搭配。
