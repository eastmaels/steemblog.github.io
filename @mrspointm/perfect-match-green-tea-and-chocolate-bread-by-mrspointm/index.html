Hi, steemit friends!💙

Recently, a kind of chocolate bread become popular on the Internet. We call it zāng zāng bāo(zāng means dirty) in Chinese. Last weekend, I went back to Leshan, Sichuan province and happened to find this kind of bread. Its name comes out of crisp bread, topped with a layer of chocolate sauce and some cocoa powder, which looks as dirty as soil. After finishing eating, my mouth and hands are stained with chocolate. That’s where it got this name. It is surprising that a cup of cold green tea I ordered matches with this bread so perfectly.

最近微博上十分流行一个名叫“脏脏包”甜品，那天回乐山无意中看到有卖这款面包的。原来脏脏包是一款起酥面包，外面淋上一层巧克力酱，再洒一些可可粉，看起来确实“脏脏”的，吃完以后嘴巴和手上会沾上巧克力，因此得名。惊喜的是，当时我还点了一杯冰绿茶，没想到搭配脏脏包那么美味。

Camera used: iPhone 8 plus
