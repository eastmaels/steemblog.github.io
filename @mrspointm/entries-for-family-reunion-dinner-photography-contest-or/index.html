Hi, steemit friends!💙

(Picture from pixabay.com)

It has been seven days since this contest began. So far, we have received almost 40 entries for it. Thanks for your support!

年夜饭摄影比赛自2月15日开始以来，已经有40篇左右参赛作品，在此我和 @mrpointp感谢大家的支持。

This post is to show the latest entries and remind you of some related information about this contest. First, the deadline is at 9:00 p.m. on Mar. 2nd, 2018. Welcome to join us to show off your reunion dinner. Besides, some posts do not meet our requirements. Now I’d like to share the rule again.

本篇文章主要想展示一下最近几天的参赛作品以及对此次“晒晒你的年夜饭”比赛相关事项的提醒。首先，参赛作品在大年十五之前都可以发布，所以我们欢迎更多的朋友参赛。其次，在我和 @mrpointp浏览截止至今天的全部文章时，发现了不少朋友的参赛作品并不符合要求，现在我重新发布一次参赛细则。

Detailed rules 规则与细节

  • The title of the post should include “show off your reunion dinner”
  • Photos related to the theme should be original work
  • At least 2 photos with description
  • Let us know your camera and your city if it is convenient
  • Add the tag #cn-reuniondinner and the main tag is #cn
  • Refer to my post to make more people know about this contest

    文章标题请包含“晒晒你的年夜饭”，可以任意添加小标题。
    作品应与主题相关，只接受原创作品。
    文章中至少有2张年夜饭照片，并附上文字说明。
    如果方便的话说明使用的拍照设备以及所在城市。
    文章须添加 #cn-reuniondinner标签，主标签依然是 #cn。
    文章中须提及本帖，这样会让更多朋友了解并参与进来。
