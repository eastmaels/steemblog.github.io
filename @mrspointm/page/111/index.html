《豆腐脑之诗》

作者：我自己✌

姑妈的豆腐脑

是全世界最好吃的豆腐脑

也许你不信

但我不在乎

姑妈的豆腐脑始终是最美味的豆腐脑

高中的时候，每周都会去姑妈那里尝尝她亲手做的豆腐脑，再多的烦恼和压力都会在闻到那熟悉的味道时烟消云散。姑妈的豆腐脑店，这一开，就是十二年。店面不大，两间门面加一个小院子，装修不华丽但却干净。我也不知道姑妈什么时候学会做豆腐脑，还能如此美味，后来工作了，也忘不了那个味道。

前几天回到小县城，照旧回去看看姑妈，姑妈的第一句话永远都是：“回来了啊，坐一会就好。” 不管此时有多忙，姑妈总会第一时间为我制好一碗热腾腾的豆腐脑。


提前炸好的酥肉、黄豆、芽菜。


上面是酱油、醋、红油等各种调料。


袖珍蒸笼里蒸着牛肉和肥肠。


我最爱的则是牛肉豆腐脑，这是豆腐脑刚端上来时候，灿黄的酥牛肉搭配鲜美多汁的豆腐脑是不是很诱人？


通常我们是这样搅拌均匀后就可以开吃啦！（也可以下饭吃）

吃了那么多年的“霸王餐”般的豆腐脑，真的很幸福。姑妈一直都很温柔，不论是说话还是做事，她把我当作女儿一样，每次回去看到姑妈的笑容，让我觉得很温暖。到现在我也数不清到底吃了多少碗豆腐脑，我不管，我永远都要吃这一顿霸王餐。

Write a Poem for You---My handwritten copy of the poem #8: Nostalgia 为你写诗--我的手抄诗歌#8：乡愁


It is better to live your own destiny imperfectly than to live an imitation of somebody else’s life with perfection.

不完美地走自己的命运比完美地模仿别人的生活还要好。

Dear steemit friends,

I want to share with you a poem Nostalgia, written by Yu Guangzhong. The original text is as follows:
今天想要和大家分享的一首诗，叫《乡愁》，作者余光中。原文如下：

Source

乡愁
小时候，
乡愁是一枚小小的邮票，
我在这头，
母亲在那头。
长大后，
乡愁是一张窄窄的船票，
我在这头，
新娘在那头。
后来啊，
乡愁是一方矮矮的坟墓，
我在外头，
母亲在里头。
而现在，
乡愁是一湾浅浅的海峡，
我在这头，
大陆在那头。

Translation version 译文

Nostalgia
When I was young,
my homesickness was a small stamp,
I was here,
my mother was there.
After growing up,
my homesickness was a narrow ticket,
I was here,
my bride was there.
Later,
my homesickness was a little tomb,
I was outside,
my mother was inside.
And now,
my homesickness is a shallow strait,
I am here,
the mainland is there.

The following is my handwritten copy of this poem.
以下是我的手抄本。

This poem can be called a classic one. Its author expressed his feelings and homesickness in the order of time and space. The Time: childhood, grown up, later and now; the space: the separation of mother and child, husband and wife, mother and child part forever and the separation of the wanderer and the mainland. Meanwhile, the author materialized his homesickness with four simple images, which made the poem easy to understand. And you can find the sentences in this poem read smoothly. This poem is something like the one On A Quiet Night written by the ancient poet Li Bai, both of which are used to express the author’s homesickeness. Now, many people use these two poems to express their nostalgia.

这首诗堪称经典，作者以时间和空间的顺序来抒发情感，表达乡愁。时间上：小时候，长大后，后来，现在；空间上：母子分离，夫妻分离，母子死别和游子与大陆的分离。同时，作者用四个简单的意象将乡愁具体化，让人通俗易懂，读起来也朗朗上口。这和古代诗人李白《静夜思》有异曲同工之妙，现在很多人都会借用这两首诗来表达自己的思乡之情。

Write a Poem for You---My handwritten copy of the poem #7: A Blossoming Tree为你写诗--我的手抄诗歌#7：一棵开花的树


Eat to live, but do not live to eat.

吃饭是为了生活，但生活并不仅仅为了吃饭。

Dear steemit friends,

I want to share with you a poem A Blossoming Tree, written by Xi Murong. The original text is as follows:
今天想要和大家分享的一首诗，叫《一棵开花的树》，作者席慕蓉。原文如下：

一棵开花的树
如何让你遇见我
在我最美丽的时刻
为这
我已在佛前求了五百年
求它让我们结一段尘缘
佛于是把我化作一棵树
长在你必经的路旁
阳光下慎重地开满了花
朵朵都是我前世的盼望
当你走近
请你细听
颤抖的叶是我等待的热情
而你终于无视地走过
在你身后落了一地的
朋友啊 那不是花瓣
是我凋零的心

Translation version 译文

A Blossoming Tree
May Buddha let us meet
in my most beautiful hours,
I have prayed for it
for five hundred years.
Buddha made me a tree
by the path you may take,
In full blossoms I’m waiting in the sun
every flower carrying my previous hope.
As you are near, listen carefully
the quivering leaves are my waiting zeal,
As you pass by the tree
without noticing me,
My friend, upon the ground behind you
is not the fallen petals but my withered heart.

The following is my handwritten copy of this poem.
以下是我的手抄本。

This poem is to compare a tree to a girl yearning for love. She have prayed for five hundred years in front of the Buddha. And the Buddha made her a tree. The author express the crazy heart so vividly; the second half of the poem tells falling petals are her withered heart, representing the sadness after missing. This adds a little melancholy to this poem. In fact, when the author wrote this poem, there is no such sentimentality. Just because when she took the train, she didn’t have time to look at the tree carefully and then wrote this poem.

这首诗是借树比喻一个怀春的少女，在佛前求了五百年只为一段尘缘以及化作一棵树，把一颗痴心痴情的心表达地淋漓尽致；而诗的后半段写道“落下的花瓣是她凋零的心”，代表着错过后的悲伤，给这首诗又平添几分惆怅。其实作者写这首诗时，并没有儿女情长那般复杂的背景，只是因为乘坐火车时因一棵“没有来得及细看的树”而有感而发。

How to Become a Little Bigger Minnow on Steemit--- Tips for New Steemians/如何在steemit上从小小鱼变成小鱼


First, I want to clarify that I am also a minnow. In fact, these tips are far from so-called strategies. I just want to share some tips summed up by myself when using this platform, especially with our friends in cn district. Some others have referred to some similar ones in their posts. But now, I want to make it more detailed. I have made some preparations before and hope it does work for you. Maybe someone will ask why I can not become a big whale but a little bigger minnow. This is my answer—Roman was not built in a day.🌞

首先声明我也是一只小小鱼，其实谈不上什么策略，想要分享在steemit的这段时间所总结出来的一些小技巧给新人, 尤其是cn区的朋友。关于这个话题，应该有人写过类似的，对此我做了一些准备和归纳。希望对大家所有帮助。也许有人会问为什么是小鱼不是大鲸，我想回答罗马不是一天建成的。🌞

1. A detailed self-introduction on steemit 一个完整的自我介绍

As the saying goes, well begun is half done. I still remembered the day when I joined steemit. I was confused at that time, and later I learned that a lot of friends that I followed have written a complete self-introduction post and their main tag is introduceyourself. So a self-introduction is needed. You are expected to give a more detailed introduction which can be related to your own interests and so on.
ATTENTION: 1. The first picture that you shared in your post needs to be your selfie, which makes it more realistic. 2. Add the date of joining steemit, its logo and your account name to the picture, like Introduction: Hi，Steemit！ I’m a new comer here to share my life and look for new friends./ 大家好，我是Steemit的新人！

一个好的开头是成功的一半。记得我当时刚入steemit的时候一头雾水，后来慢慢了解才发现关注的好多朋友都会有一个完整的自我介绍贴，主标签是introduceyourself。自我介绍尽量详尽，可以涉及到自己的兴趣爱好等。
注意：1. 第一张配图最好是自拍照，这样更真实。2. 附上加入steemit的日期，logo和你的账号名称。如：Introduction: Hi，Steemit！ I’m a new comer here to share my life and look for new friends./ 大家好，我是Steemit的新人！

2. Submit a post to @someone 投稿给 @someone

If you want to accumulate your first bucket of gold and improve your own community influence, which can be understood as Rep Value, I think the best way is to contribute. ATTENTION: 1. The article is original, bilingual(Only for the languages of Chinese and English), and is first published. (You can not summbit articles posted on steemit before.) 2. Beautiful articles are needed. Pay attention to the layout format, like Xiamen Impression, the Charm of Egret Island by @mrspointm. 3. If you write something about food, culture and tourism, it will be given priority. If you are interested, click here  to know more.

想要为自己积累第一桶金，提高自己的社区影响力，可以理解为rep值，我认为最好的方式就是投稿。但需要注意的有以下几点：1. 文章需要原创，中英双语，并且是首发。（不能发表已在steemit的文章）2. 文章尽量精美，注意排版格式。（可以参看已发表的文章格式，如厦门印象，鹭岛风情 by @mrspointm） 3. 最好的介绍美食，文化，旅游的，这些会优先发表。 具体参见中文社区内容推广计划框架(修正版) & 试运行期优惠已截止 

3. Take part in Google Calls the Roll contest hosted by @jubi “谷歌点名”活动

Google Calls the Roll is more an activity than a contest. You can share your feelings with all of us. Now, it has come to the second stage. Each stage will give a topic once a week. All participants need to write an article around the topic, preferably written in Chinese and its style is not limited. Finally, if you post can be searched through Google and ranks the first three, you will be rewarded. Of course, Excellent awards are included. I think this activity is brilliant for new steemians. First, you do not worry about what to write. Second, you can get rewarded because of your article and are likely to win the prize. Moreover, @jubi will share the article of each participant, which increases the click rate of your post. This time the topic is about “the first time”. If you have an interest, you can click here to know more.

与其说谷歌点名是比赛，不如说它是活动—分享感想。现在已进行到第二期，每一期会有一个话题，为期一周，参与者需要围绕话题写一篇文章，最好是中文，文体不限。最后以google搜索排名为准，当然也有优秀奖。：） 我认为这个活动对于新人来说是极好的，第一省去了选题的麻烦；第二既有文章原本的收入，也有最后的奖励；第三，jubi会将参与者的文章分享给大家，这样也增加了点击率，不是吗？感兴趣的朋友，可以一试。这一期主题是“第一次”。具体参见关于“谷哥点名”游戏的初步设想

### 4. Take part in Minnow Support Project Weekly Resteem 小鱼计划每周转发活动

首先，转发这个主题活动，然后在这个主题活动下留言，将你已发表的帖子链接附在上面。如果你得到的票数越高，那么就很有可能获得 @minnowsupport的转发和奖励。但需要注意，你的文章最好是星期一发布，这样的话在周四你的帖子收益还没到账，就有可能会得到更高的点赞收益。具体参见Minnow Support Project Weekly Resteem: 50 SBD Prize Pool!

5. Write a series of good articles 写出优质文章—最好是连载文章

I personally think that writing a series of goodarticles can help everyone to know you better. Especially when in a large number of posts, they can recognize your post right now. Such as @sweetssj—her articles about Miss. Delicious and Travel with me, @mrpointp—There is always someone keeping working out and Never Say Goodbye, @cnfund—After Creation and My posts—Write a Poem for You—My handwritten copy of the poem and so on. Perhaps not all of us can get such a high income as Miss Sweetssj, but believe me that if you insist on writing, you can get a stable income.

我个人认为写一个系列的文章，可以让大家更好地认识你，在海量的帖子中一眼就能看到你的。比如 @sweetsssj 小姐姐的Miss. Delicious 和Travel with me 系列文章，又如 @mrpointp的《总有一个比你忙，比你穷，比你老的人在健身》系列和《不说再见说珍重》小说系列，还有 @cnfund的《创世之后》玄幻小说系列，还有我最近的一个系列—为你写诗，我的手抄诗歌 等等。也许不是所有人都会像甜心姐姐一样有那么高的收益，但只要坚持写下去一定会有很稳定的收益。

6. Comment and upvote other steemians’ posts 多与朋友们互动和点赞

This is a free platform where you can communicate and interact with any friends. Sometimes, you read others’ articles, you gain more inspiration and harvest. Getting along with others sincerely, you will be treated in the same way.

这是一个自由的平台，你可以跟任何朋友交流和互动。有时候多看看别人的文章，反而更有灵感和收获。抱着真心跟别人相处，别人也会同样对你的。

All above are my opinions and hope they can do work for you. Meanwhile, you are welcomed to put forward your own ideas.❤️

以上就是我本人的拙见，希望能够帮助到大家。同时，欢迎大家提出自己的想法。❤️

Write a Poem for You---My handwritten copy of the poem #6: Facing the Sea with Spring Blossoms为你写诗--我的手抄诗歌#6：面朝大海，春暖花开


Don’t compare your life to others, you have no idea what their journey is all about. No one is in charge of your happiness except you.

不要拿自己的生活跟比人比，因为你无法了解别人的经历。没人能主宰你的幸福，除了你自己。

Dear steemit friends,

I want to share with you a poem called Facing the Sea with Spring Blossoms, written by Hai Zi. The original text is as follows:
今天想要和大家分享的一首诗，叫《面朝大海，春暖花开》，作者海子。原文如下：

面朝大海，春暖花开
从明天起，做一个幸福的人
喂马、劈柴，周游世界
从明天起，关心粮食和蔬菜
我有一所房子，面朝大海，春暖花开
从明天起，和每一个亲人通信
告诉他们我的幸福
那幸福的闪电告诉我的
我将告诉每一个人
给每一条河每一座山取一个温暖的名字
陌生人，我也为你祝福
愿你有一个灿烂的前程
愿你有情人终成眷属
愿你在尘世获得幸福
我只愿面朝大海，春暖花开

Translation version 译文

Facing the Sea with Spring Blossoms
From tomorrow on
I will be a happy man
Grooming, chopping
and traveling all over the world
From tomorrow on
I will care foodstuff and vegetable
I have a house towards the sea
with spring blossoming
From tomorrow on
write to each of my dear ones
Telling them of my happiness
What the lightening of happiness has told me
I will spread it to each of them
Give a warm name for every river and every mountain
Strangers, I will also give you my well-wishing
May you have a brilliant future
May your lovers eventually become spouse
May you enjoy happiness in this earthly world
I only wish to face the sea, with spring flowers blossoming

The following are the handwritten copies of this poem by me and my mother.
以下是我和我母亲的手抄本。


This one is written by me. 我的手抄诗


This is written by my mother. 我母亲写的手抄诗

I have read this poem several years before. My first impression of it is warm and serene, especially the last sentence “ I only wish to face the sea, with spring flowers blossoming” is popular among us. We often hear someone quote it. The author Hai Zi’s mood is too hard to understand. I believe that many people like me just purely love this poem. Hai Zi has used the simple words to express his feelings about freedom and real spiritual world.

很久以前就读过这首诗，带给我的第一印象就是温暖安详，尤其是最后一句“面朝大海，春暖花开”更是成为脍炙人口的佳句，常常会听见有人引用。作者海子的心境太深奥，相信有许多人和我一样只是纯粹地喜爱这首诗，诗中将海子的渴望展现地淋漓尽致，渴望回归自然和真正的精神世界。


Finally, share with you the video of the poem.

最后跟大家分享《面朝大海，春暖花开》的诗朗诵。

My first encounter with steemit/我与steemit的第一次邂逅 | 谷歌点名

自从上一次参加了 @jubi举办的谷歌点名后，瞬间点燃写作激情。感谢jubi给出一个这么好的平台，让我静下心来写作，去思考，去放空。

人生有很多个第一次，有难忘的，有失望的，有开心的，有惊喜的，也有悲伤的。对我而言，还有个特殊的第一次，那就是接触到steemit这个平台。

记得去年的这个时候，就有朋友推荐过这个网站，开玩笑说道我是一个那么喜欢发朋友圈的人，不玩这个真是可惜了。我当时没太在意，总觉得麻烦就此作罢。后来, @mrpointp开始在这个平台上发表文章后，我才开始真正地接触。还记得第一次打开网站时的迷茫，第一次学习markdown，第一次post，第一次upvote，第一次resteem, 第一次reply，第一次comment，第一次参加小鱼比赛，第一次得到别人的打赏，第一次投稿… … 经历越多，越发想要了解更多。

这是一个自由的平台，我们可以分享任何事物。现在每天写文章，已经成了一种日常。出门在外，手机随拍摄影也成为了日常。虽然有时候会觉得很疲惫，但每次看到有人回复，有人鼓励，有人赞赏，就会觉得很值得。

世界很大，我们很小。但这个平台给了我们一个更广阔的空间，学习交流一些不曾见闻的东西，每一天都是新鲜的。衷心感谢steemit !
