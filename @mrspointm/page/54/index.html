Hi, steemit friends!💙

If you speak to a local about your stay in Sichuan, they are likely to ask whether you’ve tried hot pot. Everyone living here seems to love it, and trying it should be a fixed part of eveyone’s culinary tour of Sichuan. Now, I’d like to share some photos for hot pot which I enjoyed last weekend.

如果跟一个当地人说起你会在四川逗留了几天，他们肯定会问你有没有去吃火锅。似乎住在这的每一个都爱火锅，品尝火锅已经成为了开始四川美食之旅的正确打开方式。今天想要跟大家分享我前几天吃到的麻辣火锅的摄影作品。

Hot pot

What attracted me first was the special design of the pot. This time we ordered half spicy and half clear broth which is suitable for people of all ages.

最先吸引我的是这个锅的造型—老虎头十分抢眼。这次我们选择了鸳鸯锅，口味老少皆宜。

Spicy sauce

Spicy sauce is a must for us to enjoy hot pot. We need to mix the seasonings based on our own taste.

蘸碟是我们吃火锅时的必备伴侣。我们会根据自己的口味来调制蘸水。

Side dishes

Fat beef slices and duck feet are my favorite. The ultrathin potato chips taste also very good. It only takes 15 seconds to enjoy.

肥牛和鸭掌都是我最爱的荤菜，超薄土豆片的味道也是非常赞，只需要烫15秒就能享用。

Snack

Green dumplings made me a big impression. @mrpointp has shared it before and the dumpling wrapper is made of spinach juice and flour.

绿叶饺子让人眼前一亮，之前 @mrpointp已分享过，饺子皮是菠菜汁和面粉制成的。

Hot pot is like a carnival for a group of people. They chat while eating, talking about their life.

火锅就是一群人的狂欢，边吃边聊，畅谈人生。

Hi, steemit friends!💙

Almost each kind of flower has its own flower language derived from the characteristics. Today, I’d like to share some photos of Brunfelsia latifolia, from which you may notice its color. In fact, its three types of colors stand for “ yesterday, today and tomorrow”. Some people think it also stand for an inconstant person.

几乎每一种类型的花都有它的花语，这是由花本身的习性而得来的。今天想要跟大家分享的是鸳鸯茉莉的一组照片，可能你们已经注意到了它的颜色。事实上，鸳鸯茉莉共有三种不同的颜色，分别是蓝紫色、雪青色和白色，就好像代表着“昨天、今天和明天”。不过，有人认为它的颜色有“见异思迁”的寓意。

Brunfelsia latifolia 鸳鸯茉莉


Brunfelsia latifolia, also known as two-colored jasmine, is native to Central and South America. It grows in a warm, humid and sunny climate. The weather in Sichuan is exactly suitable for the growth of this plant.

鸳鸯茉莉，又称二色茉莉，原产于中美洲及南美洲热带，性喜温暖、湿润、光照充足的气候条件。刚好四川的气候很适合这种植物的生长。

Due to its different flowering seasons, it always presents a tree with two kinds of colors. If you walk past it, you are likely to smell the scent at a distance. Its color and scent makes it an important potted plant.

由于它不同的开花期，呈现出一树两色花。从旁边经过远远就能闻到茉莉香，这使得它成为了重要的盆栽观赏花卉。

Here comes the question. Where do I find this plant? In the park? At school? In my neighborhood? Or anywhere else?

那么问题来了。我是在哪里找到的茉莉花？公园，学校，小区还是其他什么地方？
