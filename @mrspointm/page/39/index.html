「探店」星巴克各色饮品 Various Drinks in Starbucks

Hi, steemit friends!💙

In such hot summer, we probably drink some cold drinks to avoid the summer heat. Today, I’d like to introduce some special drinks in Starbucks. Imagine that when you go shopping with your friends for a while, it is a good idea to have one. Starbucks is a suitable place where you can have a rest and relax yourself.

夏日炎炎，难免想喝点冷饮，今天想给大家介绍几款星巴克的特色饮品。可以想象一下，当你和朋友逛街太久口干舌燥时，是不是很想喝一杯冰冰凉凉的饮品，星巴克的环境和味道就很适合。


Since most of you know its surroundings, I won’t focus on this part. Its chain stores can be seen in most cities with its same decorative style. I’ve created a new membership card, for there are three chances to buy a one-for-one. Do you think it is cute?

星巴克的环境就不用过多介绍了，因为许多城市都能看见它的连锁店，装潢都是一个风格。今天还新办了一张会员卡，有三次买一送一的机会。有没有觉得会员卡很可爱？


This one is a new product of Starbucks and I always like the shape of such drinks, thus ordering it. Gently take a sip, and you will feel the aroma of Mocha, which leads to endless aftertaste.

这杯酷黑摩卡是星巴克的新品，一直很喜欢这种形状的饮品，所以首先就点了这杯。轻轻吸一口，浓香的摩卡味就窜入口中，回味无穷。


Mid-night mocha 酷黑摩卡

Frankly speaking, it tastes just so so. But I think there may be a lot of people ordering this drink, for its name really sounds fresh. In addition to water and ice, some peach sarcocarp is also added to it so that the customer s do not feel it a monotonous drink.

客观地说，这被饮品味道一般。不过我觉得应该会有不少人点这杯饮品，因为它带给我们的感觉就是清新淡雅。这杯绿茶，除了水和冰，还加入了一种蜜桃果肉的东西，让客人喝起来不会感觉太单调。


Iced shaken peach green oolong tea 冰摇桃桃绿茶

This one is my favorite. For girls, we enjoy a drink just because it’s good-looking. The main color is pink. With red and white milk foam, it makes people warm. As for the taste, I like the whole taste with chocolate, which is very unique.

这杯星冰乐是最令我喜欢的，对于女生来说，喜欢一杯饮品有一半的原因或许是它的外观。它的主要颜色是粉色，红白相间的奶泡，让人感觉很温馨。至于味道，我很喜欢它整体味道中带一丝巧克力味，很独特。


Strawberry white chocolate flavored frappuccino 草莓白巧克力风味星冰乐

I remember that each time I go to Starbucks, I always order frappuccino, which can be regarded as a typical one in Starbucks. I think frappuccino is good in all respects except for the fact that it is a bit too sweet. If you drink it before a meal, it will be likely to affect your appetite. In the following ones, I like Matcha,which is less sweet than others.

记得每次去星巴克的时候，几乎每次都点星冰乐，它应该算是星巴克中特别经典的饮品了。但我总认为，星冰乐各方面都还不错，就是甜味有点太多。饭前喝的话会很影响食欲，在几种星冰乐里，我最喜欢喝抹茶味，因为相比其他的口味，它算是不那么甜腻的。

【CN区美食大赛#2】家乡菜之钵钵鸡 Local food — Bobo Chicken

Hi, steemit friends!💙

Maybe you have heard of Sichuan-style skewers or Malatang(something like hot pot), but you may not know that they still exist in another form, that is, the “Bobo Chicken” that originated from Leshan.


Bobo chicken, which has existed since the Qing dynasty, is one of the traditional snacks in Chengdu and Leshan. In our dialect, “bobo” means crock pots. The outside of the crock is beautifully painted with dragon symbols. To make this snack, some spicy condiments are needed to put in the crock.

Then some dishes must be specially processed and held up with the bamboo skewers. After the dishes cool down, people put them into the crock. In summer, we always choose this snack, because it tastes good and doesn’t burn the mouth.


When speaking of its name, most people may think there is only chicken inside! In fact, the ingredients in the pot are diverse, such as boneless chicken feet, pork bellies, beef, quail eggs, pigskin and duck intestines, etc. The vegetarian dishes include fungus, bamboo shoots and lettuce, etc. I can say that almost all the ingredients we usually like to eat can be made into the pot.

In addition to spicy soup, the soup with the taste of the Zanthoxylum schinifolium etzucc is also very popular, and another kind of soup with sesame flavor is suitable for people who do not like spicy food. It is said that the key to the delicious bobo chicken lies in its oil made of Zanthoxylum schinifolium etzucc. The unique scent makes the soup quite delicious, while the pepper oil and chili oil easily lead to excessive internal heat.


If you come to Leshan, you will find almost everyone eating this food with this local drink (as you can see from the picture above) on the table.

Compared with Sichuan-style skewers(something like hot pot), bobo chicken won’t make you sweat while eating. In Leshan, you can always find restaurants that offer bobo chicken, but if you want to enjoy the best one, you’d better go to popular food destinations such as Leshan Port and Old Park.

As for me, bobo chicken can represent one of the traditional dishes in Leshan. It’s just like hot pot can represent Sichuan and Chongqing cuisine. It is a symbol of a city. If you have a chance to visit here, do have a try.

也许你知道四川的串串香或者麻辣烫，但可能你还不知道他们还以另外一种形式存在着，那就是起源于乐山的“钵钵鸡”。

钵钵鸡,清代就已经存在,是四川成都和乐山的传统小吃。“钵钵”其实就是瓦罐，钵外面是画着红黄相间的瓷质龙纹，钵内盛放配以麻辣为主的佐料。菜品在特殊加工后用签串制，晾冷浸于各种口味的佐料中。在夏天，我们通常会选择品尝钵钵鸡，因为真的是太美味了,而且不烫嘴。

光听名字，大家一定认为里面只有鸡肉吧！其实钵钵鸡的食材多种多样，有荤有素。荤菜有无骨鸡爪、猪肚、牛肉、鹌鹑蛋、猪皮、鸭肠等，素菜有木耳、笋子、莴笋等，可以说几乎平常我们会吃到的食材都可以制成钵钵鸡。

除了麻辣味的汤料，藤椒味也十分受欢迎，芝麻味适合不喜欢吃辣的人。据说钵钵鸡美味的关键就在于藤椒油，那独特的香味使汤料变得十分鲜美，而花椒油和辣椒油容易让人上火。

如果来乐山，你会发现几乎每个人吃着这种小吃，桌上放着一瓶峨眉雪（正如上图可见）。

与滚烫的串串香相比，钵钵鸡不会让人边吃边冒汗。在乐山，几乎到处都能找到做钵钵鸡的餐馆，但想要吃味道更好的钵钵鸡，最好还是往热门的美食地，例如乐山港和老公园附近。钵钵鸡完全可以以家乡菜代表乐山美食，就像火锅可以代表四川和重庆美食一样，是一座城市的标志，欢迎朋友们来此品尝钵钵鸡。

「旅行」峨眉山游记（下）My Trip to Mount Emei

Hi, steemit friends!💙

The Golden Summit, also known as Huazang Temple, is the last stop for our trip. In this place, you can watch the four natural wonders of Mt. Emei—sunrise, the sea of clouds, the buddha surrounded by lights and Shengdeng. Please come with me to appreciate the beauty of the Golden Summit!

峨眉山金顶也称华藏寺，是峨眉游山的最终目的地，在金顶可以观看峨眉四大奇观——日出、云海、佛光、圣灯。跟我一起去看看金顶的美景吧！


「旅行」峨眉山游记（上）My Trip to Mount Emei

After getting out of the cable car, we climbed the stairs for a few more minutes and finally came to the Golden Summit we were longing to see. It was really sacred and spectacular. Looking up at the golden statue of Ten party Samantabhadra Bodhisattva, I felt a sense of reverence, and there were some tourists worshiping reverently.

下了缆车，再爬几分钟的楼梯，终于来到我们向往的金顶，真的非常神圣和壮观。仰视着十方普贤菩萨的金像，内心的崇敬感油然而生，我们也可以看见一些游客礼拜着神圣的金像。


Compared with Leidongping, the temperature in the Golden Summit is much lower as if it were early winter. Thus, if you want to visit here, do prepare thick clothes or rent a down jacket there. Standing at the top of Mt. Emei, I felt the world was getting smaller. No wonder that ancient poets standing in high places couldn’t help reading or creating poems.

金顶和雷洞坪的温差也非常明显，那种差距好比初冬和秋天，所以要去峨眉山金顶的话尽量准备厚衣服或在山上租一件羽绒服。站在峨眉山的顶端，才让我真正体会到什么是一览众山小，怪不得古代诗人站在高处总喜欢吟诗作对，实属情不自禁！



It is worth mentioning that you can also overlook Mt. Gongga. Of course, watching the sunrise is a good choice, but you have to get up at around 4:30 a.m. It’s a pity that I have been there twice but I never saw the sunrise, for I got up too late. The photos of the sunrise taken by others is gorgeous.

值得一提的是在金顶还能远眺贡嘎山，当然看日出是个不错的选择，但是早上最晚4点半就起床。光看看别人分享的日出图片就很美了，肉眼中的日出一定更震撼，无奈的是我去了两次都没看到日出。


Besides the place above, there is another good one to take photos, which is called the Golden Mouth, where you can watch the unpredictable clouds. Golden Mouth consists of numerous deep cliffs which stick far out. This place looks like a cliff mouth. On sunny days, you may enjoy the distant beautiful views on the top of Mt. Emei.

除了上图这个地方外，金顶有一处观景视线绝佳的位置，名为金刚嘴，可以观看变幻莫测的云海。金刚嘴是由玄武岩组成的柱状节理行程悬崖峭壁，峭削如棱，翘首云空，形同岩嘴。在晴朗乌云的时候，俯瞰百里平川，如铺锦绣。



The locks on the love chains carry people’s best wishes, which are about love, friendship and kinship.

两旁的同心锁承载着人们的美好祝愿，关乎爱情，友情，亲情。


In addition to the Golden Statue, there are many Buddhist constructions, like Woyun Temple, which give us visitors a sense of quietness.

除了金像，这里还有许多佛教的建筑，比如卧云禅院，带给游客清幽的感觉。



As there was no cable car service at that time, we had to walk down to Leidongping. ATTENTION: It is necessary to buy a bamboo pole or a walking stick. First, you can avoid being attacked by monkeys. Second, it is more stable when walking. It is recommended that the elderly not walk down the mountain, which may easily damage their knees.

由于缆车收班，我们只能走路下山至雷洞坪。买一根竹竿非常有必要，一是可以防止猴子袭击，二是走起路来更稳，建议老年人不要走路上下山，容易损伤膝盖。



Tips: If you do not plan to live in the mountains, it is best to set out in the early morning, for the sightseeing cars and cable cars will stop working at around 5 or 6 pm. Instead, if you want to watch the sunrise next day, you need book a room in Golden Summit ahead of time.

小提示：如果不打算在山上住的朋友最好在上午进山，因为旅游大巴和缆车下午五六点就收班了。如果你想在山上住一宿并且想看第二天的日出，那就预订金顶的酒店。


In this hot August, it’s appropriate to visit Mt. Emei for a vacation. Take your family or friends here to experience the high mountains, the unpredictable clouds and the magical charm of nature.

时值八月，正是炎热的季节，现在来峨眉山度假正好，带着家人或朋友来此地感受山的巍峨峻拔，云的变幻莫测以及大自然的神奇魅力吧。

「旅行」峨眉山游记（上）My Trip to Mount Emei

Hi, steemit friends!💙

The temperature in summer in Sichuan is not as hot as it is in other places, but its humidity may make you fully experience a “sticky” feeling, which is like a natural sauna. With the holidays going on, @mrpointp and I took a spontaneous trip to Mount Emei.

四川夏天的温度和国内其他地方相比还算不上热到令人发指，但这里城市的湿度能让你完美体验到一种“黏糊糊”的感觉，那是天然的桑拿房。趁着假期还没结束，和 @mrpointp来了趟说走就走的周边游，峨眉山。


Mount Emei is famous not only because it is the natural and cultural relics of the world but also because of the Buddhism culture. It’s one of the Four Buddhist Holy Mountains. Wanfo Summit is the highest, around 3099 meters (euqal to 10167 feet) above sea level.

峨眉山不仅仅是世界文化与自然双遗产还因其佛教文化而扬名，它是中国“四大佛教名山”之一。山上的万佛顶最高，海拔3099米。


Mount Emei, located in Leshan, Sichuan Province, is a famous tourist attraction at home and abroad, which is an ideal place for mountain climbers, but many people do not know that Mount Emei is a perfect summer resort. When the weather is hot, our locals are likely to choose a good farmhouse in Mount Emei and stay for a few days , and even some live there for one month. Next, I will take you to appreciate the beautiful scenery of Mount Emei.

峨眉山，坐落在四川省乐山市境内，是扬名中外的旅游胜地，这里是爬山爱好者理想的地方，但许多人不知道，峨眉山更是夏季避暑的度假胜地。尤其是本地的老百姓，天气炎热的时候会在峨眉山找一家不错的农家乐度几天假，有些人更是一住一个月。接下来，我将带一起游览峨眉山。


Most people like to drive up the mountain, but this is absolutely not the way I recommend. Instead, I suggest you take the sightseeing car. For one thing, there are countless bends on the road. For another, all tourists’ private cars must be placed in a parking lot halfway to the summit. If you want to go to the Golden Summit( the top of the mountain), you have to take a bus.

很多人喜欢自驾上山，但这不是我推荐的方式，我比较建议坐观光车上山。一是上山路弯道无数，二是自家的车也只能在途中的某个停车场停放，想要去金顶还是要坐大巴车。


We bought tickets at Huangwan Station and parked our car there, including entrance ticket and round-trip ticket. The price of entrance ticket for adults is 185 yuan (equal to $27) and the latter one costs 90 yuan (equal to $13). Some tourists choose to spend two days walking up the mountain completely. No matter which way you choose, you need to buy tickets. In addition, do prepare all the necessities.

我们是在黄湾站买票搭乘旅游大巴，其中包含门票和往返车票。成人门票是185元（除市民，仅10元），往返车票是90元。还有部分游客选择完全步行上山，不论用什么方式上山都是需要买门票的，另外，准备好一切必需品。


Here is a panoramic map of Mount Emei. As you can see from the photo, there are so many sights that are worth visiting, such as Wannian Temple, Leidongping etc.

在此给大家分享一张峨眉山的全景游览图，图中有很多景点都值得一去，比如万年寺，雷洞坪。


The two-hour drive is tiring, but when you get off at Leidongping, you will feel the coolness as it is in autumn. It is recommended to bring at least one overcoat or jacket, because of the large temperature difference between the foot and the top of the mountain.

两个小时的车程还是让人挺疲倦的，但在雷洞坪下车后感受到秋天般的凉爽就感觉一切都值得了。建议大家至少带一件外套，因为山上和山下的温度相差巨大。


If your destination is the Golden Summit, you will have two choices. One is to climb the mountain, and the other is to take the cable car, about two or three minutes, but it takes at least an hour to climb. There are monkeys in the mountain, and they like to interact with people, but it is said that monkeys there will grab things from tourists, so take care of your belongings.

如果你的目的地是金顶，那么你有两种选择。要么从雷洞坪爬到山顶，要么买票坐缆车吧，大概两三分钟，但爬山至少要一个小时左右。山上有猴，还会与人互动，但据说猴子会抢游客手上的东西，所以务必要保管好随身物品。


Let’s stop here. Next chapter I will share the beautiful sights on the Golden Summit. Hope you can enjoy it.

好啦！就暂且搁笔啦，过几天我将为大家具体介绍金顶的美景，敬请期待吧！

「探店」A Nice Japanese Restaurant 和风寿司店

These days I have been busy preparing for our wedding, so seldom do I have time to cook a meal at home. This nice Japanese restaurant visited by @mrpointp and me frequently. The delicious food there is of high quality, so I can’t wait to share these dishes with you.

Octopus Ball



This is one of my favorite snacks, and every time I go to a Japanese restaurant, I always order it. Octopus ball, also known as tokoyaki, originated in Japan and mainly consists of octopus ingredients, octopus burning powder, seaweed and octopus sauce, etc. (Actually, the way to make it varies from restaurant to restaurant).

Cucumber Hoso-maki





Among all kinds of sushi, cucumber rolls are easy to make. We can find the ingredients in the market easily, usually seaweed chips and cucumber. There is no such special flavor, but it tastes really refreshing.
Super Spicy Ramen





It’s a kind of famous Japanese ramen. Inside it are seaweed, fungus, egg and beef. The soup is spicy but delicious. If you are not used to spicy food, you’d better not drink the soup.
Hand-rolled Salmon





I don’t like sashimi, but salmon is OK for me, especially wrapped in seaweed slices. And there is no fishy smell. Usually I add some mustard mixed with soy sauce to this hand roll.
California Roll





To make California roll, you need to spread the rice on the seaweed slice, and then reverse the seaweed to wrap the ingredients, like crab meat and cucumber. I have tried to make California roll myself before. Compared with the one made by me, this California roll is so delicate.
Japanese-style beef with rice





This Japanese-style beef with rice is a favorite of @mrpointp. Since he doesn’t like sushi, he always order this dish every time he goes there. By the way, the free soup also tastes good.

这几天忙于筹备婚礼基本上都没在家做饭，所以都在外面吃。@mrpointp和我经常光顾这家日料店。味道满意，性价比也高，所以迫不及待地想给大家分享我们今天的美食。

章鱼小丸子是我最喜爱的小吃之一，也几乎是我去寿司店必点的一道美食。章鱼小丸子又名章鱼烧，起源于日本，成份主要是章鱼，章鱼烧粉，海苔，章鱼烧酱等（不过每家餐厅的做法稍有不同）。

青瓜细卷应该是所有寿司中不那么难做的寿司，需要运用的食材也比较容易买到，通常是海苔片和黄瓜。没有特别的味道，尝起来比较清爽。

地狱拉面是日本著名拉面的一种，里面加入了海苔，木耳，鸡蛋和肥牛，烫味鲜美。如果不太能吃辣的话，最好还是不要喝汤。

我不太喜欢吃生鱼片，但是可以接受三文鱼，尤其是包裹在海苔片里面，腥味不会那么大，通常我会蘸一点芥末和酱油。

加州卷的做法通常是把米饭铺在海苔上，再反转以海苔那一面包裹食材，再加入蟹肉和青瓜。我自己也尝试过做加州卷，但完全没有上图我们吃到的这份加州卷那么精致。

这份日式牛肉饭是 @mrpointp的最爱，由于他不喜欢吃寿司一类食物，所以几乎每次去那里吃饭他都会点它。另外，这份饭配送的汤也很美味。
