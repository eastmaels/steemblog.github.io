Wangfujing shopping mall in Leshan was opened up around two or three years ago, and the cafe named Chun Coffee has come into being there since then. Leshan, a tourist city, is lacking in such a large dessert store. Since it opened, I thought I could occasionally come for some drinks but in fact I have visited it twice in three years.


Compared with the design style of Starbucks, its style makes me more comfortable, and there are much more furnishings and decorations. Now, I will introduce to you this cafe and its delicious food.


At the entrance of the shop stands a four-meter-high tree, on which many bird models can be seen.


Here is an European-style vintage bookshelf, plant-filled stairs, birds in the golden iron cage, and bare branches.


This is the brief information for a certain kind of coffee, but what attracts me most is its creative decorations.


Lemon and cucumber soda is free of charge, which can be taken from this table, so there is no need for you to order drinks.


There are teddy bears of different colours on each table, for each one stands for a number. The waiter will serve the dish according to the bear (number) you get. Isn’t it special?

Now, it’s time for desserts! 😋🍹🍮



Multilayer Cake with Mango

With a great flavor, this one looks very delicate. On the upper layer are a blueberry, a piece of mango, fresh ice-cream and a peppermint leaf. But the mango is not fresh, looking like canned mango.


Honey Bread

Anyone who favors bread can have a try. The bread itself is sweet, and with some honey and jam added, it is much more delicious.


Milk tea with marshmallow

The taste of milk tea is brilliant, which differs from the milk tea sold in the common tea stores. The tea flavor is more than its sweetness.

I like drinks and desserts there, but I prefer its atmosphere and surroundings. With wide space, the whole store is full of natural atmosphere. If my introduction really attracts you, you might as well pay a visit to it.

乐山的王府井是在两三年前开的业，淳咖啡也随之开业了。乐山这座旅游城市正是缺少这类大型的甜品店。当时我就想着，偶尔可以来淳咖啡喝点东西，但实际上三年间也就光顾过两次。

相对星巴克充满设计感的装修风格，淳咖啡的风格更有舒适感，摆设和装饰也更加饱满。接下来我将为大家介绍一下淳咖啡的店貌和美味的食物们。

进门便是一棵四米高的树，满树都是鸟模型。

欧式复古书架、植物萦绕的楼梯、金色铁笼中的鸟儿、光秃秃的树枝。

某款咖啡的介绍，我觉得这种摆设很有创意。

柠檬黄瓜水自取台，所以来这里点不点饮品都可以。

每个桌上都有不同颜色的小熊，每一只小熊相当于一个取餐牌，有没有很别致？

芒果千层蛋糕

这份蛋糕口感不错，看起来也是十分精致的。最上层是一颗蓝莓，薄荷叶，鲜奶油和芒果丁，但唯一不足就是芒果不是使用新鲜芒果，貌似是芒果罐头。

蜂蜜面包

蜂蜜面包，喜欢吃面包的朋友去淳咖啡就一定要尝尝这款面包。面包本身带甜味，再蘸适量的蜂蜜和果酱，就更加美味了。

棉花糖奶茶

奶茶的味道很棒，和一般奶茶店出售的奶茶区别很大，茶味胜于甜味。

我喜欢淳咖啡的饮品和甜点，但我更喜欢这里的氛围，空间开阔，整个店面充满了自然的气息。如果我的介绍吸引了你，不妨去淳咖啡品尝一番。

Natural Restaurant



Today I will continue to introduce a restaurant in Phuket Town, which is called Natural Restaurant. Frankly speaking, the highlight of this restaurant is not the taste of the dishes, but its environment. The environment and atmosphere there is my favorite among the restaurants that I have visited these days. If you like taking pictures, do come to this three-storeyed restaurant, which is gorgeous and natural.

Let’s take a look at its surroundings



The small ponding next to our dining table seems particularly nice when it rains.


The fish swimming in the old TV set-shaped tank makes me interested. I also want to buy one like this.


On the first floor, there is a large dining table with local decorations featured.


Is the puppy made of wood carved around the stairway very cute?



Surrounded by vibrant green plants, it is like enjoying a big meal in the rainforest.

If it is not so hot,dining on the top floor will be quite good. The green plants, the signboards and the sky form a poetic picture.

The following are some dishes ordered by us





Braised Chicken Wrapped in Pandanus Leaves

At first we thought it was something like Chinese rice dumplings. In fact, it’s made of chicken with curry favor, and it smells nice.




Spring Rolls

Spring rolls are not the same as I imagined. The upper layer is crispy and the inside seems to be made of silk noodles. With the sweet sauce served, it’s really delicious.




Deep Fried Stuffed Tofu with Peanut Sauce

I have ordered fried tofu several times in Phuket. Compared with our Sichuan-tyle tofu, this one is mild tasting.




Thai Style Fried Noodles with Fresh Shrimp

Thai seafood fried noodles are worthy of a try, and when in Phuket, you may as well order such delicious dish.




Chicken Biryani

I favor this meal because the taste really agrees with me. With a little curry, it is spicy.




Summer Drinks

Watermelon juice is simply a good way to avoid the summer heat, which really quenches my thirst. The ice-cold milk tea is also great.

Dining in the Natural Restaurant, the most enjoyable is still the environment. If we visit Phuket in winter, we might be able to fully experience this restaurant. We will choose to eat on the top floor, enjoying the sunshine and breeze.



今天将继续介绍一家普吉岛餐厅，名为自然餐厅，位于普吉镇。客观地说，这家餐厅最大的亮点不在于菜的味道，而是环境，自然餐厅的环境是那些天所到的餐厅中我最喜欢的。如果你很喜欢拍照，真的要来这家三层楼的餐厅，漂亮得很自然。

一起来看看自然餐厅的环境：

餐桌旁的小水洼，下雨时尤其好看。鱼儿徜徉在电视机式的鱼缸里，特别抢眼，我也好想这样养一条。一楼，富有当地装饰特色的大餐桌。楼梯口的木雕小狗，是不是很可爱？四处都是生机勃勃的绿色植物，就像是热带雨林里享受大餐。

如果天气不那么热，在顶楼吃饭就非常有感觉了。绿色植物、招牌和天空整个构成了诗意般的画面。

下面将介绍餐桌上的主角们：

鸡肉团子
一开始我们都以为这是类似粽子的东西，其实它里面包着的是鸡肉，有点咖喱的味道，特别香。

春卷
这和我想象中的不太一样。表面那一层又脆又香，里面好像是粉丝做的，蘸一下酱更有味道。

油炸豆腐
这道菜在普吉岛我不止点过一次，相比四川味道的豆腐，这份鱼豆腐更为清淡。

泰式海鲜炒面
这道菜属于必尝菜，而这家餐厅的炒面还是很美味的，推荐。

鸡肉饭
我比较喜欢这份饭，因为它更适合我的口味。带一点咖喱的味道，但更主要是麻辣的味道。

饮品
西瓜汁简直是解暑神器，既能解渴又不那么甜腻。自然餐厅的加冰原味奶茶也很棒。

在自然餐厅吃饭，最享受的仍然是环境。如果我们是在冬天去普吉岛，或许能更多地感受这家餐厅，我们会选择在顶楼用餐，晒着太阳、吹着小风。
