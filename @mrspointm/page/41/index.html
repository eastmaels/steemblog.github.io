Innisfree Green Cafe


Today, I’d like to share with you a super sweet dessert shop. Located in Taikoo Li, Chengdu, it is attached to Innisfree, a cosmetics company. The desserts there taste so delicious, unbelievable! At first, I was really shocked to hear that there was a dessert shop run by this company. I even worried about whether facial cleanser would be added to desserts,lol…

Holding tried state of mind, @mrpointp together with I went to this place. Surprisingly, both its desserts and decorations are fresh and transcendent, just as the ad says. Logs and rocks can be seen in the store, allowing customers to feel the natural taste, with soft and elegant music played.

Now, it’s time for desserts! 😋🍹🍮



Mousse cake in the shape of cloud

I have to say that the creativity of innisfree style is nearly perfect. This cloud-shaped cake is really refreshing, making me want to take a big bite. Inside the cake are mousse and ice cream, separately made from matcha and strawberry.


Latte with red velvet

I have no interest in coffee, but I have a passion for this red velvet latte. It’s unique, both in appearance and taste.


Mango ice with marshmallows

Compared with cloud-shaped cake, this cloud made of marshmallows is more vivid. This one is very suitable in summer. After enjoying the “cloud”, you can mix the stuff inside, tasting like smoothie.


Sparkling Water with Green Grape

If you eat several kinds of desserts at a time, you may feel it too sweet. At this time, sparkling water is needed. Afterwards, I will always order a cup of bubble water or soda every time I eat desserts.


Cake pop

I’ve no idea whether you have heard of a lollipop made of cake before. The upper layer is wrapped in nuts. The stuff inside is soft, tasting a little bit sweet.

Generally speaking, the whole desserts there are too sweet. If you don’t like sweets, eat less. In addition to its various flavors, its environment is also worthy of being experienced.

今天将为大家推荐一家超级甜蜜的甜品店，它位于成都太古里，是悦诗风吟的甜品店，化妆品公司把甜品做得那么好吃，简直不务正业！最初我听说开了家悦诗风吟的甜品店，是感到非常震惊的，甚至会想老板会不会把洗面奶抹在甜品上，哈哈。

抱着试一试的心态，我和 @mrpointp去了这家甜品店。果然，不论是甜品还是装潢，都如悦诗风吟的广告风格那般清新脱俗。店内摆放了许多原木和岩石，周围的植物让顾客更能感受自然的风趣，再加上轻柔优雅的音乐，实在锦上添花。

济州岛白云蛋糕

不得不说悦诗风吟甜品店的创意算是接近满分了的，白云蛋糕带给人的第一感觉就是清新，好想咬一大口。蛋糕里面是慕斯和冰淇淋，分别是抹茶味和草莓味。

济州白雪红丝绒拿铁

对咖啡一类不感兴趣的我也对这份红丝绒拿铁产生了兴致，不论是外表还是味道，它都有别致之处。

济州岛白云雪花芒果冰

比起上述的蛋糕，这朵白云更加形象。白云雪花芒果冰非常适合夏季食用，把云朵吃掉后，把碗里的食物混淆在一起，就像在吃混合沙冰。

济州岛蜂蜜气泡冰果汁-青葡萄

吃了甜品，多多少少都会感到有些腻味，而我发现气泡水通常能够缓解这种感觉，于是我每次吃甜品都会点一杯类似的气泡水或苏打水。

济州奥莱棒棒糖蛋糕

不知道大家之前有没有听说过棒棒糖蛋糕？上面是包裹着各种坚果，香脆的外壳搭配软滑的内里，但有一点偏甜。

悦诗风吟的甜品总体来说就是偏甜腻，所以如果你不太喜欢吃甜食，就尽量少点一些。除了甜品的味道，这家甜品店的环境也值得大家去体验一下。

Rice noodles are a kind of classic snacks in southern China, now widely loved by our locals. The most famous is Guo Qiao Rice Noodles in Yunnan, but such rice noodles in Sichuan also taste good,with chili added. Today, I’d like to recommend a restaurant known for its rice noodles in my hometown Leshan, which is named Ma Ji Rice Noodles.

米线是经典的南方小吃，如今广受全国百姓的喜爱。最著名的莫过于云南的过桥米线，但四川米线的味道也非常棒，搭配四川的香辣味，别有一番风味。今天将为朋友们推荐一家来自我家乡乐山的米线店—马记米线。


This restaurant has been around for a few years and its taste has been maintained throughout the years. With the popularity of online promotion, the business works better. I think all the foodies know that whether the restairant is delicate or not, the best support for a restaurant is the special taste, which can be considered the main reason for the popularity. Next, let’s take a look at what’ve ordered!

这家米线店已有些年头，这些年马记米线的味道一直保持着，随着网络宣传的流行，生意更是火爆。但所有吃货们都懂，无论店铺精致与否，一个餐厅最好的支撑是味道，可能这也是这家米线店受欢迎的最大原因。接下来，请大家欣赏这些美食。


Rice Noodles with Crispy Meat 酥肉米线


Crispy meat is also very popular in Sichuan, and often mixed with rice noodles, making this dish delicious. Crispy meat must be made of pork belly. When chewing it together with rice noodles, absolutely wonderful!

酥肉也是在我们四川非常受欢迎的食物，也经常会与米线搭配在一起，这样一份酥肉米线简直是人间美味。而酥肉一定要半肥半瘦，与滑滑嫩嫩的米线嚼在一起，口感极佳。



Guo Qiao Rice Noodles 过桥米线


Guo Qiao Rice Noodles are to Yunnan what hot pot is for Sichuan, both of which belong to representatives of classic food. The rice noodles here is more suitable for the people in Sichuan. It is sour and spicy. After enjoying the rice noodles, you can drink the soup inside.

过桥米线就对于云南就像火锅对于四川，都是美食的经典代表。马记米线的过桥米线，更适合四川人的口味，酸辣麻俱有，吃了米线后再喝一口汤，回味无穷。



Rice Noodles with Pork Ribs 排骨米线


This dish is what I ordered, tasting good. Among such rice noodles, it is not the best one but includes so many ribs inside.

这份米线是我点的，味道不错，不过在这几份米线中，它算不上翘楚，但排骨量多。



Beef Rice Noodles with Raw Chili 生椒牛肉米线


I have eaten beef noodles with rice chili before, whose taste makes me unforgettable. Surprisingly, beef mixed with raw chili is really a perfect match for rice noodles. The beef is cut into small pieces and added to rice noodles, brilliant!

以前吃过的生椒牛肉面，其美味令我难以忘怀，惊喜的是，生椒牛肉同样适用于米线，且是另一番风味。牛肉大小适中，切成小片，与米线混合在一起，此乃美味。

Leshan is a popular tourist city, not only known for its beautiful scenery, but also for attractive food. If you visit Sichuan one day, don’t miss this good place —Leshan. If you want to explore delicacies here, you might as well go there!

乐山是个比较热门的旅游城市，不仅是风景优美，美食也是非常吸引人的。假使有一天，你们来四川玩，一定不要错过乐山这个好地方，又假如想要领略乐山的美食，不妨去今天我推荐的马记米线尝一尝！
