This special gift is for my dear colleagues and me.

这份特殊的礼物是给我可爱的同事和我自己的。

It’s the 3rd time that I’ve taken part in Drawing Challenge Contest held by @helene. Yesterday, I happened to find a studio called Hua She and experienced an oil painting course. And I want to take this opportunity to take this painting as the illustration of postcards and give them to my dear colleagues and myself. All of us are teaching senior 3 students now and this card can be considered as my gratitude. Although I haven’t won any prize before, yet I’m always enjoying the process. This time, the theme is to draw a card for teachers . I choose to draw totoro, which I think is cute. Now I’d like to share my painting with you(^ __ ^).

这是我第三次参加 @helene姐举办的绘画比赛了。昨天在春熙路无意中找到了一家叫花舍的画室，上了一节小小的油画体验课，想要将这幅作品当成贺卡的插图，送给我的可爱的同事们以及我自己，我们都是一群正担任着高三教学工作的园丁，借此机会想要表达一下对他们的感谢。虽然每次都没获奖，但仍然乐在其中✌。这一期主题是给老师的一张贺卡，我选择画龙猫，我觉得龙猫在我心中是很可爱的，现在就给大家展示一下我的作品 (^__^) 。

Basic materials 基本材料

A drawing board, a pencil, several brushes, various pigments and a color palette.


一张空白的画板，一支铅笔，不同的画笔刷和各式颜料，调色板。

The Process 过程图

Step1
Draw a rough outline of the figure.

用笔画出大概的轮廓。

.JPG)

Step 2 Color the background of this picture with blue. Use the brush color this part back and forth, making thecolor of background same, which may look more gorgeous.

用蓝色给背景着色。颜色要尽量均涂抹，最好不要看到笔触。

Step 3 When coloring grey, we need to make our painting a strong sense of composition.

上灰色的时候 ，注意明暗处过渡自然，相互融合。

Step 4 Color the stomach of this totoroand add some to the blank places. Take care of mixing colors.

给龙猫肚子着色和一些色彩的添补，注意调色！

Finished! 作品完成！❤

For me, teachers are like my friends. This special card consists of my painting and the background made by my mobile app. I prepare to print it out and give them as the gift of the coming Teachers’ Day. (^__^) If you receive such a special card, will you like it? Tomorrow, I want to take part in the drawing contest held by @htliao. If you have an interest, please click here to know more.
