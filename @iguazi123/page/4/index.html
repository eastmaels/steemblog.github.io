树舍·幸福的小杂院

藏区骑行60日~第64天「穿过沙漠，抵达零公里处」

http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/04/2016-06-17-204223.jpg

今天新藏线最后一天的骑行了，自此两个多月的骑行就告一段落。今天的爬坡比较顺利，才两三百米的垂直高度，上午十点就到达今天的最高点了——阿喀孜达坂。这里比较简陋，就立了块石头，标注海拔3150米，这也是今天一天行程中海拔最高的地方。随着越来越靠近叶城，我们感受了迎面而来的热浪，算完整经历一次从冬天直接进入夏天的节奏。

随着下山到山脚，我们就引入了黄色、荒凉的世界，漫天的黄沙，只是少有几个绿洲。在中间一个村庄休息了一会，吃了点传统美食，就继续出发了。行走在沙漠中，唯一能感觉到的就是渴，又热又渴，仅有的几瓶水都非常节约。路上偶尔能看到骆驼，被这沙漠精灵折服。还有偶尔的油田，以及小范围的气流卷起来的沙尘，公路上都是沙粒。不过，靠近叶城的地方，发现好多地方在植树造林，大量的水灌溉着，有一些成效，但更多的是枯枝。看到有的工人将枯枝拔了，又准备种植新的树苗，为这精神点赞，也许过不了几十年，这里又出现一大批绿洲。



路上碰见几个旅游，背着大背包，准备徒步新藏线，甚是佩服。骑行已经不易，何况徒步，只能为他们加油、点赞！傍晚时分，到达叶城县城了，赶紧去零公里处拍照留影，宣告我们的整个骑行画下了圆满的句号。留影之后，就找地方邮寄物质，准备把车车寄回成都了，忙完都快十点了，叶城的天还是亮的。今晚，不是无眠，而是安安稳稳地睡了一觉，算是圆梦了吧！下来，好好去喀什耍两天，去乌鲁木齐耍两天，就回家喽！

Today, the last cycly day of the Xinjiang-Tibet Highway, and the ride for more than two months has come to an end. Today’s climbing is relatively smooth, only two or three hundred meters of vertical height, arrived at today’s highest point at 10 am - Akhdah. Here is a relatively simple, set up a stone, marked 3150 meters above sea level, which is also the highest altitude in today’s day trip. As we get closer and closer to Yecheng, we feel the oncoming heat wave and count the rhythm of going straight into the summer from winter. As we went down to the foot of the mountain, we introduced a yellow, desolate world, and the yellow sands of the sky were just a few oases. After a break in the middle of the village, I ate some traditional food and continued to set off. Walking in the desert, the only thing that can be felt is thirst, hot and thirsty, and only a few bottles of water are very economical. Occasionally, the camel can be seen on the road and is convinced by this desert elf. There are occasional oil fields, as well as sand dust that is rolled up in a small area, and sand is on the road. However, near Yecheng, many places were found to be afforested, a large amount of water was irrigated, and there were some effects, but more were dead branches. Seeing that some workers will pull out the dead branches and prepare to plant new saplings, they will like this spirit. Maybe it will not be a few decades, and there are a lot of oases here. I met a few tourists on the road, carrying a large backpack and preparing to hike the new Tibet line. I admire it. Riding is not easy, let alone hiking, can only cheer for them, like! In the evening, I arrived at Yecheng County. I quickly took a photo at zero kilometers and announced that our entire ride had been drawn to a successful conclusion. After taking pictures, I found a place to mail the materials, and I was ready to send the car back to Chengdu. It was almost ten o’clock, and Yecheng’s day was still bright. Tonight, it’s not sleepless, but An An’s steady sleep, it’s a dream! Come down, go to Kash for two days, go to Urumqi for two days, go home!

藏区骑行60日~第63天「过麻扎达坂，抵库地小镇」

http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/04/IMG_6168.jpeg

今天一起来就开始爬坡，让人很是无语，但路一直都在，想要前行就必须上车、蹬脚踏板。麻扎兵站是新藏线上一个重要的站点，很多人去往K2就是从这里出发，到达K2大本营大概一周的时间。这让我对这个地方无形中充满了一些幻想，想着是否找个时间去克勒青河谷转转，如果不能攀登K2，去看看冰川也是非常棒的。
到达麻扎达坂的时候已是中午，山上大雾，据说在这里能看到K2的雄姿，想着这个愿望也泡汤了。继续下山吧，今天的骑行任务还是非常艰巨的。麻扎达坂应该是接下来骑行的最高海拔点了，骑行也轻松了不少。一路看到不少雪山，还有南疆特有的山峰，感觉都土化了，也没有什么植被。

下到山脚一个废弃的道班，开始煮方便面吃，吃完我们继续骑行。骑行路上就是这样，能找到一块遮风挡雨的地方最好了，找到了就补充点能量，大多数时候都是方便面了。在阿里骑行的时候，我们一天每个人，要吃掉5、6个鸡蛋，方便面也是不在话下的。吃着简单的食物，在那个条件下还觉得好吃，那是真累。
很快就到达了今天的目的地——库地，本想再向前走走，想着也算了，在此休息一下，明天再去往叶城。今晚很丰富，想着明天就能到达叶城，赶紧把我们的库存给消灭掉，好多鸡蛋，好多腊肉，还有茄子，黄瓜，苹果，一路上最丰盛的了。期待明天到达叶城，旅程也快结束了，有点不舍。明天再说吧！

When I came up today, We started climbing. It was very speechless, but the road was always there. We had to go on，so we must ride and pedal. Mazha Bing Station is an important site on the Xinzang Line. Many people go to K2 to get off from here and arrive at K2 Base Camp for about a week. This makes me invisibly full of some illusions about this place, thinking about whether to find a time to go to the Klein Valley, if I can not climb K2, it is great to see the glaciers. When I arrived at Mazda, it was noon, and the mountains were foggy. It is said that I can not see the majestic appearance of K2 here. Continue down the mountain, today’s cycling mission is still very difficult. Mazda should be the highest altitude point for the next ride, and the ride is also a lot easier. All the way to see a lot of snow-capped mountains, as well as the unique peaks of southern Xinjiang, the feelings are all soiled, and there is no vegetation. Go down to the abandoned foot of the mountain and start cooking instant noodles. After we finish eating, we will continue to ride. This is the way to ride. It is best to find a place to shelter from the wind and rain. When you find it, you can add some energy. Most of the time it is instant noodles. When riding in Ali, I have to eat 5 or 6 eggs a day, and instant noodles . It is really tired to eat simple food and feel delicious under that condition. Soon I arrived at today’s destination - Kudi, We wanted to move forward, thinking about it, take a break here, and go to Yecheng tomorrow. Tonight’s dinner is very rich, thinking about coming to Yecheng tomorrow, and quickly destroying our inventory, a lot of eggs, a lot of bacon, as well as eggplant, cucumber, apple, the most abundant along the way. Looking forward to arriving in Yecheng tomorrow, the journey is coming to an end, a bit sad. let’s talk tomorrow!
