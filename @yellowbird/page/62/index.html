区块链内容社区报告20180909期

The @yellowbird Block Chain Media Community Report is an ongoing focus that provides you with more perspective on the Block Chain Media Community in the form of data.

About this report:

This period tracks the market value and price fluctuation of this week’s project. The last KEY trend is weak. Other items of this period also began to decline after a slight rise.

yellowbird区块链内容社区报告是一个持续专注内容社区的报告，尽量以数据的形式提供给大家更多关于区块链内容社区的视角。关于本报告：

1.只关注区块链内容社区。
2.将是一个动态的，不定期更新。
3.将会是一个不断完善迭代的过程，不排除未来添加更多的项目及分析板块。

20180730发布第一期，20180805期更新补充对项目TATAU的关注，在“内容社区概”2018年项目中补充TATATU及PressOne。这一期追踪这一周项目的市值及价格涨跌情况。上一期KEY走势偏弱表现不如人意，本期其它项目“得意”了一周后又打回原形。

一、内容社区概况

2016年，steemit横空出世，一个全新的内容社区模式出现在了公众视野，一个分享内容就可以赚钱的平台出现。一个基于区块链的数据库技术的言论自由的平台出现在公众视野。

2017年，国内DPOS共识社区开始关注steemit，出现了steemit的模仿者YOYOW。

2018年，币乎KEY开启通证模式的探索并获得成功。由此，迎来2018年国内内容社区平台的大爆发。之后相继出现QUNQUN，支点，ONO等内容平台。除了绝大部分平台在通证模式的探索，继YOYOW之后，国内另外一条内容社区公链Ulord开始发力！

在2016年至2017年，内容社区主要呈现的发展方向是：区块链技术对于版权信息的存证、内容创作及分发的激励进行改造，主要以内容公链开发为主要方向。

但在2018年，区块链token经济的发展，内容社区主要呈现的发展方向是：重新构建行业利益及协作关系。基于已有成熟的区块链价值网络发行token，把关注重点从内容公链的技术开发转移到项目通证经济模式的探索和项目运营宣传上。

二、流通市值

本期报告共选取内容社区5个项目STEEM、KEY、YOYO、PST、TTU作为分析对象。从项目的流通市值及流通市值占比中，我们可以看出目前内容社区还是STEEM占据流通市值大头。KEY在上一期5个项目中的市值占比由13%涨至14%，STEEM较上一期65%占比增长到67%，本期KEY及STEEM流通市值的占比增加主要由于TTU的流通市值占比较上一期跌幅较大。

图上追踪了关注项目流通市值涨跌情况，在目前的熊市的整体大环境下，大部分项目的流通市值都在下跌缩水。前几期除TTU逆市暴涨外，其他项目走势基本趋同，上一期唯独KEY的市值表现不如人意，本期小幅上涨的STEEM、YOYO、PST得意了一周后又打回原形。

我在前几期文章提到过”区块链内容项目的系统风险与个体风险 ” 提到，目前内容项目主要受系统风险控制（简单来说看市场总体趋势），从本期关注的项目中STEEM、YOYO、PST、KEY等表现出较多的系统风险，整体涨跌走势趋同，TTU表现出一定的个体风险，波动较大。

三、涨跌趋势

由于TATATU为18年新出现的项目，coinmarketcap收录的数据有限。TTU的数据从7月份开始。以STEEM、KEY、YOYO、PST、TTU价格作为分析对象，关注项目相对涨跌趋势。

我们可以看出，大部分项目总体下跌走势，STEEM、KEY、YOYO、PST到9月9日跌幅分别为74.85%、87.60%、85.75%、74.96%，TTU涨幅由上期75.72%到本期只有3.51%左右。

本期的情况是，STEEM、YOYO、PST上期小幅上涨后，本期又打回原形。

For mobile photography, I don’t know much about skills. A little understanding of some composition, in fact, the composition is also followed by the feeling. Therefore, some of my photography is mainly the scenery, the illusory mood, the feelings, and the stories I want to express in the photos.

The sky is darker, and there is some light, reflection of the house…


The sky is completely dark. In the wild mangrove forest, the surrounding area is all dark, but these houses have some light inside. In such a place far away from the hustle and bustle, we can imagine the tourists enjoying the dinner with some weariness.

Unforgettable Trip—ZHIYIN STEAMER.


ZHIYIN STEAMER

Last year October 15th, I experienced an unforgettable trip—ZHIYIN STEAMER.As night falls, The wind blowed from the river, and the ZHIYIN STEAMER was brightly lit.

Approaching the pier of the steamer, the songs of the Republic of China have been surrounded, and the crossing of one time was about to kick off.


“ZHIYIN STEAMER” was a 1930s-style steamer, Some stories that people meet in a journey happened on the steamer.

There is no fixed auditorium and performance area in the traditional theater. The actors and spectators can move freely in the cabin. In other words, when the whistle sounds, the boat sails out of the river, and every actor in the boat begins to perform, you want to see What to look at, and will also be part of the play, you can interact deeply with the actors.

The stories on each floor of the ship are different, and the seemingly unrelated stories are independent, but they are combined by the “ZHIYIN STEAMER”. The boat is said to be, some story about the rivers, the ship and the city.

We were ready to board the ship.My wife also changed into the cheongsam of the Republic of China.

BOARDING SHIP



Before the ship was opened, the stories of different people began to unfold before boarding the ship, telling their own lines.


There were 108 actors, including bankers, singers, students, and social flowers. Their stories haved taken place in every corner of the ship from the moment they boarded the ship.

There were three routes for the entire boarding, and the order of the stories on each line is different. When boarding the ship, I also opened a unique journey through.

ONE FLOOR DANCE HALL





The first act was a ballroom. The center was a large dance floor under the spotlight. The actors danced in a ballroom dance, with a monologue of stories and a romantic dialogue between actors.

ONE FLOOR BAR




After the first curtain of the ballroom, we moved to the bar. There were people chatting and drinking, there were waiters who helped run the Lee, there were singing singers, and there were many stories. One story is a man who has been on the boat for many years. Every day, he makes a phone call at a fixed position in the bar to find a girl.

SECONE FLOOR ROOM




The second floor was mainly a ship’s room, with a single room and a bunk bed. Each actor in each room has an independent story, and the story of all rooms is happening at the same time.

THREE FLOOR ROOM



On the third floor, each of the original boarding ships also haved its own private room. Each person haved a room number on the ticket. The room below was ours, with some chronological furnishings.

Feel freed to look through the objects in the room but did’t take them away. After entering the room, the staff gaved us two postcards and a pen on the table, which could be mailed to your friends. There was still a letter on the table.

ONE FLOOR DANCE HALL



After the third floor, we returned to the first floor of the ballroom. At this time, you are a boater and you are also a dramatist in the eyes of others. At this time, everyone could move freely in every corner of the cabin, and you could dance with the actors.

The most impressive thing was that a very old foreigner wore a robe in the Republic of China. Several Chinese people accompanied him. A Chinese girl and the old man danced with music on the dance floor. Also with them.


There was also an aunt who was expected to dance in the square. Wearing modern clothes also dances with uncoordinated dance steps. The actors were also among them. It felt like a fighting dance.

At this time, I felt that the actors and each of us were members of this ship. You were also the scenery in the eyes of others. Every boater who bought the tickets is also part of the play, so every time everyone meets “It will be different.” Just like this time, I met an old foreigner and met an interesting aunt.

END


The whistle sounded, the boat in the monologue was docked, and everyone’s journey was over. Then we wore back to modern times.

STEEM的收益分配设计你真的理解吗？

一、“幂律分布”和“正态分布”的概念

数学术语中有两个分布模型，一个是“幂律分布”，一个是“正态分布”，这两个分布模型广泛存在于我们的社会之中。

我们说的“二八定律”，20%的人掌握着80%的财富，20%的网站吸引着80%的流量，20%的公众号占据者80%的点击量，steemit上20%的作者获得80%的文章奖励等等，这种少数人牛得不行，多数人“泯然众人”，这种不均衡的分布就是“幂律分布”,而幂律分布尾巴上的那80%又叫“长尾”。

还有一个分布就是正态分布，平均的占主要绝大部分，剩下的少输是极好和极差的。比如，整个社会的智商水平，身高水平。会有个平均数，大多数人分布在平均数的附近。

二、内容平台的收益分布

再次去读steem的白皮书时，我才意识到，所谓的收益不公平或许是steem的有意为之，白皮书上都写了。

Steem白皮书提到：


（STEEM白皮书截图）

“齐夫定律”非常巧妙的刻画出真实世界中涵盖的极大范围的各种现象，由内容受欢迎的程度作为粗略的测量量，由齐夫定律可由得出，如果有一百万的内容样本，则受欢迎的前100个样本贡献了三分之一的价值，后续的1万个样本贡献了另外的三分之一，剩余的989900个样本则贡献最后的三分之一。

所以，收益分配机制是为了给优良内容支付高额奖励，同时让小玩家从长尾分布中得到收获。

由此产生的经济效果类似于彩票，人们高估自己获得的投票机会，因为完成了比奖励期望更高的工作量，为服务社区而完成的工作量得以最大化。赌场为了让人们继续光顾，使用的也是相同的心理技巧，让每个人都觉得自己“有所斩获”，换言之，小奖励强化人们对更大奖励的期待。

我突然感觉我有些误解了steem，当我们吐槽收益不公平，更少的人能获得几百上千美元收益时，更多的人可能只有几毛。其实steem就是这样设计的，让部分内容获得高额奖励，让80%的内容收获那剩下的20%的收益。

三、“幂律分布”的优缺点

幂律分布最大的缺点当然就是不公，社会都是如此，“富者愈富、穷者愈穷”。

优点当然就是，它会让人有一种高期望，期望自己是那头部的20%，期望自己从一只小虾米，变成一条小鱼、海豚甚至是大鲸。彩票、赌场都给人类似的心理。这种设计也自然产生一种吸引人眼球的传播效应。

比如steemit和币乎都有类似的现象，在steemit和币乎的热门，我们可以看到一篇文章收益几百美元，几千人民币，这太吸引人了，大家都趋之若鹜，希望自己也能如此。

可是事实是，那只有极少的头部用户才能获得的，绝大多数人注定无法达到，接着的情况就是有一部分用户发现“被骗”后会离开，也会有一部分用户会留下来从长尾中获得满足。

当然，头部的20%的用户也一定是留存下来的用户，他们会努力维持目前的现状或者让自己做得更好，以对得起自己的位置。从steemit中文区大鲸鱼用户努力维持社区生态发展，从币乎热门大V作者自律保证自己文章质量都可以看到这个现象。

小结一下“幂律分布”优缺点:

  • 1、缺点：不公平。

  • 2、优点：吸引人眼球的传播效应、留存20%的头部用户及小部分长尾用户。

简单粗暴来算下，目前STEEM流通2.7亿（其实大部分在交易所，并未转成SP权重），目前STEEM注册用户110万（大部分是僵尸账户），采用正太分布的平均主义，平均一个人就是250SP，估计是个小虾米级别。

这样的简单粗暴算法和类比或许不正确，但是可以去尝试理解幂律分布与正太分布在收益分布中起到的作用。

那么你是愿意大海里全是小虾米，还是有它自己的生态，有大鲸、有海豚，有小鱼……？
