Defender of Truth - Steem Monster Auction

Need an Epic Steem Monster?

Defender of Truth

Auction Details

Opening Bid: 1 SBD
Buy It Now: 5 SBD

How To Bid

To place a bid leave a comment to this post with the word bid followed by your bid amount in SBD terms. For example, if you were to place a 10SBD bid you would leave a comment like this:


bid 10.0

The auction is being handled by @steembay. You will receive a reply to your bid once it is confirmed. You can read the full @steembay tutorial guide here.

Terms & Escrow

After the bidding has ended or a ‘Buy It Now’ is placed then the winning bidder will be responsible for buying the Steem Monster token at their respective bid. Payment will be expected to be made within 48 hours of the auction end. If the winning bidder is not a Steem Monsters Merchant then you will have the option of sending the SBD first and waiting for me to send the Steem Monster after payment is confirmed.

Alternatively, I will also use a Steem Merchant as an escrow service allowing us to each send our side to them and have them return it to the proper owners. Steem Monster Merchants issue a performance bond to @aggroed that ensures if they do not perform their duties the bond can make us whole. You can learn more about the way Merchant Performance Bonds work by reading here.

If you haven’t heard of Steem Monsters yet then be sure to check out @aggroed’s introductory post for more information on the game.

Selenia Sky - Closed - 30 SBD Buy It Now

WooCommerce Steem Plugin Assistance

I’m seeking the Steem community’s assistance in updating @recrypto’s WooCommerce Steem plugin to be able to work again.

If you’re not familar with WooCommerce Steem then I must say it is an incredible tool. What it does is allows anyone to sell products from Wordpress by accepting Steem or SBD for payment. When working it would query the Steem network for the payment then make all of the adjustments to WooCommerce orders based on finding the sent transaction.

Unfortunately the plugin stopped working once the Steemful node went offline and has never came back. The plugin just needs a way to query the blockchain to find any sent transactions but I’m not equipped to make it happen myself. If you feel you would be interested in helping bring this plugin back to life please leave a comment below and let me know what you would need to get it back online.
