Supporting Local Business











I love stopping in my local coffee shop for their Campfire specialty. If you’re ever in Nicholasville, KY then I recommend stopping by J’s place! It has a friendly atomosphere and the coffee is well made. #coffee #supportsmallbusiness #local #share2steem



I was also able to get $1 off because I used Square’s CashApp to make my purchase. If you’ve never used Cash then you should check them out. They will send you a card that you can fill with your debit or bank account and when you shop you can get discounts using their boos program. If you’ve never signed up before then check out https://cash.me/app/LSRWVXF. If you use my link we’ll each get $5.
