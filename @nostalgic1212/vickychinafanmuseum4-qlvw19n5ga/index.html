Folding Screen Fan with painting of “A Panoramic View of the West Lake” and Edge Carvings

On 30th January 1994, a big fire broke out, burning out the Tian Gong Yi Yuan, a large department store and unfortunately, the adjacent Wangxingji Fan Factory, with all its workshops and raw materials burned to ash too. However, this biggest fan—2.6m high and 5m long (after being unfolded) should be intact except some marks and stains left while being rescuing from the fire.
