【AAA】唐人街探案2 당인가탐안2 Detective Chinatown Vol 2 (2018)

Today I would like to introduce the Detective Chinatown Vol 2, the second one of this series, which is directed by Sicheng Chen and starring Haoran Liu and Baoqiang Wang. After watching this movie, many people will wonder the question “WHO IS Q “. The answer is still at the end of the movie, so it looks like there could be a third one.

The film’s starting point is not very reasonable, and there are too many invalid information. Besides, some comedy elements seem to be quite abrupt. As a result, many audiences said that Vol 2 is not as good as the former one after watching the film.

However, the movie still has some advantages. The director’s attempt to mix elements of comedy with relatively serious detective is a good attempt. What’s more, we cam find some heated issues, such as LGBT in this film. Compared with the first film, there has been a lot of progress in shooting, which definitely shows director Sicheng Chen’s ambition.

If there is Vol 3, I think I will continue to watch it. Who is Song yi? Who is the bis boss Q? Will the character Qin Feng insists going on the road of “Genius finally turn into madness”? I hope the answers to these questions will be found in the coming Vol 3.

今天想介绍给大家的是由陈思诚导演，刘昊然和王宝强主演的该系列的第2部电影《唐人街探案2》。看完这部电影以后，想必很多人都会好奇“Q到底是谁”。这个答案在第二部的结尾处依然没有揭晓，由此看来可能会有第三部。

电影的出发点设置的不太合理、无效信息偏多并且一些喜剧的设置显得比较突兀，使得很多观众在观影结束后表示，第二部远远不如第一部精彩。尽管如此，这部电影还是有一些优点的。导演将轻松的喜剧和较为严肃的侦探这类元素融合在了一起，是一次不错的尝试。电影中还涉及到了LGBT的话题，取景上相较于第一部也有了较多进步，看得出陈思诚导演的野心。

如果有第3部的话，我想我应该还会继续看。宋义到底是谁？幕后大boss的Q又是谁？秦风这个角色是否会继续黑化走上“天才最终变成疯子”这条路？这些问题的答案，希望在也许会推出的第三部中找到答案。
