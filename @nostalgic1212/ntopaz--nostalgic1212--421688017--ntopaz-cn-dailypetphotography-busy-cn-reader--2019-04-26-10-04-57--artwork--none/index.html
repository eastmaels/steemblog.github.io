「Life & Activities」情感艺术 / nTOPAZ Visual Art Contes

Her name is Yellow. She was adopted by my friend since she was a little kitten, and lived in the dormitory with eight boys for four years. As a result, she’s not afraid of human beings.

In my impression, Yellow can be summarized as several words: Sensitive, Fuzzy, Bad-tempered, Greedy for Food, Fond of Sleeping…

This picture was captured by my friend when she yawned. Can you see how sleepy she was?

「中文部分」

她是Yellow，是朋友在念书时候捡到的小奶猫。据说和他们宿舍八个大男生一起生活了四年，是个非常自来熟的毛孩子。

在我印象里，Yellow可以用几个词来概括：贪吃、敏感、毛茸茸、坏脾气、贪睡…

这张照片呢，就是朋友在她打哈欠的时候抓拍下来的，大家看得出她有多困了嘛？
