Simmering Beef Noodles 红烧牛肉面

I have developed great interest in this kind of noodles since I came back from Taiwan. The photograph above is the one I ate in there, and you can see the noddles itself is quite different from Hangzhou.

Whether the noodles or beef portion is very sufficient. It’s a little greasy, while it’s delicious with the shaved ice that sold in the restaurant.

我从台湾回来以后，就对红烧牛肉面产生了浓厚的兴趣，上图就是我在那里吃到的。从面条本身来看，就和杭州本地的面条差别非常大。

这碗面无论从面条还是牛肉的量来说都非常足。吃起略微有些油腻，不过配上店铺里面卖的红豆冰的话，应该会非常不错吧。
