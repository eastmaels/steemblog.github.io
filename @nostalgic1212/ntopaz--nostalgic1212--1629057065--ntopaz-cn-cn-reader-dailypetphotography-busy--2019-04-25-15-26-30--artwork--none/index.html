「Life & Activities」情感艺术 / Visual Art Contes

The photography was token during my trip in Taiwan last July, and the location is the cat’s paradise: Houdong.

The temperature in the summer is absolutely high in Taiwan. When I arrived in the village, there’re few cats lying in the shadow and taking a nap. Most of them are indolently lying or walking around and it seems that they are not afraid of human beings.

I’m a supper cat lover, and I feel so excited there. Under a big tree, I met this cat that was dozing off. His expression fully showed his pleasant mood at the time: D

Finally, I would like to ask @catwomanteresa what’s the name of the cat in my photo?

「中文部分」

這是我去年七月在台灣旅行的時候拍攝的照片，地點是在貓咪們的天堂貓村猴硐。

可能由於是夏天氣溫著實很高，我去村裡的時候僅在陰涼處看到一些在睡午覺的毛孩子們。他們大多很慵懶也不算太害怕人類，讓我這樣的愛貓人士超激動的。在一棵大樹下我遇到了這只在打瞌睡的貓咪，他的表情充分展現了他當時愜意的心情吧:D

最後還想請教一下@catwomanteresa，和我合影的這位貓貓叫什麼名字呀？
