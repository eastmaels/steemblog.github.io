PengLai - “the fairyland on earth”

This is the picture I took in Penglai，Yantai city，Shandong province in 2016.

Penglai is called a fairyland on earth. There is a beautiful myth in China called “Eight Immortals Crossing the Sea.This is where the legend happens.

You can see from the picture，Next to it are the mountains，Sailing boats passed in the distance，Canoeing speeded by in the vicinit，A bright white light spreads over the sea.There is a sharp contrast between movement and silence.

The magnificent scenery of Penglai deserves to be a fairyland on earth.

The pearl river in GuangZhou

This is the photo I took in the winter of 2014 on the cruise ship of pearl river in guangzhou.

On both sides of the pearl river are modern Chinese buildings, especially the new TV tower in guangzhou, which is known as “small waist”. With a total height of 600 meters, it is the highest tower in China and the second highest tower in Asia.

Different from the ancient buildings on both sides of haihe river in tianjin, the pearl river is more of a modern flavor, which is the epitome of China’s rapid development in decades.

HaiHe river of TianJin

This photo was taken while I was touring haihe river in tianjin on a boat in the winter of 2013.

Haihe is famous not only for its distinctive Bridges, but also for its historical buildings on both sides.

These buildings are not only antique European architecture, but also Chinese architecture with a very modern sense. Especially at night, under the background and foil of neon lights, these buildings seem to be endowed with new life, as if telling the unforgettable history.
