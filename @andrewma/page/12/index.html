Autumn in Beijing

Autumn in Beijing is the best season of the year. The colors of the plants are very layered, ranging from dark green to light green to yellow leaves，full of vitality.

The fountain in the middle of the pond is also at work, shining brightly in the sun and showing a brilliant rainbow, Can you see that?

The length of autumn is relatively short, so we should cherish this moment, enjoy the peace and warmth.
