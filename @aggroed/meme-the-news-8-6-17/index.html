I have mild moral conundrums about this series now. Memes are inherently meant to be shared. Many authors will “tag” their memes with the name of the person or group who created them. Many creators won’t bother because to them it’s just a silly meme. I snag these by scrolling through facebook for a few hours looking for stories and things to write about. Many memes come from libertymemes, muricatoday, TFTP, policethepolice, and a few other groups that actively produce these as a way to spread their protest quickly. I’m providing the memes without tags sans attribution as I think if they really wanted attribution they would have spent the 3 seconds to add their tag. If you happen to be the owner of said meme and take umbridge, offense, or feel economically damaged by me by all memes please contact me.
