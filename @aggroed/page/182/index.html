Steem pricing is depressing...

https://images.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=http%3A%2F%2Fbeta1.sandiegofamilytherapy.net%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2010%2F12%2FDepression.jpg&f=1

I mean that in two ways. Way 1. It’s sad that it’s that low. Way 2. It’s sad that it’s going down more.

I keep saying this. Things aren’t going to change until this is addressed. Whales with 90% of the steem in 1% of the people make what is otherwise an awesome system really unegalitarian system.

The way this changes is that whales have to sell a lot of shit. This will happen slowly over 5-10 years you’ll see the ownership slowly spread into more hands at which point it will take off. Or there will be a serious event that will bring the price crashing that will almost bankrupt the system, and may in fact bankrupt the system, before it would then be a more equitable position for everyone and then the price will take off.

So, for now. Enjoy your $0.15 steem. It’s only going to go down for the next year.
