No, the price of steem is not going stratospheric.



Before you go investing your shirt into Steem because of the recent price rise I just want to draw your attention to something.  Above you can see the price of steem relative to btc.  Steem looks to have been hammered for a little bit, and now appears to be on the rebound.  You might get some greed into your eyes thinking about buying into that up trend, but I don’t thinka it means what you thinka it means.


Steem isn’t priced to dollars on poloniex.  It’s priced to bitcoin.  Bitcoin fluctuates like a mofo.  What you see below is the price chart for BTC vs USD.  The story goes that Chinese people suddenly started investing in bitcoin real quick.  I don’t know if I believe that or not, but you can see a quick pump from the hype, and now we’re dropping back down.  


Here’s the logic:  Price of bitcoin goes up.  Steem stays the same price/usd.  So the amount of btc needed to purchase a steem goes down.  So, the price drops.  


Then, the hype fades.  The price of bitcoin comes down.  Steem stayed the same price/usd.  So, the amount of btc needed to purchase a steem goes up.  So, the price (in terms of btc) rises.



You’re not looking at investors suddenly swarming to steemit.  You’re looking at investors pricing it correctly now that BTC has dropped.  The price per btc will keep rising because it looks like the chinese thing was all hype (pump and dump).  So, you can go back to your regularly scheduled impending drop in steem price.
