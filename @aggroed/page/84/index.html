Steem/SBD and AgX evolution - 2018-02-23

https://agx123.herokuapp.com//2018-02-23.jpg

This is the daily AgX chart for today 2018-02-23.
Today’s 200 day moving average is 0.92 for a STEEM/SBD ratio of 0.89.

What is AgX?



The Mayer’s multiple (200 days moving average of the BTC/USD price) is known to be a good indicator of the BTC price evolution.
AgX aims to be a similar indicator regarding the STEEM/SBD ratio, in order for users to find the right moment to exchange their SBD for STEEM at the best possible rate.

Historically a good time to sell steem for SBD is when the blue line is at the top of the chart, and it’s a good time to sell SBD for Steem when the blue line is at the bottom of the chart.

I originaly intended to take this off my wall today, but I’m not planning on moving it Saturday or Sunday to @agx.

Californication

I’m out visiting my Dad this week out in California. One of the things that I truly enjoy is the ability to drive my Dad’s dream car. Back when he was in his 20s he had a 1964 1/2 Mustang. My grandfather had them too. Dad did pretty well financially in his life and one of the gifts he gave himself was this classic Mustang.

I remember one of the best days of my life as being when I drove around in one of these predecessors. I was on I-5 right next to the ocean. I was driving a classic Mustang that purred when it idled and roared when it was go time. I had 20 bucks in my wallet, and I had a beautiful girlfriend I was sharing the day the with.

Getting to the beach in my Dad’s classic Mustang

The bluffs

After heading to the ocean in the Mustang I went out and took a walk on the bluffs. This bluff walk is one of my favorite views in the world. You got a blue sky, deep blue water, and a touch of desert landscape.

https://youtu.be/BcbnfaWzLZs

It’s a touch of a vacation

I’m still online a ton, still checking DMs, still working on projects, but with a nice change of scenery and a nice a visit with dad.
