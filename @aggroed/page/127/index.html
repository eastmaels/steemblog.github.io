Peace Academy Episode 1 Show Notes

Peace, Abundance, and Liberty

Peace, Abundance, and Liberty are not simply nouns or static ideas.
They are constantly in motion.
They are our ultimate goals, but the path is not always easy or clear. We continually make adjustments due to our experiences and challenges in order to figure out what the best solution is for all three.
Just like when sailing a ship, the wind may shift the boat’s direction, but it doesn’t change our destination.

The question to consider is how can we achieve these goals within our own lives, in our communities, and ultimately within the world?

  • How can we bring about Peace?
  • How can we actualize Abundance so there is plenty to go around for everyone?
  • How can we negotiate Liberty - to say or be who we are while not hurting our neighbors?

Peace, Abundance, and Liberty in this sense are verbs and actions.
They require a mindfulness to consistently be able to actualize them.
There aren’t always clear answers, decisions often fall into the grey zone.
So we try to find guideposts and choose a path where we can live with ourselves and live with our neighbors.

Curriculum

Because we can’t just work on these ideas once a week during a radio show, we have decided to start a Curriculum for Peace, Abundance, and Liberty.

The first step to achieving peace with others is first attaining a sense of inner peace within ourselves.
If we can’t sit with ourselves and feel some sort of inner peace then how can we have peace with others?

Self-care is key.
Think of Maslow’s hierarchy of needs.
We start first by taking care of basic physiological needs then move up the chain, ultimately working on our Mind, Body, and Spirit.

You can read more about self-care and inner peace Here

Energy

Part of this work includes the realization that we are all connected through Universal energy.

Technology has shown the energy that our thoughts create through experiments where devices can measure brain waves and we can interact with other devices by only using our brain waves.
Our thoughts are energy and this energy interacts with other people.
How you think and feel will impact the reality around you.
So, be aware of your thoughts throughout the day.
Unnesecessary negative thoughts lead to negative emotions.
This energy is sent out and then reflected back.

We attract who we are, not what we want.

There is so much more to this world than what we have been taught and concepts like these are often misunderstood or disregarded, but they are the basis of our existence.
Think about radio waves. They are here, everywhere, all around us. Just because we can’t see them doesn’t mean they don’t exist.
We just aren’t tuned into a frequency to receive them unless we use a receiver.
We are energy and we can tune into one another.
We can either resonate constructively or destructively.

We are not isolated, detached beings.
We are interconnected beings that share in this thought matrix of the universe.

And all of us together can make this world a better place with Peace, Abundance, and Liberty.

Self-Care and Inner Peace

The first step to achieving peace with others is to first find peace within ourselves.
Self-care and inner peace go hand in hand.
We must first secure our own oxygen masks before assisting others.
If we aren’t taking care of ourselves we can experience a multitude of problems that can not only affect our inner selves, but our relationships with others as well:

  • Anxiety
  • Burn-out
  • Health problems
  • Problems sleeping
  • Problems with mental clarity
  • Frustration
  • Negative Thoughts
  • Arguments

We all lead very busy lives and building a successful Steemit account can sometimes feel like a full time job.
The following self-care tips are a gentle reminder of what we all should be doing in order to lead healthy lives and achieve a sense of inner peace:

  • Get the amount of sleep that you need to feel rested
  • Eat as healthy as possible
  • Exercise regularly
  • Take some time for yourself to relax

Once we have these basic essential needs covered then we can spend some time on self-renewal and inner peace:

  • Meditation - Many people get overwhelmed by this concept. All you need to do is sit quietly for 10 minutes a day and attempt to clear your mind of mundane concerns.
  • Write down what you are grateful for
  • Keep a journal of dreams and goals
  • Read
  • Listen to music
  • Laugh
  • Spend time with friends and loved ones
  • Help others
  • Make a daily list of your concerns and/or things to do so they aren’t constantly floating around in your head in an unorganized way causing stress.
  • If you feel overwhelmed with many things to do then create a schedule and stick to it.
  • Take a break from Steemit!

The next part of The Peace Academy Curriculum will focus on achieving Peace with others.

Thank you for your support!

The Peace Academy

Msp-Waves is proud to present its newest radio show, The Peace Academy with @aggroed and @isaria.

The Peace Academy is a service brought to you by The Minnow Support Project and the Peace, Abundance, and Liberty Network Discord Channel. It was created by @aggroed who has enlisted the help of @isaria to bring it to fruition.

The Peace Academy is meant to help relatively healthy people (physically and emotionally) learn how to demonstrate peace as frequently as possible. It is not a rehabilitation service at this time, but instead is designed to help Steemit and MSP community members learn to interact peacefully with one another and to demonstrate what that looks like when that is executed well.

@aggroed and @isaria will be presenting these ideas during the radio show as well as discussing the concepts of Peace, Abundance, and Liberty and how we can best implement them here in our community, as well as in our daily lives.
