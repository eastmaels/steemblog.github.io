So, it’s been an interesting three weeks.  I started screen casting and made a fresh YouTube channel on September 6th.  At the time I was about three weeks old on steemit, and really hadn’t gotten anywhere with it.  


One of my main points of interest was to discuss the Mandela Effect.  It’s this weird thing where what large groups of people remember in specific detail is not what matches recorded history.  I started with a 90 min screen cast on a paper I wrote using data from 25,120 people that took a survey/quiz.  After that I did a few more Mandela Effect videos to show “reality residue” for a few examples.  Mandela Effect researchers think of Reality Residue as leftover evidence from a different world merging into this one.  Those that tend to doubt the changes that are occuring think of those same things as the cause of why everyone seems to misremember specifics or be confused.
