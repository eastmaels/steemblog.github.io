Hillary Clinton is still Hillary Clinton's second body double.


This post attempts to show that Hillary Clinton had steady age progression from November 2015 until September of 2016.  She was rushed off of the 9/11 memorial site.  She went to Chelsea’s apartment.  When she emerged from the apartment she lost 50 lbs and looked 10 years younger making many think she was a body double.  She left the 9/11 memorial to go to their place in Chippequa NY.  From there her next campaign appearance was after several days where she showed up in Greensboro, NC with a third look extreme enough to indicate a second body double.  She was recently filmed talking about explosions in NY while on her plane.  The second body double appears consistent with the woman on the plane talking about the explosions.


Several interesting things of note.  In the footage of her getting in the black van leaving the memorial site one of the secret service members film the event.


When closely inspecting the footage of her leaving the memorial site it appears the bars on the grates merge into the metal supports and back out of it.


There appears to be one photo the morning of the 9/11 service that is more consistent with Hillary’s first body double.




My initial thoughts now leave me more open than before that the memorial service event was staged and planned.  The fact that her leaving was filmed from afar and up close suggests some planning.  The fact that the grates go in and out suggest that the footage may also have been green screened and put together.  With those two parts looking planned and now a single image that seems to indicate Hillarysbodydouble was on the scene early in the morning when it was still dark there are multiple elements suggesting the whole operation was staged.  Definitive proof.  No.  Worth looking for other images.  Yes.
