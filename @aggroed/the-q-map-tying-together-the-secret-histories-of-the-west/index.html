The Q-map: Tying together the secret histories of the West

I had an hour long conversation with a guy by the name of Dylan Monroe last night. I found him because he made a giant map of interconnecting secret societies and major events in history, it actually made it on a comedy central news show, but they modified it before posting it. The fact that they modified pieces of it was really fascinating to me. It’s a subtle nod that this chart had information that was worth hiding and powerful enough that it was worth mocking.

Turns out Dylan likes Jordan Sather like I do. He’s in a facebook group with him so I was able to track him down that way. And we setup the fall listed above.
