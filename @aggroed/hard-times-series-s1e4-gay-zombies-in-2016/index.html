Hard Times Series- S1E4- Gay Zombies in 2016



Zombies snarl…


“Mom… Dad… I want you to know I’m gay.  Also, I think I may have been bitten.”


Zombies smash at the front door.


“What’s the Jacob?  You’re gay?!?” Jacob’s father snapped!


“Harold!  Don’t you talk to him like that.  Jacob what your father meant to say is that we’ll love you no matter what.”  Jacob’s mom chirped in.


“No son of mine is going to be gay.  Zombie or not.  I’m not having it!”  Harold blurted out.


Zombies growl and keep banging on the door.


“Harold!  That’s not how he feels Jacob.  You father just gets angry when he’s surprised is all.”  Ingrid pushed against the door to keep the horde out, but managed to give a loving look to her son.”


“I’m feeling woozy mom.” Jacob’s face seemed to be draining of blood.


“No woman.  This is not just a passing anger thing.  I for one will not stand for having a gay son.  You are obviously going through a phase, possibly more than one…”  Harold looked at the blood soaked sleeve of his son.  ”Anyway, this isn’t the right time to choose to be gay.  We’re in the middle of something right now if you hadn’t noticed.” 


Harold cocked his shotgun and blew out the face of a zombie who had managed to get his head through the widening gap.


“I didn’t choose this Dad.  Any of this.  It’s not my fault!  I just am who I am.  Why can’t you accept me.”  Jacob started sobbing as both the betrayal by his father and the pain of torn flesh and a partially broken wrist started setting in.  He sat down and leaned back up against the wall.


Ingrid stabbed a zombie in the eye with a kitchen knife and then sliced through the wrist of another who had gotten his hand into a new crack in the door.


The sound of zombie snarls filled the room.


“Jacob, honey, I’ve known you were gay since you were 5 years old and played with dolls.  Your brother played with dolls to spend time with girls.  You played with dolls because you liked fashion.  Sweetheart I’ve always known!  And I’ve always loved you.  Besides it’s 2016.   It’s ok to be who you are now!”  Ingrid was very supportive.  


A zombie grabbed hold of Ingrid’s wrist.  She took the knife and pushed it down hard.  As she shifted her weight to do that the zombies inched the door open.  Once she had her hand free she pushed the door back closed.


Harold starting shouting over the growls.  ”You knew since he was 5?!?  When were you going to tell me Ingrid?!?  I’m the boys father.  If I had known back then maybe I could have done something about this.”  Harold came to the aid of his wife and shoved a sharpened stick through the head of a zombie pushing hard against the door.  Blood blasted out of the zombies face and covered Ingrid and Harold.


“Oh now look what you’ve done.  You’d soiled my last blood free blouse!”  Ingrid shouted.


Jacob let out a low groan.  ”Mom?  Dad?  I can’t see.  I love you…”


—


Later that night… the house was quiet.  Very quiet.  And then there was a low groan coming from against the wall.
