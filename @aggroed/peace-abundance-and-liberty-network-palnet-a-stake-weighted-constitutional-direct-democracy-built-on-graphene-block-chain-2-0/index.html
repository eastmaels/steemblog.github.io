Peace, Abundance, and Liberty Network (PALNET)- A stake weighted, constitutional, direct democracy built on graphene (block chain 2.0 tech). AKA how to make a block chain government.





Continuing the discussion of creating block chain government and what that might look like.  I’ve started a good conversation with one of the software architects for STEEMIT and we’re working on tweeking it to function as a full fledged government.  Here’s what the first round of tweaks will look like.




A Graphene blockchain based on Steem.




Deliverable: The current Steem source code, with the following functionality added:




- Half of all newly minted coins go into a Public Fund


- A transaction to propose a project paid from the Public Fund, which describes the project (limited to one of the following essential service types: commerce, common defense, communication, education, environment, food, health, news, shelter, transportation, water) and price (price is variable but capped at 5% of Public Fund balance at time of proposal)


- A transaction to vote for/against a Public Fund project, with public comments available


- A transaction to veto a Public Fund project (must be signed by an active witness)


- A week after a Public Fund project is proposed, if more stake is voting for it than against it and the Public Fund contains sufficient funds to pay, the project price is paid to the proposer; otherwise the project is discarded


- A Constitution file is encoded in the genesis block


- A transaction to propose a replacement Constitution


- A transaction to vote for/against the replacement Constitution


- 60 days after a replacement Constitution is proposed, if 2/3 of voting stake is in favor, and 2/3 of witnesses are in favor, the Constitution is replaced


- Support for listing pending Public Fund projects


- Support for listing pending replacement Constitutions


- CLI wallet support for all of the aforementioned transactions and listings




> Under consideration: A vesting period for Power stake (analogous to Steem Power) such that, after powering up, it requires 1-2 years for this stake to reach full voting rights. Objective is to prevent whales from buying in and hijacking the voting process.




I’d love to get your thoughts.  What other basic functions are we missing to get launched.  What services would you want an internet based blockchain government to provide?  Do you want to help us?
