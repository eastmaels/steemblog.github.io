So, first off! Giant thanks to those that supported me with their Witness Vote, resteems, and upvotes! That’s awesome! This is a giant learning experience and I’m stoked to be going through it.

Secondly, I’m standing by my first thought of when SBDs are over $1USD ramp the printing press. It allows growth, it shorts mooning, which is better for a fair distribution, and it helps newbies and merchants. It doesn’t hurt holders of the SBDs, but it doesn’t help them either. It makes it a poor store of value, but a higher utility medium of exchange.

Thirdly, I think we should institute term limits for Witnesses. As folks stay in the top 19 spots there should be a penalty applied to their total Vote count (it’s calculated in vests, so I’m thinking after two years we should apply a 1/365 penalty for every 4 days they are in the top 19, and they should regain it at the same rate when they aren’t in it). We want lots of voices, and time to complete things, but we don’t want the same people in there indefinitely.
