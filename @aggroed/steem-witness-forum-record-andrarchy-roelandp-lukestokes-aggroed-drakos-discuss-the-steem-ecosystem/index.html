Steem Witness Forum Record: @andrarchy, @roelandp, @lukestokes, @aggroed, @drakos discuss the Steem ecosystem

This is the discussion that we had today regarding Steemit Inc and the Steem blockchain. I’d like to extend a large thank you to @andrarchy who spent an hour with us sharing some concerns and perspectives from Steemit HQ. As you may not know @andrarchy is Director of Content at Steemit. He’s in a unique position as the guy crafting the message to share some language that can be used to describe this ecosystem, this blockchain, and the vision of what we’re trying to accomplish.

Here’s the Discussion

Steemit

I hear a fair amount of unreasonable discussions of what Steemit should and shouldn’t be doing. I appreciated @andrarchy’s candor of what is and is not Steemit’s responsibility. He’s on the show for the first hour or so.

A major concern: chain bloat

We spoke a bunch today about concerns over scaling. @andrarchy’s answer was essentially I’m not an engineer, but they’re working on it. Some of it is user error of putting to much in RAM and some is program changes that will allow more to sit in fast hard drives. This seems pretty important as many of the public full RPC nodes (like the Minnowsupport node @followbtcnews runs on behalf of that group) are currently using 512G of ram. We’re seeing 256g servers failing although it is possible. These are expensive and cost hundreds of dollars a month. For folks looking to build new dapps and apps on the chain they need these servers to interact with the chain.

So, the concern is there for witnesses now, and it’s good to know that it’s a priority at Steemit to make it better.
