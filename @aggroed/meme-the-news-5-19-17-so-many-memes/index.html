Meme the News 5/19/17- So many memes!

Whole articles? Ain’t nobody got time for that! That’s why Y’all need Meme the News. Memes are salty. Think before you scroll.

There’s roughly 100-150 new followers this week. So, you get a quick intro to Meme the News. I have a facebook page that has all kinds of people on it. The political spectrum is full, the life choices spectrum is full, the page is pretty nuetral. I go through that looking for news and memes that I think capture the zeitgeist of the moment. I’m not advocating every meme here. I’m presenting the mindset of the populace according to my wall. You may chuckle, you may feel anger, you may piss your pants laughing. These are just to poke open your mind and opinions on some of what you may take for granted.
