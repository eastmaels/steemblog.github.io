Minnow Mayor Town Hall with special guest @an0nkn0wledge (25 SBD up for grabs)

Should be a fun night. Once again I’m blessed to have an intrepid journalist join in on MSP-Waves to talk about a variety of discoveries that he’s made, truth he’s released, and a give us a picture of politics and the USA you can’t find anywhere else.

Show Logistics

Show starts at 9pm est. That’s 0100 UTC. @an0nkn0wledge will join us about an hour into the show. That gives us all some time to settle in, talk about what’s happening on the block and share a story or two.

To tune in join us in the Peace, Abundance, and Liberty Network Discord channel. We have 17,000 Steemians in there. Should be fun.

To connect with the audience and the guests hop into some channels.

Delegation

I stopped doing personal delegations for the most part, but I sectioned off a bunch of SP for msp-active. If you’re not getting votes from msp-active but think you’re active in the Minnow support community than reach out ot me in Discord with a 1 sentence pitch of why i should include you.

Dice Bitches

We’ll play dice. Up for grabs is 25 SBD. Typical rules. I roll. You roll. You tie or beat my roll than you move onto the next round. Last person standing wins.

If I win once we start from scratch. If I win twice I have a special extra gift for Suz.
