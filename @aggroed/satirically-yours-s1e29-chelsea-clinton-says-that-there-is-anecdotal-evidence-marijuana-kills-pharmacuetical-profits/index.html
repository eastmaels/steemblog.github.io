Satirically Yours S1E29: Chelsea Clinton says that there is "anecdotal evidence marijuana kills pharmacuetical profits"



In a fairly irregular press conference and campaign stop made recently by Chelsea Clinton, daughter of Democratic Presidential Nominee Hillary Clinton and Webb Hubbell former Democratic President and known philanderer Bill Clinton, she made it known just how dangerous marijuana can be to corporate profits.


After a public campaign stop and fund raiser Chelsea attended another fund raiser at a separate private pharmaceutical company event.



She said “There are many medical discoveries that over time have proven effective at generating money.  You can obtain long term very profitable patents on them.  You can then remix the same ingredients and repackage the same drugs, but apply for a new patent on them.  This is a good industry!  Hell, we can even mandate the use of some of this stuff!  How awesome would it be to have a president who supports us all, right?”  She gave a big wide mouthed hedge fund owning shit eating grin.



She continued “What isn’t helpful is when low cost, farm raised, easy-to-grow crops, that can come straight from your back yard get involved!  No one wants that!  We have all seen the stories of how narcotics and prescription pill usage drops after legalization of drugs.  We can’t allow marijuana legalization to kill any profits, and we want you to know that if there’s a Clinton in the White House we won’t let you down.”
