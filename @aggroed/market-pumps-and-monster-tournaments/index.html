Market Pumps and Monster Tournaments

Hey, fun fact. I’m still making tournaments manually. When I do it I make them two weeks in advance of when they are going to happen. Overall we’re in a bear market still, but Steem seems to be heading out of it.

Steem Chart

There really isn’t that much resistance between here and the 90 cent range. We’ll see if we can make it up there! But for now it looks like Steem is set to go on a nice bull run for the next couple of weeks. I don’t have a crystal ball and can’t tell for sure, but that looks like a good chart to me!

Monster Tournaments

Anyway, what this is doing is inflating how much money is being paid out in tournaments for Steem Monsters.

There’s less than two weeks of tournaments planned and there’s $5400 to earn. If prices keep rising that’s gonna be a lot of money very quickly!

Marketcap

There’s a bot in Discord that runs a marketcap command. It tallies prices. There’s some argument around how perfect the number is, but it provides a steady means to evaluate how the cards are doing.

We’ve completely turned around prices, gone up $60,0000+ in under two weeks and it’s all been since the launch of tournaments!

Tournaments are going, monster prices are up, and now Steem Prices are spiking. What do you think will happen to the value of cards?

Combine that with some cool new features like card rentals, and a whole new in game currency that you earn by winning matches that we’re rolling out and then consider how we’re working to bridge other communities/blockchains to come play Steem Monsters and you’ll get why Matt and I are feeling rather bullish!
