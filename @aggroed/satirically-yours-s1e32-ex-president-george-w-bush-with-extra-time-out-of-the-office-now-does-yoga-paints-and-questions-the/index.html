Satirically Yours S1E32- Ex-President George W. Bush with extra time out of the office now does Yoga, paints, and questions the official 9/11 narrative



Satirically Yours Fake News Correspondent @aggroed was able to sit down with the former President for a candid interview. 


@aggroed- Mr. President.  Thank you so much for your time.  


 GW- Sure, life’s been’a’whole lot easier since I got out of office.  I got time for this and some other new things in my life.


@aggroed- That’s excellent.  What stands out as new?


GW- Well, you know, actually, me and Laura started doing Yoga together.  My heart rate is down.  My flexibility is up.  I’m feeling great.  My teacher is this old Indian guy.  I can’t pronounce his name.  I just call him “Teach.”


 


@aggroed- that sounds delightful.  I know that Yoga has loosened up my back a ton.  Anything else?


GW-Sure.  I’ve been working on my paintings too.  You know trying to relax.  Especially with all the negativity in politics these days it’s so important to find some peace and quiet.  Did you ever see my work?


@aggroed- No, I haven’t seen that before.  Wow, it’s great to see those.  You did those while you were in office or are these new?


GW- Those were from the good ol’ days as pres.  Guccifer was kind enough to leak all the paintings I did.  It was a kinda warning to the world that he had the photos and could release more stuff.  It freaked Dicky and Rummy out.  You shoulda seen them!  I guess I lucked out because a lot of the emails that Guccifer was looki’ for weren’t in my email.  They were between Dicky, Rummy, Colin, and Condi.  I guess I didn’t have any of the stuff Guccifer was lookin’ for.


@aggroed- Well, what do you think he was looking for when he hacked your emails?


GW- Well, I’ll tell you. I don’t know.  My friends at the yoga class keep telling me he was looking for evidence that Dicky and Rummy planned that whole 9/11 collapse.  I read the 9/11 commission report.  At the time I was absolutely certain that the twin towers came down because of those 2 planes, but now I’m not so sure…


@aggroed- really?  That’s shocking that a former US President would question the narrative that came out during your own administration.  What’s got you thinking?


GW- Well, after talking to Norma from Yoga about it for a while a few things don’t make sense.  Did you see where flight 93 crashed?  It was a big dang hole in the ground, but where’s the plane?  I sent this question to Condi about it, but she never got back to me.





GW- I mean do you see a plane?  I sure don’t!


@aggroed- Wow, ok.  Well, what do you think happened?


GW- I don’t know!  That’s the whole thing, but I don’t know.  Every time I see a plane crash there is a plane.  The whole dang thing doesn’t just disintegrate.  Laura says I should just let it go…. Let it go, you know, like in Frozen.  But this thing is bugging me.  Did you see where a plane hit the pentagon?


@aggroed- I haven’t to much at those photos… what do you see there?


GW- Well, it’s the same dang thing.  You look at photos right after a plane flew into that building and it’s like “WHAT!  Where’s the dang plane!”


Here’s the photo I sent to brother Colin, but he was out golfing and never got back to me.



@aggroed-  that’s fascinating, but couldn’t a building swallow a plane?  I mean that’s reinforced steel and the plane is just aluminum, maybe it got smashed.


GW- Yeah, well, that’s what I was thinkin’ at first, but the engines are steel.  Where are those?  Hell, where’s a wing?  It went through 3 layers of that building and I don’t see anything resembling a plane.


@aggroed- wow this is fascinating!


GW- Oh wait, it gets better.  You ever hear about building 7?


@aggroed- No, what’s that?


GW- Building 7 is a building that wasn’t even hit by a plane and still went down!  I mean, it wasn’t even hit by a plane.  The commission report says it was just office fires, but no one I know believes that at all.  Look at this.  It goes at free fall speed and falls into it’s own footprint.  Now that I’m looking at it and not thinking Osama is just gonna jump out at me I’m thinking maybe there were explosives in there.



@aggroed- I never thought about that.  I’ll look into it for sure.


GW- What makes me mad is I was’askin’ Dicky about it.  He told me to shut up and leave it alone.  I mean, what kind of response is that?  I keep messaging him on facebook and twitter about it, but he won’t say anything about it.  Oh, hey Laura is signalling me that Yoga is about to start!  You wanna come?


@aggroed- No thanks, but I appreciate your time and will look into the links you sent.
