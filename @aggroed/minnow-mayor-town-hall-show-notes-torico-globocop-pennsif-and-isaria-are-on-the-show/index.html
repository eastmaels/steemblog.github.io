Minnow Mayor Town Hall Show Notes- Torico, Globocop, Pennsif, and Isaria are on the show.

Had a nice show. There weren’t too many people looking to come on air so I was able to give more than 5 minutes per guest. It’s a good time. I know lots of people have concerns about how the platform is doing and how the Minnow Support Project is growing. It’s fun to have people on to let them promote themselves and their projects. It’s pretty good

@torico came on the show and we had a pleasant conversation.
@globocop came on the show and talked about mspwaves (our streaming radio station) operations and needs.
@pennsif came on to talk about witnesses and a project he’s working on
@isaria came on the show to talk about our collective attempt to create more Lovejuice benefits for minnows as well as our upcoming MSP related project called The Peace Academy.

We played Dice Bitches. @clayboyn won the dice roll of during the show and I sent him 25SBD.

@isaria won the 250 SP delegation that was available on the show to people that came on air. She’s at a total of 375 steem power delegation from me. She’s kinda a delegation magnet.

The station

MSPwaves is hosted at mspwaves.com. It’s a streaming internet radio station. We have lisence to play over 18M songs in a streaming format (you won’t hear music in our interviews). We have background music that plays through royalty free music. You can hear the shows by listening at mspwaves.com/listen. If you want to DJ please talk to @globocop or @r0nd0n or @uniwhisp.
