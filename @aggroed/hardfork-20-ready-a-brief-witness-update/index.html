Why Steem is different

In all the other cryptos every time there’s a disagreement you can assume there will be a fork. At that time you can look around and roughly half your community is gone. That’s not the case here. People come and go with the seasons and the price and faith in leadership, but there’s one chain.

We all go to the same Moon!

We’re not forking new chains. So, make peace with your neighbor. We’re gonna be together for a long time.
