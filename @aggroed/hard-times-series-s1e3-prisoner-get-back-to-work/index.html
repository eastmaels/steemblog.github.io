Hard Times Series- S1E3- Prisoner get back to work



The Hard Times Series covers the life and times as government collapses and the life of those that live on.


“Prisoner 001776.  I will not tell you again to get back to work.”  Officer Grip rode over to where the prisoner stood.


“Sir, sorry sir, but there’s something in the field.”  Prisoner 001776’s vision focused far to East.  In the background he could see something large moving.  It wasn’t normal.  It seemed to limp.  Maybe it only had three legs.


Grip made sure he was close enough to 001776 to get a sense of where he was looking, but far enough to ensure that he couldn’t try something stupid.  Grip pulled out a pair of binoculars and looked in the direction that the prisoner indicated.  Sure enough he could see the beast.  It was larger than a dog, but smaller than an elephant.  Maybe it was a bear.  Grip had heard bears once roamed here before America had been colonized.


Grip turned to the prisoner “It’s nothing son.  Get back to work.  Nothing is coming over here to interfere with this operation.”  Grip went back to the shade.  He could feel his blood pulsing a little faster, but today wasn’t the day the beast would challenge the field.
