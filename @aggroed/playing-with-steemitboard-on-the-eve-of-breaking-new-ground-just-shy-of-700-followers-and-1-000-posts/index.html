Steemitboard

I started with nothing back in September

So, I think this is pretty good progress. It’s pretty crazy that this past week has been a major one for me. I rocked out about 350 followers just this week!!! I have a few hundred in rewards coming my way. My account is creeping up to $10k value. I’ve spent hours working on this “business,” but most of the time it’s been fun… so, it didn’t and it doesn’t feel like a chore.

https://images.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.heyterry.com%2Fblog%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F10%2Fron-burgundy-that-escalated-quickly.png&f=1

Actually, when people interact with me I find it personally really rewarding. I’m really honored and stoked that so many people upvoted my “Explain steemit like I’m 5” post. I’m really touched that @moormanclan decided to write posts to their kids because of my gifting post.

Honestly, I didn’t know how fun or cool it would be to have so many people interact with these posts. I’m really grateful you’re all here and thanks for taking the time to read my stuff. I hope you find it entertaining and find some nuggets worth keeping.
