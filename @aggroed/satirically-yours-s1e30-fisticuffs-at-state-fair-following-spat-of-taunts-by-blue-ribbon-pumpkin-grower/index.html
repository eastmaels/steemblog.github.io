Satirically Yours S1E30: Fisticuffs at state fair following spat of taunts by blue ribbon pumpkin grower



Farmer Patrick Santorum is no stranger to growing large pumpkins or getting into fist fights and arrests over them.  This past year at the Georgia State Fair Patrick delivered a prize winning 854 pound Pumpkin.  Fair attendees were stunned as they gazed upon the sheer mass of pumpkin.  That said, not all fair goers were pleased.



Dale Norquest McGinacutty the Third was not pleased at all as his pumpkin came in second weighing in at 812 lbs.  Originally he was quoted as saying  ”I hate losing to that son of a bitch.”


Apparently the altercation happened at the State Fair after the weights of the Pumpkins were announced and Patrick approached Dale to say “Hey Dale, you did a great job.  That’s great effort.  You did everything you set out to do assuming you meant to grow a second place pumpkin.  Can’t win them all bitch.”


Dale was quick on his feet to remind Patrick of his wife’s previous publicly known case of infidelity.



“Funny you should mention that.  I was just speaking to your wife Chessie about this with Roger.  You remember Roger.  He banged your wife like half a million times and everyone knows about it.”  Dale wore his shit eating grin proudly.


According to local fair goer Trisha McNartamier the fight started shortly after they had that exchange.



“Well, Patrick took to tauntin’ Dale about his pumpkin, and Dale took to tauntin’ Patrick about his wife Chessie, Bless her Heart.  Anyway, once he made that one comment about Chessie Patrick came out a’swingin’.  I thought for sure they was gonna smash right through their own prize winnin’ pumpk’ns.”


She added “When the cops came that’s when it all escalated!”



Officer Velent Cillor showed up on the scene and delivered taser shots to both suspects.  Dale has since been hospitalized and all of America awaits to find out if he’ll survive the altercation.



Lastly, prizes were stripped from both contestants letting Shirley Delight win for the first time in 6 years.
