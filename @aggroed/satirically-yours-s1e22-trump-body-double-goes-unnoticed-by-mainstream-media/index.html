Satirically Yours S1E22: Trump body double goes unnoticed by mainstream media


While the story unfolds around body double usage by Secretary of State and Democratic Presidential nominee Hillary Clinton following her collapse on 9/11/16 another story is brewing that has largely been unreported.  It appears that Trump is also using his own double on campaign stops around the country.


There is growing suspicion that the double is actually Lotni Frotelli of a notorious family gang, but both the assertion that Trump is using a double and the origin of the double is highly dubious.  While some are claiming there is no physical resemblance many are arguing that mannerisms are the same.  Namely, they both like chocolate, sometimes can be quite loud, and at times appear somewhat oddly social.



Many people remark about the general resemblance of the billionaire candidate and his alleged double.  Researchers scrutinize just about every aspect of the photos, but many walk away empty handed convinced it’s one and the same person.  Satirically Yours remains tracking this developing story along with the likely use of 2 body doubles by the Clinton Campaign.
