You need browser incognito mode and a disposable email service

Here is how, first open the contest page in incognito mode from the browser which usually tied to shortcut CTRL + Shift + N if you are using Chrome.


Secondly, open the Tempmail or you can use other services that do the same thing.


Here is the trick, every time after casting a vote:

  • Close the contest page which is in the incognito browser.
  • Open it up again and the previous cookies would be cleared.
  • Change the Tempmail to a new email address by clicking on the Change button on the left panel.
  • Rinse and repeat.
