Some notes to take before installing the download:

With the standalone AMTEmu, you can go to the official Adobe and download all the products you want and crack them with this tool. This way you are likely to bypass the risk of a funny altered version of Adobe version.

I would just stick with CCMaker for the ultimate convenience and reputation. And the only bad thing is that it is not open source, I gotta trust someone that this tool is not gamed before my download.
