ROR:Something about auction,Durability

自从昨日开始上架拍卖物品之后，陆陆续续很多玩家都参与了拍卖。短短一天，拍卖行的货物便有9件。

在仔细查看了每一件物品的属性时，发现所有装备类物品都缺失一样关键的属性。每件装备会有攻击力、防御力，但是都没有持久力。

在我自己包裹里的装备，除了显示武器的攻击力是多少外，还会显示这把武器的持久力是多少？

如果在拍卖行看不到这一属性，会导致购买的人无法辨别这件物品是否还有持久力，是否能正常使用。

所以，建议游戏开发方在拍卖行上架物品时，务必显示每一件装备的持久力！同时建议各位玩家暂时不要购买装备类物品，因为买回去的尽可能是持久力为零的装备。

Since the auction items were put on the shelves yesterday, many players have participated in the auction. Only one day, there were 9 items in the auction house.

When you look closely at the attributes of each item, you will find that all equipment items are missing the same key attributes. Each piece of equipment has attack and defense, but it has no Durability.

In my own package, in addition to showing the weapon’s attack power,so do the Durability!

If you don’t see this attribute at the auction house, it will cause the purchaser to be unable to tell if the item is still durable and can be used normally.

Therefore, it is recommended that the game developer must display the Durability of each piece of equipment when placing items on the auction house! At the same time, it is recommended that players do not purchase equipment items for the time being, because the equipment that is bought back may be no Durability.@bitpizza,@golden.future,@bobokyaw,@glory7,@ror.pay
