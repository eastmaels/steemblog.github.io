At present, ROR only opens maps of South Korea, with 11 towns. It is basically centered on Daejeon, radiating on all sides, and then connecting 11 small towns with Seoul, Yeongwon, Busan and Gwangju as transportation hubs.

The distance between two adjacent cities is usually within 2:30 hours. The farthest distance should be from Samcheok to Jeju Island, which takes 12 hours.
