steem从小鱼到大鲸的必备工具——排行榜

从小鱼到大鲸的steem必备工具——排行榜


新人进入steem，一切都是那么新奇。整个steem世界是寥若星海、宽阔无垠。新人犹如一只撞入高楼大厦的小鹿，茫然无知，不知如何起步，走向何方？路是走出来的，面对未知的世界，我们可以沿着前人的脚步，缓慢向前！刚进入steem世界通过什么途径可以找到优秀的先行者，推荐的第一个工具就是排行榜，通过排行榜找到你学习的对象，研究他们是如何在steem发展壮大，他们在做什么、怎么做。模仿他们成长的路径找到自己的方向！

1.steemitboard-ranking

网址：https://steemitboard.com/ranking/index.php


排行榜提供12类排行索引，包括声誉、发布文章数、评论数、回复数、点赞数、被点赞数、支付数、SP总量、关注数、被关注数、转发数、被转发数，点击表头任意一个关键字就按新的方式排列（目前并不提供倒序排列，建议增加倒序或增加底部按钮直达最后一名@steemitboard）。


（图一）


图一是按照声誉进行排名，第一名@haejin,声誉83.97。哦，我的神，也许一辈子也难以企及了，想看看大神是怎么成长的，点击他的名字可以直达他的博客，你可以关注他，任意参观他的家……


(图二）


图二是按照发布文章数排名，目前排名第一名的是@jlkreiss,已经发布文章30816篇，比第二名的@cryptomonitor多了7211篇。哦，我的神呀，他是怎么做到的，不过他的声誉为负数（-8.3）。到他的博客遛了一圈，最近更新是三个月之前了，看来他放弃了这个账号。各位欲在steem长期发展的朋友，发文需谨慎呀，不是越多越好。这位朋友也可以成为我们的前车之鉴。



（图三）


图三是按照SP总量排名，排名第一的毫无悬念是@steemit，拥有SP34898168，一个惊人的数字。研究每个人的SP也有特殊用途，在今后的文章中会有介绍。不过提醒大家看见这么一大笔财富，不要眼红呀。你关注我后，你的SP会有方法慢慢累积起来的。

3.steeminsights——同城找人


网址：https://steeminsights.com


网站目前提供两大榜单：账户榜单和应用榜单，详细的用法可以参照发布者 @dmitrydao的介绍

https://steemit.com/steem/@dmitrydao/announcing-steem-insights-steem-search-engine-based-on-hivemind



(图七）


在这里推荐给大家还是因为一个目的，找高手学习。

前面两大榜单都能够帮你找到高手，但是只是网上而已。有时，有许多问题需要面对面沟通的，steeminsights提供了一个很好的方式，同城找人。





点击“账户”进入页面，在“位置”输入你所在城市或任意地方，再点击“启用”后就可以得到你所要地方的所有steemit用户，你们就可以联系，约好在茶馆小聚，探讨steem心得和各种人生体验。很遗憾目前长沙就我一个人，希望是长沙的联系我，谢谢。

如果你愿意让别人通过这种方式找到你，那么你可以在steemit上做如下设置，将你的位置信息添加后更新，别人就能通过同城找人，找到你了。

黑白分明 [hēi bái fēn míng]
(literal: black and white clear)
It means have a distinction of black and white, which can be used to say a clear boundary, or a clear handwriting/drawing. Further it can be used as a metaphor for telling right from wrong. You can use this phrase to describe a person: “他这个人黑白分明。[tā zhè gè rén hēi bái fēn míng]” (He is someone who makes clear what’s right and what’s wrong).

How to play the new version of Drugwars?

How to play the new version of Drugwars?

There are lots of changes between the new version of Drugwarsand the old version from the page display, most of the friends prefer the original old version. The main content update includes the official start of the war and bootcamp units.


Rules of war:

1. resource :

All resources, including drugs, weapons, andALCOHOLS, will be robbed.The storage is now protecting 25% of your resources. That should make you more comfortable.In result the attackers can steal a maximum of 30% of the 75% which are not safe.

2. Protection mechanism:

After suffering a defeat, a 12 hours shield will be automatically activated, but it will disappeared once you attack another player within the 12 hours. if you are the offensive party, even if you fail in the battle,you can not get the12 hours of protection rights.

3. Bootcamp units:

In the very near future the Army that will leave for the war will be limited to 200 population(200 population rule is not applied yet. You may be attacked with as many units as possible); The bootcamp units and resource requirements have changed; nine new types of combatants have been added, including high-attack and high-defense personnel.


Recent personal game suggestions:

1. Look more and do less.

After all, the war mode has just started, everyone is exploring and growing up in battle. There was once a player who rushed out without knowing his opponent. As a result, the two sides fought hard and ended up with a tragic victory. Sadly, he did not grab any resources and lost nearly 200 steem worth of combatants.

2. Grab the bank to earn steem.

During the period of familiarity with the rules and the stability rules of the game organizers, it is recommended to grab a bank every 4 hours to use the drugs.

3. Recruit combatants.

if you do not want to grab others, it is recommended to use defensive and offensive matches, personal preference for Big mama (high defense) and Bazooka Guy (high attack) 1:3 .



After guaranteeing your own safety (in your storage there are only 100 drugs to be grabbed,and you have combatants of equivalent value to 5 steem in defense, others will not risk the 5 steem combatants to grab your 100drugs resources) ,Return to normal construction, and gradually recruit attack and defense high combat units, such as MERCENARY (attack 120, defense 75).

红红火火 [hóng hóng huǒ huǒ]
(literal: red red fire fire)
Both red and fire convey energy in Chinese. This phrase can be used to describe a thriving and rich life. It can also mean “flourishing” or “vigorous”. For example, you can wish someone’s business “红红火火”, which is the same with “best luck”, “make a lot of profits”, but only more vivid and subtle. A shorter version “红火” is also acceptable.
