Some tips about Partiko Mentors(Partiko 导师之旅）

It has been nearly a month since I applied to become Partiko Mentors. Through this month, I am comfortable with the use of Partiko, but from the perspective of a Partiko Mentor, it is still not enough.

There are three aspects of the harvest to be a Partiko Mentor.

First, I can use the various functions of Partiko skillfully. Of course, the most used one is to watch the advertisement ^-^!

The second is to guide four Steemit users, download and use Partiko.

The third is to help Partiko plan a third anniversary celebration of Steem, and send points to users with special Partiko points.

It took a month of hard work to bring 4 new users to Partiko. In the mainland of China, using Partiko must use VPN, that blocks some users. In addition, Partiko’s invitation to send points is also difficult to get.Every time the invitation is successful, but I did not receive the invitation points, which also led to less enthusiasm to invite new users.

Finally, I hope Partiko can also have a web version, and have its own VPN! In addition, the messaging chat function mentioned by @rosatravels, I did not find in the IOS version?


picture resource pixabay

申请成为Partiko Mentors已将近一个月。在这一个月中通过不断摸索，自己对使用Partiko 到是畅通无阻、得心应手，但是从Partiko Mentors的角度来说，还是远远不够。

这一个月以来觉得做Partiko Mentors取得成绩有三个方面：

一是自己能熟练使用Partiko的各项功能，当然使用最多的是看广告^-^!

二是引导四位Steemit用户，下载使用了Partiko。

三是帮Partiko策划了一起Steem三周年庆生活动，给有特殊Partiko积分用户送积分。

通过一个月的努力才为Partiko带来4位新用户，确实有辱Partiko Mentors的称号。个人觉得推广新用户使用Partiko主要还是登陆问题，在中国大陆登陆使用Partiko必须使用VPN，所以阻拦了部分用户。另外，Partiko邀请送积分也很难送到位，每次邀请成功了，却没有获得邀请积分，这也导致主动去邀请新用户的热情减少。

最后，希望Partiko也能有网页版，也能有自己的VPN！另外，@rosatravels提到的 messaging chat功能，我在IOS版并没有发现？@partiko
