The secret of Drugwars' upvote(Drugwars 点赞的秘密）


图片来自pixabay

Drugwars的点赞同万神殿的等级挂钩，现在大部分玩家都已经知道，于是上周便掀起了一波抢夺资源升级万神殿的高潮。大家万神殿的等级升上去了，可是Drugwars许诺的大额点赞还是飘忽不定、可遇不可求。

这段时间同朋友交流之后发现一个小秘密，Drugwars的点赞分两种，一种是普通dealer的点赞，这种金额小而且不百分之百保证有；另一种是king dealer的点赞，这种金额高一般会有$0.7左右，而且必点。如下图：


 


Drugwars的点赞规律到底是什么：

个人认为万神殿低于20级的是普通点赞，金额低而且不保证必点；高于20级的点赞金额高，而且每个分享帖必点。


picture source

Drugwars’ upvote value agrees with the level of the Pantheon, which is now known to most players, so last week it set off a wave of robbing resources to upgrade the Pantheon. The rank of the Pantheon has risen, but the big amount of upvote that Drugwars promised is still erratic and unbearable.

During this time, I found a little secret after talking with friends. There are two kinds of upvotes from Drugwars. One is the upvote of the ordinary dealer. This amount is small and not 100% guaranteed. The other is the upvote of king dealer. The high amount will generally be around $0.7.

What is the drugwars’s law of praise: Personally think that the Pantheon is lower than the 20th level is the ordinary upvote, the amount is low and does not guarantee the upvote; the higher than the level of 20 the upvote is high, and each share must be.
