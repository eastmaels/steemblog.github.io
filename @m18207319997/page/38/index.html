Some questions about the battles of Drugwars

1000 steem was injected into the drugwars prize pool at 19:57:24 2019-04-01.

1000steem已注入drugwars奖金池的区块链记录:2019-04-01 19:57:24转入。drugwars-dealer transfer 1,000 STEEM to drugwars pool prize。

 

After discovering that the drugwars prize pool has less and less steems, I spent all the chance to recover funds and expand troops. After I created a gang named DRAGON, I begin to know about some of the battle rules.

发现drugwars奖金池越来越少之后，一直埋头回本和造兵，没有去掠夺其他玩家的资源，建立帮会后，以后总难免涉及到战斗，开始了解一些战斗规则，尤其是看了@deanliu的[DrugWars] 戰鬥的邏輯，更新！『矛B盾R』之后。分享一些个人看法。

 

1.Combat investigation

Send a hobo or rowdy to investigate the strength and resources of the opponent, then decide whether to launch a formal attack on the opponent depending on the situation.

1. 战斗侦查

派一个小兵去侦查对手的兵力、资源情况，视情况决定是否对对手发起正式进攻。小兵自然越便宜越好。

 

2. Battle simulation

After you know about the strength and resources of the opponent, you decide to launch the attack, then use the simulator provided by Drugwars for actual combat simulation.

2.1. Entering the simulation

Click on “battles”, then click on “fight” and click on “Access to the Fight simulator” at the bottom of the page.

2. 战斗模拟

了解对手的兵力和资源情况，决定发起进攻后，利用Drugwars提供的模拟器进行实战模拟。

2.1. 进入模拟

点击“battles”，再点击“fight”，在该页面的最下方再点击 “Access to the Fight simulator”。


2.2. Select version

After entering the “Fight simulator”, there are currently two versions of the Fight simulator alternative. Click “Try the next version of the simulator” to enter the current version “DrugWars fight simulator v0.0.16”

2.2.选择版本

进入“Fight simulator”后，目前有两种版本的Fight simulator备选，点击“Try the next version of the simulator”进入目前的版本“DrugWars fight simulator v0.0.16”

2.3. Simulated battle

In the place where the number after the name of each unit is “0”, enter the data according to the investigation and your own force, and then click the “Submit” at the bottom to start the simulation.

2.3. 模拟对战

在每个兵种名称后面数字为“0”的地方，根据侦查和自身兵力录入数据，然后点击最下面的“Submit”开始模拟对战。

The two versions of the battle rules vary greatly, and different versions may have different war outcomes. Be sure to choose the correct version for the simulation.

两个版本的战斗规则变动较大，不同的版本可能会有不同的战争结果。请务必选择正确的版本进行模拟。

Drugwars gang membership application and gang function introduction (帮会入会申请和帮会功能简介)

I built the gang yesterday.I will introduce you to the application process and the functions of the gang.

1. The joining process

1.1. Enter the gang menu

In the function menu on the left, find “gangs”, the right side will show the currently established gangs, the current gang staff limit is 100 members.

终于建好了帮会，现将入会申请流程和目前帮会的功能介绍给大家：

1.入会流程

1.1.进帮会菜单

在左侧功能菜单找到“gangs”,右边会显示目前已建立的帮会，目前帮会人员上限是100人。

1.2. Find a target gang

Use the mouse to pull down on the right side until you find your target gang like “DRUGON”.

1.2.找目标帮会

在右侧用鼠标下拉一直找，直到发现你的目标帮会比如”DRUGON”.

1.3. Apply for membership

Click “apply”, after successful, there will be a successful application prompt in the lower left corner.

1.3.申请入会

点击“apply”，成功后左下角会有成功申请提示。

2. Introduction to the gang function

At present, the gang has some simple functions, they are gang setting and personnel management.

2.1. Gang settings

When you create a gang, fill in the name of the gang here, the gang icon and the gang promotion website, and finally the gang introduction. After this part of the content is created, it can be modified later.

2.帮会功能简介

目前帮会功能简单，只有帮会设置和人员管理两块功能。

2.1.帮会设置

创建帮会时在这里填写帮会名称，帮会图标和帮会宣传网站，最后是帮会介绍。这部分内容创建后都可以后期修改完善。

2.2. Personnel management

2.2.1. Personnel apply for approval

Click on “APPROVE” or “REJECT” to agree or reject the newcomer application.

2.2.人员管理

2.2.1.人员申请批准

点击”APPROVE”或“REJECT”,同意或拒绝新人入会申请。

2.2.2. Member level adjustment

There are two levels of gang members, Soldiers and Captos. If you join the new member as a Soldier, you can upgrade to the Capto (you can’t downgrade if you upgrade, the Capto can accept or reject the membership application for new members), or you can choose to kick out the gang.

2.2.2.成员级别调整

入会新成员为战士，可以提升为队长（提升上去了就不能降级，队长也可以接受或拒绝新会员的入会申请），也可以选择踢出帮会。

Finally, everyone is welcome to join “DRAGON”, there are already a large group of brothers and sisters waiting for you!

最后欢迎大家加入”DRAGON”,已经有一大帮兄弟姐妹在等你！

DRAGON 欢迎您的加入！
