ROR：挖完石矿之后的出售问题

现在一般3-4天会挖满一背包180公斤的石矿，每次往返Busan和Sokcho能赚到64800个金币。看着钱包的金币不断往上涨，也能暂时忽略无休止的重复劳动。比如，每个小时逐雇佣兵派遣挖矿任务、统一结束任务、统一安排到医院治疗，往返Busan和Sokcho，在商店出售石块。
只是希望，卖石块的时候能简化一点，希望一次能把包裹的已有石块卖完。不用一组100个的卖。现在一次只能卖一组，18组就要卖18次，每次输入100。确实烦人。

It will be full of my backpack of 180 kilograms of stone for 3-4 day. Every time I run around between Busan and Sokcho can earn 64800 gold coins. Looking at the gold coins in the wallet constantly rising, I can also temporarily ignore endless repetitive labor. For example, every hour, the mercenaries dispatch mining tasks, end tasks, uniformly arrange for treatment in hospitals, commute between Busan and Sokcho, and sell stones in stores.
I just hope that when selling the stones, it can be simplified a bit, and I hope that the existing stones in the package can be sold out at once. Don’t sell a set of 100. Now only one set can be sold at a time, and 18 sets will be sold 18 times, each time entering 100. It is really annoying.
