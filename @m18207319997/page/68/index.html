The first sketching time for a girl(第一次户外写生的小姑娘)

During the Tomb Cleaning day, Qiqi’s painting training classes were replenished on Saturday and Sunday. So there was only one day left in holidays, and that afternoon was used for shopping.

The Saturday class is arranged for outdoor sketching. Outdoor sketching is an inevitable way of drawing. The sketching is in the Bafang Park next to the school. The scenery is very good. There are mountains and water. When they draw in the shady corridor, they attract a group of small partners.

清明节期间奇奇的画画培训班周六、周日都补课。所以清明三天假就只剩下了一天，那一天下午就用来购物了。

周六的课程安排的是户外写生。户外写生这是画画的一个必由之路，自然的慢慢学会。写生就在学校旁边的八方公园，风景很好，有山、有水，他们在阴凉的走廊画画时，引来一群小伙伴的围观。


文章照片来自@foursi的清明节踏青


With the @wherein, She saw a lot of beautiful photos from my friends and made a loud sound 0f admiration. After the return of the sketches, some photos were sent to the steemit through the @wherein. I hope that all the photographers and paintings will give some advice.

用wherein看到很多朋友发的特别漂亮的照片，发出一声声惊艳的声音。写生归来后将一些照片也通过wherein发到了steemit，希望各位照相和画画的高手多指点。
