## Hunter's comment
This iStaging App is a perfect demonstration for AR's (Augmented Reality) application scenario. Many people must have the experience of furnishing their own or rented houses, apartments or rooms and it is always a challenging task trying to figure out which furniture or decoration goes well with the space ... Issues might come from the colors, sizes, or just the feeling of the overall look ... What if we can have a look ***virtually*** without actually need to putting them all together? 

Yeah, this **iStaging** App utilizes the technology of AR, VR and 3D to the user achieve this goal! It's not only useful for ordinary users and not only for furnishing your houses - if you are an interior designer, you can use it to show your work to your clients; if you are a real estate agent, use it to let potential buyers explore more about this property! 

Watch the following two videos and you will get the idea of its convenience and innovation!
