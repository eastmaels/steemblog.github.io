![DSC_6122.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmYwmtePXeBXUXYBRUPVDNikY2i7R3hNrtGj59opoLobw3/DSC_6122.JPG)

People say that this **Kourakuen** restaurant is one of the must-eat ramen store in Japan. We only found out out this after we went. To us, this was just a restaurant that looked good and suitable for kids for a Sunday family lunch. We love it. 

This is their Roppongi branch. 

![DSC_6104.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmYRCDMiqM4v2b9etRRiop21DvCumdcSmfnUEAS5km6JDW/DSC_6104.JPG)

Many tables inside. It isn't usually so for many Japanese restaurants since many of their customers come in to eat alone.

![DSC_6124.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmZrxNoaZ9wWyNaBcJLqTGkHiG2P9kAe5SpFt413qXKnc8/DSC_6124.JPG)

We were surprised to see many older people. It wasn't a common scene here. Good to had them with us... :)

![DSC_6125.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmbSjuVgmY5Tn1tUDRbgdN44LMBLyyeqeAtNocMo3pwTFc/DSC_6125.JPG)

Noodles, ramens, dumplings.... 

![DSC_6123.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVzVtijRx9UAwyZGT9v5JuGdgQY9XUtRHnSLLsSgJmn2D/DSC_6123.JPG)

Iced water - drink as much as you want.

![DSC_6128.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmb278BohWv7fMtDVE2eqkuKwUFPXgsXpxnXRtWE4j5qFD/DSC_6128.JPG)

This one looks bland but actually kind of salty... BUT! It's delicious no doubt! I love it!

![DSC_6129.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmcrQwgqQzeHKK5RpWwYG7twPXTPuHsq73jmBiQUTmjkrn/DSC_6129.JPG)

Fried dumplings and rice. I must say that I don't enjoy Japanese dumplings no matter how expensive they are. Should be just a taste issue. But... this time, I think I had the best Japanese fried dumplings!! Although the best here cannot compare the best in Taipei at all, it still means something! So, I am glad that we ordered this! ^^

![DSC_6130.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmRonp1afcVRRin1qLNsojbns6WbCWmzv92ntyetE3UjTB/DSC_6130.JPG)

This ramen was also good but I forgot its name... >_<....  So, in general, we highly recommend this restaurant given its prices are very reasonable too!

![1539860866778.jpg](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQme4SNg5AvRs8evFSL3xukUXe1pW56R2tnd2fNDAvt8RMb/1539860866778.jpg)

My son's turtle bowl.

![DSC_6131.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmTUvQbQPGiMR9nfiY7oQcfRvSPw981snHr28fcsNMd39D/DSC_6131.JPG)

![1539860864453.jpg](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmbxLR2LZmxiNqSaH6wuaZ7Q18rSPCPuPi826dYwz5cP47/1539860864453.jpg)

Asahi as always. Beer is cheap here and good quality!

![1539860862094.jpg](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmPDhkSYeVPPHAgnFu3pCE88JYi22XjtdPY7QgCaiifAcy/1539860862094.jpg)

They also have some frozen noodle packs for sale too!

![1539860869012.jpg](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmSvBWcnkA2zq9LN1JiRJ7owQNu7AiHrw1rxbTF4QyU5tJ/1539860869012.jpg)

We will be back again! ^_^
