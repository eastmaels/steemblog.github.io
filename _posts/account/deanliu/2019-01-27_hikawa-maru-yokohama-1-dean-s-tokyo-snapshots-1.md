Haven't done this series for a while... Let me continue to share my Japan trip... 

Last time I was still in Yokohama... Today, I'll show you the GIGANTIC  **"Hikawa Maru"** - a Japanese cargo and passenger ship back in 1930 and now a maritime museum for tourists ...

This huge ship is really amazing and ..... huge! Taking a tour inside the ship is really fun and nostalgic as well... Remind me of the Titanic movie scenes sometimes ...

Let's go, come with me...  ***On board!!***
