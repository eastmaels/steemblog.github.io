We need to go to Taipei Main Station for doing something, so we took our risk (of getting infected) and went there today with masks. We first arrived at the Beimen MRT Station which is the connecting station for Airport Metro (Danger!!)

我們周末有事得去台北車站一趟，於是帶著戒慎恐懼的心情，做好準備，戴上口罩，一家人就出門去台北車站了。先到了北門站，北門站是桃園機場捷運的連接點，怕怕~~~~

![photo6093454580726737266.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmU7Z79L3Gpj1q6VdafyZNhhQXZQakDGP4ruk6WgMoZmUS/photo6093454580726737266.jpg)

This is one of the exits. Not many passengers though. A guard is holding a sign to remind you to wear masks before you enter the gate.

還好，車站人不多，看來禁入境令已經限制了人流... 空蕩蕩的，只有一個警衛站在閘門前，提醒旅客要戴口罩才能搭乘入內...

![photo6093454580726737262.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmY3cg4UV5bT4Vmc3jX7H8XPucUc2wx4w4Ch3J9upDFVNE/photo6093454580726737262.jpg)

A first rate Japanese style ramen restaurant is still promoting on the wall ... Ironic. 

一家微風的高級日本拉麵餐廳還在牆上電子看板打廣告.... 真是.... 反諷...

![photo6093454580726737258.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmZBQgaYCX21feEinmCAS3ubbAYhnf9UCUFR6KhZsUFE96/photo6093454580726737258.jpg)

Maybe a photo e-book featuring a pretty lady with hot body is more suitable at this timing? lol

另一邊牆上廣告的是美女的寫真書，似乎比起餐廳，更適合現在來購買？lol

![photo6093454580726737260.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmfUuq95m8bZEfrcEFKPYXCtsedJ87rDi4YGswiuRVGEbs/photo6093454580726737260.jpg)

After walking for 10 minutes, we arrived at the dining area of the Taipei Main Station on 2nd floor. Again, not many people. Weekends in TMS? This is really a rare scene!!

我們走路約十分鐘到台北車站，到了二樓微風的美食廣場，看看，人不多。周末的台北車站，恐怕還沒有過這麼少人過...

![photo6093454580726737264.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmXVee2Lx9hQwwqk75X7bWoBEBbhk5CBsHUKdfVQQn8CyG/photo6093454580726737264.jpg)

Food court with open dining space is more welcome. We decided to have dinner here. The spacing seems alright.

美食區的人氣較高，可能是因為開放用餐空間，我們決定在此吃晚餐，桌間的間距有被拉開，感覺還夠。

![photo6093454580726737263.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQma3L3WKQh6T7Bxf6XZSqgAtMG8aBqJS1jCymxJbDXTvXF/photo6093454580726737263.jpg)

Social distancing: 1.5 meter. It's an official rule.

室內要保持1.5米的社交距離，這是官方公告的。

![photo6093454580726737259.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmS1rssRChZeSheeDLMwC3rzuxkYanmjiSxNzeciREN9BY/photo6093454580726737259.jpg)

Pick a table and order our meals.

選張桌子坐下，開始四處點餐。

![photo6093454580726737265.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmNNhWpi5YTKEk1HNPAhtWFA4FsN2H1tJAsBwPu272F8zm/photo6093454580726737265.jpg)

People have to eat. Here are what we had. This one is Tainan Dan-dan noodles with pork ribs. 

分享一下吃的比較開心，第一道，台南擔仔麵配上炸排骨。

![photo6093454580726737257.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmU2NLTwGLrXM4vwx3NaV3TUvXaVe2aVEuEvK32Wvmi5B7/photo6093454580726737257.jpg)

Chicken rice, vegetables and a Taiwanese sticky rice dumpling. Yum!

雞肉飯、燙青菜跟肉粽。

![photo6093454580726737256.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmSEopJJ7jJhuavz3buMzssxYaeG77J9znwjcJA6j2sLLZ/photo6093454580726737256.jpg)

Braised pork on rice and another pork ribs. This one tastes better!

滷肉飯跟炸排骨，這一家是專門賣排骨的，所以勝出！

![photo6093454580726737255.jpg](https://images.hive.blog/DQmPuEvx7nCXsEmPKSu9EjDs8JXTCYDPe1m1nJDBWnLiJjH/photo6093454580726737255.jpg)

We are very careful about sanitizing our hands whenever we touch something. But we cannot prevent the risk of air transmission since everyone needs to take off the mask to eat. That's the risk we need to bear. Since we only have very few new cases recently, guess that's ok.

很久沒去人多的公眾場合，更別說是交通樞紐的台北車站了，所以我們都小心翼翼的，記得碰到什麼都要消毒一番。但吃飯總得開口，別人也是，這是一定繞不過的風險.... 這幾天新增案例都很少... 應該沒事吧~~~~

Good health to everyone reading this post and keep safe!

希望大家都能保持健康，保持警覺，早日能找回往日生活！
