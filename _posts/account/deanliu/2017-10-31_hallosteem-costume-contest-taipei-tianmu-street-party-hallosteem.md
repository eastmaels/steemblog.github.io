Last weekend, we took our kid to TianMu in Taipei (see the links at the end for more info) and we decided to let him dress up a little bit for Halloween since we know that there would also be many kids dressing up on the streets there. Let the little one have fun is always the most important thing for us... So we did a little bit shopping en route and selected nothing fancy but just enough. He loved his outfit. So I decided to participate this Hallosteem Costume Contest with his photos. [I will probably be disqualified as I did not let him hold anything Steem related, but that's ok! Just wanna have fun!]

>上週末帶孩子去台北天母玩，行前就是決定要讓他在街上穿穿萬聖節服裝，讓小朋友開心玩玩！我們知道萬聖節氣氛在天母應該是最熱鬧的（對於天母有興趣的話請看文末連結），所以此行就是為了萬聖節啊！我們先在路上一間購物店，買了一些服裝與用品，剛好就好，也不標新立異，他的第一次，有參與，有開心就好！所以，我那時就決定可以來參加這次的比賽囉！不知道要讓他拿個什麼Steem相關物品，但沒關係，我志在參加不在得獎，開心就好囉！
