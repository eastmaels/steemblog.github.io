TT, my 6 years old son, is fond of NBA lately.

Not because now he understands the game, but he just likes to ask about anything including when I am watching NBA games. 

Also, he likes to win. So he always pick the team that was winning at the moment to cheer for and ask me to cheer for the other (losing) team.

While I am watching NBA All Stars highlights this noon, it reminds me that TT recently drew his first NBA drawing and it's kinda cute... 

![photo6291665092349110554.jpg](https://s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/dclick/image/deanliu/1550468490489)

So I am sharing here below... It's natural that he recognizes GSW and HOU. Before he drew, he asked me about the specifics of the game like how many players should there be on the field and etc... 

Besides the funny alien-like players (actually NBA players indeed *are* kind of aliens to us ordinary people, aren't they?^^), I think he captures many great details... haha... 

So I put it up onto the chain and share with you...
