## <center>世界地圖上的文化探索 </center>
## <center>When Culture Vulture Meets Steemit World Map</center>

<hr>

Steem的美妙之處在於，創造出的不只是一個區塊鏈，而是從此而延生的跨地域社群。跟其他的區塊鏈項目相比顯得更多元，因為被吸引而來的不僅僅是geek，而是各個領域的blogger、vlogger、各種媒介的創作者，以及各路奇人異士。

> The beautiful aspect about Steem is that it is not just a new technology, but it created a cross-regional community. Not many other blockchain projects like Steem which attracted so many bloggers, vloggers and content creators from various fields. All the cool and talented and enthusiastic people are gathering here on this platform.

Steem區塊鏈上各種展示多元文化的內容是非常珍貴的。我們CN區有一個翻譯計畫已經持續了兩個月——[《文化讀癮．一週譯報》](https://steemit.com/cn/@deanliu/culture-vulture-for-cners-issue-07-7)（*Culture Vulture for CNers*），目的就是希望把這個平台的多元文化面貌帶到CN區。

> The most precious things here are those contents representing cultural diversity of the world. The *Culture Vulture for CNers* project aims to bring these valuable contents to the CN community. 

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcRcpFGXwLcywJSCPjS176iD1W6eAoumGtJWatDndWRnp/2017-11-04%2015.58.26.jpg)

<br>這是現場捕獲的 @eroche 本人！是的，就是先有他的 #CultureVulture 徵文比賽計畫，才有了我們的*Culture Vulture for CNers*。Culture Vulture計畫在短短四個月裡累積了超過900篇文章。

>Here we have @eroche with us. He is the one behind the project “[Culture Vulture](https://steemit.com/culturevulture/@eroche/culture-vulture-challenge-october-results)” which then give birth to our "Culture Vulture for CNers" project. He has been talking to the Spanish curation team to bring the CV project to Spanish community as well. The project has been run for less than four months, but already accumulated more than 900 posts from authors around the world. 

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmTw73GtrZWeNeVbRCKRRbPS8ZuZsGSPqGaRW5fbcgKjUk/2017-11-04%2015.57.18.jpg)

SteemFest真是處處驚喜。 @eroche 與 @martibis湊在一塊，又是一個未來合作與資源整合的火花。 @martibis就是[Steemit World Map](# "http://www.steemitworldmap.com/")的靈魂人物。看著Steemit的文章化成世界地圖上的一個又一個座標，這確實是另一個閱讀世界的角度。所以，Culture Vulture與Steemit World Map的碰撞真的很讓人期待！

> Eroche was having a conversation with  @martibis, the creator of [Steemit World Map](# "http://www.steemitworldmap.com/"). They were talking about a very much anticipated collaboration between the two projects.

<div class="pull-left">https://steemitimages.com/DQmT94trdeTeaaVLC8KXWaPPBVhzp51oD2WBqQJgq3E35MS/2017-11-04%2015.57.58.jpg</div>

<br>但這裡先要恭喜 @martibis，Steemit World Map是前一天的Hackathon其中一個得獎項目！

> Congratulations to @martibis for his winning in the SteemFest Hackathon.

## <center>SteemFest 人物小特寫 </center>
## <center>Meet the SteemFestians</center>

<hr>

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmX4Td1ZwK6LH2TjNSGTKpUD7i6zthFJh6Zev1skZywyQ5/2017-11-04%2016.42.01.jpg)

永續生活社區「[Garden of Eden](https://www.intothegardenofeden.com/)」（ @gardenofeden）的發起人 @quinneaker 。是個非常有意思的Steemian，做的更是非常有意義的計畫。他在SteemFest短講裡，聊著他的手作皮革外套——當然，是一件符合永續地球理念的外套。

> This is @quinneaker, the creator of Garden of Eden. He was talking about his sustainable handmade leather jacket!

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmbYRazLssbUEg9u56isPvixx9vN7drDyYXFoZmF674qB1/2017-11-04%2016.40.47.jpg)

@starkerz的短講討論的是有關Steem的宣傳，以及社群自律（比如負責任地SP使用規範）的相關事情。[這裡](https://steemit.com/promo-steem/@tomasgeorge/starkerz-steemfest-talk-or-2nd-nov-2017-or-promo-steem-and-responsible-upvoting)可以讀到更詳細的報導。

> @Starkerz was giving a very inspiring talk, regarding Steem promotion and responsible use of Steem power.

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcAoiAEMnYbLVBWqnejCEfaA5eETvw4GEoWGTMTXY3vu9/2017-11-04%2016.43.01.jpg)

來自倫敦的 @allasyummyfood，分享她在Steemit的經驗。她說，她的YouTube頻道有三十萬個訂閱者，但其實並沒有給她帶來什麼收入。現在，她的Steemit帳號只有六千個follower，比YouTube少太多了，但這裡的收入卻是讓她十分滿意與開心呢！

> @allasyummyfood from London was giving a presentation, also very inspirational. She has 300,000 YouTube followers but not earning much money; now the 6000 Steemit followers provide her a decent amount of income which is incredible.

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmeZPwVGajMvMGWiwWW1jLUpa6fnYaZaF1DksTibi3RYrc/2017-11-04%2016.44.04.jpg)

@jerrybanfield，大家對他應該不會太陌生。他在現場的搞笑演出，就像他在Steemit平台上發表的內容一樣，總是讓人印象深刻。我們的現場記者說，他可是個非常nice的人呢！

> @jerrybanfield performed a comedy show. According to our Angel Reporter, he is such an easy-going and super nice guy. It's very pleasant to talk to him in person.
