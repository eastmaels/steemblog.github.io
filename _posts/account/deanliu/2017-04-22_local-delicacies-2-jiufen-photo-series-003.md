<p>This little restaurant is called "<strong>Jiufen Old Noodles House</strong>" (九份老麵店). It is almost the oldest local restaurant here so we come here to have meal every time as a support for the original Jiufen. Their dishes are also quite "old" or traditional which also fit my appetite as I am not... well... young. lol...</p>
<p>This time we had (in the order of photo appearance) <strong>beef noodle</strong>, <strong>beef tendon &amp; belly</strong>, <strong>wonton soup</strong> and <strong>rice with minced pork and bamboo shoots</strong>... oh, some side dishes are <strong>boiled vege</strong> and <strong>dried bean curds</strong> ... The bill for this dinner is about 11-12 SBDs... actually not cheaper than city prices... damn the tourists other than us... lol</p>
