<center><h2>THE ROOM</h2><br>
<p>https://steemitimages.com/DQmbvzuU4xpKrP9oci45cXZb7AmmejyWbqvAwCwirdR4jLC/image.png</p></center>
<p>朋友們，這次我們來到北投，台北日式溫泉的知名景點。離臺北市區只要搭乘地鐵30分鐘，馬上就可以來到這綠意盎然，方便熱鬧卻又鬧中取靜的，讓你身心靈放鬆的地方。我們幾週前去的。是的！這麼熱的夏天泡湯？傻啦，夏天才便宜嘛！反正有冷氣，就跟你夏天吃火鍋，冬天吃冰一樣，沒有問題的啦！</p>
<p>看看飯店房間先！這間屬於中上價位水準，不到五星，甚至勉強四星可能都不到。但也曾經輝煌，只是舊了。窗戶很大，景觀很綠，一切都可以。</p>
<p>When I met Steem an year ago, I feel that its logo looks so familiar and later on I realized ... it's like the logo of hot springs only without a circle underneath. Finally today I have the opportunity to connect both ... a hot spring experience on Steem blockchain!! Woohoo~~~&nbsp;</p>
<p>My wife and I went for a little get away weeks ago to Beitou, a suburban Taipei district famous for its hot spring dating back to the Japanese colonial times hundred year ago... You only need to take subway for around 30 minutes from downtown Taipei and you can get away from your busy city life in this place with large stripe of green woods, history, hot steamy spring and many good food! Take a look of our hotel room! This one is a slightly better than average hotel whose location is very very good. The room is quite alright... With a large window and a park-side view.</p>
