<p><code>Yesterday I use google to search for some content on Steemit and I clicked one of the steemit page in the search results. Everything looked fine. I can check any information and read posts as well. But when I wanted to upvote someone, a login page popped up and I felt weird as I was logged in 10 mins ago.</code></p>
<p><code>中文區最近有很多新朋友到來，區內的帖子也很多，這是好事。剛好昨天我碰上一件事有關帳戶安全，想特別提醒大家一下，連我這滿月的小魚都差點犯錯，希望最近加碼的肥魚兒與新魚苗都能注意一下。我用了Google來搜尋Steemit內容，點了一個搜尋連結，看似一切正常，但當我要upvote的時候，卻出現輸入密碼的視窗，但我記得明明10分鐘前還是登入的啊？</code></p>
<p>https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/16/vlj2ee529.png</p>
<p><code>All of a sudden I found that the url wasn't the "steemit.com/...", rather it showed "</code><code><strong>steemit.vlj.lt/...</strong></code><code>". How weird is that? So I googled "steemit.vlj.lt"&nbsp;it up to realize that it is a </code><code><strong>MIRROR site of Steemit</strong></code><code> (which, according to @originate there is no relationship between them). Gosh! I was this close to had entered my "keys". It is quite a lesson for me so I feel that I have to tell others, especially to many new comers recently at cn community. </code><code><strong>Pls be very CAREFUL</strong></code><code>! See the search results? This site has everything that Steemit has.</code></p>
<p><code>這時我發現連結的url網址不太一樣，出現的不是steemit.com/…而是</code><code><strong>steemit.vlj.lt/... </strong></code><code>怎麼回事？所以搜尋一下發現這是個</code><code><strong>鏡像站(mirror site)</strong></code><code>，根據 @originate 的說法這站跟Steemit.com沒有任何關係。天啊，差一點我就輸入密碼了。所以我想趕緊提醒別人，因為 @originate 已經提早5天前通知英文區了，所以我趕緊來發一篇中文帖，尤其是給新來的朋友提醒一下。</code><code><strong>千萬小心</strong></code><code>！看看以下畫面，什麼都有的。</code></p>
