Last night, I checked my account and found that my wallet seemed to have more STEEMs than it should. It was due to a big transfer from @oflyhigh... but why!? Oh, the memo said something about  the ***Steem Torch*** ... what is that? So I dig it out. This is a very interesting project initiated by @geekgirl, check her original and followup post:

[Steem Torch - Social Experiment on Steem](https://steemit.com/steem/@geekgirl/steem-torch-social-experiment-on-steem) 
[Steem Torch](https://steemit.com/steem/@geekgirl/steem-torch)

I am the 25th person on the list (*quite an honor I must say, especially during [Steem's third birthday](https://steemit.com/steem/@steemitboard/happy-birthday-the-steem-blockchain-is-running-for-3-years)* ) and now I am passing it on to **@team-cn** - ***the most active community of Chinese speaking users on Steem*** - so the whole community can know them a little bit better!

The following part will be in Chinese.

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmfZrw9uRCxfKwrzCs24ePqpSyknEeQPD5L9iMo3Z7x1VB/image.png)

昨天才剛看到O哥這一“天降橫財”帖，下一秒才意識到我原來就是下一個"幸運兒"～～ 

![螢幕快照 2019-03-24 上午10.21.05.png](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmeaW1ppUYHZCkwjQB5qvpB8Fp6FUJf7REGbYBCg2VfLc4/%E8%9E%A2%E5%B9%95%E5%BF%AB%E7%85%A7%202019-03-24%20%E4%B8%8A%E5%8D%8810.21.05.png)

於是我好奇地研究了一下，詳情可以參考這兩篇發起人 @geekgirl的文章：

[Steem Torch - Social Experiment on Steem](https://steemit.com/steem/@geekgirl/steem-torch-social-experiment-on-steem) 
[Steem Torch](https://steemit.com/steem/@geekgirl/steem-torch)

我簡單說一下內容：

* **Steem Torch**是由 @geekgirl受到比特幣閃電網絡類似項目啟發而發起的！透過一棒接一棒的轉移與增加資金，目的大概是希望展現我們區塊鏈社區成員的可信任與區塊鏈的效率
* 每個收到Steem Torch轉帳的人，應該選擇一位他信任且還活躍的社區成員，把收到的資金轉給他，附上相關說明的memo，並且“不減少”整筆資金，增加也以1 STEEM為上限（但後似乎是有人沒看清楚規則，非常大方地增加金額，所以這一條算是廢了？）
* 達到1337人次後，此人可以任意處置這筆資金，捐給慈善或是詢問社區如何利用都是可以的～～（為何是1337，我還沒查出）

哎呀，我跟O哥一樣，見財眼開，腦中想了一百萬種私吞的辦法（轉給小號撇清責任...），不過，畢竟，眼睛都集中在我這兒呢！發起人都來留話了，怎麼好這樣搞呢～～ 

所以，我就決定轉給CN新手村， @team-cn，讓他們有一些曝光機會！（原先是想轉出CN區了，畢竟也是得花錢）

我也只增加1 STEEM，因為這最符合原先的精神（其實是想省錢），呵呵呵～～

🔥🔥🔥 新手村！那接下來就看你們火一把囉！～～ 🔥🔥🔥
