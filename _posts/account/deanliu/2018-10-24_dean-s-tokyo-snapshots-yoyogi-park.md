The Yoyogi Park of Tokyo is located adjacent to Meiji Shrine. We did not enter the Shrine but we accidentally went to this park next to it and spent one of our most relaxing afternoon in Tokyo - by taking a nap on the lawn. ^_^

The park is so beautiful and has the atmosphere of layback and we saw people lying down on the grass so we followed... We asked the little one to guard the bags when me and my wife were taking the nap and he accepted the mission with excitement!

Such a cool afternoon in our long and busy trip.... zzz ZZZ ....
