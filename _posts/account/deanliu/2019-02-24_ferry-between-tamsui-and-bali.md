After leaving the [beautiful cafe](https://steemit.com/travel/@deanliu/a-beautiful-cafe-by-the-riverside-ancre-cafe), we decided to take the ferry to Bali, the place across the Tamsui river. It only takes about 5 mins for one-way trip. We spent a little time there, so when we returned, it was night already...

在[超美河岸咖啡館](https://steemit.com/travel/@deanliu/a-beautiful-cafe-by-the-riverside-ancre-cafe)待完之後，我們決定去搭渡輪到八里去。淡水渡船頭渡輪是蠻知名的，一趟只要五分鐘，就可以到達對岸的八里。早些年，八里其實只有幾間攤販跟餐廳，這幾年慢慢發展，已經越來越熱鬧，甚至有海岸休閒區了呢～～
