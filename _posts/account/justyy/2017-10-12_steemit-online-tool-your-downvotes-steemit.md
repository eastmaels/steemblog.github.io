This SteemIt tool lists the flag history (downvotes) in the last 365 days. The downvotes that are cancelled at a later time are still included in the table. An fair-to-use API to list the downvoting history is provided as it is.

**Tool URL**: [https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/flag/](https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/flag/)
The related version of this SteemIt tool is: [Check Who Downvote You](https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/who-downvote-you/)

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmNzHTXmr6otnbQTFMT6eYHbY3XthXvkAvV7BJdBHjsgKr/image.png)

有时候你踩过谁你是不是都忘记了？这个STEEMIT在线小工具可以列出过去365天你踩过的历史。踩过但是后来取消的也在历史中。该工具提供了一个简单好用的API可以列出踩过的记录。

**工具地址是** [https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/list-of-flag/](https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/list-of-flag/)
这个工具的另一版本是：[看看你被踩过](https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/who-downvote-you-steemit/)
