Currently, around [40 Steemians](https://steemit.com/cn/@justyy/daily-cn-updates-cn2017-10-17) in CN community delegate around  [6400](https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/delegators/?id=justyy) SP to @justyy who will send daily interests (currently at 14.6% APR) and [upvote](https://helloacm.com/best-upvoting-strategy-steemit-voting-power-matters/) the delegator's content worth of 200% vote (at least once per day). 

Many have trouble using steemconnect.com to fill the delegate request because they don't know how to fill the URL according to requirement and it is obviously not user-friendly... 

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmWqcrwbK9VD4vDTMEovwhv4qPfg2PL74XGfmFQszYNBDw/image.png)

Therefore, here comes with this tiny tool

Tool URL:  https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/sp-delegate-form/

It allows you enter the delegator ID (optionally, means 'you' if left empty), delegatee ID and the amount of SP or VESTS to delegate.

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmPnscgBJLxqC7P8uPz7GLZUYoDTLqc1i9NuY9yLvodxH7/image.png)

Click the button will redirect you to *steemconnect.com* with correct URL parameters so it won't go wrong!

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcC5xDe5GSpggXJPHMahARkLKhPJp2mLSLN37YbaLXLuC/image.png)
