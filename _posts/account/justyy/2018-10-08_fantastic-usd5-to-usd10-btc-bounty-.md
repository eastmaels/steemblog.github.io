[Wirex](https://anothervps.com/out/wirex) is a visa debit card that allows you to spend your cryptos easily (BTC, LTC, XRP or ETH). 

Now you can earn $5 if you open an account in [Wirex](https://anothervps.com/out/wirex) and buy BTC, LTC, XRP or ETH worth of $100, then you will be rewarded $5 in BTC.

If you order a [Wirex](https://anothervps.com/out/wirex) Visa Debit card and make the first in-store purchases, you will then be rewarded another $5!!

That’s all there is to it! There’s no minimum spend requirement for the first card purchase. Your crypto rewards will be deposited into the Rewards section of the app and can be redeemed into your Bitcoin wallet to use as you like.

Highly recommended: [Wirex](https://anothervps.com/out/wirex)
