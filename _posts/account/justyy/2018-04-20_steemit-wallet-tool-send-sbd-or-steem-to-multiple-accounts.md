A few while ago, I added the Wallet Tool to [SteemTools](https://helloacm.com/steemtool-v0-0-9-sending-money-to-multiple-addresses/). Later I realize that people want to use `Sending SBD/STEEM to multiple accounts` easily without installing the [SteemTools](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/steem-tools/emjfpeecopppojbhkigjjmcahbfahhbn) Therefore, I decide to make a simple web tool that does this job:

## SteemIt Wallet Tool
https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/wallet/

## Simple to Use
Fill the form:  the *Private Active Key* will be safely in your browser. One account per line.
![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmcXCqHkTFA4vqKJbRRCsmmtJpvUtSwmJHus1MQoN2AYgu)

Confirmation before sending.
![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmbBoErbEyUHqqFc7eioqQ6yfGzprVFgotMt5ogmQyWoZa)

The memo with *[username]* will be replaced by the recipient.
![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmbdoeBzPdP1V1ZHniPtvXirwX4p7j3M1JV9DZAMuzGmsT)

Verify it on blockchain!
![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmSjFeuv2tYzvRzmKvCQzkjVjBdyHAZuDqU49L4ykMheve)
