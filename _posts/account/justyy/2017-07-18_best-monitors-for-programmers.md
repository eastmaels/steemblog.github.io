<p>As a programmer, we normally want <a href="https://helloacm.com/how-to-connect-laptop-to-three-monitors/">at least two monitors</a> or bigger monitors to enhance the work efficiency. However, with regular monitors, you can turn them into differences.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Rotate your monitors 90 degrees (clockwise or counter-clockwise), and put them together. Now, you should be able to view more lines of code in a screen. you don’t need to scroll as often as you did before. Wow!</p>
<p>You can also read as many emails you can in a page. This works best if you have a long explorer tree which was not possible to display all previously.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/vertical-screen.jpg" width="1024" height="574"/></p>
<p><em>// 我公司的同事（软件工程师），一89年的帅小伙。 My colleague is a software engineer and he likes to turn the monitors like this.</em></p>
<p><br></p>
<p>作为资深程序员,我们总是想着更大的屏幕,<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1410">更多的屏幕(比如这个)</a>,但是实际上,很简单,只要这么弄,你就可以发现马上不一样了.&nbsp;</p>
<p>旋转90度 并排显示器(最好是无边框的),然后你就可以在一个屏幕里显示更多的代码. 另一个屏幕可以显示更多的邮件.之前左边(比如THUNDER BIRD)的分类树现在一个屏幕上就可以全显示出来了.&nbsp;</p>
