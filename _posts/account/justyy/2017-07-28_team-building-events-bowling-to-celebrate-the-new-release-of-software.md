<p>Every time the company has released a new version of software, we go out for celebration (also known as team-building events).</p>
<p>Yesterday, we went to Cambridge Leisure Park (CB1 7YD) to do the bowling. And here are my suggestions according to my own experience:</p>
<ol>
  <li>The bowling is scheduled at 5:30 and the dinner is at 7:30. I think it is better to dinner first then bowling so people are not having a empty stomach when doing activities.</li>
  <li>The company should have paid for the taxi fee instead of us driving and parking on own own.</li>
  <li>The team-building event should not be in the evenings when we should spend time with family. It could be scheduled on Friday afternoon so that we have a happier weekend.</li>
  <li>The company should pay for extra drinks and desserts.</li>
</ol>
<p>昨天晚上公司组织到剑桥 <a href="http://www.cambridgeleisure.co.uk/">Cambridge Leisure Park</a> (坐标: CB1 7YD) 打保龄球 庆祝<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/2305">软件</a>发布新版本.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;我其实对打保龄球并没有多大的追求, 本身就是一个发泄压力的好运动 只不过扔两球之后就得等下一轮, 整体体验并不是很好, 原因如下: &nbsp;</p>
<ul>
  <li>安排在吃饭的点(5:30)先打保龄球 再吃饭(7点半). 没吃饭怎么运动</li>
  <li>打保龄球其实是件比较好的团队建设沟通(Team Building) 的活动 但是没有HR来分组 还是熟悉的人自己一组, 都很熟悉了也没啥意思, 何况又没有美女.</li>
  <li>公司没有安排统一接车, 得自己开车去, 邮费+停车费得自己出, 虽然不多 但是毕竟不爽</li>
  <li>公司没有统一在打球的时候给我们买饮料 这钱也得自己出!</li>
  <li>到餐厅用餐的时候如果需要点甜点或饭后咔啡也得自己出钱!</li>
  <li>时间安排在晚上, 这点很不好因此有些离家远的(开车1个小时的) 都没有来. 晚上时间是和家人在一起的, 为啥不从下午就开始组织活动然后傍晚吃个饭就散了呢?</li>
</ul>
