What happens if you comment on somebody who mutes you? It turns out that your comments will be *on the blockchain* but they will be hidden to whom that mutes you.

Let's see, I muted my wife @happyukgo and asked her to leave a comment, when I refresh the post, I can see the comments loaded, but got disappeared milliseconds later. Like this:

![test.gif](https://steemitimages.com/DQmNNxmNA8Jh9u18agUyo3hHLJ6YrFu5B4SkMwaUBpf5678/test.gif)

The comment count is still two. and it is indeed saved to the [blockchain](https://helloacm.com/technically-images-can-be-stored-on-blockchain/).

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmSt4WKgtMS2darki4vAiEECS2NnZmgZxCkGzJsKRBnU22/image.png)

And the comment is still visible to those who not mute my wife. So it is just me then.

Also, as you can see in the above gif, if some others comments on the muted comment, they will be killed also. 

If you manually navigate the muted person's page, you still can see his/her post and comments, but on the comments page, [they are grey-out](https://helloacm.com/steemit-your-comments-will-be-hidden-if-you-are-muted/).

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmScQQ7SMWwx9add6Tb5aDbvL5uWg7qns8QSYQqznj3qec/image.png)

------------------

别人MUTE你，但是你给他留言了，他能看得见么？

试了一下， 我把我媳妇  @happyukgo MUTE了，然后让她给我留个言，结果我刷新页面的时候，可以看到评论，但是立马评论就被隐藏了，截图GIF如下

![test.gif](https://steemitimages.com/DQmNNxmNA8Jh9u18agUyo3hHLJ6YrFu5B4SkMwaUBpf5678/test.gif)

评论还是保存在区块链上的

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmSt4WKgtMS2darki4vAiEECS2NnZmgZxCkGzJsKRBnU22/image.png)

只不过我选择不看。但是其它人还是能看得见的。但是如果该不可见的评论下还有其它评论，那么它们也一概会被隐藏。 

被你MUTE的人的帐号，你还是可以手动去看的，只不过评论那页就成灰的了。

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmScQQ7SMWwx9add6Tb5aDbvL5uWg7qns8QSYQqznj3qec/image.png)
