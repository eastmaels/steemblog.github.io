We are often told that we are minnows and some others are big whales. But we are never told on what basis are we categorized as such. Today, I am going to show you a very very easy way to tell if you are a minnow or a big whale.

经常听大家说大鱼小鱼，也没有个很确定的判断准则，今天我无意发现，原来判断可以如此的简单，也就是只要把你的STEEMIT ID 填入以下的URL中，然后图片就会显示你是大鱼还是小鱼。

All you have to do is the replace *YOUR_STEEMIT_ID* with your actual STEEMIT ID in the following Image URL

`https://steemitboard.com/@YOUR_STEEMIT_ID/level.png`
