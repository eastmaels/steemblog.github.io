Sometimes, I want to know when was  my upvoted (or even downvoted) by a specific Steemian (e.g. your friend). Therefore, this tool is made for you. You can input your ID and your friend's ID (optional, could be many that are separated by commas), then the following tool:

https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/lastvotes/

will do the lookup for you, like this:

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmaVsNvqmbcVnuuBKoXyDWFg563Uj6cEmGgXnzZ2QP7LqM/image.png)

The key feature has been extracted to API that are available on four API servers worldwide.
