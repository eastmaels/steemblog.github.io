It has been a while since I developed steemit online tools. @nationalpark has published a few posts on steemit top delegations using @arcange's [SteemSQL](https://steemit.com/steemit/@arcange/steemsql-com-a-public-sql-server-database-with-all-steemit-blockchain-data). Thus, I think it is a good idea to develop a handy tool that lists the top delegations that occur given a date ranges (by default, in the last 7 days).
