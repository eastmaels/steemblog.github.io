<p>Last time we went to the Old Macdonald Farm, and the sons asked me, "Why we don't see crocodile?" &nbsp;I have to tell them, &nbsp;"son, because it is not a real zoo"</p>
<p>https://steemit.com/cn/@justyy/taking-kids-to-old-macdonald-farm-photography-and-a-16-sbd-give-away-old-macdonald-16-sbd</p>
<p>Now, this time, we go to a real zoo which is at: &nbsp;2 Hamerton Rd, Steeple Gidding, Huntingdon PE28 5RE<br>
<a href="http://www.hamertonzoopark.com/">http://www.hamertonzoopark.com/</a></p>
<p>Below photos are taken using Canon T3i + 50mm F1.8 (yes, the budget DSLR and budget portrait lens)</p>
<p>In a bright day with lots of sunshine, I had to reduce the aperture to avoid over-exposure. My friend told me to get a &nbsp;filter if I want to blur out the background nicely without over exposure the photos in a clear sunshine day.</p>

<p>&nbsp;20P能喂这些山羊这个动物园感觉要比之前的 <a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1716">Linton Zoo</a> 好, 价钱也差不多. 主要几个好的:</p>
<ol>
  <li>有小火车坐, 每人2英镑一次可以坐火车两圈 差不多 10分钟左右.</li>
  <li>有给小孩子玩耍的 Play Ground, 孩子们玩得很开心</li>
  <li>园里绿色草坪很多 所以随便都能坐下休息</li>
  <li><a href="https://justyy.com/archives/3117">地方很大</a> 这次只逛了一半左右 还有很多动物没有看到</li>
</ol>
