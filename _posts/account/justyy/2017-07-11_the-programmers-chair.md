<p>The company has ordered a few chairs like this. The chairs do not have the lean-back and you need to rest your legs in the specific position.</p>
<p>Without lean-back, it is not possible to sit with the ultmost comfortable position e.g. lean down and don't want to move my as* even a tiny bit.</p>
<p>Then you have to sit straight, which helps focusing on coding. The knees are sore if you sit for a long time. Therefore, I have to regularly stand up and go grab a coffee.</p>
<p>This is good for your health, as I believe.</p>
<p><img src="https://justyy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/programmer-chair.jpg" width="1512" height="2016"/></p>
<p><br></p>
<p>公司前不久购买了一批办公坐椅, 每个人有几个型号可以选择, 其中有这样的椅子.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://justyy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2017-07-11-14.31.32.jpg" width="1600" height="1200"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;我的工作环境</p>
<p>据说是对脊椎好, 因为以前的坐椅都是能有靠背的, 人很懒 一坐就一上午, 屁股不爱挪 (各种葛优躺) , 怪不得很容易发福.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://justyy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/07/2017-07-11-14.40.42.jpg" width="1600" height="1200"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>程序员的 专用椅子</p>
<p>现在可好了, 这个椅子你没有背椅可以靠, 而且当我把双腿架在上面, 一会儿(不到五分钟) 就能感觉膝盖有点酸, 久了就会自然而然的起来 放松一下, 比如接杯水泡杯<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1316">咖啡</a>之类的.</p>
<p>幸运的是, <a href="https://justyy.com/archives/3779">公司</a>HR已经考虑了　我这种想尝尝鲜但是试用之后又不喜欢的情况, 公司多订了几把很舒适的<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/4765">背靠椅</a>, 所以我也就偶尔心血来潮换着坐一坐.&nbsp;</p>
