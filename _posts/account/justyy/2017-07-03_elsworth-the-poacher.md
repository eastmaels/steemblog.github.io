<p>The company has just finished a new release cycle for the software so we went to a pub (The Poacher) in Elsworth, Cambridge UK for celebration. It is just a simple celebration for the new release!</p>
<p>The food is OK, the UK has just entered summer (with 26 degree) and there is a sign on the wall saying "Free Beer Tomorrow!" &nbsp;LOL</p>
<p>Elsworth 是剑桥西边9英理处的一个小镇 (<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elsworth">Wiki</a>). 这次<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/3916">公司聚餐</a> (庆祝软件新版本发布) 就选了镇上的一个小酒巴 The Poacher. 地址是: 1 Brockley Road, Elsworth, Cambridge, CB23 4JS, 小酒巴也<a href="http://www.poacherelsworth.co.uk/">有个网站</a>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;离公司开车不到10分钟就能到, 离家里也10分钟左右, 剑桥郡除了剑桥市其它环绕着很多小<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1522">村庄</a>, 几乎每个村庄都有个酒巴 而每个酒巴都有自己的特色而且都有网站(网站上除了食物介绍 还会有酒巴历史, 村庄历史和一些酒巴的活动, 比如请歌手等)&nbsp;</p>
