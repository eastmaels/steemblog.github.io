One of the biggest problems of steemit is that new users lose interests quickly when they realize soon that they can't really earn  'that much' just by writing good contents. A few penny per post will soon turn them away because even good contents are nor discovered (few followers) neither promoted to the front-pages.

I manage a SteemIt CN wechat group and the motivation of #promo-cn (as part of my witness campagins [Witness Thread](https://steemit.com/witness-category/@justyy/justyy-just-another-witness)) is to promote steemit in CN community.

@dailychina selects a few quality posts daily, publish them in @justyy 's daily CN post e.g. [this](https://steemit.com/cn-stats/@justyy/--daily-cn-updates-cncnpower-downyy2018-05-20) and rewards them.

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcuw5wQdCybjUk9EjzbDqWf2aZYHiKaCptD8SFsX6vKth/image.png)

The idea is to engage the steemians and reward them when they have good contents. Machine learning algorithms help to generate the list. There is even a blacklist to help improve the list.

The process has been running for months and really good feedbacks are received. I am so happy to see this actually makes the world a little bit better. ^_^
