## Hunter's comment
This is by far the most handy tool that allows me to convert Steem/SBD to Fiats (EUR, GBP and USD). 

Ordering this plastic visa debit card is free and quick (arrives in 3 days). You can easily exchange your bitcoin, litecoin to your fiat account, then you can spend it just like normal visa debit card.

The good thing about this is that you can withdraw cash! To spend STEEM/SBD, you need a exchange e.g. bittrex.

The exchange rate between BTC and GBP is very close to the Google's rate. Compare to coinbase or others, it saves the fees.

Highly recommended as it also gives you 0.5% cash back which will return to your account as bitcoin! Simply WOW
