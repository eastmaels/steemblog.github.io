I was using [conductor feed](https://helloacm.com/steem-witness-replay-time-takes-longer-and-longer/) to publish the feed previously however it is not stable at all. Sometimes it works, and sometimes it doesn't. I have to manually run the command, enter password, press Ctrl + Z to stop it, run `bg` to put it at background and use `disown` to detach it (stay in background).

During the past few months, I observed that the feed publishing was kinda random sometimes the interval was a few hours, and even worse, it randomly quit.

Recently, I came across the [Steem Witness Price Feed Publishing Tool](https://github.com/MattyIce/pricefeed) and it works perfect.

Now, @justyy as a witness (loyal to steem) has been publishing feed regularly once every hour. By using the `npm pm2` process management tool, the feed publisher stays controlled in background nicely.
