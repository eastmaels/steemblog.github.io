I guess, it is rare that witnesses are rewarded for 0-transactions blocks. A block is a block no matter how many Txs are in it.

For example [#26286440](https://steemd.com/b/26286440)
![image.png](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmbtDJy6DaCFc6Fjtm4SHR4cwDhb5p9FWxpZdvVBTBEArN)

## What happened?
When HF20 was rolled out - nearly all accounts could not post, comments, votes due to negative Resource Credits. The steem blockchain was read-only...

![image.png](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmVvxmRQgdB994wCuK5npPyL3i4bz8cYwb6gSSt6Mqf2hL)

Thanks to recent patches e.g. 0.20.5, the steem blockchain is recovering... I still have some faith in STEEM!
