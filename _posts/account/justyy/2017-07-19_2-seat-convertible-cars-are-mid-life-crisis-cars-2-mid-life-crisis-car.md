<p>My colleague Chris told me that the BMW Z4 is a mid-life crisis car. He said, some men dump their wife in the mid age (40 maybe) and buy such 2-seats convertible car just to recapture the youth with young ladies (age of 20 maybe) sitting aside.</p>
<p>I don't know if this is true. But, this car (BMW-Z4) has a very low MPG and a very high insurance group. It is definitely not a practical family-use car.</p>
<p>Check the photos below (It is not my car... it is my friend's car.. LOL)</p>
<p>周五和同事CHRIS在聊天 说到 BMW Z4 跑车&nbsp;</p>
<p>他说 这车是公认的 Mid-life Crisis CAR 我不太理解 他解释说: 一些男人到了中年就把他老婆 给 休了 (dumped) 然后 为了 重拾青春 (Recapture the Youth) 买两门的跑车 就为了旁边能坐上20多岁的小姑娘证明自己还年轻. <a href="https://justyy.com/archives/152">人到中年</a> 男人女人都会有更年期症状的.&nbsp;</p>
<p>2座跑车 一是贵 维修费 稍微碰了擦了那个心疼 二是保险贵 三则是不实用 四则是费油(不环保) 不过像上面说的 开起来起的很拉轰. &nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://justyy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/bmw-z4-2.jpg" width="980" height="735"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;底盘太低了 过个小BUMP都得慢 要不然就碰坏了; 引擎声挺大 一踩油门挺拉轰; 加速感强 0 – 60公理每小时 一踩油门就有了; 回头率高; 就差一妹子坐旁边了 🙂&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://justyy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/bmw-z4.jpg" width="980" height="735"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;不过一年的路税(按CO2排放量) 需要 290英镑 <a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1260">保险</a>也很贵(Insurance Group) 这车适合小情侣开 我这有家有儿的人还是 开个小轿车 或者 SUV好了. 我那<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1210">AUDI A6</a> 就不错 里面空间很大 装孩子很够.&nbsp;</p>
