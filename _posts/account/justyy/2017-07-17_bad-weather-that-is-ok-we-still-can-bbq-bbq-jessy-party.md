<p>Last week, we went to Jessy (my friend's daught)'s birthday party (in the Cambridge Lammas Land Park). The kids are having fun and there are lots of activities: &nbsp;throwing water balloons to each other, playing football, swimming, badminton.</p>
<p>The kids are energetic. When the meat are cooked, they all come. Everything is so <a href="https://steakovercooked.com/wedding/cooking-recipe-fried-fish-with-tomato-and-mushroom/">delicious</a>. There are king prawns, chicken wings, sweet potatoes, pork, burgers, squid and salmons. &nbsp;</p>
<p>The British talk about weather that is because all these can happen in a single day: &nbsp;sunny, rainy, windy, and even snowing.&nbsp;</p>
<p>It even rained a little bit for 20 minutes, but that is OK, we can still BBQ.</p>
<p>What a day out!</p>
<p>上周和朋友在<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/4703">剑桥草地</a>上BBQ烧烤就觉得是件很舒服的事情, 于是就临时敲定了在这个草地上举行朋友孩子Jessy的<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/2559">生日Party</a>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>美中不足的是: 天气阴天 中间还下了一小阵雨, 只好躲到大树下避雨, 不这这丝毫不影响我们BBQ的热情, 特别是孩子们有吃有玩, 真是开心.&nbsp;</p>
<p>英国的天气素来以多变著称, 一天中能经历刮风 下雨 下冰雹, 出太阳.. 所以英国人都见怪不怪了 (英国人见面聊天讨论最多的就是天气), 也因此, 雨伞并没有雨衣来得实用, 下阵小雨跑树下商店躲躲一会儿就好了. 草地上还有许多人把帐篷还就遮雨的篷都太来了 真是风雨无阻.&nbsp;</p>
<p>这次BBQ吃的可真丰富: 大虾, 鸡翅, 红薯, 猪肉, 汉堡, 鱿鱼和三文鱼. &nbsp;孩子们一看到食物好了就都跑过来吃了, 好香好好吃.</p>
