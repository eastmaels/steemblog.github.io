As you probably notice, @justyy 's witness node has been updated to 0.19.3 for two days and everything seems smooth as we expect.

## My steps of updating from 0.19.2
Run `./run.sh build` but it may be faster using `./run.sh install`

![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmXJC5uWc91uVhJDS9N1MWC6ctooPEW8qWhnobQLLjy3NA)

Then `./run.sh restart`
![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmVZowZDpdN5cur1pTno9P8pQkPu6r7zLC3FaTXrgJJBuj)

When you view the logs, you will see something like this:
![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmbEE8yZUeMQQUT8fDSN5LM7RrUqjCreTfmJsfM3pzqtBA)

And when you see these, it usually means now the node is [updated.](https://helloacm.com/steem-witness-node-updated-to-0-19-3/)
![image.png](https://gateway.ipfs.io/ipfs/QmTU5swRVFgjs1eBnfbNbFxqhmzqbbtTDkD35BwnviPgCX)

However, it may require you to produce your next block before `0.19.3` is shown in the [blockchain](https://helloacm.com/london-cryptocurrency-show-steem-is-the-no-1-blockchain-in-the-world/).
