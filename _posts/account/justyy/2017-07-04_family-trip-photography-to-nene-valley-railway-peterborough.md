<p>Weekend is always the time that should be spent with family. &nbsp;A few months ago, we went to the NVR Peterborough railway station and my sons love it (Especially the Thomas Train, which I think all boys are just crazy about).</p>
<p>The full address is: &nbsp;<a href="http://www.nvr.org.uk/">Nene Valley Railway Peterborough</a> Wansford Station, Stibbington, Peterborough, PE8 6LR&nbsp; (United Kingdom)</p>
<p>It only took 30 minute drive to the nearest NVR station, which is Peterborough Wansford station.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>内罗瓦铁路彼得堡 <a href="http://www.nvr.org.uk/">Nene Valley Railway Peterborough</a> Wansford Station, Stibbington, Peterborough, PE8 6LR&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;孩子们一直喜欢火车, 特别是 托马斯火车 (Thomas Train), 在周六的时候我们全家来场说走就走的旅行, 当天去当天回, 还好开车到指定的Peterborough Wansford火车站只需要30分钟左右.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://justyy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/wansford-station-nvr.jpg" width="1037" height="692"/></p>
<p>You don't need to book the tickets in advance. The costs are: &nbsp;16 pounds for adult, child 8 pounds, and the elderly 13 pounds. It is free for kids under 3. If you come as a family (2 adults and 3 children), you can buy family ticket for 40 GBP.</p>
<p>车票不需要提前预定, 到了就可以买, 票价不便宜(英国火车向来不便宜): 成年16英镑, 小孩8英镑, 老人(超过60岁) 13英镑. 3岁以下孩子免费. 两大人三小孩可以买家庭票 (Family) 40英镑.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;可惜的是去的那天 并没有 托马斯<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1233">火车</a>的活动. 每年一般在孩子的假期会有搞一些活动 (<a href="http://www.nvr.org.uk/special-events">官网</a>): 特定的火车只有在这个时候才会开放给孩子 (这时候则需要提前定票否则现场去肯定票已售罄).&nbsp;</p>
