<p>Today, we went to the Lammas Land Park in Cambridge. We love this place because:</p>
<p>1. the park is free (no admissions fee)</p>
<p>2. the park has a play ground for kids</p>
<p>3. the park has a water pool (only less than 0.5 meter deep, so in general quite safe)</p>
<p>4. You can picnic and BBQ on the grass, if you don't burn the grass</p>
<p>5. The park has a handy grocery shop so in case you forgot something, you can buy it from</p>
<p>6. The park has a toilet (so you don't need to run far)</p>
<p>7. You can just leave the kids running on the grass, it is quite safe area.</p>
<p>8. Most importantly, the grow-ups can sit, eat, talk, and enjoy the day!</p>
<p>HIGHLY recommended!</p>
