I have written an online tool to check who downvoted you in the last 365 days. 

https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/who-downvote-you/

Enter your STEEM ID, and press Enter you should get a list of downvoters very quickly.

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmQy8QXznmgPYV5HwT1kenU6Jmt1C71G7gWNp9mYFmcYQL/image.png)

The API is also made public free to use so that you can integrate in your tools/applications.

https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/who-downvote-you-steemit/

输入ID，按回车即可查询：

![](https://steemitimages.com/DQmTGr938Y6P4fvKLyKwuWAaKCKSotkWKkLoJciHcB1ayGY/image.png)
