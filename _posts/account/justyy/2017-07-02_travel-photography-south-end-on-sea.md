<html>
<p>South-end-on-sea is a popular town in the south east London (<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southend-on-Sea">WIKI</a>). Last summer, we have been there for the first time and it indeed is a good place for family especially if you have young kids. They have a big outdoor <a href="http://adventureisland.co.uk/">Adventure Island</a> which is just fun. There are entertaining games for grown-ups as well.</p>
<p>South-end-on-sea has a beach, but it is far from perfect e.g. the sand is too tough to walk with bare feet. But the kids just love it, so prepare the toy plastic bucket etc.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The sea food is highly recommended. This time, we tried the rice with seafood, which is just yummy!&nbsp;</p>
<p>We are definitely going there again!</p>
<p>去年已经去过了 South-End-On-Sea了 (<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/2825">照片1</a> 和<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/2857">照片 2</a>), 这是一个伦敦东部海滨度假圣地, 离家开车近2个小时. 今年英国的夏天, 又来了一场说走就走的旅行. South-End-On-Sea 是个好地方 因为海边有大型的儿童乐园, 带娃的朋友一定要去了. 而且 South-End-On-Sea 有海滩 有2000米世界最长的码头, 有购物街, 吃海鲜特别方便, 其实住在South-End-On-Sea是相当幸福的.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;其实去 South-End-On-Sea 一天就差不多可以了, 时间充裕的话不妨可以待两天住一晚, 上次住的就是海景酒店(一晚100多英镑, 还是相当的值, 可以看到海景). 去South-End-On-Sea主要做三件事情: 去儿童乐园(大人小孩其实都能玩), 去海滩上坐坐(晒晒太阳 看看美女 拍拍照), 去吃海鲜. 女人们还可以去逛逛街. &nbsp;</p>
<h2>SOUTH-END-ON-SEA 儿童乐园</h2>
<p>儿童乐园 可以买天票也可以按次来买 (Ride Ticket = 1.2英镑一次). 很多项目小孩需要大人陪着, 大人是不需要票的, 这次玩了碰碰车 很是开心 (一辆3.8英镑大概5分钟吧). 还坐了摩天轮, 不过时间有点久, 转了大概8圈后才放下来.&nbsp;</p>
