Utopian Moderators & Supervisors is a [Chrome Extension](https://helloacm.com/adding-unreviewed-contributions-list-of-approved-rejected-posts-to-utopian-chrome-extension/) that is aimed to provide useful information and statistics to the [utopian](https://helloacm.com/being-part-of-utopian-moderating-team-is-great-intro-post/) moderators & supervisors.

## New Features in v0.0.5
This version has the following changes:

###  Adding Unreviewed Contributions
Clicking the item in the list navigates to the Utopian corresponding category for review.

![image.png](https://res.cloudinary.com/hpiynhbhq/image/upload/v1517614807/ohclcpeihsuia9etmxex.png)

Pie Chart that gives you quick idea on which category needs help most.
![image.png](https://res.cloudinary.com/hpiynhbhq/image/upload/v1517614848/vfikqw7vuxfm1ezqwak4.png)

### Listing the Approved/Rejected Posts
Sometimes, you need to quickly revisit your decisions, so the latest reviewing posts is a good tool to go back to the posts.

![image.png](https://res.cloudinary.com/hpiynhbhq/image/upload/v1517614931/nrjqtrwenwyqwpvptzso.png)

### Other changes
Removing the Tab `ShortCuts` as it is now integrated in the `Unreviewed Contribution`
