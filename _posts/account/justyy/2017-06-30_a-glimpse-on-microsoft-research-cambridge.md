<p>&nbsp;昨天去了<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/4429">剑桥</a>火车站边上的微软研究院 (Microsoft Research Cambridge) 参加一个工业界的<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/4641">无线技术研讨会</a>.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;I recently <a href="https://helloacm.com/a-glimpse-on-microsoft-research-cambridge/">attended a seminar</a> in Microsoft Research (Cambridge, UK) and was impressed by its office.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/microsoft-research-building.jpg" width="1512" height="2016"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;The Microsoft Research Cambridge Building</p>
<p>微软研究院(<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/3569">剑桥</a>)就在剑桥火车站边上, 地址是 21 Station Road.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;The <a href="https://helloacm.com/microsoft-interview-question-get-the-area-of-the-triangle/">Microsoft</a> Research Cambridge Office Building is next to Cambridge Station (21 Station Road), therefore it is very convenient if you take a train from London.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/microsoft-research-21-station-road-cambridge.jpg" width="1600" height="1200"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;The Microsoft Research Cambridge Building is at 21 Station Road, Cambridge, Next to Railway Station</p>
<p>微软研究院(剑桥)很敞亮, 特别是进门的接待处.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;Nice, clean, and Spacious.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/lobby-microsoft-research.jpg" width="1512" height="2016"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;Long Lobby at Microsoft Research</p>
<p>墙上有很多海报, 特别振奋鼓舞, 比如 “Pushing the boundaries of Programming 把编程推向极致.”.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;I do like the posters on the glass, you can read the Microsoft slogans everywhere e.g. “Pushing the boundaries of Programming”.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/microsoft-pushing-the-boundaries-of-programming.jpg" width="1600" height="1200"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;The Poster says “Pushing the Boundaries of Programming”</p>
<p>&nbsp;A view of the Conference room, oh, that is why I was there.&nbsp;</p>
<p>梯形会议室&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/microsoft-research-conference-room.jpg" width="1600" height="1200"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;Conference Room</p>
<p>墙上很多显示器: “Microsoft Research”, “Welcome to Microsoft”.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;Many Monitors hang on the wall, showing “Microsoft Research” or “Welcome to Microsoft”.&nbsp;</p>

<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/microsoft-research-reception-hall.jpg" width="1512" height="2016"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;Reception Hall</p>
<p>很多很激励的话. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Inspiring posts from Microsoft Research.&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/posters-microsoft-research.jpg" width="1412" height="1059"/></p>

<p>Visual F#? 是不是(微软)现在是个<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/3383">编程语言</a>都是可视化的?&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;Visual F#? Almost every programming language is Visual now?&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="https://helloacm.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/visual-f-sharp.jpg" width="1512" height="2016"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<blockquote>Microsoft Research created the functional programming technology that allows developers to program succinctly and expressively against rich spaces of data.<br>
Microsoft Research创建了功能编程技术, 允许开发人员简洁而富有表现力地编写丰富的数据空间.</blockquote>
<p>研讨会和很多<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/1318">会议</a>一样, 中间都会有咖啡茶甜点时间, 这是个交换名片扩大社交的好机会, 能见到很多工业界上的大牛.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Social Networking via the Coffee Breaks. Good chance to meet and talk to the Giants in industry.</p>
<p><img src="https://justyy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/coffee-breaks-microsoft-research.jpg" width="1512" height="2016"/></p>
<p>&nbsp;研讨会都会有休息 是交换名片的好机会</p>
<p>虽然没有机会参观<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/3177">微软</a>员工办公环境, 但是大概能透过玻璃窗看到员工的办公桌, 微软研究院这楼里据说还有各种健身餐厅等设备, 而且微软还给员工免费停车, 真是个不错的公司.一到这种高大上的地方一下子也变得特别有能量了, 真希望自己和孩子有一天能在这里工作.&nbsp;</p>
