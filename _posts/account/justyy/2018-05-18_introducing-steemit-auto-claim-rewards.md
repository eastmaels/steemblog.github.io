## Motivation
- Not every steemians are programmers.
- Not every steemians can afford a 24/7 VPS.
- Not every steemians enjoy claiming rewards **manually**.

## SteemIt Auto Claim Rewards
A web-based tool that is built upon [steemjs](https://github.com/steemit/steem-js) library. Pure client based tool. You only need the ID and the private posting key to claim rewards.

You can open multiple pages, set interval, and let it run in the browser while your are surfing..

**Tool URL**:  [https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/claim-rewards/](https://helloacm.com/tools/steemit/claim-rewards/)
