2年前刚搬到 Cambourne 的时候去吃了一次 77号[泰国酒巴](https://justyy.com/archives/1392)，回味无穷，但由于各种原因，一直没有再去，最近实在想念，于是我们又开车半小时顺着记忆找回熟悉的路。

英国的秋天下午5点天色就有点暗了，我们着急赶路只肚子饿得咕咕叫。

![2017-10-14 18.37.39.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmU2xG4V3wqBtSzbB9hRbK2CMJSf9Ww5ht5KJTYfiUHa2x/2017-10-14%2018.37.39.jpg)

穿过几条无人烟的街道，就看到了 乡间小路旁的泰国餐馆。（媳妇 @happyukgo ）

![2017-10-14 18.04.53_副本.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmW3Yum7W5QxziNWsLwLzkjrsUR8n4WVjEXnMh4jQLQ4HD/2017-10-14%2018.04.53_%E5%89%AF%E6%9C%AC.jpg)

餐厅内部和两年前没啥变化。

![2017-10-14 16.56.49.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUUnMNdumMoubaxFDxnfZNCkjdbFFH5u34QUcuYD7a3Yd/2017-10-14%2016.56.49.jpg)

菜单则变化较大。Menu has changed quite a lot compared to 2 years ago.

![2017-10-14 16.57.15.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmPKPMxo86axYiQUhW9132TqpPdTDyn11aa6hKK4tfdzu7/2017-10-14%2016.57.15.jpg)

泰式炒饭 Thai Egg Fried Rice
![2017-10-14 18.37.10.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmexx8dYU5wvEdKYjS86b3QkaizuV6fAzbbagNZiSFU1ee/2017-10-14%2018.37.10.jpg)

泰式大虾，份量有点少，不过还是强烈推荐一下。Goong Pao (Grilled Shelled Tiger Prwans, Served with Chilli Sauce and Jasmine Rice)
![2017-10-14 18.37.16.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUQ3jjz2tNZgu1DPnNAmCS5VCWe6zR2cd6p6rf3MPzrAx/2017-10-14%2018.37.16.jpg)

泰式炒面, 脆脆的干辣椒和花生真是点睛之笔。 Pad Thai, Rice noodles with eggs, seasonal vegetables, crushed roasted chilli and peanuts.
![2017-10-14 18.37.22.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmZEHGTdXRAwqgSm8YcJ8N1MsE8cXdez8FpjefkbvRaWgZ/2017-10-14%2018.37.22.jpg)

每次吃泰国餐必点这个[神奇的汤](https://steemit.com/cn/@justyy/my-favorite-tai-tom-yam-soup)，吃得很上瘾，又酸又辣，欲罢不能。泰式冬阴汤（虾），Tom Yum Goong Soup with King Prawn
![2017-10-14 18.37.28.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUAyFnaNvayEXBcRjwobJkAZD343q5FwjU7NtgXpPzjiY/2017-10-14%2018.37.28.jpg)

再回首看[两年前的照片](https://justyy.com/archives/1392)，感叹时光飞逝。不过，在英国这么鸟不生蛋鸡不拉 sh*t 的地方，能吃个泰国餐就是一件很美好很享受的事情了。
