<p>I 'd like to share a few reasons that I think it is good to have two boys:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Ryan was conceived unexpectedly when Eric was 6 months old. &nbsp;It was hard to have another boy when Eric was still little, but it is worth it. Boys like to play together, they share the same/similar interests on toys, snacks... They grow up together.</li>
  <li>Ryan can wear the Eric's clothes and shoes, so you save money!</li>
  <li>They can play same toys together, &nbsp;but you have to get them each a same toy otherwise they will fight.</li>
  <li>Many nursery schools or summer camps offers sibling discounts!</li>
  <li>Eric likes to talk and teach Ryan. When you teach somebody, you also learn faster.</li>
</ol>
<p>The following photos are original/untouched (I really need to learn how to PS though.) The camera is Canon T3i and the lens is Canon &nbsp;50mm F1.8. I used the AV mode which blurs the background easily.</p>
<p>&nbsp;越来越觉得<a href="https://justyy.com/archives/4357">两男孩子真心好</a>. 好处有:</p>
<ul>
  <li>两孩子年纪相仿: 我家老大6个月的时候 媳妇一不小心意外怀孕了, 这才有了老二. 最开始两人一起带很累, 但是并快乐着, 现在两娃可以兴趣一样 可以玩到一起, 一起成长.</li>
  <li>老大的衣服鞋子可以给老二穿哈, 不浪费.</li>
  <li>玩具可以一起玩, 但是.. 我得意思是一样的玩具得一人一份. 要不然吵起来了</li>
  <li>幼儿园(或夏令营)一般会给兄弟姐妹打折</li>
  <li>老大在家里会教老二, 一起学习 一起进步. 你在教别人的同时 也是自己再学习的过程</li>
</ul>
