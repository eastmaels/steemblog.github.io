<p>Everytime I go to Tai restaurant, I will &nbsp;have Tai Tom Yam Soup. It is sour, spicy and sweet. There are slightly a few different versions of this. My favorite is the one with coconut, king prawns and mushrooms.</p>
<p>I always wonder: &nbsp;did they put something like marijuana that makes you addicted to it?&nbsp;</p>
<p>Everytime I think about it, &nbsp;I feel hungry, the feeling for hunger is so real and yummy that my mouth is watering..</p>
