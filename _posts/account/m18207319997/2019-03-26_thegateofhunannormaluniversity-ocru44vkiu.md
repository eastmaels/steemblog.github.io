The three major universities in Hunan,Central South University, Hunan University, and Hunan Normal University all share a common feature. The  campuses is open and there is no wall to isolate the entire school from the surrounding environment. Because there is no way to isolate it, there is an avenue through the campus. The three universities are built according to Yuelu Mountain. The Lushan Road passes through the campuses of the three universities. The library and gymnasium of the Hunan Normal University are another side of the road. The campus of Hunan University is divided into three blocks, the Central South University campus and the dormitory area is separate. So the campus gates of the three universities  are not like the gate, but like a memorial arch!

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13272" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/901553347440_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

Lushan Road passes through the Normal University and goes south. The university gate was rebuilt near the Lushan Road. It used to be a slope. And there was a row of small shops. Now all the stores are demolished and a grand gate is built. A large open space next to the planting of flowers and trees has become a small park. Above the small park is the School of Journalism and Communication at the Hunan Normal University.

The campus ofHunan Normal University continued to the Xiaoxiang Middle Road on the edge of the Xiangjiang River. A magnificent university gate was also built on the roadside, standing alone on an empty land.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13277" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/1211553347503_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1603" height="1079" /><br/> <img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13275" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/1181553347501_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

湖南的三大高校，中南大学、湖南大学、湖南师大都有一个共同的特点，校园是开放的，没有围墙将整个学校同周边环境隔离，因为没法隔离，有一条交通大道穿过校区。三所大学都是依着岳麓山而建，麓山路穿过三大高校的校园，师大的图书馆和体育馆是在马路的另一半，湖大的校区被分成了三块，中南大学校区和宿舍区是分开的。于是几个高校的校门就不像门了，倒像一个个牌坊！

麓山路穿过师大，向南，在临近麓山路的旁边改建了校门，以前只是一道斜坡，然后边上是一排的小店，现在将所有的店面拆迁，修建了一道气派的大门，校门的旁边一大块空地种植花草树木成了一个小公园，小公园的上方就是师大的新闻传播学院。

师大的校园延续到湘江边上的潇湘中路，在路旁同样修建了一道气势恢宏的校门，孤零零的矗立在一片空旷的大地上尤显高大。
