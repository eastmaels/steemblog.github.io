Zhao Wenzhuo's father was a martial artist, and his mother was a professional sprinter（短跑选手，duǎn pǎo xuǎn shǒu） who broke the record（打破纪录，dǎ pò jì lù） for being the fastest female sprinter in Harbin. Under the instruction of his father, Zhao attended martial arts lessons at the age of eight. In 1990, he was accepted by Beijing Sport University（北京体育大学） to study martial arts.
