今天，当我在研究新开发的地图是发现一个小错误。

Today, when I was researching the newly developed map, I found a small mistake.

![divn25.png](https://img.esteem.app/divn25.png)

在首尔的东北方向的Wonsan，有一个采集地图叫Waterfront。那里获得的资源是：Pisces，我认为这是一个拼写错误，应该是Pieces。
1.看资源的外观同首尔-Bukhansan获得的是一致的。
2.在右边的注解也是：piece。

特反馈@ror.pay，希望修正小错误！

In Wonsan, northeast of Seoul, there is a collection map called Waterfront. The resource be gotten there is: Pisces, I think this is a spelling mistake and it should be Pieces.
1. The appearance of the resources is in line with Seoul-Bukhansan'pieces.
2. The annotation on the right is also: piece.

Special feedback to @ror.pay, and I hope you will correct the small mistake!
