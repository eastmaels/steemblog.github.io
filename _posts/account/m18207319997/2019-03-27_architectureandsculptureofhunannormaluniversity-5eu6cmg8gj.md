Hunan Normal University is located at the foot of Yuelu Mountain. Undoubtedly, the air quality is first-class, and the coverage of green plants in the school is also high. Flowers  bloom everywhere . There is a beautiful campus in Hunan Normal University.

湖南师大坐落在岳麓山脚下，无疑空气质量是一流的，校内绿植覆盖率也高。到处盛开的花就可以知道，师大有一个美丽的校园。

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13330" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/981553347475_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/> <img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13331" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/991553347476_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/> <img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13332" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/1001553347477_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

Being a qualified college only flowers and trees is not enough. Much more College Students are studying in the renovated library. Although the gymnasium is old, the students who exercise are everywhere in the basketball and badminton court.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13336" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/841553347434_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/> <img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13335" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/03/1171553347500_.pic_.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

In fact, the most fresh experience is the canteen of the Hunan Normal University. The first is a beautiful environment. The dining room seats on the 3rd floor are clean and comfortable, and the soothing light music continues to play. Secondly, there are a wide variety of dishes, at the same time the rice  and fresh soup are free; the last is the novel pricing method. All foods, whether they are vegetarian or fruit, are all priced by weight. There are no more than 8 varieties in a plate, only 23 yuan.

事实上最有新鲜感的体验还是师大的食堂。首先是优美的环境，整个3楼食堂座椅干净舒适，还有舒缓的轻音乐持续播放；其次是品种丰富，荤素各类菜肴琳琅满目，还有糕点、水果，而且米饭、稀粥和鲜 汤还是免费的；最后是新颖的计价方式，所有的食物无论荤素还是水果，一律按重量计价，满满一盘不下8个品种，一共才23元。
