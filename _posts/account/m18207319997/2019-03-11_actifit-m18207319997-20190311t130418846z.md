Sammo Hung began learning Peking Opera at Yu Jim-Yuen's China Drama Academy when he was six years old. He is considered the most pivotal figure（关键人物，guān jiàn rén wù） in Hong Kong's action film（动作片，dòng zuò piàn）industry. He is the big brother（大哥，dà gē） of the "Seven Little Fortunes" performing group.
In 1961, Hung started his career as actor, martial artist, action director, film producer（制片人，zhì piān rén） and director（导演，dǎo yǎn）.
