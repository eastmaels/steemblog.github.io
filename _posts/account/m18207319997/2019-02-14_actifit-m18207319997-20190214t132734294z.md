Language tips: Chinese expressions about “Good luck”and “Bad luck”
锦鲤：koi fish
zhōng guó hé rì běn zài yǎng jǐn lǐ fāng miàn yǒu zhe yōu jiǔ de lì shǐ tā men xiāng xìn zhè me zuò kě yǐ dài lái hǎo yùn
中国和日本在养锦鲤方面有着悠久的历史，他们相信这么做可以带来好运。
In China and Japan, people have a long history of keeping koi, which they believe could bring good luck.
