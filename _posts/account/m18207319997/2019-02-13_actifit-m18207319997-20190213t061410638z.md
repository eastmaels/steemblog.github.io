Like common people, the ancient emperors would wear special clothes and arrange a special dinner for the big occasion.
On the Chinese New Year's Eve, the 30th day of the 12th month in the Lunar Calendar, the emperor would solemnly wear a bright yellow dragon robe（龙袍 lóng páo） stitched with gold and colored thread, and embroidered with dragon patterns and 12 ornaments. The emperor would also wear a fur coat, a crown on the head and court beads on chest.
