20191107日，ROR更新了地图。新地图在大地图的西北方向，增加了Gaeseong、Pyongyang、Wonsan、Hamhung四个城镇。每个城镇的怪物都是在5级以上。是目前等级较高玩家的升级圣地。将整个地图的距离进行了更新！

![g6a9a5.png](https://img.esteem.app/g6a9a5.png)

On 20191107, ROR updated the map. In the northwest direction of the big map, the new map has added four towns, Gaeseong, Pyongyang, Wonsan and Hamhung. The monsters in each town are above 5-level. It is the upgrade destination for players with higher level. 
The distance of the entire map has been updated!
