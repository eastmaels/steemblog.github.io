<p><center><img class="aligncenter" src="https://steemitimages.com/0x0/https://steemitimages.com/640x0/http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/04/nbc.jpg" /></center><br/></p>
<p class="part">新手币NBC 刚刚发行了两个月， @team-cn 总结了经验及观察NBC 的每日分红运作后，发现很多新手币持有者的户口已经半经营状态，甚至已有一段长时间没再参与Steem 社区。 @team-cn 希望让积极参与 Steem社区的 NBC 持币者获得更大的益处。避免每日分红奖励被长时间没参与 Steem 社区的帐号摊薄，故此有以下变更：</p>

<ol class="part">
 <li class="">NBC 每日分红只会发放给「持有新手币 NBC，并且于 Steem-Engine内锁仓 (Stake) 的持币者」；</li>
 <li class="">NBC 持币者若想取消锁仓 (Unstake)，取消时期为4星期，每星期解锁 1/4的锁仓NBC；</li>
 <li class="">NBC 持币者的每日发帖点赞比例，为手持 NBC 并加上 锁仓 (Stake) NBC 的总数。</li>
</ol>
<p class="part">此项更改，希望达至鼓励创作及推动博主积极参与 Steem 社区为目的。</p>
<p class="part">备注: 三位新手村创办人 @ericet @honoru @softmetal和@team-cn的帐号，仍旧不会获得 NBC 新手币分红。</p>
<p class="part">这个变更将于<strong>北京时间6月10号00:00生效</strong>。</p>
<p class="part">请持有NBC币的用户在变更生效前锁仓，以便及时获得每日的NBC分红。</p>


<hr />
<p class="part">It has been two months since the issue of the Newbies Coin (NBC). After a close observation of the function of the NBC daily dividends, @team-cn has noticed that many accounts that hold NBC are now in ‘semi-operation’ status. Some of them have not been engaged in the Steem community for a considerable long time. In order to avoid inactive NBC holders to share the daily dividend so that active NBC holders can receive more dividend, @team-cn makes the following announcement:</p>

<ol class="part">
 <li class="">Only accounts that hold NBC AND stake in Steem-Engine will receive NBC daily dividend.</li>
 <li class="">The unstake period is 4 weeks. Each week, 1/4 of the total staked amount is unstaked.</li>
 <li class="">The daily upvote ratio is calculated according to all the NBC amount (staked and unstaked) that the account has.</li>
</ol>
<p class="part">The aim of this change is to encourage more users to be engaged in the community.</p>
<p class="part">P.S. @team-cn and its founders, @ericet @honoru and @softmetal, will not receive NBC daily dividend.</p>
<p class="part">This change will be in effective on<strong> June 10, 12:00 a.m. Beijing time.</strong></p>
<p class="part">In order to continue receiving daily NBC divided after June 10, 2019, please stake immediately.</p>
