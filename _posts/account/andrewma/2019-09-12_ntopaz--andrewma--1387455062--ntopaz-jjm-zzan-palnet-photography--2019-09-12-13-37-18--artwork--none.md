![ntopaz-image-0](https://artisteem.io/media/aef085d9-0c21-43a8-b8ec-682c26276625)This is the picture I took in Penglai，Yantai city，Shandong province in 2016.

Penglai is called a fairyland on earth. There is a beautiful myth in China called "Eight Immortals Crossing the Sea.This is where the legend happens.

You can see from the picture，Next to it are the mountains，Sailing boats passed in the distance，Canoeing speeded by in the vicinit，A bright white light spreads over the sea.There is a sharp contrast between movement and silence.

The magnificent scenery of Penglai deserves to be a fairyland on earth.


I used my cellphone HUAWEI NCE-AL00 for this photo.
