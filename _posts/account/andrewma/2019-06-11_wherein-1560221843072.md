难得安逸的端午节
Rare and comfortable Dragon Boat Festival

端午节放了3天假，对于生活在喧嚣闹市，奔波于繁忙工作和家庭琐事之间，受制于两点一线的固定生活节奏的打工一族来说，确是一个不小的福利了。
Dragon Boat Festival has 3 days holiday, to the life in hustle and bustle, rush about between busy work and family trifles, subject to the fixed life rhythm of two points a line of working clan, is a not small welfare indeed.

带着娃们来看爷爷奶奶，和家人在附近的一个小公园里散散步，蓝天白云，绿柳池塘，内心也难得的平静安逸，能享受这一刻的宁静和安详，感觉真是太棒了！
I took my childrens to see their grandparents, and took a walk with my family in a small park nearby. The blue sky and white clouds, the green willow and pond, and We felt very calm and comfortable in our heart.
