Apricot flower never make public, is always a fresh and refined feeling.

Apricot flower sends out light faint scent, attracting small bees to fly towards it，as you can see from pictures １ and ２．

The branches of the apricot flowers are also very distinctive, with vicissitudes but not folds, which have both a sense of age and a sense of being straight and upward, as you can see from pictures 3 and 4．
