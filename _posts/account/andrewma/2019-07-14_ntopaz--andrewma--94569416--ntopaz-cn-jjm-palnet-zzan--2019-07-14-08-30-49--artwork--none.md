![ntopaz-image-0](https://artisteem.io/media/fa74c709-862b-43e7-928a-caa4d2594764)Every time I see children's toys, I think of my own childhood. Childhood is always so carefree, full of laughter and joy. Everyone wants childhood to be still, however Time is always in a hurry，Then let us keep a childlike innocence，Let the moment stop，Enjoy this moment quietly.


每当看到孩子们的玩具，就会想到自己的童年，童年总是那么无忧无虑，充满着欢声笑语。每个人都希望童年能够静止下来，然而，时光总是太匆匆。
那就让我们保留一份童心，让时光在此刻停下来，静静地享受这一刻。
