![ntopaz-image-0](https://artisteem.io/media/8e55252b-c8ed-4e54-923d-1fa81a907b46)How can there be no ginkgo in autumn?

It seems that ginkgo has turned yellow overnight，The yellow ginkgo leaves shine brightly in the sun, with a wisp of sunlight and gentle autumn wind between the leaves, both warm and comfortable.

秋天怎能没有银杏？

似乎一夜之间，银杏叶就已经黄了一大片。

银杏在阳光的照耀之下更加灿烂夺目，伴随着从树叶缝隙之间透过来的缕缕阳光和徐徐秋风，既温暖又舒适。
