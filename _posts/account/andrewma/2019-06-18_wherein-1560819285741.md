Harvest a green pepper   收获一枚青椒

Green pepper has been growing for a month and a half, looking at the fresh green, can not help but make people salivate, there is a desire to swallow the stomach
Just joking, green pepper has grown to about 5,6 centimeters, decided to harvest it today, this will not only reduce the pressure on the small pepper plant, but also help the fruit after better growth.
