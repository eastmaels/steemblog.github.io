![ntopaz-image-0](https://artisteem.io/media/a7d530dd-6255-4d0c-9390-36651a5ed0ed)![ntopaz-image-1](https://artisteem.io/media/d37a63a5-a42b-46fd-b282-414ac1df2ffc)Autumn is the most beautiful season of the year，Not only the climate is pleasant, but also the scenery is beautiful.

I took this photo at the old Summer Palace in Beijing.

Green and yellow are one integrated whole, green is so bright, yellow is so brilliant, in the background of the bridge and the lake is like a beautiful picture, especially with the reflection in the water brings out the best, people will have a kind of illusion, as go into the picture, intoxicated in reality and fantasy.

秋天是一年四季中最美的季节
不仅气候宜人，而且景色秀美
我是在北京的圆明园旧址拍下的这张照片
绿色和黄色都浑然一体，绿得那么透亮，黄得那么灿烂，在小桥和湖面的衬托之下就像一幅美丽的画卷，特别和水中的倒影相得益彰，会让人们有种错觉，就如同走进了画卷，陶醉于现实和幻想之中。


I used a Canon 700D for this photo.
