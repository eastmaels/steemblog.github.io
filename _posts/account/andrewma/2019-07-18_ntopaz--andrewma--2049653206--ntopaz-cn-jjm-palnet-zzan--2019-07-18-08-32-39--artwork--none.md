![ntopaz-image-0](https://artisteem.io/media/72a50dde-b46f-43e7-b6a0-f5b77dc5e159)Minions are known around the world for their cute, naive and lively shapes.

What happens to minions made of tin?

Still so lovely, and at the same time，Isn't it textured?

The same shape, different material, will give a person a different feeling.

Minions become little iron men~

Anyway, it's still the world-famous cute

小黄人以憨态可掬，活波可爱的造型闻名全世界

如果是用铁皮做的小黄人会怎么样呢？

还是那么的可爱，同时，是不是很有质感？

同样的造型，不同的材质，会给人一种不一样的感觉

小黄人变成了小铁人~

总之，还是那个世界闻名的小可爱
