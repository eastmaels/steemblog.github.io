![ntopaz-image-0](https://artisteem.io/media/279554df-5a74-433b-a026-18dd1a7f385f)In the winter of 2012, I took this photo in Victoria harbor of Hong Kong.

The night view of Victoria harbour is beautiful, showing not only the modern prosperity, but also the magnificent architecture.

Although it is night, but the sky emerged a piece of white fog, especially in the dark night, there is a feeling of deep wonderland.

I used my cellphone HUAWEI NCE-AL00 for this photo.
