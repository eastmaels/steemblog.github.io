Rice Vermicilli in Sichuan Spicy Soup at Tamjai Samgor Mixian. This is a local Chinese cuisine, especially for people who enjoy hot and spicy taste. Choose your own degree of spicy and indulge in the burning sense inside your mouth.


 ![image](https://img.esteem.ws/ae09pyyglp.jpg)

三哥雲南米線是香港著名的雲南米線連鎖店，它最近剛被日本的丸龜製麵所收購。馳名的四川麻辣湯底有不同辣度供顧客選擇，來感受一下令你舌頭著火的味度吧

[Tamjai Samgor Mixian]
Address: Site 13-13A, Site 9, Whampoa Garden, 8 Tak Fung Street, Hung Hom
