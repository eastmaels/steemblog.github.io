## Hunter's comment
Today I hunted a mobile game for you. It’s a free game that you can play everywhere, even without internet connection. If you have played  snooker before, you will probably know how to play the game. Just strike the target balls into the pockets, but this time the pool is infinitely big!

* Simple rules, a lot of fun.
* You have five lives(cues) in total. If you score by striking the target balls into the pockets, you can immediately get one extra life. 
* If the white ball falls into any pocket, game over. 
* The display language automatically changes according to your country. (That’s why you can see those Chinese words in the screenshots) It’s currently available in French, Chinese, English, German, Japanese and Spanish
* You can also buy equipment (e.g. a hat) from shop to enhance your ability.

* You can unlock special abilities of the hats to ace the game, and upgrade your shop to make it look cooler!
Does it look fun and interesting? Let’s download the game and try it out.
