大家好，收集銀幣是我的一個小興趣，才二十多歲就有這種興趣你大概會說我像個老頭子吧。 lol  中文區好像較少貴金屬投資人，所以就在這與大家分享我的珍藏，開了個 #cn-silver標簽，也希望能為大家帶來更多元化的帖文 。但這次介紹的是銀條，不是銀幣啦。實貨的銀條買來投資保值很不錯的，但投資年期一般都要數年，當有金融危機爆發時白銀價格也會暴增。這篇文章也會介紹一些形象特別的銀條哦。


Hello Steemians today I am going to share the part 6 of my silver coins collection. However, today I am going to share some beautiful silver bars, instead of silver coins. I believe that silver bars will rise significantly in value when years pass, especially during economic crisis and that's a wise investment decision.

10盎司4枚Aces銀塊套裝



![IMG_5057.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmeTMp5zTzB9W31enuYSSXUtt6uUPNgmgCVKW13oZToVEa/IMG_5057.JPG)

10Oz 4 Ace Silver Bars Set


這是KITCO買的銀塊，有4枚，我很喜歡它啤牌式的設計，很帥，還附送一個收藏用的木盒

This silver bars set is produced by KITCO, and I like its design of poker cards very much. A decent wooden collection box is also included.

這是背面的設計。

![IMG_5058.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcfgSm1UCUPeBD9kqgzBhYgmG18u4c85TK3CnyCrUTWfx/IMG_5058.JPG)

This is the background design.

第二個要介紹的是經典的加拿大皇家鑄幣廠的10安士楓葉銀塊，大概是貴金屬投資者最愛買的吧。

![IMG_5061.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmb2kCrixSNH1jCYE7aXAFi9D8ThqLgTQVoqeq78GqUMUg/IMG_5061.JPG)

The second one to introduce is the famous Royal Canadian Mint 10 Oz silver bar, it's probably the most popular one among the metals investors. So beautiful, isn't it?

第三個要介紹的是斯科茨代爾鑄幣廠的銀塊。它最著名的就是奇形怪狀的銀塊。每個都很有特色。這塊1公斤Tombstone nugget採用了銀礦的設計，每一塊都有細微的不同。

![IMG_5064.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmTrRzTzDtSxbeLNy7tQ68d8uUr3TQQwXv6uE6rz3x8ZV5/IMG_5064.JPG)

The third one to introduce is from the Scottsdale Mint. It is a prestigious mint producing the most weird forms of silver coins and bars. This one is the 1kg Tombstone Silver nugget, and it has a great design.

還有一個精美的袋子送呢。

![IMG_5065.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmQkzXDtH2T85opLbHhZjd6ZitgXXYHzvWmSqstr5ubupB/IMG_5065.JPG)

It comes with a beautiful bag.


最後要介紹的是我們香港利昌金舖出產的銀塊，它是香港金銀業貿易場的主要成員。雖然看上去比較殘破，但它的價錢是最貼近銀價的。單位是用兩計算，很有中國特色。
![IMG_5063.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcM3k1XZbNU3ZEKELEY4HPXs6hjPiwoH9KgtAKgigYFPh/IMG_5063.JPG)


The last one to introduce is the silver bars from Hong Kong, the Lee Cheong silver bar. It's the cheapest among the silver bar I have introduced, and it comes in 4 tael and 500g form.
