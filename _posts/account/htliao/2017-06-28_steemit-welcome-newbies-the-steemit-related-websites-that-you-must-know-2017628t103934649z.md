作為Steemit的新手，我們很多時候都不知道怎樣把文章寫得更好、運用我們的voting power、我們的投票的價值和其他與我們帳戶相關的資訊。這裡就一次性地介紹幾個網站給大家，方便大家查閱。


![tips.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmdE6PSPZi473KpnwpeJqfiusMEZ6mKTYLBRuf3yEaW8QQ/tips.jpg)


As a newbie in Steemit, we often find that it is difficult for us to find some useful informations about our voting power, account and how much our vote worths. So I am introducing some very useful websites here that you can find a lot of useful information related to Steemit.

![divider-1024.png](https://steemitimages.com/DQmb9i2KiKpKHoj63jj482Z2HpRPSbx1UyAPXrRGYxXzhEj/divider-1024.png)



* # Steemtools
首先要介紹的是Steemtools，這是一個陳列了所有對Steemians有用的網站，包羅萬有，無論你是author，curator 還是developer，都可以輕易在這兒找到對你有用的網站。


The first one to introduce is Steemtools. You can find almost everything you need here, no matter you are an author, curator or a developer. There are some incredible websites that you must visit.

<hr>


作為一個作者，最需要的就係靈感和怎樣排板令文章看上去更美觀， 以下就有幾個網站適合作者去瀏覽，有豐富的資訊令你能把文章寫得更好。



 ![IMG_5184.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmXxJFJ2mVTJW3yJ7z1agXdDKBS2epkoEscpkJFkfFmV63/IMG_5184.JPG) 

As an author, we always wonder how can we make our articles more beautiful and easy to read, and where can we find the ideas. These websites offer great help to authors like you and me.

<hr>

當然你也可找到一些方便的工具來查一下自己在Steemit上的數據。

![IMG_5185.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmc6LUrRTj797XMwXryBrSziUXGmbrCV3gCwe5khqTWPgu/IMG_5185.JPG)

Of course you can also find some useful tools here to know more about your statistics.

<hr>

當然也有不少程式開發員有機會用到的工具，但我不是專家，就不一一陳述了，在介面上你很容易就能找到各種有用的工具。

Of course there are also many useful tools for developers too, but I am not an expert in this field. You can easily decide which kinds of resources you need and know where you can find them on this website.

![divider-1024.png](https://steemitimages.com/DQmb9i2KiKpKHoj63jj482Z2HpRPSbx1UyAPXrRGYxXzhEj/divider-1024.png)

* # Steemwhales 

這是一個設計得非常好的網站，你可以在這裡看見你帳戶的成長，看到帳戶的估算價值，奬勵和追隨者數目的圖表，十分適合用來反思一下自己在某段時間的表現。

This is a well designed website. You can see your account growth here in terms of estimated account value, followers and rewards.

<hr>

我的帳戶在過去一個月呈爆發性的增長呢，希望能夠繼續維持下去，哈哈。

![IMG_5178.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUU8M6jWJ1iQAhdYEVQedc5cXMef2DL3Ytn7vVDAwcQQb/IMG_5178.JPG)


As you can see, this is how my account grew in the past month, it's just like a rocket taking off. If I were a stock, you would be pleased to have invested in me. Thank you my followers. Hope that it can keep moving like that!


![divider-1024.png](https://steemitimages.com/DQmb9i2KiKpKHoj63jj482Z2HpRPSbx1UyAPXrRGYxXzhEj/divider-1024.png)

* # Steemnow
Steemnow是一個非常實用的網站, 你可以在這看到很多有用的資訊。

Steemnow is a very useful website. You can find a lot of useful information here.

<hr>

例如在這裡，你能看到你現在的100% upvote值多少錢。

![IMG_5180.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcJKsohBTtTzqteGqpZCLHi5nVmMP7bbyfahx3MKgKTEt/IMG_5180.JPG)

For example, you can see how much your 100% upvote worth here.

<hr>

在這你可以看見自己現在的voting power 

![IMG_5179.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmfWdycXcfCQwTmJ66YPohLQmD3Ev7rqayxoZyyUa9Hf4x/IMG_5179.JPG)

You can also see how much voting power you currently have 

<hr>

這裡可以看見別人最近給你投的票，投了多少%。

![IMG_5182.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmd1iQCEL5Nh9aJwqFe1xiQcZyepCErqKKDhuiX8Lv6pmR/IMG_5182.JPG)

Here you can see how many upvotes you received recently and their %.


<hr>
你也可以輕易得知自己過去一星期寫的文章大約有多少奬勵

![IMG_5183.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmc9C52AmPqN3L4bcJgGpQcC6G6mA7UQsGFFnBhu9MNjYJ/IMG_5183.JPG)

You can also easily learn how much rewards did you get in the past week.

![divider-1024.png](https://steemitimages.com/DQmb9i2KiKpKHoj63jj482Z2HpRPSbx1UyAPXrRGYxXzhEj/divider-1024.png)

* # Steemstats
Steemstats 是一個由 @jesta所研發的網站，介面簡潔，你可以在這直接看到最近投過的帖子和將會得到的curation rewards，還可以一次過看多個帳戶哦！

![IMG_5177.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmZyDfqtwBMsGcv4XSxoxNE2eEPp4BESv67EDYnChGJVtd/IMG_5177.JPG)


Steemstats is a website developed by @jesta. It  is neat and clear. You can directly see what are the posts you have voted and how much curation rewards are expected. You can view multiple accounts here!

<hr>

當然在這你也可以得知自己的voting power 啦，但大家不會關心我的voting power啦，更關心的應該是這個吧，有用esteem 手機應用的可以根據good-karma 現在的voting power來決定要不要用esteem回覆。（當然也可以知道A神現在的voting power啦哈哈）


![IMG_5174.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUbKe1b47aV1aKHK44mKHfiGFxeSZjSQzMe4BJ91j1Qex/IMG_5174.JPG)


You can also know how much is your current voting power here

<hr>
在Witnesses裏，你可以得知現在的見證人排名、你投了誰和他們的票數。在這也再一次呼籲大家投A神一票啦。

![IMG_5176.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmYcN35crdxoTctSVaQgc438abauNt1pMcePjbw3PETwT7/IMG_5176.JPG)


You can know about the votes of witnesses and their ranking on the witness page, also who you have voted.

![divider-1024.png](https://steemitimages.com/DQmb9i2KiKpKHoj63jj482Z2HpRPSbx1UyAPXrRGYxXzhEj/divider-1024.png)

* # Steemit.deadfollowers.info
最後一個比較簡單，在這個網站，你可以知道你實際上有多少追蹤者，有哪些是已「死」了，就是已不活躍，很久沒上線的意思啦。如果你也有追蹤他們，當然也可取消追蹤啦。

![IMG_5186.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmdC7XqRiicRRkujTASFFWQQrZr6Zu9oDeBBcbnUD4bgXC/IMG_5186.JPG)

You can know how many actual followers you have, who are already 'dead', which means they haven't been online for a long time.
