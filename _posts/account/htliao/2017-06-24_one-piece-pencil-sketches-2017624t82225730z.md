Cn區有喜歡畫畫的同好嗎？雖然小弟喜歡畫的都是漫畫，沒有經過系統性的學習，也只是照著漫畫書上的臨摹，沒多少藝術細胞、創作天份，卻自得其樂，也能使自己安靜下來。

# English Version

Does anyone love drawing and pencil sketches? I am just an amateur in drawing and sketches, but I used to love comics, so I tried to  use a pencil to imitate the drawings in comics and follow the lines. I am not an expert, just a comics lover who tries to imitate, but I have enjoyed the process.

The posts shows some drawings of the characters in One Piece, the famous Japanese anime.

Actually these are drawn years ago, so I am sorry that I cannot find the finished version. They were lost and I had spent hours to draw them T^T. Now after I entered the workplace, I don't have the opportunity to pick up this hobby again.
