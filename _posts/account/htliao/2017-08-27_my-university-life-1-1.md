Hello my Steeemit friends,

 This is a new post series about my university life, hope that you can know more about me by reading this. I graduated from the University of Hong Kong in 2016, it's almost one year from now and I would like to make use of this opportunity to recall and record my university life.

> 這是我剛想好的關於我的大學生活的新系列，希望你們喜歡和能認識到我更多吧~~，我可不想再被認為是什麼典型的理工科宅男。~~ 我從香港大學畢業也有一年了，也趁這個機會回憶一下大學的生活，順道紀錄一下。~~順帶一提關於諗書學習什麽的太悶了，我應該不會寫。~~


![IMG_7039.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmXPqtsQo2XrttJAnxssSssSF2eE2DNMPQ3vHbCf6z2wcA/IMG_7039.JPG)

![IMG_7038.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmXkJifXe9jsM8cwUgufMEAywcZeV48pse1b9YTa2UCCRQ/IMG_7038.JPG)


I joined the orientation camp organised by the Student Union, what I played was similar to what I wrote in [this post(available in Chinese only)](https://steemit.com/cn/@htliao/48htih). That was totally new and fun to me and I also knew my best friend in this camp!



> 剛進大學的時候，我參加了學生會舉辦的迎新營，玩的東西都和我不久前寫的[這個帖子](https://steemit.com/cn/@htliao/48htih)裡的差不多。幸運的是在這裡我認識到我最好的朋友。


![IMG_7048.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmRFxqMMi1dps42mhLrrKVMWJqwQqNUvTwBY5PhMPjNVMF/IMG_7048.JPG)

Since I studied Engineering, I joined the orientation camp of the Engineering Society too.

> 也參加了工程學會的迎新營。

![IMG_7043.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmZ23Pvrv4AayNLtkLigShfeSBMPuMtJdcfuNsKy6XdgQx/IMG_7043.JPG)


![IMG_7042.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcbevQyGcn3fMgp2AETC9nuxrquPBNX2bxudhfUr9Lfqe/IMG_7042.JPG)

There is a culture in HKU called high table dinner and the students have to wear suits and the green gowns provided to enjoy the dinner together. In my freshmen year, I joined an activity called Engin' Nite, trying to make more friends who were also studying Engineering.

> 香港大學還有個文化叫做高桌晚宴，就是一大堆人穿著西裝和港大獨有的綠袍一起享用晚宴，那年我也參加了一個叫作Engin' Nite的活動，認識一下其他工程系的新生。~~東西不太好吃，也頗貴，之後幾年也沒參加了。~~

![IMG_7041.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmSJwLw4i7H9eR6ge1xP1HhSoCsacQcEpQMNtNq4pfkt3u/IMG_7041.JPG)

Taking photo with the board.
> 和那塊簽名版拍照。




Similar with most freshmen, I wanted to experience the hall culture of the university. I applied for the hall but unfortunately I didn't even get the chance of interview. Therefore, I chose to join the activities organised by the clubs.

> 與很多新生一樣，剛進大學時我也想體驗一下港大獨有的的舍堂文化和宿舍生活，認識多點朋友，不幸的是我連面試的機會也沒有，所以在第一年我只好參與一下各個學會舉辦的活動了。


![IMG_7046.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmYU9A8aJwjPEfmV6xU7E2xr7H1HUJLMFbvMP4RrbbkqYC/IMG_7046.JPG)

![IMG_7047.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmTJsmJMp2KB6pmcaQk6ewJdsH6gmiiimiY7LRSL9ocPmb/IMG_7047.JPG)

For example, I joined the volunteer work activities organised by the  Social Service Group and made some artwork with the elderly.

> 我參加了社會服務團舉辦的義工活動，和老人家一起製作紙巾花。


![IMG_7053.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmQcJnJusjEgzX4YDfZyLcEpr1vPB7D5Y8ncTEwFwmUWbn/IMG_7053.JPG)

I also joined the image team of drama society, helping the actors' to put on makeup.

> 也參加了劇社的形象設計組，學習幫演員化妝，還試過幫演員化喪屍妝，挺有趣的。


![IMG_7050.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmXhLDp8Yb5x9RHQ5BRpbVdRxcTeDHKz1toY4cAp9Ga1DY/IMG_7050.JPG)

Go cycling with my university friends.
> 和剛認識不久的大學朋友去騎單車。

Went to Inter-school Athletics Championship to support my younger friends in my secondary school.

> 上了大學也不能忘記中學的同學呀，我們一起到了學界田徑比賽支持一下中學的師弟，學界田徑比賽總是很熱血很有氣氛呢。


![IMG_7055.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcuVwikTRFYo4EdHyqmE4s6nHTMrt55byh1PexU3nP6JQ/IMG_7055.JPG)

I also went to the gathering of my primary school classmates. We went for art jamming this time. That was a fun experience.

> 也去了小學同學的聚會，這次去了Art jamming，你能看出我畫了什麽嗎？留言最快答到的能得到我的2% upvote。

After lectures, my friends and I sometimes go to the Art Club's studio to play card games. That was fun!
> 課堂過後，我和我的朋友有時會到藝術學會的工作室玩這款叫作三國殺的卡牌遊戲，很懷念那時候的時光呢。

Time flies and one year passed quickly, next time I will share more about my university life with you, stay tuned!
 
> 時光飛逝，不知不覺很快一年就過去了，還在諗書的同學們要珍惜一下在學校~~盡情玩樂~~學習的時光呀。下次再跟大家分享我Year 2旳大學生活吧。
