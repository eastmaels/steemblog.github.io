It’s the first time I join the “Getting Fit for Steem Fest with Actifit and Blocktrades”contest. I am really excited because I want to join Steem Fest as it’s the first time it’s held in Asia! 

Today I was unusually sporty! I played basketball and badminton on the same day! In the morning, I played basketball with my high school friends, and we had a really nice match! Though we lost in the match.




![](https://usermedia.actifit.io/77508612-720F-457C-8ACD-6F33FB279090637005268548892800)

In the evening, I went to my friend’s clubhouse to play badminton. There were nine of us and we had to take turns to play. It has been a long time since I played badminton last time, but it turned out that I played quite well. We played for two hours and I was exhausted when that’s over.
