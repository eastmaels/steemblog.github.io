The most signature food in Melaka would be the Nyonya cuisine, or Peranakan cuisine. 

> **Peranakan or Nyonya cuisine** comes from the [Peranakans](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peranakan), descendants of early Chinese migrants who settled in [Penang](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Penang), [Malacca](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malacca), [Singapore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singapore) and [Indonesia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indonesia) inter-marrying with local Malays and combines [Chinese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_cuisine), [Malay](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malay_people) and other influences.

We were going to visit this restaurant claiming to serve authentic Nyonya food in town, the **Nancy's Kitchen**.

娘惹餐是马六甲的招牌菜色。娘惹文化可以追溯到早期近1800，中国华人南下移民定居于槟城，马六甲，新加坡和印尼，和当地的马来土著的结合而衍生出来的文化。所以娘惹等于是华人和马来人的结合体。今日来到马六甲城中有名的娘惹餐馆，**Nancy's Kitchen**。

典型的娘惹式灯笼，很明显的和传统的华人灯笼不一样。听说由于纯手工制作，程序繁重，价格也不菲。

## The Food

总的来说，这间店的性价比还是不错的，七个人消费 RM100 。味道也和从小吃到大的没太大出入，不熟悉娘惹餐的我表示原来平常吃的家常菜就是娘惹风味了啊。
