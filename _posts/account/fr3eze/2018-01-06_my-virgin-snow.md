I arrived in China during this winter season with a little hope to unlock an achievement, experience my first real, natural snow at 27 years old.

Starting from the Northern side like Qingdao and Weifang, we never expect is actually happen in the warm Southern region, Jingzhou. Although we had to go through a rather [unpleasant 10 hour long ride on a train](https://steemit.com/travel/@fr3eze/ten-hours-on-a-train), it was totally worthwhile the moment we stepped on the white snow.

我这个二十七岁南方小子这次初次来到中国旅游，而且是在冬季，这就让我抱着了解锁初雪的小心愿。

万万没想到我们从北方的青岛和潍坊一路玩下来，居然在较温暖的南方地区，荆州终于看见了雪。虽然这中间还要[经历意想不到的高铁停顿的十小时之旅](https://steemit.com/travel/@fr3eze/ten-hours-on-a-train), 踏上雪地的一刻，所有幽怨都一扫而空了。

![IMG_20180105_132642~2.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmZtpFP247ZyWU24s7aQE4UHusMK625AEP6dHw2pzxTvi3/IMG_20180105_132642~2.jpg)

Everything is covered in white color. Absolutely lovely to be able to see it via my naked eye! 

However I still have a small regret that we did not make it in time to witness the falling snow. Now they have all rested on the roofs, steps, leaves, grasses and begins to somehow melts. This leads to the completion of whole photography session using my frozen fingers. Never been this cold!

一切事物都被白色所覆盖，能够用肉眼见证这一切景象真的是很 amazing!

唯一的小遗憾就是没能赶得上看到降雪的过程。现在它们都已静静地躺在檐上，阶上，树上，草上。也已经开始慢慢融化，这也造成整个摄影过程都是用几乎冻结的手指进行的。从未试过这么冷爽！
