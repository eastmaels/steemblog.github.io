Grew more comfortable with digital drawing and started to draw with the "digital" in mind. When you started to apply some techniques that is only applicable in digital art, you are getting away from the old 'paper-and-pen' kind of traditional art thinking.

Tried to use the mirror effect for the first time for the reflection of upper half picture. Toned the reflection with heavy shades and some ripple effects, Tada! A nice reflection is done within 5 min of work which is simple unachievable with physical tools.

Draw with Ipad Pro and Pencil though the Procreate
