Less than 17 hours to go before the <a href="http://contest.gonetcoins.com/">Free coin listing on Netcoins Contest</a> ended, and we already see a huge competition comes up from the Verge community to dethrone Steem from its short life No. 1 rank.

This is very much expected as everyone would try their best to sprint for the last mile before the finish line. And here I'm gonna share an unlimited vote method which approximately able to cast 1 vote per minute.

<h2>You need browser incognito mode and a disposable email service</h2>

Here is how, first open the <a href="http://contest.gonetcoins.com/">contest page</a> in incognito mode from the browser which usually tied to shortcut <code>CTRL + Shift + N</code> if you are using Chrome.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmXkaewsYrKg6trVDV2dJA4SQo9nspar7twqDzQaXgFKpV/chrome_2018-10-20_21-27-35.png" alt="chrome_2018-10-20_21-27-35.png" /><br/>

Secondly, open the <a href="https://temp-mail.org/en/">Tempmail</a> or you can use other services that do the same thing.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmUq3xF5nvXUSezooqgSgRabn31msr5cD6BD7UrjfgyD7P/chrome_2018-10-20_21-50-28.png" alt="chrome_2018-10-20_21-50-28.png" /><br/>

Here is the trick, every time after casting a vote:
- <strong>Close the contest page</strong> which is in the incognito browser. 
- <strong>Open it up again</strong> and the previous cookies would be cleared.
- <strong>Change the Tempmail to a new email address</strong> by clicking on the <code>Change</code> button on the left panel.
- Rinse and repeat.
