<a href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/user/13265936/">Tabliss</a> in Firefox gives the new page a new life by swapping a new wallpaper in every time you open a new page. There is no way to configure anything on the page, strictly just a clock with a greeting base on the time it detected. Sometimes I would just leave the new page on while my PC is left idling, it serve the wallpaper purpose really well and gives me a better mood each time I open a new tab. <br /><center><hr/><em>Posted from my blog with <a href='https://wordpress.org/plugins/steempress/'>SteemPress</a> : https://fr3eze.vornix.blog/when-you-are-sick-of-cluttered-and-messy-new-tab-page/ </em><hr/></center>
