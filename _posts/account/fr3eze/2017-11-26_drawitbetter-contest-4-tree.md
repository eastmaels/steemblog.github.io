I've spent 3 hours on this artwork using charcoal and ink pen on the black book.

This is an entry for [tree drawing challenge](https://steemit.com/drawitbetter/@sirsensei/4-drawitbetter-contest-winners-and-new-theme) hosted by @sirsensei.

这幅作品花了三个小时来完成，用的主要是白色铅笔和白色墨笔于黑色绘画本之上。这同时也是由 @sirsensei 所举办的[绘画比赛](https://steemit.com/drawitbetter/@sirsensei/4-drawitbetter-contest-winners-and-new-theme)的参赛作品。
