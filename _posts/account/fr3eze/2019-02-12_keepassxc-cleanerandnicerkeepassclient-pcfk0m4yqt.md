Back in the days when MacOS is my only device to perform my daily tasks and there was a need to access my crypto keys that stored in Keepass, I had a hard time finding original Keepass client to be available on MacOS. Here is where <a href="https://keepassxc.org/">KeePassXC</a> shines. Not only it has a rather nicer UI, more importantly it is cross platform for the major system like Linux, MacOS and Windows.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmcMCeeEFm7u8beuytnCwaPDi2NLULBgrfBKjDF9e5krJ4/firefox_2019-02-13_00-17-56.png" alt="firefox_2019-02-13_00-17-56.png" /><br/>

KeePassXC is also open source and that's the everything in the essence of making it the perfect crypto vault personally.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmW46Pr4k6usjs5ErZ3AY9BmAzQ9APCp96pv22oYjEGSpR/firefox_2019-02-13_00-25-01.png" alt="firefox_2019-02-13_00-25-01.png" /><br/>

It looks similar to the original Keepass client but just cleaner and nicer, I will switch to this client on my Windows PC as well.
