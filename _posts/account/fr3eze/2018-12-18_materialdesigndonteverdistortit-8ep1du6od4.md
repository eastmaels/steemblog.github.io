Google's <a href="https://material.io/">Material Design</a> is clearly not a big fan of distorted design style. I can tell it clearly makes sense by plenty of demonstrations the page presents. Be it a logo or any graphical illustration, they exist to grab the attention of content consumers and the last thing they have to do is to distort themselves up to grab more eyeballs. It would be like attention whores who are demanding while destroying its own natural beauty.

<strong>Keep the design straight and simple</strong> is a timeless lesson all designers should learn.
