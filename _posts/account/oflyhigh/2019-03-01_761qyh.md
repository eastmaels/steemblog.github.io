还记得我发过的七星公园灯会吗？如果没印象，我来几张图帮你回顾一下。

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmNziyLEKygfUaADSxvuERNkdRyy6NxQREu8qSPfYVRBdj/image.png)

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmXr5gheKdtDqTY1suwUrqR9oEZixmrZoCCyLbC3vDKd5z/image.png)

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmfDc82D3RhuM9ugtA7gVn78oMswqC1uKVujFdx4FuDK5h/image.png)

是不是非常漂亮啊？可是这两天我再路过这里时，发现灯展的各种造型灯都已经撤光了，只剩下零星的灯笼了。

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmSAaUSuiLQuuQwEqTM5uqCzwm4WPFVxVq5eZVThH8nTq3/image.png)

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmZPGGoMfJC1F2kGTuAgHUeAS5LxzhJwHQ8UGawwD4diTW/image.png)

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQGi6AXJKMn4SnQQSU3RyPuZRQ7ecXmW5Ba2sHSAxjgib/image.png)

不过也没关系，用不了多久（大概半年后？）这里就会变成一片花的海洋哦。

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaVjpoUFRiLGbGWvKgNPdHoaow6EcSe28KJqoALFsWstq/image.png)

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQCjgm6wxp7cLA9K7re5v63UCP6nnsJyLVRntG7RHrNfC/image.png)

![](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmc5qeZMXyu4zjYPWqFmSxwsMyNiZpZnVwgjGLeMcKNHRa/image.png)

其实相比灯海，我更喜欢花海啊，毕竟花海不冷，看灯的时候可把我冻坏了。
