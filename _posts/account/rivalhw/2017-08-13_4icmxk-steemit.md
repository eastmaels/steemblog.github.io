<html>
<p>steemit文学写作群建立不到两天，已经超过八十人加入，针对一些朋友提出的一些问题，在这里做下统一答复。</p>
<p>The Steemit Chinese Literature Group has been formed less than 2 days and we already have over 80 members! Here are the answers to some frequently asked questions:</p>
<p><br>
　　<strong>1、哪些人适合加入Steemit文学写作爱好群？</strong></p>
<p>　　<strong>Who should join the Steemit Chinese Literature Group?</strong></p>
<p>　　如果你是文学爱好者，无论是作者，或者爱好者，或者是围观吃瓜群众，我们均欢迎你的加入。<br>
　　Anyone who enjoys Chinese literature, whether you are a writer or a reader, or part of a general audience, we welcome all!</p>
<p>　　<strong>2、cn-reader标签如何使用？<br>
</strong>　　<strong>Should I use “cn-reader” as the main tag?</strong></p>
<p>　　为了方便群内朋友互相点赞，文学群号召大家在发帖时使用统一的cn-reader标签，cn-reader大家可以看出，是属于cn区下的，主标签仍然是cn(坚持一个CN区一万年不动摇 :) &nbsp;)，次标签可以使用cn-reader,其他标签大家可以根据自己文章内容来定。</p>
<p>　　For the convenience of upvoting each other, we encourage you to use the “cn-reader” tag in your Chinese literature related posts on Steemit. As you can tell, it’s a sub-tag under the “cn” community, while the main tag of a Chinese post should still be “cn” (we shall maintain the “cn” tag forever :) ) and use “cn-reader” as your 2nd tag. The rest of the tags shall be set according to your post content.&nbsp;</p>
<p><br>
　　<strong>3、使用cn-reader可以发表哪些文学类文章呢？</strong></p>
<p>　　<strong>What are the topics that “cn-reader” covers?</strong></p>
<p>　　cn-reader标签主要是面向中文社区的文学类文章，内容题材目前不限，包括文学类、小说、诗歌、散文等，也可以是生活体验、写作技巧等，所谓文学高于生活，但毕竟还是来源于生活嘛。</p>
<p>　　”cn-reader” tag is used for Chinese posts on Steemit, at the moment there is no restriction on topics. It could be anything about literature, novel, poem, essay, or life experiences, writing techniques, etc. “Literature is larger than life, but it’s also inspired by life.”<br>
<br>
　　<strong>4、文学写作群里应注意哪些事项呢？<br>
</strong>　　<strong>Anything to take note in the group chat?</strong></p>
<p>　　这点没有严格的限制，原则上我们鼓励主要以文学相关话题讨论为主，但要注意避免一些低俗类话题或者严重偏离文学相关的话题。（该条建议由@satanas提出）如果真想讨论其它相关，我们建议您可以私下交谈。</p>
<p>　　成员之间相互提问时，如果有人解答了希望可以@一下提问者，有时候大家加入一个群会关闭消息提醒，@了提问者会让他更快查看信息。(<a href="https://steemit.com/@mangoanddaddy">@mangoanddaddy</a> 提出该条建议)。</p>
<p>　　<strong>加入群内的成员，我们鼓励您发更多优秀的文章，对于高质量优秀的文章，我们定期会筛选，拿出一些奖励措施，进行奖励，鼓励大家为了CN社区的繁荣做出的贡献。</strong></p>
<p>　　There is no restriction at this point, our main goal is to encourage everyone to engage in literature discussion. Try to avoid lame or irrelevant topics. (Suggested by @satanas) If you wish to talk about other topics, please use private chat. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>　　When members raise a question, if anyone has answered it, please remember to tag @the asker. Sometimes we mute group message notifications, tagging @the asker will remind the person to read it. (Suggested by @mangoanddaddy)</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>　　<em><strong>We encourage the group members to publish more well-written posts. Outstanding pieces will be rewarded accordingly, this is to promote our cn community on Steemit.&nbsp;</strong></em></p>
<p><br>
　　<strong>5、关于群内成员互粉。</strong></p>
<p>　　<strong>How about following each other on Steemit?</strong></p>
<p>　　我们强烈建议，加入群内的文学成员之间相互follow，以达到互相增粉，共同受益的目的。对于文学相关的文章，多些鼓励，多些建议，多些评论。对于成员推荐自己的作品文章，不要觉得不好意思或耻于主动去宣传自己的作品，我们是鼓励的，在你通过自己的辛勤劳动创作的同时，我们也希望你的作品文章得到一定的鼓励和支持。</p>
<p>　　We strongly recommend our group members to follow each other on Steemit, to connect better as mutual benefits. For the cn-reader posts, do give your warmest encouragement, suggestion or even criticism. Members are welcome to promote your own posts in the group chat, don’t be shy! While you create diligently through your writing, we would love to give you some encouragement and support too!<br>
<br>
　　<strong>6、在这里你可以收获什么？</strong></p>
<p>　　<strong>What can you benefit from this?</strong></p>
<p>　　目前文学群刚成立，但已经有很多steemit上的朋友加入了，其中不乏一些大家耳闻目睹的老熟人，如我们茶馆的茶东们，<a href="https://steemit.com/@lemooljiang">@lemooljiang</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@ace108">@ace108</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@oflyhigh">@oflyhigh</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@deanliu">@deanliu</a>，CN区早期的元老级人物：<a href="https://steemit.com/@jademont">@jademont</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@wongshiying">@wongshiying</a>，也有steemit上知名网红作者<a href="https://steemit.com/@helene">@helene</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@sweetsssj">@sweetsssj</a>等，优秀作者<a href="https://steemit.com/@cnfund">@cnfund</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@jubi">@jubi</a>(jubi最近搞的谷歌点名很是成功，有兴趣的朋友赶快去围观)、<a href="https://steemit.com/@coldhair">@coldhair</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@mrpointp">@mrpointp</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@chaofanjun">@chaofanjun</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@tvb">@tvb</a>、<a href="https://steemit.com/@sylvia1997">@sylvia1997</a>等，不仅可以获得一定文学上的交流提升，也可以顺便交友认识一大波重量级人物。:)</p>
<p>　　Our literature group was just formed, and many Steemit friends have already joined. You are probably familiar with some of them, such as our Tea House hosts: @lemooljiang, @ace108, @oflyhigh, @deanliu. A few early CN community members, like @jademont, @wongshiying and trending Steemians like @helene and @sweetsssj. Other good writers such as @cnfund, @jubi (He successfully started a Google search contest recently, if you’re interested please check out his page.) @coldhair, @mrpointp, @chaofanjun, @tvb, @sylvia1997 and many more. Being part of this group really helps us to improve ourselves and make friends with those who are at the top of their game.<br>

<p>　　<strong>Our gourp’s main objectives.</strong></p>
<p>　　引用群内成员<a href="https://steemit.com/@coldhair">@coldhair</a> 的评论：</p>
