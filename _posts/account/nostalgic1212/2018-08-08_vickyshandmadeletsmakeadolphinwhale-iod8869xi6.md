Before I write this post, I carefully recalled when is the last time I did something handmade. I searched my articles through the latest to the very first one, and finally found this post <a href="https://cnsteem.org/monthlyauthorchallenge/@vickylin/smac-9">Make a Thing by Using Pindou</a> under the account @vickylin. Okay, let's start nowğŸ‰ This time, I'll still do some funny stuffs by using the amazing material Pindou.

On the Internet, Pindou comes in various colors. However, for people like me who just occasionally do some handwork, the 48 colors set is reasonable. Of course, I highly recommend you to buy more white and black beans because they are super fast to use.

<a href="https://imgchr.com/i/Psvgkd"><img src="https://s1.ax1x.com/2018/08/08/Psvgkd.png" alt="Psvgkd.png" /><br/></a>

According to the drawings found on the Internet, we can put the beans with corresponding color number on the plate. There's nothing technical hereğŸ˜ While the beans are small, so you need to use tweezers.

<a href="https://imgchr.com/i/Psvfpt"><img src="https://s1.ax1x.com/2018/08/08/Psvfpt.png" alt="Psvfpt.png" /><br/></a>

Later, we put a piece of grease paper (usually sold in the shop where you buy the beans) on the Pindou and start ironing with a small iron. It's really a technical operation... If the ironing isn't hot enough, the beans won't stick together and will come apart when the grease paper is removed. Nevertheless, if the heat is too high, it will probably burn out.

<a href="https://imgchr.com/i/Psvh1P"><img src="https://s1.ax1x.com/2018/08/08/Psvh1P.png" alt="Psvh1P.png" /><br/></a>

I suggest steemians who have children at home, might consider buying a set of Pindou. I've heard from my friends that since they purchase the little stuffs and give them to their children as gifts, the naughty children can play with them quietly for an hour or two. That's really a paradise for the parents. However,  there are two important things I wanna to mention:

<ul>
<li>The beans are very small and brightly colored. So, if the children are playing with beans, the parents must keep attention on them, in order to prevent children from eating the beans by accident.</p></li>
