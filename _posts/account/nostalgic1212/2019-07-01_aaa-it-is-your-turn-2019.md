Do you have someone that you hate him/her so much that you wanna them dead? Would you be happy or scared if the person actually died after you wrote down their names? This time, I'll recommend you guys a heated Japanese drama named ***It Is Your Turn***.

When we watch ordinary thriller, most of the suspects will be limited in 3-5 people. According to the speculation step by step, the audience will lock the suspect in the end. When it comes the J-Drama *It's your turn*, things gonna be quite different for it adapts the method of anonymous intersect killing. This hint  makes it difficult for both the police in the drama and the audience outside to find the real killer behind the scenes.

By the way, most of the 37 people on the show are relevant characters in the case. In the first few episodes, the biggest griping among the audience was that "I can't remember the characters' names, faces, room numbers and the relationships between each householder!"

**It's your turn** has released four series so far: **あなたの番です**、**あなたの番です 扉の向こう**、**あなたの番です 特別編** and **あなたの番です-反撃編**. If you are going to watch this J-Drama, I suggest that you should follow the order given above :D

你有那种恨到想要他去死的人吗？如果在你写下这个人的名字之后对方就真的死了，你是会觉得开心还是觉得害怕呢？冰冰今天想要介绍的这部日剧，讲的就是一个**「交换杀人」**的故事。

我们在看普通悬疑片的时候，犯罪嫌疑人大多会锁定在3-5人之中，再根据层层推理锁定最后的那一个人。《轮到你了》采用的匿名交换杀人的手法，使得无论是剧中的警方还是场外的观众，在寻中幕后真凶的过程中困难重重。

顺便一说，这部剧出场的37人中绝大多数都是重要人物。在最初的几集里面，观众感叹最多的就是“记不住人名、长相、住的房号以及彼此间的人物关系”。

《轮到你了》目前已经播放了四个系列，分别是《轮到你了》、《轮到你了番外篇：房门之内》、《轮到你了特别篇》以及《轮到你了反击篇》。大家如果准备观看这个系列的话，推荐按照文中所述的顺序唷:D
