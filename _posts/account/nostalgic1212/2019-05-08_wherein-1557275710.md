This is a movie about two girls' dreams and desires. 

The protagonists Rui and Nina are in a mutualistic relationship. Nina takes advantage of Rui's excellent acting skills to make herself become a foremost actress. Meanwhile, Rui makes use of Nina's beauty to fulfill her dream that being a spectacular actress under the spot-line just like her mother.

As a female cartoonist, Daruma Matsuura puts these two characters who are absolutely different in character and appearance on the stage at the same time. The two girls are guarded from each other at first and then become friends that would be able to share happiness as well as sorrow. 

However, love is the primer that ruin the balance. 

Rui immersed in her endless desire, and steps on the same way like her mom. Nevertheless, Nina feels so depress for her appearance, voice, career, even her family is took away from her by Rui. Finally, Nina jumps out of the building and becomes a vegetable.

At the end of the movie, the director only shows the back of Makoto Nina on the stage and never even reveals whose face it is. Perhaps the charm of Japanese films lies in that they reveal the ugliness of humanity to you while still retaining the space of endless imagination for the audience.

IMDb: -/10
Rotten Tomatoes: -/-

「中文部分」

这是一部两个女孩子有关梦想和欲望的电影，女主角渊累和妮娜是一种互利共生的关系。妮娜利用渊累出色的演技帮助自己成为一线女演员，而渊累则利用妮娜的美貌实现自己演戏的梦想。

作为女性漫画家的松浦达磨，将性格和外形如此迥异的两个角色同时置于舞台之上。两个女孩子从彼此试探到分享成功，再从理解对方到因为爱情而互相猜忌。最后，由于源自内心的欲望而让这段表面上看起来已经达到平衡的关系走向终结。

渊累沉浸于自己无尽的欲望，最终走上了和母亲一样的路。而被夺走容貌、声音甚至是人生的妮娜，在孤独绝望之中转身从高楼跳下。

电影的最后，只留下了舞台上「丹泽妮娜」的背影，导演甚至不曾放出到底是谁的脸。也许日本电影的魅力就在于它向你揭开人性的丑陋，又保留善意的让人可以无尽想象的空间。

豆瓣评分：7.6分
