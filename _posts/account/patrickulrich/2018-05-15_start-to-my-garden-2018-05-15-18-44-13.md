One of the things that I looked forward to most about buying a home was having land to be able to plant my own food. I'm so excited this year to finally get the opportunity to make my garden a reality. This morning I was able to till the land and have now planted seeds for everything from tomatoes to watermelons. Lots of tough work now will certainly pay off in a few months!
