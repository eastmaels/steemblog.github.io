As part of my trip I wanted to stop by a restaurant where I could pay using BTC directly. After checking my Airbitz app I came across Terry and Kim's Iron Rail Diner in Mt. Savage, MD. Unfortunately they weren't able to take my payment but they were so hospitable that they took me in and made me a fabulous meal regardless. I couldn't speak higher about this couple nor the quality of the food they make. Meeting them was a real pleasure!
