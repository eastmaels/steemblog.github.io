I'm driving down the interstate when a red truck begins to pass me and the driver begins to flag me down. After I look over and make sense of the situation I realized it was an old buddy from college. He had recognized my car as he was going home and I'm thankful he did. We we're able to stop at a little pub and catch up for a minute. This trip is already turning out exciting!
