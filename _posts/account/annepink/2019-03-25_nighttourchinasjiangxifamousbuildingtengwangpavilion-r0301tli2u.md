今天呢...我很开心地要与大家分享点我国的江南三大名楼之一的滕王阁...它也是江西的第一美楼喔...😊 它还是南方唯一一座皇家建筑呢 ...位于江西省南昌市沿江北路赣江东岸，与湖北黄鹤楼、湖南岳阳楼为并称为“江南三大名楼”，因在初唐才子王勃作的《滕王阁序》让其在三楼中最早天下扬名，故又被誉为“江南三大名楼”之首。滕王阁是国家重点旅游区...为首批国家5A级旅游景区。

Hi～ Dear friends... Okay.

Today... I am very happy to share with you the Tengwang Pavilion, one of the three famous buildings in Jiangnan... It is also the first beautiful building in Jiangxi... It is also the only royal building in the south.. It is located on the east bank of Minjiang River along the Yanjiang North Road in Nanchang City, Jiangxi Province. It is also known as the “Three Famous Buildings in Jiangnan” with the Yellow Crane Tower in Hubei and the Yueyang Tower in Hunan. The “Tengwang Pavilion Preface” by Wang Bozu in the early Tang Dynasty made it the earliest in the third floor. The world is famous, so it is also known as the "three famous buildings in the south of the Yangtze River". Tengwang Pavilion is a national key tourist area... it is the first batch of national 5A tourist attractions.

前些天晚上有幸与老友故地重游一番...晚上来参观滕王阁还是第一次呢

I had the privilege of revisiting the old friends’ places in the past few days... It’s the first time to visit Tengwang Pavilion in the evening.

沿着大门内的右边通往游轮码头边灯笼上的“滕王宴乐”，是滕王阁内夜晚的节目

Along the right side of the gate, the "Tengwang Feast" on the lantern on the side of the cruise ship terminal is the night show in Tengwang Pavilion.

楼阁共9层 ... 5层是观景最好的楼层，也是整个滕王阁的精华。中厅墙上铜板雕刻的《滕王阁序》是苏轼的手笔。6层是一个仿古戏台，大约每小时会有一场古装演出，也是游人游览的最高层。

The pavilion has 9 floors, the 5th floor is the best floor for viewing, and the essence of the entire Tengwang Pavilion. The "Tengwang Pavilion Preface" engraved on the copper plate in the middle hall is Su Shi's handwriting. The 6th floor is an antique stage, and there will be a costume show every hour, which is also the highest level of tourists.

当灯光乍起 ... 月色暗淡 ... 滕王阁屋脊瓦沿、飞阁檐牙都镶嵌上金黄的灯盏，像串联起金色铠甲的金线，金光闪耀，将整座滕王阁笼罩在一片金黄的光晕里，光彩夺目，威武神圣，不可侵犯！我试图透过婀娜的树枝拍摄，然而，它庞大的身躯依然威严，巍然傲立。那树枝树叶也一律黯然失色，反成了滕王阁雄性的反衬，让它更加辉煌！

When the lights rise... The moonlight is dim... Tengwangge’s ridges and ridges are all inlaid with golden lanterns, like gold wires in tandem with gold armor, golden light shining, and the whole Tengwang Pavilion is covered in a golden In the halo, dazzling, mighty, inviolable! I tried to shoot through the branches of Dina, however, its huge body is still majestic and proud. The branches and leaves are also eclipsed, and it has become the contrast of the male king of Tengwangge, making it more brilliant!

满厅金辉 ... 华丽堂皇 ... 璀璨灯光下的滕王阁在巨型光帘的映衬下格外雄伟，淡绿色的阁顶，暖橘色的阁体，清新典雅的感觉，让人眼前一亮，很有被“惊艳”的感觉呢！

Full hall Jinhui... Gorgeous and grand... The Tengwang Pavilion under the light is super majestic against the backdrop of giant light curtains, the pale green pavilion, the warm orange body, the fresh and elegant feeling, let people shine. Very "stunning" feeling!

然后我们走到了今说古演的内厅 ... 观看了仙女们精彩美艳的表演 ...

Then we went to the inner hall of the ancient performance... I watched the wonderful performances of the fairies.

流光溢彩的滕王阁 ... 仿佛享受了一场视觉盛宴 ... 心也随之快乐起来啦👏

滕王阁的夜景 ... 看到了别样的滕王阁，你说还能不美吗
