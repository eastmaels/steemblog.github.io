These photos are photos taken by me in the evening of March this year in Tengwang Pavilion, Nanchang, China.

> _它是中国江南三大名楼之一的滕王阁...它也是江西的第一美楼喔...😊 它还是南方唯一一座皇家建筑呢 ...位于江西省南昌市沿江北路赣江东岸，与湖北黄鹤楼、湖南岳阳楼为并称为“江南三大名楼”，因在初唐才子王勃作的《滕王阁序》让其在三楼中最早天下扬名，故又被誉为“江南三大名楼”之首。滕王阁是国家重点旅游区...为首批国家5A级旅游景区_

It is one of the three famous buildings in Jiangnan, China... It is also the first beautiful building in Jiangxi.It is also the only royal building in the south... It is located on the east bank of the Minjiang River along the Yanjiang North Road in Nanchang City, Jiangxi Province.  Hubei Yellow Crane Tower and Hunan Yueyang Tower are also known as “the three famous buildings in Jiangnan”. Because of the “Tengwang Pavilion Preface” in the early Tang Dynasty, Wang Bo’s “Tengwang Pavilion Preface” made it the earliest known in the third floor, so it was also known as “the three famous buildings in Jiangnan”.  "The first."  Tengwang Pavilion is a national key tourist area... it is the first batch of national 5A tourist attractions.
