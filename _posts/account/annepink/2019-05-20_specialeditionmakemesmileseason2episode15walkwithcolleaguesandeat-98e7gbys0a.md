那天和往常一样...9点半我们在公司开完早会后接着就是业务学习...基本每天都是常态了~

大概一至二小时差不多的时间工作学习...就到了饭点，也是照常不例外饭点时间依然还是师傅请客吃饭咯~😁

他带着我们走了大概20分钟就到了另一条街的一家土菜馆，哦我忘了拍菜馆店名啦...sorry😅 在这路上当时行人比较少...因为这条马路上不是很多行人步行的地方，主要车辆多...大马路饭店也比较少，这条路是属于我们南昌红谷滩新区的主干道，叫红谷大道...

听师傅说，公司附近就这家馆子店味道赞...价钱也不是很贵~师傅人呢很慷慨大方...而且是枚帅锅喔😎 🙊 图片里那位红色着装的就是师傅了...他人很潮流...尤其稀饭红色服饰，跟我着装有点同感哈~

我跟他呆一起共识学习工作几年了，人还是很不错的，基本吃香的喝辣的都照着咱们这些徒弟们呢~😁

话说回来哈... 这家菜馆2层楼...我们在二楼用餐，师傅点了我们都稀饭吃的下饭菜，6个菜都很好吃😋 有红烧黄丫头...红烧花甲还有小青笋和豆皮煮猪肚几等...

边吃边聊一小时左右用完餐就回公司去了...下次有机会拍点公司附近照片吧...

That day is the same as usual... At 9:30, we are in the business after the company’s morning meeting... Basically, it’s normal every day~

About one to two hours of work and study... I went to the meal, and as usual, I still have dinner time for the chef.

He took us for about 20 minutes and went to a kitchen in another street. Oh, I forgot to take the name of the restaurant...sorry😅 There were fewer pedestrians on this road...because there are not many pedestrians on this road. In the walking place, there are many major vehicles... There are also fewer hotels in Dalang Road. This road belongs to the main road of our new district in Honggutan, Nanchang, called Honggu Avenue...

Listening to the master said that the restaurant near the company tastes like this... The price is not very expensive~ The master is very generous... and it is the handsome man 😎 🙊 The red dress in the picture is the master. .. others are very trendy...especially porridge red dress, with me with a sense of the same ha ~

I stayed with him for a few years of study and work for a few years. People are still very good. Basically, they are very spicy and they are taking care of these apprentices~😁

Saying it back... This restaurant has 2 floors... We ate on the second floor, the master ordered the next meal we ate, and all the 6 dishes were delicious. There was braised yellow scallions... braised broccoli There are small green bamboo shoots and bean skin boiled pork belly, etc...

After eating and talking for an hour or so, I will go back to the company after using the meal... Next time I have the opportunity to take photos of the company nearby...
