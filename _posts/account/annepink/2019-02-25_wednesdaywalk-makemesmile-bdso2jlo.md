> 秋水广场
> 是位于江西省南昌市红谷滩新区的赣江之滨
> 紧邻行政中心广场
> 于2004年1月28日落成
> 是利用荒滩兴建
> 既防洪 ～又观景
> 与中国著名的滕王阁隔江相望
> 再现了千古名篇《滕王阁序》中的"落霞与孤鹜齐飞 ～秋水共长天一色"的意境
> 秋水广场正是由此而得名
> 它是一座以喷泉为主题
> 集旅游、购物、观光为一体的大型休闲广场

---

Qiushui Square
It is located on the bank of the Lijiang River in Honggutan New District, Nanchang City, Jiangxi Province.
Close to the administrative center square
Sunset on January 28, 2004
Is to use the wasteland to build
Both flood prevention and viewing
Across the river from the famous Tengwang Pavilion in China
Reappearing the artistic conception of "Luo Xia and Lonely Qi Fei ~ Qiu Shui Gong Chang Tian Yi" in the famous "Teng Wang Pavilion Preface"
Qiu Shui Square is named after this
It is a fountain theme
A large leisure plaza integrating tourism, shopping and sightseeing
