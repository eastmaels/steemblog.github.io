Xixia Guaishiling Eco-Park is located in the northern suburb of Nanchang City, only 25 kilometers away from the urban area of Nanchang, covering an area of about 4200 mu. Xixia Guaishiling is a national AAAA-level tourist attraction integrating sports, leisure, sightseeing, entertainment and health preservation. The scenic spot has the only natural rock climbing body in the country - the grotesque rock tribe and the 4 km long leisure agriculture sightseeing corridor. The main attractions are "Xihai Xinggong Guaishi Group", "A Line of Heaven", "Dolphin Looking at the Moon", "Love Cave", "Zhuangyuan Cave" and more than 40 hieroglyphs and natural caves.
