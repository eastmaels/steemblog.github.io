中国江西南昌秋水广场
世界第2超级音乐喷泉
拥有目前全国最大的音乐喷泉群
也是亚洲最大的音乐喷泉群更是国际化观光旅游的大型休闲广场
特别是夜幕降临
霓幻的彩灯衬印下延绵数十米的喷泉伴随着音乐跳跃
带动着人们欢快的情绪

---

Qiushui Square, Nanchang, Jiangxi, China
World's 2nd Super Musical Fountain
Has the largest music fountain group in the country
It is also the largest music fountain in Asia and a large leisure plaza for international sightseeing.
Especially the night falls
The fascinating lanterns lining up the tens of meters of fountains accompanied by music jumping
Drive people's cheerful emotions
