 On their homepage they state that in 2017 the card offering the equivalent of:
* 6.9% cash back rate for Bitcoin rewards
* 16.3% cash back rate for Ethereum rewards

There are some problems with this credit card:
* This card didn't exist in 2017 and still doesn't exist.
* The high return rates are due to massive gains both cryptocurrencies made in 2017, and there is no guarantee the rate will be that high once it's released.
* No mention of fees, issuing bank or payment network

If you find this credit card interesting to you, you can sign up for their waitlist and get early access.

Anyway, this is a interesting credit card to folllow.
