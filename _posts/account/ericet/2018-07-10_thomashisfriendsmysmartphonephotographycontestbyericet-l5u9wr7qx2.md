Hi Steemians,

Here is my today's entry for the smartphone photography which is holded by <a href="https://busy.org/@vandadream">@vandadream</a>.

<hr />

<ul>
 	<li>这是我参加由 <a href="https://busy.org/@vandadream">@vandadream</a>举办的<strong>【MySmartphonePhotography Contest】</strong>（手机摄影比赛活动）。</li>
 	<li>拍摄地点：美国新泽西</li>
 	<li>拍摄手机：<strong>Iphone 8 plus</strong></li>
 	<li>拍摄设置：无</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<strong>What are the rules?</strong>

1.Please put MySmartphonePhotography in your titles.

2.Please put mysmartphonephotography in your tags.
3.It must be original work. If you copy from others, you will be flagged.
4.Post at least 2 shots.
5.Please introduce a little bit about your photography.
6.Once a month, from the first day of every month to the end of the month (EST).
<em>The First Place will get 2 SBD, the second place will get 1 SBD and the third place will get 0.5 SBD。Welcome to the contest！</em>

<hr />

<strong>规则是什么？</strong>

1．请把MySmartphonePhotography加入你的标题。

2．请把mysmartphonephotography加入你的标签。
3．一定是原创智能手机作品。如果你的照片是复制来的，你将被踩。
4．至少2张照片。
5．请简单介绍一下你的摄影作品。
