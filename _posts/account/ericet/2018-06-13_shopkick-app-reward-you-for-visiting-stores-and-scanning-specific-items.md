## Hunter's comment
The basic idea is that it's an app you use when you're shopping in a store or a mall to collect kicks (points), browse specials, and earn real-money rewards, including gift certificates.

Ways to earn kicks(points):
* Walk-in: Earn kicks at the entrance when you walk into select partner stores
* Scan: Hunt for select products in-store and earn kicks when you scan barcodes using in-app scanner
* Purchase with linked card: Earn kicks for every dollar you spend at select partner stores when you shop with a linked credit card
* Receipts: Purchase select items and submit your receipt through the app to earn kicks
* Visit online stores: Earn kicks by visiting select online partners through shopkick
* View online products: Check out select online partner offers through the app to earn kicks
* Make online purchases: Earn kicks for every dollar you spend when you visit online partners through shopkick
* Watch videos: Earn kicks and discover new products when you watch in-app videos

Use the kicks to redeem wide range of gift cards. Minimum kicks to redeem a gift card is 500 kicks, which give you $2 in gift card.
