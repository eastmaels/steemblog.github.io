Six years I’ve been trying to learn how to make Chinese dumplings. And in 6 years, I’ve managed very little progress. But still that doesn’t mean I don’t enjoy trying! The best part is, even when your jiaozis look like sad little sacks, you still get to eat the expert dumplings prepared by grandma and grandpa. 😍

六年我试试学习怎么包饺子好。六年过了，我还是不进步很多。可是我不说我不享受啊！最好的方面是如果我包的不好，我还可以吃专业的饺子。谢谢，姥姥和姥爷！

Happy Chinese New Year!

新年快乐！
