## 
Greetings everybody! Here's my second entry to the Art of Love contest hosted by @ntopaz and @livvu. 

It's a collage of 7 photos that I took from around the world. As you can see, each object is heart-shaped. =) Clockwise from top left, 

the 1st one: a naturally heart-shaped jackfruit I found in a market in Bali of Indonesia;

the 2nd one: a heart-shaped stone painted red by a native Mapuche woman who runs a guest house in central Chile;

the 3rd one: a crispy cracker made by me from the leftover rice;

the 4th one: neatly trimmed heart-shaped bush in the countryside of Belgium;

the 5th one: a candy from a cafe in Addis Ababa;

the 6th one: a fossil stone I spotted near Ouarzazate in Morocco;

the middle one: my decoration from the garden. :)
