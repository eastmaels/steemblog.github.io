Hello everyone! Here's my 3rd entry to the Opposing Beauty photo contest by @ntopaz. It was during a business trip in Chile when it's raining heavily.

The rain drops kept hitting the car window, and I found some interesting contrasts and textures in the frame - the soft fragile flowing rain water VS. the hard firm fixed window glass. So I took out my phone and shot this picture from outside. :) 

<br />
这张照片用来参加ntopaz“对比”主题的摄影活动。当时在智利南部出差，途中狂风大作，暴雨如注，雨点不停地狠狠敲击车窗。轻缓流动的水珠和坚硬厚实的玻璃形成有趣的对比，我就掏出手机拍下了这一刻。
