Hey everyone! The Easter holiday is starting here officially from next Monday, and since it’s weekend now, many have started traveling. So following my last Flat Lay post (somewhere 2 months ago... 😅), I’m sharing more flat lay photos today which is related with traveling:

![](https://steemitimages.com/0x0/https://img.esteem.ws/zhtb2x3zt5.jpg)

I arranged these travel objects on a big piece of white card stock, and shot a few photos from above. Most of these items were found during my trips around the world. See that lovely eye cover with “dreaming of next destination” (it’s from Brazil 🇧🇷, so it’s printed in Portuguese) and the mouse mat with “Wanderlust” on it?

![](https://steemitimages.com/0x0/https://img.esteem.ws/v320tiqc1s.jpg)

Here’s another one I did:

![](https://steemitimages.com/0x0/https://img.esteem.ws/c919smugq6.jpg)

Now we are on the way to the Austrian Alps for a short skiing holiday. I’m posting this with my phone over a coffee break at a gas station in Germany. So I’ll keep it short.

What about you? When do you have a Easter holiday? Any plans for traveling? :)

![](https://steemitimages.com/0x0/https://img.esteem.ws/m66kywx5pe.jpg)

这边的复活节假期开始了，很多人外出旅行，我们也在去奥地利滑雪的路上。昨天开了一晚，现在德国境内，途中加油站咖啡间歇，抽空分享几张我平铺旅行小物件俯拍的照片。刚看了下，上一篇flat lay还是两个月前的事😅。以前提过，这类照片在图库网站比较受欢迎。所以，旅途中可以拍拍风景、人文，在家可以拍拍这些小物件，总之，永远拍不完。:)

这些小物大都是旅途中淘的，连手机都是在美国出差时买的，手机壳一看便知来自袋鼠国🇦🇺。我最❤️的那个眼罩上写着“正在梦见下一个目的地”（在巴西圣保罗一个市场上淘的，所以是葡萄牙语），哈哈。还有那张印有wanderlust的鼠标垫我也很喜欢。

你有什么旅行途中淘到的心水小物吗？欢迎留言分享～ =)
