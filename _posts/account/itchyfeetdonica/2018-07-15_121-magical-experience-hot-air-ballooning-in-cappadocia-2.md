##
This is the second half of Hot Air Ballooning in Cappadocia, following my last post. You can find the introduction  about this place and my ride [here](https://steemit.com/colourfulphotography/@itchyfeetdonica/120-magical-experience-hot-air-ballooning-in-cappadocia-1). 

I described the incredible fantastic feeling in my last post, and now towards the end of the journey, our balloon is getting lower and lower, ready for landing. At the same time, there are more and more hot air balloons appearing in the sky.

接着上半段的土耳其卡帕多奇亚热气球之旅，我们再一起来看看后半部分的空中旅途。在[上一篇](https://steemit.com/colourfulphotography/@itchyfeetdonica/120-magical-experience-hot-air-ballooning-in-cappadocia-1)里，我介绍了这块神奇地貌的形成，描述了飞行的美妙感受。现在这后半段里，热气球的高度渐渐下降，为着陆做准备。在靠近地面的空中也突然出现越来越多其他色彩图案各异的热气球。

##
Landed... I was still alive - so excited!  :D

成功着陆啦！捏了几下自己的胳膊，还有知觉地存活着，好开心~！

![Cappa2 (2).jpg](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmNStTwh7KoLEvbRygooFj7URLZLUiMDJ5ettY3DwthJL9/Cappa2%20(2).jpg)

##
After landing, the balloon was packed, and we were offered champagne and other drinks. It felt great to celebrate together and warm up after the long flight in the cold morning. Each passenger was also awarded a flight certificate, woohoo~ I know, this is probably the easiest certificate you can get in your life - you only need to stand in a small moving basket for about 1 hour. And there you go - you did a historical flight over a moon-like landscape!  ;)

落地后，大气球被打包。飞行员发给我们每人一杯香槟，还颁发了飞行证书。一篮子乘客聚在一起举杯欢庆这一幸~~存~~福的时刻~  =)  这可能是最容易得到的证书了吧 -- 只需要呆站在一个在空中缓缓移动的篮子里，大约一小时后，飞行证书就到手了！还是如此奇幻地飞过地球上最像月球的地貌，简直酷毙了有木有？
