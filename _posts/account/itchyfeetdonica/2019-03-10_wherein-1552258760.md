## 
Hi everyone! In this post I’m sharing my recipe of making a savory crêpe/galette. It's inspired by the Chinese style pancakes at street stands across China, and donicanized by whatever I have at hand (as usual ;p).  Hope you will enjoy it and give it try! Easy, tasty and healthy - it's a great breakfast or brunch choice!  

##
嗨~ 这次来分享个自己今天做的早餐 —— 改良版天津煎饼果子、山东煎饼混合体和放飞自我的结晶。:) 所谓改良版，就是家里能找到什么食材就先用着，哈哈。比如没有油条或薄脆就用薯片代替，没有绿豆粉做面糊就省略，还有加了些这边常用的绿叶沙拉roquette（芝麻菜）、培根、三文鱼之类，可以分别裹在不同的饼皮里。当然你也可以就地取材、自由发挥，简单美味又健康，有空也尝试一下吧~  =)

## 
What I used:
我用的食材：
![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/12ab98866e054af6872f71a6d32ccc43.png)

##
Mix wheat flour, corn flour and water:
混合搅拌面粉、玉米粉和水：
![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/aec070e4ab424ffebea5de6c368a5aa4.png)

![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/bf7c74edc3ae4f66bfc7c008a4cadaf7.png)

##
Slice the cucumber and spring onion:
黄瓜和大葱切片/丝：
![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/5ff1dbdbacfa431a859738d08e83efd0.png)

## 
Heat the pan and spread the dough:
加热平底锅，摊平面糊（不禁想到街头那些煎饼艺术家们手中的神奇小耙子^^，我只能左右晃动手中的平底锅来摊平面糊，失掉好多乐趣~）：
![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/7bc576137747443b8f11d660e5dcc1f2.png)

##
Spread one egg over the dough, and sprinkle some spring onion and black sesame seeds on top: 
加个鸡蛋抹匀，撒点葱和黑芝麻：
![6.JPG](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmY1L6ERxqc6UAvHcUAN4Gq25qehNYJtUMuEMEXnBeSE3M/6.JPG)


##
Flip the crepe, and spread soybean paste and pickled veges (you can use other sauces like bbq sauce, curry sauce...)
翻面，另一面涂上豆瓣辣酱，撒点榨菜，或者萝卜干之类：
![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/7fbcdb919c8e48b8b4b2fee1e951ff14.png)

## 
Place whatever you want in the middle (I put salmon, bacon, cucumber, roquette, dofu slices and chips in different crepes):
中间放上你喜欢吃的或者家里能找到的东西：
![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/4f655a09366940598edbd7603aa4e618.png)

## 
Roll it:
卷起来：
![](http://newappaz.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/wherein_images/post/20190311/f7e8693b02b4427b9ee7356fb4f65447.png)

##
Enjoy! :) 
开吃～
