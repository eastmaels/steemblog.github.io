## 
You are right, this is the famous view from Victoria Peak of Hong Kong. It is known as The Peak, the highest hill on Hong Kong Island, with an elevation of 552 m. You might have seen this classic view lots of times from internet, TV, magazines or newspapers. Yeah, I visited it every time too when I went to HongKong as a tourist. I've no idea why - I guess it's just beautiful. ;) As it's so hot, I didn't feel like carrying a camera, so I snapped this shot with my phone.  

This is my entry to @axeman's Cityscape Photo Contest. Check them out and join the fun if you are also interested in photography. =)

##
没错，这就是在香港那有名的太平山顶朝下拍的维多利亚港有名的百万夜景。山顶是香港岛上的最高峰，550多米。主峰被官方叫作「扯旗山」，据说在粤语里名字来源颇为不雅？

就像所有有名的景点，总是人山人海，但是我也不知道为什么，每次跟亲戚朋友去香港玩，都要历经波折地跑去那里看夜景。因为天太热，懒得带相机上山，就手机随便咔嚓一下。:)

这是我参加@axeman的城市摄影活动，有兴趣的童鞋也来参加吧~
