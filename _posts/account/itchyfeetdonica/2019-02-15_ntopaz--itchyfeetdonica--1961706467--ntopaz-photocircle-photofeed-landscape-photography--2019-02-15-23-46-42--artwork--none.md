##
Unlike most travelers going by land transportation, I reached Torres del Paine National Park in the Chilean part by boat through Rio Serrano. :) And it proved to be a great choice.

After some hiking, I was stunned by this view at sunset - five elegant guanacos grazing at the altiplano steppe, with the magnificent snow mountains as the backdrop. These camelids are native to South America, closely related to their more famous cousin - llama. =)

I also felt lucky to have visited Patagonia during autumn time, as the landscape in the warm tone was surely a feast to eyes!
