## 
Greetings everyone! So I uploaded my first entry to @ntopaz's Skyline Photo Contest a few weeks ago, and asked you guys to guess where it was shot. Some friends mistook it as Toronto because of the similar tall tower. @betterthanhome commented with the correct answer: it's Sydney. And he even got my shooting point right - from the Watsons Bay! 

 
Now I'm entering a second one... I believe it's easier this time to figure out where? Since it's a closer up shot, and the landmarks are pretty obvious. :) And do you know from which place I took this photo? 

## 
前几周我上传了张城市天际线的照片，让大家猜猜是哪里，有几位朋友误以为是在加拿大的多伦多，因为那座瘦高的电视塔很相似。其实是在悉尼，有一位朋友猜对了，而且还看出来我是从Watsons湾拍的，很厉害！

现在让我们继续来猜猜看，这次应该会容易很多，因为是近景，而且有很多标志性建筑在照片里哦！你知道我是站在哪里拍的吗？
