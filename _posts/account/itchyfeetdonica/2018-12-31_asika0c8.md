## 
How are you doing my Steemit friends? Any plans for New Year's Eve? (Or in some parts of the world - what did you do for it? ;)) 

I'm going to Maastricht of the Netherlands soon for the New Year fireworks by the river. I was in this amazing town a few times and visited its Christmas market with some friends 6 years ago. Time flies! Only 1 year ago, I was watching the famous New Year fireworks in Sydney on a road trip, also by water.

Now it's cooooold and glooooomy here in Belgium. I miss the warm sunshine in the Southern Hemisphere, so I'm sharing with you a dancing video of me and other travellers in the desert of Bolivia! 

It was the January of 2017. My long journey traveling through South America almost came to an end. The Uyuni trip in Bolivia was my last one before going back to Chile to work. I got to know the Chilean couple and 2 Portuguese girls from the same jeep, and we improvised this group dance in the desert that morning. It looks silly, I know, but we had so much fun recording it. Great memories! ğŸ˜ƒ 

Hope you enjoy it like I did! And have an awesome 2019 everyone!
