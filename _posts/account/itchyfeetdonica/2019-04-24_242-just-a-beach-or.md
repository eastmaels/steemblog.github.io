## 
The local host couple brought me to this beautiful beach in Cancún, Mexico. We had a great time here. The place is kind of a gem in the very touristic city. Cancún is also a popular destination for students during universities’ spring break period.

But it’s touristic for a good reason right? Soft white sand, forever blue sky, and the aqua-blue Caribbean sea with crystal clear water. A tropical paradise. =)

![F50E9C89-BC29-4B22-8705-714EBDB8AC65.jpeg](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmfAHwYoN2urtmBeKYXimvA428Q3A75AqtnkJLPRRtyRL2/F50E9C89-BC29-4B22-8705-714EBDB8AC65.jpeg)

## 
去过很多海滩，热带风情的也不少，还记得第一次看到传说中的加勒比海就是在这里 —— 墨西哥的坎昆。这个旅游胜地虽然非常touristic，尤其来自美国的游客众多，一到春假时就变成学生的派对天堂。但这一块的海滩海岛确实不负盛名，比如我照片里的这片城市海滩。碧海蓝天、水清沙幼，夫复何求？:)
