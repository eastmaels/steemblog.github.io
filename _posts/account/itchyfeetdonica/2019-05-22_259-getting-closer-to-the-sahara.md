##
So this is my look on the way to the almighty Sahara dunes. :D The blue tagelmust on my head was bought from a Berber vendor at the roadside. It is an indigo-dyed cotton garment that acts as both a veil (see my second photo below) and a turban. He told me the blue's a lucky color for the Berber men in the desert. 

Ok, though I'm neither a Berber nor a man, this long piece of fabric was pretty handy to shield the incredibly strong wind, scorching sun and spreading sand. I'm glad that I went local, and the color scheme of my outfit was even in line with the environment. :)
