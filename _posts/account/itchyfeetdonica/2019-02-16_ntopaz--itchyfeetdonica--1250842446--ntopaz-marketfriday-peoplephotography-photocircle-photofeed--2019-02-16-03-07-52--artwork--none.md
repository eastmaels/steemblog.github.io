##
Greetings everyone! Today I'm sharing my experience of visiting a textile market in the Sacred Valley, Peru.

I'm joining the fun of #marketfriday initiated by @dswigle, and the head image is my entry to @worldcapture's Peoplephotography. Check out these challenges and share your unforgettable moments with us! :)

##
大家周末好~ 今天请跟我到秘鲁神圣山谷的小村庄，来看看一个本土特色的纺织品市场。这里的织物都是由天然的植物染料染色的，当地的织女演示了染色和织布的过程，很有意思。🙂
