This is a moment I captured in front of the Central Market in Cambodia's capital city, Phnom Penh. The large market constructed in 1937 was in the shape of a dome with four arms, and it's a busy place full of visitors. 

After I tried some great local food at one of the street stands outside, I walked towards this indoor market. At the entrance, I saw these 4 boys having fun with a phone, and I quickly took out my phone to freeze this scene. To them, all the hustle and bustle didn't seem to exist. The little joys of life~ :)
