##
Greetings everyone! I'm sharing with you some minimal style photos I took right after sunrise. It was at around 7am in Erg Chebbi, the large seas of dunes formed by wind-blown sand in Morocco. 

The sand in the Sahara Desert here looked more orangish than in the other dunes I've seen before. The heavy storm of the previous night also contributed to the deeper colours. I just couldn't have enough of the gorgeous curves and shades formed by nature. Hope you would enjoy them as I did! =) 

The head image is my entry for the #TravelPhotoContest by @lightcaptured. Don't hesitate to join the fun if you also love travel and photography! 

##
今天分享几张撒哈拉沙漠的简洁风沙丘照片，拍于早上七点左右，日出之后。
这儿的沙子呈现橙黄色，比我以前在其他沙漠地带看到的沙丘颜色要深，跟前一晚下的暴雨应该也有关系。
看着这美妙的光影、色调和线条，我又一次被大自然的造化所深深折服。
