Hi there, today I'm sharing a shot from my last skiing trip in the Austrian Alps. It's my last entry to the Black and White Photography contest hosted by @ntopaz and @axeman. Thank you guys! 

I was at another hilltop then and found this flow with the snow quite interesting. I waited a bit till the skiing/snowboarding people on the slope formed  this pattern within my frame and captured the moment. What do you think of it? Hope you enjoy it as I did! :)
