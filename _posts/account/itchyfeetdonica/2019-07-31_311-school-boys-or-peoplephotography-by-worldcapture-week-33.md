## 
These boys were saying hi to me when I was strolling on a street in Colombo, Sri Lanka. They just came out from their school nearby and waved happily to my direction when they saw me pass by. The cute naughty boys and the typical vehicles at the background looked very photogenic, so I captured this moment with my camera. :)
