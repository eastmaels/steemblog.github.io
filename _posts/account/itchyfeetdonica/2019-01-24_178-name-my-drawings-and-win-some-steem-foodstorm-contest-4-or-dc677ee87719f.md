##
How's it going my friends? I just painted 4 types of chili peppers that I love and eat a lot. It's inpired by @deemarshall's Foodstorm Contest. 

She asks participants to make some art about aphrodisiacs food. So the first thing coming to my mind is chili pepper. I have loved hot spicy food since a very young age, mainly influenced by my grandparents' cooking. I don't think I had any idea of aphrodisiacs then though... 😄

To make it a bit more fun, let's play a little game! Can you tell me the names of these 4 peppers? I'm not saying "guess", since I painted these peppers in a pretty realistic way. It shouldn't be too diffcult right? The first person who get their names right will have 1 Steem. So come join it! Look forward to your answers.
