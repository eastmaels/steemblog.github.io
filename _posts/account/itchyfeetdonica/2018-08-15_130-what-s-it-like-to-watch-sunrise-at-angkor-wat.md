- 7-day pass is a good choice since thereâ€™s quite some distance between certain sites, and thereâ€™s so much to see at each site. If you are short of time, you can also choose the 3-day pass. 

- Remember to bring sunscreen and cool clothes for the heat, and cover your shoulders and knees as required by the religious culture.
