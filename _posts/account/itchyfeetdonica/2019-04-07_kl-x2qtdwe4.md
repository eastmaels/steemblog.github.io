##
This is the Kuala Lumpur skyline viewed from Titiwangsa Recreational Park. I strolled around the huge park for a few hours with my mom. It was the golden sunset hour when we reached this spot, and I found it's a great point to look at those landmarks in the city such as the famous Petronas Twin Towers and the tall tower Menara Kuala Lumpur. The clouds, lake and reflections added more layers to the beauty. 
  
Titiwangsa Recreational Parks spreads across an area of 95 hectares, more than half of which is a big lake. When it was under British Rule in history, this area was full of tin mining activities. Later on, it was cleaned up and developed into a green space. When we walked around the lake garden, we saw people jogging, cycling, boating, kayaking and having picnic with families or friends. This green lung in the gigantic hectic city seemed to be a popular place to relax a bit.
