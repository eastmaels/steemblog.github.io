##
If you have been to Cusco of Peru, you might have visited the famous site of Sexywoman (Saqsaywaman). It is a citadel on the northern outskirts of the Inca Empire’s historic capital. 

At the site, I tried sliding down the massive stone slopes, just like what the boy’s doing in my photos. I loved the changing emotions shown clearly on his face. :) Imagine the thrill and fun we had at this amazing natural playground of the Inca! 

This collage is my entry to @ntopaz’s Emotive Art contest. Below are the 2 original photos:

##
如果你去过秘鲁的印加古都库斯科，可能也去看了北郊有名的Saqsaywaman遗址。如果你还没去过，将来记得去试试这里的天然大石梯！

就像我照片中的小男孩一样，我也沿着这石头坡滑下去，很有意思的体验～ 不知道古代的印加人是不是也常常在此寻欢作乐，哈哈。

迅速下滑的过程中，耳边风飕飕而过，那种既有点恐惧又极度兴奋的快感交织，明明白白地写在了这个小男孩的脸上。:)
