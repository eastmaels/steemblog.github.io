I captured this man dancing on the road, close to Lake Chamo in the south of Ethiopia. 

This is my entry for the Portrait Photography Contest hosted by @jasonrussell with the theme MOVEMENT. And Friday is also the portrait photography day by another awesome contest host @juliank. Check out their pages and join the photo fun if interested. 

Hope you like my shot! Tell me what you think in the comments below. See you next time! =)
