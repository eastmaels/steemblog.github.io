##
Greetings everyone! The new theme of @ntopaz's photography contest is Opposing Beauty. When I see this topic, the first photo that pops up in my mind is this one I took in Bolivia. I used it as background in my Cross-culture Challenge cover image, but haven't posted it in any photography content yet. I love this fun theme and would submit more photos in the coming days. :) 

I was walking on a street in La Paz when I came across this scene. I found it interesting, layered with social commentary. Now I don't feel like analyzing all the elements in this image like in my art criticism class. ;) They say a picture is worth a thousand words. How do you interprete this photo? Please tell me in the comments.

大家好呀~ 这是我参加以“Opposing Beauty”为主题的摄影活动的照片，用手机拍于玻利维亚首都La Paz街头。

我觉得这个主题很有意思，还会继续po其他相关照片。这张街头的抓拍我在最近的【跨文化活动】题图里用作了背景，因为充满对比的元素，所以我最先想到提交这一张。感觉如果像上文艺评论课一样来剖析这些元素，还不如听听你们的想法。:) 一图抵千言，你是怎么解读的呢？有没有类似主题的照片分享？
