##
This is a drone shot taken at the Grossglockner High Alpine region. It's my entry to Qurator's Photo Friday competition. 

At 3,798 metres above the Adriatic, the Grossglockner is the highest mountain in Austria. Driving in this area is a real feast to eyes. The winding roads are like ribbons flowing between the majestic mountains, lush pastures and flowery meadows.

##
这是在奥地利大钟山地区的一张无人机航拍。大钟山海拔3798米，是奥地利最高峰。在这块阿尔卑斯高山地区的公路上行驶，周围不断变幻的风景真是一场视觉盛宴！蜿蜒崎岖的盘山路就像长长的缎带，飘散在雄伟的山峦之间，眼前不时伸展开一片片绿意盎然的牧场和鲜花盛开的草甸。
