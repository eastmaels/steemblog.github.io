##
I shot the photo at a temple in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. These 2 happy girls came towards me and waved hello. They posed for photos in front of the temple after we all visited it. Enjoy the sweet smiles! =) 

As I said in a previous post about [Some SMILE Portraits in My Travels](https://steemit.com/portraitcontest/@itchyfeetdonica/3yfcnn-52-portraits-smile#) :

>A smile costs nothing, but gives much. Pretty often we underestimate the power of a smile, the gorgeous curve that sets everything straight. Have you ever had the experience that someone's lovely smile just brightens your day? It happened to me a lot in daily life and during my travels.


##
这两张照片是在柬埔寨首都金边的一座寺庙拍的。我正打算进入寺庙参观，就看见这对小姐妹朝我跑过来，还挥着小手打招呼，脸上挂着可爱的笑。我指着相机，问可不可以给她们拍几张照片，她们很开心地配合。拍完给她们看了照片，她们又快活地笑起来。可惜语言不通，看她们的比划，意思可能是她们家就住在这庙附近。

以前在好几篇帖子里都发过旅途中捕捉到的笑脸，比如：[一些旅途中的微笑](https://steemit.com/portraitcontest/@itchyfeetdonica/3yfcnn-52-portraits-smile#)。世界各地常常会遇上语言不同，但是微笑却永远相通。不管是出门在外的路途中，还是日常生活的琐碎里，这些发自内心的快乐总是让人受到感染，让日复一日上演的无聊剧目变得生动起来。
