这张照片是我几年前在丹麦首都哥本哈根的Tivoli游乐园里拍的，用来参加单色摄影比赛。

I clicked this photo inside the Tivoli amusement park in Copenhagen, Denmark, with my old friend - an entry-level camera Canon Kiss X3. 

This is my entry for the photo contest #monomad by @brumest, and it's my first time participating this challenge, since I just discovered it. I would like to say thank you to these amazing hosts who are building an active photography community on #Steemit.
