## 
Hi everyone! Today I’m sharing a picture shot with my phone in Singapore. It's my entry for the Photo Friday competition. 

I was walking around by the Marina Bay with my mom when we caught a sudden and heavy tropical shower. It only lasted for a short while, and afterwards we got to see this spectacular scene. Do you see the rainbow there? If not, open your eyes wider and look hard! ;) I hate that I didn't bring a decent camera with me at that moment to catch the amazing rainbow colors. 

Over the vibrant bougainvillea, you can clearly view the signature structure of Marina Bay Sands and the lotus-shaped Art Science Museum, both touched by the warm sunlight.  :)

##
今天分享的是新加坡滨海湾暴雨过后的景色。很幸运地看到一道彩虹显现在色彩缤纷的天空（看见了没？可能要睁大眼睛仔细看…… ;p 可惜当时天太热懒得带相机，用手机完全拍不出彩虹的绚烂效果~）。视线越过眼前这丛茂盛的三角梅，对岸立着的著名地标金沙和状似莲花盛开的艺术科学博物馆都被夕阳微微染上了金色。=)
