##
The horrible attacks in Sri Lanka reminds me of my happy time spent in the country. Without any doubt, this sad news would heavily impact the local tourism as well. As a tourist in Sri Lanka, my experience was lovely and enjoyable. In fact, I mentioned a few times in my previous posts that Sri Lanka is one of my favorite travel destinations, and I will surely visit it again in future. 

I took this photo at a buddhist temple in a small village of the tea country. People bought fresh flowers from the nearby vendors as offerings. They lit incense sticks and then prayed in front of them. It is my entry to the Emotive Art contest hosted by @ntopaz.
