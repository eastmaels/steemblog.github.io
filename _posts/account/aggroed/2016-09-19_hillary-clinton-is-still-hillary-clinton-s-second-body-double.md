<p>This post attempts to show that Hillary Clinton had steady age progression from November 2015 until September of 2016. &nbsp;She was rushed off of the 9/11 memorial site. &nbsp;She went to Chelsea's apartment. &nbsp;When she emerged from the apartment she lost 50 lbs and looked 10 years younger making many think she was a body double. &nbsp;She left the 9/11 memorial to go to their place in Chippequa NY. &nbsp;From there her next campaign appearance was after several days where she showed up in Greensboro, NC with a third look extreme enough to indicate a second body double. &nbsp;She was recently filmed talking about explosions in NY while on her plane. &nbsp;The second body double appears consistent with the woman on the plane talking about the explosions.</p>
<p>Several interesting things of note. &nbsp;In the footage of her getting in the black van leaving the memorial site one of the secret service members film the event.</p>
<p>When closely inspecting the footage of her leaving the memorial site it appears the bars on the grates merge into the metal supports and back out of it.</p>
<p>There appears to be one photo the morning of the 9/11 service that is more consistent with Hillary's first body double.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>My initial thoughts now leave me more open than before that the memorial service event was staged and planned. &nbsp;The fact that her leaving was filmed from afar and up close suggests some planning. &nbsp;The fact that the grates go in and out suggest that the footage may also have been green screened and put together. &nbsp;With those two parts looking planned and now a single image that seems to indicate Hillarysbodydouble was on the scene early in the morning when it was still dark there are multiple elements suggesting the whole operation was staged. &nbsp;Definitive proof. &nbsp;No. &nbsp;Worth looking for other images. &nbsp;Yes.</p>
