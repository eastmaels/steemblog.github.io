# <center> A Very Merry Peace Academy with @aggroed and @isaria </center>


---
The Holiday season is upon us and it's time to spread Peace, Abundance, and Joy.
The goal of the Peace Academy is to help us experience those things all year long!
Join @aggroed and @isaria tonight for a Very Merry Peace Academy where we discuss ways we can do that.



As the Charlie Brown Christmas song, *Christmas Time is Here* says,
