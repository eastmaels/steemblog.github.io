<p>In a lot of ways I'm the least likely person to come out and say this, but I think the real Secretary of State Hillary Clinton was on Jimmy Fallon the evening of September 19. &nbsp;Deep face wrinkles, moles, teeth alignment, blue eyes, "jokers smile", heavier with at least a double chin, nose shape, a long and pronounced neck crease, and a quality to her voice all seemed to reemerge on the show.</p>
<p>I've spent hours pouring over these photos and making comparisons going back over a year looking for discrepancies between people reported to be Hillary Clinton. &nbsp;I have a viral video that received 205k hits in the five days it's been posted that shows it isn't her. &nbsp;I kinda made a place by saying "that woman isn't Hillary." I'm also not a fan of hers (though in all honesty I used to be). &nbsp;So, as much as I may personally want to see her fall into the story line of arrested, incapacitated, and/or dead (as reported by 4 sources: abc tv, abc web, chuck todd tweet, ben fulford weekly report) it appears a very different story is emerging. &nbsp;She's back.</p>
<p>Yeah, I'm shocked too.</p>
<p>Honestly, I had to watch this stuff over and over because I couldn't believe my eyes as the last thing I would expect is her return after seeing not one, but two doubles replace her. &nbsp;That said, she appears to have returned, she sounds as though her throat congestion is better, and that her natural voice has come back. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Especially for all of those who repeated a constant drum beat of "Have you even heard of Botox?" on the YouTube comments I would argue Botox isn't the kind of thing that you take while seriously ill because it's literally a poison that's used in a small dose. &nbsp;You don't introduce poisons into very sick people. &nbsp;I'll also add that Botox isn't something that would change her face from the 11th to 19th to smooth out all of her deep wrinkles, nor would her face suddenly revert to deep wrinkles in the same time span. &nbsp;For everyone that says it's all just make up, why can't the news crews hide it before 9/11, hide her deep wrinkles from 9/11-9/19, and then suddenly make up fails to hide them again. &nbsp;That doesn't make sense.</p>
