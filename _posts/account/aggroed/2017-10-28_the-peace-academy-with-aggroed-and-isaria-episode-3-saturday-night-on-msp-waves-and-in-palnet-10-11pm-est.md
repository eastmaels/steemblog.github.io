# <center> The Peace Academy with @aggroed and @isaria </center>


---


Come join us for our 3rd episode of The Peace Academy this Saturday night on MSP-Waves!

We've had some interesting conversations so far regarding Energy and its relationship to Peace.

This week we will be discussing Personal Boundaries and techniques for Conflict Resolution.

***We also want to hear from you!***

If you are interested in sharing your thoughts regarding Peace, Abundance, and Liberty then we would love to hear from you! 
Just let us know in the audience chat and we will get you on the air!


---

The Peace Academy is designed to help Steemit and MSP community members learn to interact peacefully with one another.

Each week, @aggroed and @isaria will be discussing the concepts of Peace, Abundance, and Liberty and sharing how we can best implement them here in our community, as well as in our daily lives.
