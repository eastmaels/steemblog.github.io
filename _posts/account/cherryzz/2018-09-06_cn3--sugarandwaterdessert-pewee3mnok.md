今天我做的这个甜品：桃胶雪莲子雪耳冰糖糖水，这个甜品美容养颜的，我最爱的甜品之一，美女可以自己试一下，帅哥也可以煮给心爱的太太吃，哈哈。顾名思义，这个甜品的材料就是桃胶，雪莲子（又叫皂角米）加雪耳和冰糖。
Today I made this dessert: peach gum snow lotus seed snow ear candy, this dessert beauty, one of my favorite desserts, you can try it on your own。
Materials:
1: peach gum.
2: Saussurea involucrata.
3: snow ear,
4: rock candy.

<hr />

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-407" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/681974823808551549.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

<hr />

桃胶，桃树树皮中分泌出来的树脂，干的桃胶呈结晶石状很硬，看着有点像琥珀，有清血降脂，缓解压力和抗皱嫩肤的功效。需要几颗就够了，一会能泡出一大盆呢。<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-404" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/309661739974736655.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>雪莲子，也叫皂角米，这个非常好吃，煮了后口感很好呢。

<hr />

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-405" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/418142722682386750.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="809" /><br/>雪耳，（银耳）我买的是古田雪耳，煮久了也很爽口。

<hr />

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-412" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/921971310267290381.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>把这三样放水里泡大概5小时，我一般泡3-4小时就煮了，这个是泡好之后的图，桃胶一小粒就能泡成很大。Soak these three kinds of water for about 5 hours. I usually cook for 3-4 hours.

<hr />

<div class="pull-right"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-417" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/464644001633789283.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="809" /><br/></div>
桃胶泡好以后，需要把泡出来的黑色的沉淀物隔离开来，那些是树皮之类的。泡好的桃胶，透明可爱，看起来QQ 的，好像果冻。
Water filled peach glue，It is necessary to isolate black sediments from bark. The good peach gum is transparent and cute, much like jelly.<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-403" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/232414916747938571.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>泡好之后，加入冰糖，然后就自动煮了，煮开后，小火煮大概30分钟就可以了，我用电壶煮的。
