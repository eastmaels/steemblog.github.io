台风的前一天，天气是非常闷热的，傍晚，和孩子们去打篮球，这个是球场上的云，孩子说是火烧云吗?台风前的一天，天气还是很好的到了晚上还是风平浪静的。<hr><div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-515" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183044.jpg" alt="" width="750" height="1334" /><br/></div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-516" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183052.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1920" /><br/></div><hr>
睡了一晚起来，就已经开始暴雨的天气了，随着台风的逼近，风越来越大。准备吃午饭的时候，风开始变的大起来，当时最接近风圈，阳台窗口的玻璃窗忽然被风吹的快变形了，好险，我赶紧用手顶住窗口，和台风抗战啊。找不到合适的东西支持窗口，只好用手撑住，家人轮流在窗口旁边顶住（后来才知道很多朋友在家也是这样）。<hr><div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-517" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183100.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-518" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183110.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><br><br>一连3个小时的狂风暴雨，阳台的窗开始漏水进来，家里的空调孔的位置也开始漏水，我都装了好几盘水，房间客厅的窗都在漏水，外面风声不断。孩子们也第一次经历那么恐怖的台风天，妈妈说在深圳住了那么多年，第一次那么大风。到了傍晚台风终于小了一点点了。经历了12级的台风，再到10级也觉得不是那么恐怖了。<hr><div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-528" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917222023.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/>
</div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-526" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917222009.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><hr>晚上，我下楼看了一下各种树倒的横七竖八了，路上根本开不了车。<hr><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-529" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917222035.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/><br>今天台风过后，路上倒了很多的树。<br><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-520" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183150-1.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/><br>令我感到最难过的是下面这棵快两百年的古榕树也倒下了。<br><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-522" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183206.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/><div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-524" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183231.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-525" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183240-1.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-521" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183158.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-523" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180917183216.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/> <br /><center><hr/><em>Posted from my blog with <a href='https://wordpress.org/plugins/steempress/'>SteemPress</a> : https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/2018/09/%e5%8f%b0%e9%a3%8e%e5%89%8d%e5%90%8e </em><hr/></center>
