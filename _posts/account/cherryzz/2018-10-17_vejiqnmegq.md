<hr>今天分享个超级好吃的自制辣椒酱，我们家孩子奶奶出品的辣椒酱，制作很简单，味道也很好，可以用来沾肉，沾饺子，下饭，都非常美味。想想就流口水了。喜欢辣一点或者不那么辣的都可以自己自由搭配，大家和我一起来做吧。<br>Today, we share homemade chili sauce. Mama's chili sauce is very simple and tastes good. Let's make it with me.<hr>https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmST6TeMF9DwrSmFyCwcH6CpMfJXghqi2qvhg88fiRnZCS<hr>材料：指天椒/大蒜/花生油 /盐<br><hr><div class=pull-left>https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmQ9Ku8n5JDJPbwLsCxQguVaiaaLENSitjvDQTnpaxJ1j7</div><div class=pull-right>https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmNSkPs2MagQUstApAcmSygiGYMX91Ks49wN4RUWnz4DXf
</div><br>首先，把大蒜剥好皮，剁碎，我先用了个锤子把蒜拍碎，然后再用刀剁碎，剁的有点累，我就拿了个道具出来，哈哈。<hr><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-722" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017205210.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/><hr>要切成大概那么碎就可以了，剁好了就用盘先装好，注意盘子不能有水。<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-729" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017205227.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/><hr>接着来剁辣椒了，新鲜的指天椒来做辣椒酱特别好吃，如果喜欢比较辣就放多一点辣椒，如果再加辣椒粉，就超级辣了，我不喜欢太辣，所以我放的大蒜会多一点。<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-725" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017205221.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/><hr>辣椒先切成这样，然后也还需要剁碎一点。<div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-724" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017205216.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-726" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017205232.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-727" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017205247.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/><hr>终于都剁好了，手工剁的话就比较累的，最后就轻松一点了，把剁好的大蒜和辣椒混合在一起。<br><div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-728" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017205254-1.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-735" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017210025.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/></div><hr>混合均匀后，把花生油油和盐也混合一起。花生油要多一些，快没过辣椒酱。<br><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-736" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/微信图片_20181017210041.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080"
