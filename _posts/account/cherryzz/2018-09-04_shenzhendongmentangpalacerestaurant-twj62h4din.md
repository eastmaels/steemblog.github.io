孩子的假期终于结束了，很久没有来唐宫了，孩子开学就约了朋友一起来了，唐宫的环境和味道都非常不错的，在罗湖区来说是很受欢迎的酒店，味道特别好，位置也方便，在深圳地铁站1号线F 出口，深圳戏院的旁边。
The children's holidays are finally over. They haven't come to the Tang Palace for a long time. The environment and taste here are very good. It's a very popular hotel in Luohu District. It's very delicious and convenient. It's near the F exit of Shenzhen Metro Station Line 1 and Shenzhen Theater.<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-380" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/666305336056928170.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

每次来唐宫酒家要经过这里，深圳的深南大道，东门路段。深南大道一路上的高楼非常多。这个角度拍照车来车往的也特别有感觉。地王，京基100，赛格，平安金融大厦。
Every time we come to Tang Palace restaurant, we will pass through Shennan Road and Dongmen road in Shenzhen.
