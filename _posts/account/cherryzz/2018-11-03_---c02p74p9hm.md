This one is a raw material. I cut small pieces and then add olive oil and orange essential oil, heat them and let them melt together.<br><br>![微信图片_20181103103345.jpg](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/Qme57Ld1kQca8BfSkyz7AkvoUPxz1dikPVcRychfCNTwGe)![微信图片_20181103103435.jpg](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmQRtmo4wZVRN1pr4gdUAo83Ms4v6Wgi88SRFZRifbkqwR)![微信图片_20181103103506.jpg](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmcX5egRisx4fKAHDWc8uaVQ1WBwnEZYc1pLNd7UQ5P5BW)<br><br>融化后就基本完成了，现在只需要装进润唇膏的管子里面就可以了。After melting, it is basically finished, and now it only needs to be put into a lipstick tube.<br><br>![微信图片_20181103103528.jpg](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmS9emiuNZC7ZQeG7sf2ad3h4BHnbjwZ1zGQh44NejY4kR)最后放 冰箱一晚上润唇膏就可以用了，哈哈，没错，我在做润唇膏，因为冬天快来了天气开始干燥，这些是我为家人准备的。The last night in the refrigerator, lip balm can be used.
