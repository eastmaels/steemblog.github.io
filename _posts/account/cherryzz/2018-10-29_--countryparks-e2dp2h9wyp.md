Ma Yushan, this mountain in Shenzhen is not very famous, there are not so many people in Wutong Mountain, I am also here for the first time, this time I went in the spring, I really want to share such a good place for everyone.<br><br>The Maji Mountain Country Park is in the mountains of eastern Shenzhen. The mountains are not too high. The peaks are about 300-500 meters above sea level, and the slope is moderate. Therefore, it is not too difficult to climb the mountain. Our group of people drove to the vicinity of Xiaomeisha, and then they needed to walk up the mountain.<br><br>马峦山，在深圳这座山并不太出名，没有梧桐山那么多人熟知，我也是第一次来到这里，这次是在春季的时候去的，很想把那么好的地方分享给大家<br><br>马峦山郊野公园在深圳东部的山区，山不算太高，山峰大概海拔300-500米左右，坡度适中，所以登上山不会太困难。我们一行人开车到小梅沙附近，然后就需要步行上山了。![IMG_2783.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmRgVNMJm1drc3GwUvPnrthAriTwwWwfGDXgNqQ1ptk7Hg)![IMG_7986.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmSaGsuDztZCPAt9ft1URZVRaYwJmoa3TxiSEjgXU2UQji)
<br><br>When you get to the middle of the mountain, you can see the harbor next to the sea. The air is very good and the scenery is beautiful.<br><br>上到半山的时候，靠近海的一边可以望到旁边的海港，空气很好，让人如此心旷神怡。![IMG_8036.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmYCeJNrJs28PcM8jZmENigw2szmFzfPQNbdUExmGcFSHy)![IMG_8035.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmYMo6UHJCbyy1PhahCdBJ1s8C1rgYc16VBZMQgHGPCnnx)
![IMG_8039.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmcwaGaJ144PamS4Tqam6g2N3bMtRmrzMazGSeYEvocyAc)<br><br>
The road up the mountain is a stairway. It is also very relaxing. There are not many people coming here. We have to go to the mountain for lunch at noon. There are Lingnan Hakka houses on the mountain and some farmhouse restaurants have been opened. <br><br>There are some ancestral halls and urns and ancient wells. These have more than 600 years of historical residences, as well as the address of Sun Yat-sen’s personal lectures.<br><br>上山的路多是楼梯为主，坡度不高，上山也是很轻松的，来这里的人不多，我们这次上山主要赶在中午去吃农家菜，山上有岭南客家民居，开了一些农家饭馆。<br><br>上面有罗氏宗祠、张氏宗祠，还有碉楼及古井，这些有着600多年的历史民居，还有孙中山当年亲自讲学的地址。![IMG_7998.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmSH43Xeg98vNzmdnUaNuqrtL9gdqpM4CPpMVYuCBBa59J)![IMG_8009.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmYy6MCNCk6BorqjR2LzNuKUeumPSJpREry2FLiMdBB1FB)![IMG_2759.JPG](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmQsf7cJWfhK9BEoGHPde7iP7xizYYCNm1ppoTxbngrxGs)<br><br>Going up the mountain, going in from a small road, some houses. The most visible is this red brick house, which is now a restaurant.<br><br>上了半山腰，从一条小路进去，稀疏有些村落，和房子。最显眼的是这个红砖房子，这里也是吃饭的地方。旁边是张氏宗祠。<br><br>Come here, an old house, written with the School of Qianghua. These words were originally Sun Yat-sen’s school that wrote the name of the school.<br><br>来到这里，一栋老旧的房子，写着强华学校，这几个字原来是孙中山亲笔题写校名的学校，当年的庚子首义也在这里。
