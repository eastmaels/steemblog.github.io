<strong>Steemit的朋友们，大家好，  今天向大家推荐这道美味的客家菜：煎酿三宝</strong>

<strong>Hello, Steemit friends.I recommend this delicious Hakka dish to you today：Three treasures</strong>

<strong>说到煎酿三宝，我们客家人最喜欢吃的一道菜，这道客家风味的猪肉酿茄子，酿辣椒，酿苦瓜，酿豆腐。Eggplant pork, chili pork, tofu pork in Guangdong。</strong>

<strong>上次和大家说过我是客家人，我最喜欢酿茄子和酿辣椒，方便煮又很好吃，这个是从小一直喜欢吃的一道菜，这次特意把3样菜分开煮。煎酿三宝还有一种做法是用油煎干，皮会比较脆，但是我们喜欢煎了焖一下，小孩吃了没那么上火。</strong>

<strong>I like eggplant and chili best.This is a dish that has always been enjoyed since childhood.This time we cook the 3 dishes separately.</strong>
<strong><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-106" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/38.jpg" alt="" width="1071" height="1165" /></strong>

<strong>首先准备好，茄子，辣椒，豆腐，这次的茄子是现摘的，农村的茄子煮出来的颜色和买的茄子会不一样。</strong>

First, prepare eggplant, chili and tofu.The eggplant is picked now.The color of eggplant cooked by myself is different from that of eggplant.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-84" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/20.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-86" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/22.jpg" alt="" width="1080" height="1440" /><br/>

这次的茄子有点小了，不过是全天然无公害的，哈哈。

The eggplant is a little small this time.But they all grow on their own.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-96" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/33.jpg" alt="" width="1805" height="1079" /><br/>

猪肉剁碎加点油加少许盐，加上葱花，一起拌好，准备好酿进茄子里面。

Cut up the pork, add some oil and salt, add the scallion, and put it in the eggplant.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-92" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/29.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

我们喜欢切厚一点，放猪肉进去。也可以煎干一点比较香脆，煎的话就切薄一点。

We like to cut it thicker and put the pork in.

&nbsp;

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-90" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/27-e1534594104269.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

酿辣椒，切开一个口，猪肉放进去，如果很辣的辣椒，会把手也辣到了，如果担心太辣，可以用水清洗一下辣椒的肚子。

Pepper, cut a mouth.Pork in。

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-83" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/19-e1534594532152.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

这次用的是黄豆腐，味道也很好。中间开个小口，用筷子把肉酿进去，酿豆腐的时候需要小心一点，因为豆腐比较容易裂开。

This is yellow tofu and it tastes good.A small opening in the middle.You need to be careful when you put pork in, because tofu is soft.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-93" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/30.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

&nbsp;

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-94" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/31-e1534594289481.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>
<h3>放进锅里加油煎一下，再放水焖熟就可以上桌了，味道很好，很下饭。</h3>
Put it in the pan and fry it with oil, then cook it with water. It tastes good.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-91" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/28.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-89" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/26.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-95" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/32-e1534594358140.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/><h3>     再来两张用柴火大锅做的酿豆腐和酿辣椒，现在能吃上柴火做的菜是非常难得的了。这是我妈妈在煮菜，最喜欢妈妈的味道了，想念小时候。
Two more dishes cooked in a big pot of firewood.This is my mother cooking, I like my mother cooking, miss when I was little.
