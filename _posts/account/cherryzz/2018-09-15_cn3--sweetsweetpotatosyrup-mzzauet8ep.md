今天来煮一碗番薯芋头糖水，番薯有各种功效，可以蒸着吃，烤着吃，我喜欢煮糖水吃，加点芋头味道更好。七大功效：1、预防动脉硬化。2、护眼。3、预防感冒。4、减肥。5、平衡酸碱度。6、预防结肠癌。7、美容排毒。
Today boiled sweet potato taro sugar water, sweet potatoes have a variety of effects, can be steamed to eat, boiled to eat, I like to boil sugar water to eat, add a little taro taste better. Seven main functions of sweet potato: 1, prevent arteriosclerosis. 2, eye protection. 3, prevent colds. 4, lose weight. 5, balance acid-base. 6, prevent colon cancer. 7, beauty detoxification.![微信图片_20180915190143.jpg](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmTG4LbLEjU1Vj81BHfkN9E42QfGrNtkJpSGnKnxoinyJJ)

我先把番薯和芋头的皮削干净，切成小块。I first peel the sweet potato and taro and cut them into small pieces.<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-496" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180915190126.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-495" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180915190103.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>加黄糖，加水，然后放进高压锅里煮20分钟左右，就可以了。Add sugar, add water, then put it into a pressure cooker and cook for 20 minutes or so.<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-494" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180915190021.jpg" alt="" width="1440" height="1080" /><br/><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-499" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180915190327.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

很怀念小时候的打瓮番薯，又叫打土窑，爸爸在家里的院子里面，把地里的泥土一挖成一块块的，然后一层层叠起来像小塔一样，放柴在下面大火把泥土烧的火烫，接着在塔上面开个口把番薯放进去，再把土窑用棍子打倒，敲压平泥土，大概过一个小时吧，把番薯找出来，就能吃了。小时候觉的番薯太好吃了。其实是过程很有趣，现在想带孩子去体验一下，都找不到地方了。
I miss the kiln when I was a kid. In the courtyard of my family, Dad dug up the soil into pieces, then stacked it up like small towers, enlarged the torch and heated it, then opened a hole on the top to put the sweet potatoes in, and then flattened the soil. After about an hour, he found the sweet potatoes. I can eat it. When I was young, the sweet potato was delicious. In fact, the process is very interesting.![微信图片_20180915204958.png](https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmXy5TecgJxBB79b77J2MkGubsVn8mZstkKXYs6dAkRDvr)<img class="alignnone  wp-image-501" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180915205059.png" alt="" width="632" height="423" /><br/>
