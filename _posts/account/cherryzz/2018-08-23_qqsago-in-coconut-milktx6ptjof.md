材料Material Science：
西米Sago
芒果Mango
浓缩椰汁Coconut Juice
牛奶milk
蜂蜜Honey <hr><div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-185" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/_20180823114735-e1535001659439.jpg" alt="" width="3024" height="4032" /></div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-186" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/_20180823114742-e1535002011643.jpg" alt="" width="3024" height="4032" /></div>

做法 Method：
1：首先把水烧开，把西米放进去水里（水需要多一些），煮到西米里面的白点剩下一点。关火盖盖子，大概等10分钟。<br>2：把煮好的 西米用筛子捞出来，用凉水冲洗2次。<br>3：在煮水，喜欢甜可以在水里加点糖，水开了把西米再放进去。煮开了关火。放凉。<br>4：把椰浆和牛奶混合好用容器装起来。<br>5：芒果切好小块。<br>6：吃的时候装出来，倒混合的椰浆和牛奶，加上芒果，美味的西米露就完成了。<hr><div class=pull-left><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-184" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/_20180823114728-e1534996981426.jpg" alt="" width="3024" height="4032" /><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-190" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/微信图片_20180823114809.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /></div><div class=pull-right><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-192" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/微信图片_20180823114823.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/微信图片_20180823114801.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-191" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/微信图片_20180823114818.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /></div><hr>感谢大家投票并关注我，如果需要用到我的照片，请询问并在文中写明出处。
