我很喜欢小动物，可爱的动物啊，养过小狗，小猫，小鸟。今天说一下我养的鱼吧。
I like small animals, lovely animals, puppies, kittens, little birds. Let me talk about my fish today.![微信图片_20180920125945.jpg](https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmfWv284hnA5GqU81wUVyW5uMbr5t9tqxRQKSzhWqjMG62/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87_20180920125945.jpg)

<hr />

在我小时候大概6岁左右吧，隔壁邻居养了一缸鱼，当时的鱼缸比我还要高，我能清楚的看到里面的小鱼，都是小小条的带着扇子尾巴花花绿绿的小鱼，邻居告诉我说这个是孔雀鱼，因为有着像孔雀一样漂亮的尾巴。
When I was about 6 years old, my neighbors raised fish. The aquarium was taller than I was. I could clearly see the small fish in it, all with fantails and colorful little fish. The neighbors told me this was a peacock fish because it had a beautiful tail like a peacock.
孔雀鱼很美丽，特别是游起来尾巴也跟着摆来摆去。邻居告诉我尾巴长的好看的是雄的，身上颜色单一的是雌鱼。太久远的事情了，当时只记得鱼缸里面的鱼有更小一点的，就知道了原来孔雀鱼会生很多小鱼。
The peacock fish was beautiful, and my neighbors told me that it was a male with a long tail and a female with a single color.
<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-567" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180920130006.jpg" alt="" width="2816" height="2112" /><br/>

<hr />

所以我到后来长大，一直没有忘记，直到出来工作，有了时间，我买来了4条孔雀鱼，雌的孔雀鱼真的是会生小鱼，原来孔雀鱼是胎生鱼，就是不用生卵，生出来的就是小小鱼。当时养的4条鱼，后面生了50多条的小鱼，哈哈。每次换水我都小心翼翼的，经常换完水我就在数，看看会不会少了。
So I grew up and never forgot, until I came out to work, and had time, I bought four peacock fish, peacock fish is a viviparous fish, then raised four fish, after the birth of more than 50 small fish, ha-ha.<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-562" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180920125915.jpg" alt="" width="2816" height="2112" /><br/>

<hr />

一直到现在我还是非常喜欢孔雀鱼的，也好想养一缸孔雀鱼，里面的小鱼生小鱼，会越来越多。这两条是养了大半年了，当时和孩子们一起，他们选的这两条，孔雀鱼生小鱼的时候我没有在家，小鱼被雄鱼咬死了，好伤心的。<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-564" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180920125950.jpg" alt="" width="2816" height="2112" /><br/>

<hr />

原来鱼是那么的难拍照，刚对好焦，它又游走了，平时看网上的图片多好看呢，呵呵，一定要花很多的时间去拍的。
It is very difficult to take pictures of the fish. Just when it is in focus, it swims away. It must take a lot of time to take pictures of the fish.<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-565" src="https://cherryzy.000webhostapp.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/微信图片_20180920125956.jpg" alt="" width="2816" height="2112" /><br/>
