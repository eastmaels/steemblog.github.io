Hello everyone, I would like to recommend this place this week. This shop has been in Shenzhen for many years. It specializes in cooking chicken.
大家好，这周我要推荐这里，这家店在深圳很多年了，一家专门做鸡肉煲的地方！

![e31e80defc99d59fc38c6e66e5389af.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170263/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

When you come to the door of the store, you need to go to the next staircase. The wall of the staircase depicts the history of the store.
来到店门口要下一层楼梯，楼梯的墙上画了这家店铺的历史。

![2b519ca3dad0704d23a521d8907567c.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170266/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

![6a7ff3f52d2487204b4f6de69531ad5.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170265/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

This day we came at noon. We came earlier and there were not many guests.  The environment here is comfortable. I came here with my friends.

![d040d010db0181706cc94a105817335.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170267/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)


![0015b9547026105ad7ad69cdbc6d1d0.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170275/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

![22a283b9c943d03091c7faad6ef22c5.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170277/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

The two of us ordered a pot of chicken and a pot of rice.The two of us ordered a pot of chicken with pot belly and a pot of rice. Soon we had a pot of pepper soup.
这天我们是中午来的，来的比较早，客人还不多。这里环境舒适，我和朋友一起来到这里。 


![d7b49910c63e1109e36d98a2ba9dcec.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170335/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

![fab8db48eefdbcf77e05541c465ad16.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170280/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

The waiter poured all the chicken into the pan, while it was still cooking, we went to mix some sauce.
服务员把鸡肉全部倒了进去锅里，趁还在煮的时候，我们去调了一些酱料。

![41bf9e851ae97fb1402b5dd9205cad4.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170332/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

![77fb46ea6160177b31c4687d755fe6e.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170331/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

The pepper chicken soup here is made into milky white, especially delicious soup.  We drank several bowls.
这里的胡椒鸡汤，煮成奶白色的，特别好喝的汤。我们喝了好几碗。

![89351504fc8309ec4c0c3a4ea10ae06.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170333/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

Here comes our bacon and hot pot rice, which contains sausage, bacon and spareribs.  Cook chu in casserole

我们的腊肉煲仔饭来了，里面有腊肠，腊肉，排骨。煲仔煮出来的饭特别好吃。
![a6d24fb68c6a3e0710350338bdf76ee.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170338/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)


![175e7a1b64de0d6ae30f166d2d5ab10.jpg](https://static.tasteem.io/uploads/image/image/170336/4fa0ae46-c140-4665-b8a3-669ac6c1dda6.jpeg)

There is a discount for the two of us for this meal, which cost us 14 dollars in total.  The price here is well worth it.
我们这一餐还有折扣，一共就花了14美金。在这里的价格是优惠了。
