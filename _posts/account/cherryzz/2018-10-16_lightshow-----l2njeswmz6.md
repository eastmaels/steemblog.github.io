那天去了图书馆准备回家，来到旁边市民中心，这个时候已经傍晚6点多了，发现广场这里很多人，才想起来深圳改革开放40周年，这里晚上有灯光秀，那天假期6点40分加开了第一场，于是我们就在这里等着了。
On the way home from the library that day, we passed through the civic center. It was more than 6:00 p.m. and there were many people in the square. There was a light show in the evening, so we waited here.https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmfDcaXiHjEvyZ7Zzsju3Fr9pPv1onvTco4XXC2s5j3kuE

这里是深圳市民中心，位于深圳市中心区的福田区，可以直接坐地铁到市民中心站，也可以地铁到少年宫站下车。深圳市民中心，是深圳的行政中心，周围有深圳政府，博物馆，深圳会堂等，深圳最具标志性的建筑物。
This is the Shenzhen Citizen Center, located in Futian District of Shenzhen Central District. Take the subway to the Citizen Center Station, or the subway to the Children's Palace Station. Shenzhen Civil Center is the administrative center of Shenzhen, surrounded by the Shenzhen government, museums, Shenzhen Hall, Shenzhen, the most iconic buildings.
https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmTi8FJ3D87r6nRaENPGESesNkrwexpXg4C2YLGqt2Wh3X

我们走上市民中心这个大广场，市民中心的北边是莲花山公园，很多小朋友周末会到这里放风筝，玩耍。
We walked to the square above the People's Center. To the North was the Lotus Hill Park. Many children would fly kites over the weekend.

灯光秀就快开始了，广场的平台上都是人，我们找了旁边人不多的地方，孩子们表示很兴奋。
The lights show is on the way. There are many people in the square.The children are very happy.https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmbKUzVGrmkrGLR1RSiBiWNpLXAGw8c5kHeBQkxvsbuPM6

这场灯光秀，是为了庆祝深圳改革开放40周年，深圳从一开始的小渔村，到成为特区，经过四十年的发展，变成现在的大都市，开始了，响起了音乐：我爱你中国。
The light show is to celebrate the 40th anniversary of Shenzhen's reform and opening up. Shenzhen has changed from a small fishing village to a metropolis after 40 years of development.

<hr />

https://ipfs.busy.org/ipfs/QmYAfXSNhACQRKMvnedTn7qajHenzrVAGEcsqZqQURGtvg

这次灯光秀一共有43栋大楼进行表演，平安大厦为地标，通过智能控制的立体演示。主题是辉煌新时代，第一段山海之城，第二段改革之窗，第三段创新之都，第四段和谐之境。我们一起来看看灯光的效果吧。
