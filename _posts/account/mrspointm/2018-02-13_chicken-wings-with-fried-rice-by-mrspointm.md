#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

Happy Tuesday! Here is my entry for daily food photography contest held by @howtostartablog.
>这是我参加的由 @howtostartablog举办的每日食物摄影比赛的作品。

![IMG_0694.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmaLxfTyUAPyoz6pZLowwJjTG7CZhkhqq8cSPpqa673RQF/IMG_0694.JPG)

This is the first time that I have eaten chicken wings with fried rice, which is more delicious than I thought. Rice wrapped in wings does not seem to be ordinary rice, something the taste of glutinous rice. 
>这是我第一次吃鸡翅包饭，比我想象中更美味。鸡翅里面包的饭好像并不是普通的米饭，有一点像糯米的味道。
