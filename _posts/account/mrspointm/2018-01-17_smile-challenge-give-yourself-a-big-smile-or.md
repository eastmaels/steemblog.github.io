#### Hi, steemit friends!💙
First, thank @herlife for launching this #smilechallenge and nominating me in it. I really appreciate this behavior that can make our community more vividly and colourful. Whatever we meet, we are expected to have a positive attitude. Now, let me pass on!
>首先感谢 @herlife发起的这个微笑挑战活动，并且在活动中提名了我，我觉得这个活动非常棒，不仅在steemit社区，生活中也应该多笑笑，积极乐观面对人生中遇到的一切。

![IMG_7071.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmNqKSSgiuRa3ar1CSry9e6LPfs6tL8Nb2ujZT3CMTFAG7/IMG_7071.JPG)

Every time you smile at someone, it is an action of love, a gift to that person, a beautiful thing.
>每当你对某人微笑，都是爱的礼物，是一种充满美感的事物。


If you have an interest, feel free to join us. Below are the rules.
>如果有兴趣的话，不妨加入我们，以下是挑战规则。

Pick a photo with your best smile in it Nominate your friends to pass your smile, and you can nominate as many as you want Share a post and put the smile of the one who nominated you on the left and put your smile on the right.  Use #smilechallenge as one of the tags in your post 
>选一张你最好笑容的照片
 点名你的朋友传递你的笑容, 点名人数不限 发布一篇帖子，左边放点你人的照片，右边放上你自己的照片 
#smilechallenge 作为其中一个标签
