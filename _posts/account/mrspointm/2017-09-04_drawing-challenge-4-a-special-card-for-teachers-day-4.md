****
It's the 3rd time that I've taken part in Drawing Challenge  Contest held by @helene.  Yesterday, I happened to find a studio called Hua She and experienced an oil painting course.  And I want to take this opportunity to take this painting as the illustration of postcards and give them to my dear colleagues and myself.  All of us are teaching senior 3 students now  and this card can be considered as my gratitude.  Although I haven't won any prize before, yet I'm always enjoying the process.  This time, the theme is  <a href="https://steemit.com/drawingchallenge/@helene/drawing-challenge-winners-3-and-new-theme-4-sbds-giveaway-3">to draw a card for teachers</a> . I choose to draw totoro, which I think is cute. Now I'd like to share my painting with you(^ __ ^).
<blockquote>这是我第三次参加 @helene姐举办的绘画比赛了。昨天在春熙路无意中找到了一家叫花舍的画室，上了一节小小的油画体验课，想要将这幅作品当成贺卡的插图，送给我的可爱的同事们以及我自己，我们都是一群正担任着高三教学工作的园丁，借此机会想要表达一下对他们的感谢。<del>虽然每次都没获奖，</del>但仍然乐在其中✌。这一期主题是<a href="https://steemit.com/drawingchallenge/@helene/drawing-challenge-winners-3-and-new-theme-4-sbds-giveaway-3">给老师的一张贺卡</a>，我选择画龙猫，我觉得龙猫在我心中是很可爱的，现在就给大家展示一下我的作品 (*^__^*) 。</blockquote>

****
#### Basic materials   基本材料
A drawing board, a pencil,  several brushes, various pigments and a color palette.
<blockquote>
一张空白的画板，一支铅笔，不同的画笔刷和各式颜料，调色板。</blockquote>
