#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

Happy Monday! Here is my entry for daily food photography contest held by @howtostartablog.
>这是我参加的由 @howtostartablog举办的每日食物摄影比赛的作品。

![IMG_0660.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmSXfKg2s7KS7BNT4Fuk45LKDc2HZtzNc6bzziTCPS66TR/IMG_0660.JPG)

I like barbecue very much especially streaky pork. What surprises me is that this squid tastes great. In particular, the thinnest part roasted was very dry and sprinkled with spices, which matches streaky pork very well.
>吃烧烤我最喜欢吃的肉类是烤五花肉，但没想到烤鱿鱼的味道也那么棒。尤其是最细的那一段烤得很干，撒上调料，这番美味一点不比烤五花肉差。
