Several days before , I have noticed that @nicolemoker, @krischy and @aaronli were holding some contests about Hallosteem. At the very beginning, I thought it was impossible that I could take part in any one of them, for we seldom celebrate such a festival in here. No wonder I had no chance to join. Today, when I went to Wanda Plaza, I found I was wrong. The whole shopping mall was full of such decorations for Halloween. I couldn't help take out my phone and took photos for them. All photos above are taken by @mrspointm and I hope you will like my post.
>好几天前，我注意到 @nicolemoker, @krischy和 @aaronli在举办Steemit社区万圣节活动。起初，我认为我应该参加不了这样的比赛，因为我们这里很少会举行庆祝万圣节活动。直到今天去万达看电影，发现整个商场都被各种万圣节的小摆件点缀着。于是，就有了以上的几个参赛作品。所有照片都由我 @mrspointm拍摄，未经修图。希望大家会喜欢。😊
