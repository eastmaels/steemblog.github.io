The 3rd Drawing Challenge held by @helene has begun.This time, the theme of this contest is fruit,  and I also want to say that the process counts most. But I still want to win, for that day is just my birthday. Without hesitation, I choose to draw a painting of  strawberries. There are two reasons--- one is that strawberry is definitely my favorite fruit and the other is that every year, I want to buy a strawberry cake to celebrate my birthday  but I am always told that it is not seasonal fruit. So, this strawberry painting is considered as my birthday gift! Now I' d like to share my painting with you(^ __ ^). 
<blockquote> @helene姐举办的绘画挑战赛第三期拉开了序幕，这次主题是水果，还是那句话重在参与✌，<del>不过还是想获奖</del>，因为那天刚好是我的生日: P。这次没有犹豫，直接选择了草莓。原因有两个，一个肯定是我最爱的水果，二是我每年生日想要订草莓蛋糕，却总被告知这不是当季水果，只好作罢。所以，这个草莓画就当是给自己的生日礼物吧！现在就给大家展示一下我的作品 (*^__^*) 。</blockquote>

****
