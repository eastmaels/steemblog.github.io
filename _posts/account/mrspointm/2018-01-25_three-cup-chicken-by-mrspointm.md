#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

![IMG_0064.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmchDxghrKcbXUqxqvPuyVnmc31UrPK9BNsGh6SnVpNf3r/IMG_0064.JPG)

This dish is the most delicious dish I've ordered in this restaurant! San Pei Chicken tastes a bit like coke chicken wings, but also a bit like Leshan's local food sweet-skined duck. Although not spicy, yet with its unique flavor, even the garlic tastes also good. You can refer to my <a href="https://steemit.com/food/@mrspointm/vlog-a-city-a-story-photo-weekly-contest-10-food-in-bellagio-10">previous post</a> to know more.
>这道菜是我在这里点的所有菜中最美味的！三杯鸡的味道有点像可乐鸡翅，又有点像乐山的甜皮鸭，虽然不辣，但具有其独特的香味，就连大蒜也非常美味。
