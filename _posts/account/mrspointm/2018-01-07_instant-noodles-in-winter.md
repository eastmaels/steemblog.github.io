#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

It has been a period of time since I ate instant noodles. This afternoon, I didn't want to go out and asked @mrpointp to buy some for me. I still remember when I was a child, I always wanted to eat instant noodles every day. As I grow older, I seldom eat this junk food. But today is exception. Now, follow me to enjoy this post.
>有好长时间没吃泡面了，今天下午不想出门，提前让 @mrpointp买好泡面在家。记得小时候最向往的事之一，就是能天天吃泡面，然而现在却很少吃这种没太多营养的食物。不管怎样偶尔放纵一次就给大家分享一下。
****
Here are the basic materials for cooking noodles.
>以下就是煮泡面所需的简单材料，三包泡面，一根火腿和两个鸡蛋。当然，还有一包榨菜。

![IMG_6820.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmWWFSzfrCJwJeaEPbb2M8sbivfokkY3NgsTAGXCqQSoay/IMG_6820.JPG)

![SelfieCity_20180106161749_org.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmWE5aor7hUVGMYPTHAo2Z8FDgQjtyQMz51oNK2d8so9f9/SelfieCity_20180106161749_org.jpg)

Heat the water in the pot and then put the instant noodles into boiled water.
>接下来特别简单，烧水待煮开后放入方便面。

![SelfieCity_20180106161734_org.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQma5U2hynPTX1kvRNYNB3jff26neu9AjwzoThjpLmAmwKb/SelfieCity_20180106161734_org.jpg)

Add seasoning to the soup successively. Pay attention to fire temperature to bring out the flavor that you want.
>依次加入佐料，但要注意火候，泡面煮太软了就不太好吃了。

![SelfieCity_20180106161649_org.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmapYSyoXm6Y6f1TnjHoAZphC9Lk3zuGMp8udxRE8iyoXK/SelfieCity_20180106161649_org.jpg)

Mix them with each other. 
>最后加入火腿肠和鸡蛋，并且搅拌均匀即可。

![34ddfda927678813.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmVoJimgXZ3Jp3WphU6FbWvNQR7Hqy1ajd86H7jTqQNeJq/34ddfda927678813.jpg)

Let me enjoy the meal!
>开动啦！:P
