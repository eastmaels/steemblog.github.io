A few days ago, I went to a dessert store called Cun Craft, a popular one on the Internet. I had been for twice but it was always closed. This was the third I'd visited here. Luckily, it was open at that time. Cun Craft is located in the old district of Leshan near the Minjiang River. Because the place is very lively, this store is increasingly popular.

>前些天前往一家乐山网红甜品店，名为村上造物。这是我三次造访这家店中唯一一次开了门的。村上造物坐落于乐山靠近岷江的老城区，也正是因为这里十分热闹，它的人气也慢慢变高。

In fact, its style doesn't match the surroundings, for most buildings there are old teahouses or feature local snacks. The overall style of this store is fresh, mainly consisting of white. Colors, tables and chairs are frame-like shapes with simple decorations.

>其实这家甜品店的风格和周围的环境有些不搭配，因为那边大多数都是老旧的茶馆和富有城市特色的小吃店。而村上造物的整体风格是清新的，素雅的，白为主色调，桌椅属于框架式的形状，装潢也十分简单。

<hr />
<p><center><strong>Pumpkin cake 南瓜小方</strong></center></p>
<img class="alignnone wp-image-74 size-full" src="http://mrspointm.vornix.blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/w_38b539f16d7c11e8bc2d066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="2400" height="1800" /><br/>

This is a very special dessert made of vegetables. When I first tasted it, I thought it was the egg yolk, Instead, it was indeed the taste of the pumpkin. Compared to other desserts, it's sweet but not greasy.

>南瓜小方是一道很特别的甜品，因为它是由蔬菜制作而成。尝到的第一口时，我还以为是蛋黄的味道，但细细一品，确实是南瓜的味道。相对于其他甜品来说，这是一道甜而不腻的甜品。

<center><strong>Daifuku 雪胖子 </strong></center></p>
<img class="alignnone wp-image-75 size-full" src="http://mrspointm.vornix.blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/w_59c86eb56d7c11e8bc2d066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="2400" height="1800" /><br/>

Daifuku grows out of Japan, formerly known as "dà fú", is one of Japan's local snacks. This contains three kinds of flavors——Durian, Oreo and Mango. The outer ice skin is filled with cream. It's awesome!
>雪媚娘源自日本，原名“大福”，是日本的地道点心之一。我们点的雪媚娘有三种口味，分别是榴莲，奥利奥和芒果，外面的冰皮搭配着奶油馅，简直太赞了！

<center> <strong>Strawberry yoghurt 草莓酸奶杯</strong></center>
<img class="alignnone wp-image-76 size-full" src="http://mrspointm.vornix.blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/w_3258801a6d7d11e8bc2d066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="2400" height="1800" /><br/>

This drink really left me a deep impression, for I like both yogurt and strawberry. When I saw this one in the menu,  I ordered it with hesitation. Isn't it gorgeous?
>这杯饮品是我此次探店中最喜爱的收获，因为我既喜欢酸奶又喜欢草莓，所以我毫不犹豫地点了这杯饮品。有没有觉得颜值很高？

<center><strong> Grapefruit soda 西柚苏打 </strong></center></p>
<img class="alignnone wp-image-77 size-full" src="http://mrspointm.vornix.blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/w_231cee116d7e11e8bc2d066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="2400" height="1800" /><br/>

It's necessary to order a cup of sour drink when enjoying desserts. So sweet does it taste with each bite. No matter how delicious the desserts, you will feel bored. Take a sip, you'll feel it fresh. 
>吃甜品的时候点这么一杯酸酸的饮品是很有必要的，每一口都是甜食，不论再美味的甜品吃太多都会觉得闷，喝几口苏打水，胃口又再次清爽了。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-78 size-full" src="http://mrspointm.vornix.blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/w_04d235a56d7f11e8bc2d066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="4092" height="3072" /><br/>

In such era when there exist many popular stores on the Interent, Cun Craft is worthy of its name. If you visit Leshan, this store can be included in your list.
>在如今这个所谓网红店众多的时代，村上造物算是名副其实了。如果朋友们来乐山旅游，想找一家舒适味道又好的甜品店，村上造物可以纳入选项。
