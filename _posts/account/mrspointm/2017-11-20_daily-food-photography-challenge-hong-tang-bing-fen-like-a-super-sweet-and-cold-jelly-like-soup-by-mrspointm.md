Theme: Daily Food

Location: Chengdu, China

Camera used: OPPO R11

Hong tang bing fen is like a super sweet and cold jelly with soup. It's my favourite dessert which can relieve summer-heat. Often in the evening, @mrpointp and I go out to buy a bowl of it. There are various flavors, such as roses, pineapple and brown sugar etc.
> 每到夏天，冰粉是我超爱吃的消暑美食。我们经常会在傍晚去吃一碗，有各种口味，玫瑰，菠萝，红糖等。
