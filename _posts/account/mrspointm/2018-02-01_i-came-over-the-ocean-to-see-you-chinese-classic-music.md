      <hr>This is the first time that I've shared music on DSound. The song <i>I came over the ocean to see you</i> is written and sung by the famous Taiwanese musician Jonathan Lee, but I really liked this song  when I watched a TV show called<i> the Voice of China</i>. A girl named Mingxiang, Liu used her sweety voice to perform this song perfectly, which left me a deep impression. Later, the eponymous drama of this song was launched in 2017. Now, You may know how popular this song is.

这是我第一次在DSound平台上第一次分享音乐。这首《漂洋过海来看你》是台湾著名音乐人李宗盛作词并演唱的歌曲，但我真正听见并喜欢上这首歌是在看了节目《中国好声音》的某一期歌手刘明湘在舞台上演唱后，给我留下了十分深刻的印象。再后来，这首歌的同名电视剧也于2017年上线了。
