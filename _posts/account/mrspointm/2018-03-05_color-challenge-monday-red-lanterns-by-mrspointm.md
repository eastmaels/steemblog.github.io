#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

The Lantern Festival has passed several days before.  There are various activities on that day, such as stealing green vegetables(偷青) and guessing riddles. Of course, during the festival, red lanterns can be found everywhere. In the eyes of Chinese people, the red lanterns symbolize family reunion, prosperous business.
