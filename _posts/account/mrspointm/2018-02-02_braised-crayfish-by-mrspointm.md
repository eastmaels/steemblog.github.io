#### Hi, steemit friends!💙
Compared with spicy crawfish, braised crayfish is more suitable for all types of people to enjoy. After all, it’s not so hot. More importantly, the deliciousness of braised crayfish is the same as spicy one and the former is more memorable !
>卤煮小龙虾相比麻辣小龙虾来讲更适合各类人群享用，毕竟辣味没有那么重，更重要的是卤煮小龙虾的美味程度一点也不亚于麻辣小龙虾，并且前者更加回味无穷！
