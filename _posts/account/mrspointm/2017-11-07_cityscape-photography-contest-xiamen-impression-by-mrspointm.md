Theme: Cityscape Photography

Location: Xiamen, China

Camera used: iPhone 6

Xiamen, also called Egret Island, is the provincial city of Fujian Province. It is an important city, port and a tourist city in China. Xiamen has left me the impression of sunshine and beaches. The coastline is surrounded by high-rise buildings. This is a city with the combination of gorgeous scenery and modernization.
>厦门，别称鹭岛，是福建省的省辖市，是中国重要的中心城市、港口、风景旅游城市。厦门带给我的印象一直都是阳光沙滩，高楼大厦围绕着海岸线，是一个集海滨美景与现代化的城市。
