#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

Merry Christmas to all of you. Here are my entries for this contest hosted by @aaronli. This photo was taken in Taikoo Li, a new landmark of Chengdu. Since it was built, it has been a popular gathering place for people. 
>祝大家圣诞快乐，这是我参加由 @aaronli举办的城市故事比赛的作品，这一周的主题是圣诞。这张照片是在成都远洋太古里拍摄的，这个地方可以称作成都的新地标。自从它建成以来，就成为了游客和市民的休闲购物聚集地。

![IMG_1862.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmaMFi2HfisctcmGBnt8kVv5JJs9szcjhBYnYM9wrTXQTC/IMG_1862.JPG)

These decorations for Christmas are so shining and gorgeous. From the photo, you can see there is a stage in the center where there will be a performance later. 
>这些圣诞装饰物在夜晚看着闪闪的，很有气氛。照片中的舞台是有庆祝圣诞的活动表演。

![IMG_1865.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmZ9BzBZ5XWAo3Yj2QxCijP2hQsZfPREB8YxK7T3n42cbm/IMG_1865.JPG)

Theme: Christmas

Location: Chengdu, Sichuan province 

Camera used: iPhone 6

This is on the second floor and every other two meters you can see this Christmas tree. Is it cute？
>这是在太古里的二楼，每走两米就能看到同样的圣诞树，有没有很可爱呀？
