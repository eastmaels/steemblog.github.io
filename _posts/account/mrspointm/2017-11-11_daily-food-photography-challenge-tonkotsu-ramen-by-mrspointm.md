Theme: Daily Food

Location: Chengdu, China

Camera used: OPPO R11

Tonkotsu Ramen is the specialty of the restaurant and noodles in this bowl taste greater than I have imagined. Do you want to have a taste? Inside are a piece of seaweed, bamboo shoots, fungus, a soft-boiled egg and roast pork. The most delicious is the soup.
>豚骨拉面是这家店的招牌啦，这碗面比想象中的更色香味俱全，有没有觉得看起来很想吃的感觉？里面加入了一片海苔，笋子，木耳丝，溏心蛋，叉烧肉，最美味的是面汤，有种特殊的浓香味。
