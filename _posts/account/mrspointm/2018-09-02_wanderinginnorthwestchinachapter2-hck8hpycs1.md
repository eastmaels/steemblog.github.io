<strong>Heima River</strong>

The next morning, we were awakened by the owner of the B&amp;B at around 6 to watch the sunrise. The location of this B&amp;B is quite good, which is in the center of the grassland and close to the river. As can be seen from the photo, it is a great place to enjoy the sunrise. This was the first time we had seen the sunrise and witnessed this fantastic moment. We were really shocked by this natural landscape.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmSgm1u9Uvk6EGZXu8xRdUbQ4djprg3rgmVdxcU8FBUhj1/IMG_9130.JPG" alt="IMG_9130.JPG" /><br/>

The rising sun was not glaring. Instead,  it touched the entire grassland with its warm light, and drove away coldness. After that, we hurried to leave for Chaka Salt Lake, around 100 kilometers away. It was a new day. We all were looking forward.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmewVKA52miH2iBdY92mqSevwV7gnjgBMQrt58T91Kkt8X/IMG_8086.JPG" alt="IMG_8086.JPG" /><br/>

Looking out of the car window, we could see the mountains and sometimes there were hundreds of sheep grazing on the hillside leisurely. Without herdsmen accompanying them, this place was their paradise. Soon, we arrived at the popular tourist attractionâ€”Chaka Salt Lake.

<hr />

<strong>Chaka Salt Lake</strong>

The Chaka Salt Lake is located between two mountains. The mountains are covered with snow all the year round. The snow-capped mountains are reflected on the surface of the lake, forming a unique natural view of the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau where the water and the sky meet.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQKsymR3ZNk59QTFidFqLjqbwDMLRzi9bKFLfcr5MsiuQ/IMG_8203~1.jpg" alt="IMG_8203~1.jpg" /><br/>

When we came to the salt area, we saw many salt sculptures. This salt sculpture in the photo is named <b>_The Legend Genghis Khan_</b>, 50m long, 30m wide and 8m high, using 4,800t salt. The sculpture is spectacular, representing the nobility and aggressiveness of the legend Genghis Khan; with innovative and unique artistic methods, it displays the marvelous spectacle, perfectly integrates the salt and sculpture art.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmUE36HrZotCxmGf6nomzrgsNxhPjTKHNM7C5xaxoKMzrb/IMG_8110.JPG" alt="IMG_8110.JPG" /><br/>

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQma12RRjF6Vh3GQHh51waY6T7DBifcshJpHoKF6FdwAkEC/IMG_8108.JPG" alt="IMG_8108.JPG" /><br/>

Along the way, we stepped on the road covered with salt crystals as if it were snow. The Chaka Salt Lake was really gorgeous. The endless lake, blue sky and white clouds combined to compose such beautiful scenery. But there were too many tourists, and even to take photos was difficult.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQkiUA58snkG5dEMpGNWmYFTfocHJKrnAgzjfTUJMcAjB/IMG_8366.JPG" alt="IMG_8366.JPG" /><br/>

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmYCg1bEaS7SjpyTxpyW2uRdsuLaNuFmGF8F2T1rvXH2yg/IMG_8202.JPG" alt="IMG_8202.JPG" /><br/>

It is worth mentioning that there is a lively shopping street in the exit of Chaka Salt Lake Scenic Area. When tourists are tired, they can rest here, eating food or buying special products.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmW2YrwfbAAq2QUCWPeWmLUc9Yx35QvDDZsYYGYXquyd7n/IMG_8204.JPG" alt="IMG_8204.JPG" /><br/>

Our next stop was to Feicui Lake in Da Qaidam, which was also a salt lake. Compared with the lively Chaka Salt Lake, it was totally different.

<hr />

<strong>Fuicui Lake</strong>

There were fewer tourists visiting here, for this Lake was relatively remote. As for me, it was more beautiful than Chaka Salt Lake.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmUowLtBMFQGXZ5VQnvj1Nu4zjrCqyRk8Bkb3dwCuPJnPV/IMG_8229.JPG" alt="IMG_8229.JPG" /><br/>

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmRtiXeqMam5LCdGHSfPuDx3VdLffpY6jVNnBPN2oTJAuK/IMG_8257.JPG" alt="IMG_8257.JPG" /><br/>

The water in Feicui Lake is very clear, just like the shallow sea water. The most important is that the salt lake is not surrounded by any modern elements, as if we came to an unexploited land. This is completely a magnifcent and mysterious world of nature.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmeFL6S4yt3VH9eA4XYJVxkyqgKpAqKCg2XKm4t8mN7GLQ/IMG_8233.JPG" alt="IMG_8233.JPG" /><br/>
