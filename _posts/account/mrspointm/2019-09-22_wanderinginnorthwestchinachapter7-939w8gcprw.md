<strong>Hi, steemit friends!</strong>💙

Colorful Danxia scenic spot covers a large area with three entrances, exits, and four stands in all. There are a great many attractions, such as Colorful Screen, Colorful Clouds, Colorful Mountain Range etc. Each scenic spot has its own unique characteristics. If you have enough time, you’re expected to enjoy the scenery one by one.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20758" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/3-7.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

It is said that Zhangye Dan Xia appears to be much more gorgeous with the sun shining. Unfortunately we didn’t see the most beautiful Danxia scenery for it was cloudy that day.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20759" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/1-9.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20760" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/4-7.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

We are still impressed with the colors here. Different colors can be found in a mountain, some white, some yellow, some brown and some black as if they were arranged in order. This have been described in a poem. I can imagine how fascinating it would be if it were in the sunshine.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20761" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/5-5.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

Danxia is known for its natural beauty. Every mountain is carved in different shapes by nature. They look like humans, worms, birds or beasts and the most famous one is Sleeping Beauty.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20763" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/2-6.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20762" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/6-5.jpg" alt="" width="3024" height="4032" /><br/>

If you have the opportunity to visit here, be sure to carefully appreciate these grand mountains and rocks, and also appreciate the wonders after long-term weathering. It’s a gift from nature.

I hope that netizens won’t overstate any attractions, which may result in people’s high expectations, although it does attract a large number of tourists. When seeing the real scenery, they may feel disappointed. Instead, they will miss more.

The End

<img class="alignnone wp-image-20312" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/JvFFVmatwWHRfvmtd53nmEJ94xpKydwmbSC5H5svBACH81iYAG71fXtarmD5Ar64GWrvvcLDw7bqL1v6hN4QQ2UmVE6LZxorreiMYoVQWjQjGDJhT48po2RcaCXiYhyDFi3Eb4M9eN.png" alt="" width="976" height="61" /><br/>

七彩丹霞景区面积之大，有三个进出口，共四个看台，景点内又有许多大小景点，如七彩屏、七彩飞霞、七彩嫁衣等，每个小景点各有其特色，若是时间充裕，大可以逐个细细观赏。

据说张掖丹霞一定要在阳光的照耀下才显得出绚丽本色，可惜那天我们正是遇见阴天，并未能看见最美的丹霞景致。

我们依然感叹丹霞地貌的色彩之神奇，同一座山体，有的白、有的黄、有的褐、有的黑，可谓是色彩分
明、鳞次栉比，诗云：“色若渥丹，灿若明霞”，可以想象若是在阳光的照耀下，该是一番多么梦幻的景象。

丹霞之美源于自然，每一处山石都是出自大自然的精心雕刻，以形态各异、变化万千来形容丹霞再形象不过，似人、似虫、似鸟、似兽，最著名的莫过于丹霞睡美人。

倘若各位有机会来到这丹霞地貌，一定要仔细地欣赏这些林立的奇山怪石，用心体会这岁月和烈日风雨打磨后的奇观，这是大自然赠予我们的礼物。

更希望网上不要再肆意炒作任何一个景点，尽管一时间吸引许多游客，但人们必定心存过高期待，就会对真实的景色感到失望，于是便错过更多的美妙。

（完结）
