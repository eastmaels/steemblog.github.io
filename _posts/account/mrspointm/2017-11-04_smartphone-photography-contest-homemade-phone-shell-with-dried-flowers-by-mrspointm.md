Theme: Smartphone Photography

Location: Yunnan, China

Camera used: iPhone 6

This photo is taken outside the railway station in Yunan Province. The phone shell in the photo is made by me. I made it by using dried flowers. I think the color of the flowers really match the blue sky. That's the reason why I take this.
>这张照片是在云南的一个火车站拍下的，手机壳是我用干花自制的。当时无意发现蓝天和手机壳的颜色很搭，就拍下了这一幕。
