Going further, we came to the legendary Crescent Spring, which indeed looks like a standard crescent shape. The lake is a veritable desert wonder. In this barren place, plants and water are symbols of life.

Staying here for around an hour, I always felt that my mouth dried out. Thus we chose to take the sightseeing car back. 

In addition, you can experience taking the glider or helicopter here to enjoy the panorama of the whole desert. Of course, this is for-pay service.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20316" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/w_3e3520a9d13011e8b135066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

It's time for dinner. As for me, nothing can be compared with delicious food. That afternoon, I had a sound sleep in the hotel and then went to the near local night market, which was really lively here, and almost every restaurant placed tables and chairs outside.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20317" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/w_dc8ef71dd13911e8b135066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20318" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/w_e1af2ac5d13911e8b135066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="4032" height="3024" /><br/>

Although crowded and noisy, but everything was in order. When you visit northwest China, you can't miss the skewers here.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20319" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/w_1081f265d13811e8b135066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="3072" height="2304" /><br/>

It is said that the skewers in Dunhuang are slightly inferior to those in Xinjiang, but I still think it is very delicious. If I have a chance, I will definitely go to Xinjiang to experience the traditional skewers in the future.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20320" src="http://cnstm.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/2019/09/w_d5f6bc42d13911e8b135066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="" width="4092" height="3072" /><br/>

This local drink named Da Yao Jia Bin is really amazing. Looking like beer, it is a kind of drink whose taste is sour and sweet. The iced drink with skewers can be a perfect match.

It was very late when we returned to the hotel. The experience of this day was very wonderful, making me pleased. 

Even before I went to bed, I thought of the dinner we had had. With such satisfcation, we and we went to sleep.

再往里走，就是传说中的月牙泉，它的确是个标准的月牙状，不得不说月牙泉真是名副其实的沙漠奇观，在这寸草不生的沙里，植物和水源是生命的象征。

游玩鸣沙山的这一个多小时，最大的体会就是口干舌燥，所以我们返程选择搭乘游览车。另外，在鸣沙山可以体验滑翔机和直升飞机，从天空观察整片沙漠的感觉一定是不一样的，但价格可以想象。

没有任何事物能抵过美食对我们的吸引，下午在旅店好好睡了一觉，空着肚子去敦煌夜市觅食。这里真的非常热闹，每家餐馆都会在门外摆放桌椅，虽然拥挤嘈杂，但一切都井然有序。到了祖国的大西北，一定要尝尝这边的烤串。据说敦煌的烧烤略逊色于新疆那边的，不过我觉得已经很好吃了，以后有机会一定去新疆体验一下当地的烤羊肉串。

算是个小惊喜吧，非常喜欢当地的一款饮品，大窑嘉宾。样子像啤酒，其实就是纯粹的饮料，味道酸酸甜甜，冰镇大窑嘉宾搭配烧烤真是绝了。

回到旅店的时候已经很晚了，这一天的体验也是相当精彩，就连睡觉前还想着刚刚的烧烤，回味无穷，我们带着这份满足感进入梦乡。
