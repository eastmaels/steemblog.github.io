****
Yesterday, I noticed that the 2nd Drawing Challenge held by @helene had begun. But I missed the last contest.  This time, I want to have a try. <del>Aimed at the first three places, </del>the process counts most. This is the theme---flowers, and the red rose comes to my mind first.  For one thing, my favorite flower is the red rose. For another,  the red color makes my life full of vitality. I am not good at painting, previously studied watercolor in the Children's Palace for only 2 years, and now I' d like to share my painting with you(^ __ ^).
<blockquote>昨天看到 @helene姐举办的<a href="https://steemit.com/drawingchallenge/@helene/drawing-challenge-2-will-now-begin-welcome-to-join-this-challenge-and-win-the-prize">绘画挑战赛</a>第二期，上次没赶上，这次想来试试，<del>冲着前三去的，</del>重在参与✌。这一期的主题是花，我立马想到的是红玫瑰，一是因为我最喜欢的花当属玫瑰，二是火红的颜色让我的生活充满灿烂的气息。画画不是我的特长，以前在少年宫学过两年的水彩画，现在就给大家展示一下我的作品 (*^__^*) 。</blockquote>

****
#### Materials   准备材料
Two gel ink pens (0.5mm, one is black and the other is red) &  a blank piece of paper
<blockquote>
两支0.5mm中性笔（一黑一红）、一张空白的纸。</blockquote>

****
#### The Process 过程图

Step1   
Draw a rough outline of the figure, starting from the petals.  PS:Using a pencil to draw this outline is a better choice and you can modify it at any time.
<blockquote>用笔画出大概的轮廓，从花瓣开始。（注：用铅笔画草图是更完美，还可以随时修改。）</blockquote>

Step 2  Draw the leaves and stems of flowers.
<blockquote>画出花的叶子和茎。</blockquote>

Step 3 Widen the outline, which makes it more layered.
<blockquote>把轮廓描粗，这样看上去更有层次感。</blockquote>

****
Step 4 Color the petals of the rose back and forth with a red pen, and then this painting may look more gorgeous. This technique was taught by my teacher when in the Children's Palace.
<blockquote>给玫瑰花着色，用红笔沿着一个方向来回涂抹，这样效果更好。（以前学画画的时候，老师教的。）</blockquote>

<blockquote>感谢 @helene 姐的这个比赛，让又重拾了以前小时候的兴趣，这次画得太草率了，工具也不全。大家可能注意到了我的玫瑰花的叶子没有颜色，我想暂且留着，就像断臂的维纳斯一样，不完美的完美。</blockquote>
