#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

Now, I'm taking  part in Drawing Challenge Contest held by @helene. This time, the theme is to draw a picture of our own hands ( either left or right hand can be accepted). It sounds easy but if you want to draw it well, you need to work hard, especially for me, a beginner in painting. Let's take a look!
<blockquote>我正在参加由 @helene姐举办的第五期绘画挑战赛。这次的主题是画自己的双手，当然也可以画左手或右手。听起来感觉挺容易的，但如果想要画得很好，就得下大功夫了。尤其是对我，一个绘画初学者更是难上加难了。在这里只有献丑了。一起来看看我的作品吧！</blockquote>

#### Basic materials 基本材料
****
![DQmX5Y54ungQNPc24BxaeDKvBerzQv35FhevoA8xjYFGqQg.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcMtoL3nNXVM22dfkFJXfKVTMirCkenQrj5krudbJ6Z5v/DQmX5Y54ungQNPc24BxaeDKvBerzQv35FhevoA8xjYFGqQg.jpg)

A book, colored pencils and a sketch book
<blockquote>一本绘画书，彩铅笔和速写本</blockquote>

#### The Process 过程图
****
![IMG_4145.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmbKGi8C2x3p4eQTZtJAu2Uw43ChcrTGzSE34PqHCXBN4Y/IMG_4145.JPG)

Step 1 
This time I follow the steps as the book says,  starting from the large area of ​​the palm of my hand, which is roughly a square composed of an arc. And then add a wrist, the root of the thumb and the small oval used to connect fingers.
<blockquote>这次我按照绘画书上的步骤来进行的，先从手掌的大面积开始画，大致是一个弧线构成的方块。然后加上了手腕、大拇指指根以及连接指头的小椭圆形。</blockquote>

****
![IMG_4149.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmc6rffrRwnRaucKYtau32N22A9UKEqrE5HWsu9SjHMgNV/IMG_4149.JPG)

Step 2 
Try to make the general outline bold.
<blockquote>把大体的轮廓加粗勾勒。</blockquote>

****
![IMG_4146.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQme1eqBHVXCRvFaxnm3VRnev9DBPeFWC4UWgNprYcvzncy/IMG_4146.JPG)

Step 3
When modifying the hand of the skeleton, you can draw some rugae  on the joints, as well as the addition of nails.
<blockquote>在替手的骨架「加料」时，可以在指节处画上皱褶，还有加指甲。</blockquote>

****
![IMG_4148.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmPN3GBvfDSpgrLXSbBTe3KFyVkhiESJ8QSBu4iADofN96/IMG_4148.JPG)

Step 4
Add some rugae to the back of the hand, but it seems an old man's hand, lol...
<blockquote>完善手背的褶皱，似乎看起来更像是老人的手呢，♪(^∇^*)</blockquote>

#### Before & After 看看对比图吧！
![IMG_4153.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmTCTmUcquWgpgE9b1hQE3TFNuXmDvuxHtDdEfKxNYioQR/IMG_4153.JPG)

Although the painting is not so good, but I enjoyed the process. Painting is not a burden for me. Instead, it is a way to relax. Drawing a picture every week makes me slow down to observe the surroundings. The life is what I’m expecting.
<blockquote>尽管画得不好，但享受了过程。画画于我而言并不是什么负担，更多的是一种放松的方式。 每周画一幅画让自己的心静下来，慢下来观察周围，这样的生活亦是我所期待的。</blockquote>
