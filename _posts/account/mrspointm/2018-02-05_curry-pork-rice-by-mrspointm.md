#### Hi, steemit friends!💙
Curry pork rice is regarded as the welfare for the friends who like eating meat. For me, what the most attractive to me is not pork chop, but curry. Curry sauce is poured on the rice, which tastes great. Besides, the pork chop in this restaurant is also very delicious.
>咖喱猪排饭是算得上是喜欢吃肉的朋友的福利，对我来说，最吸引我的不是猪排，而是咖喱味。咖喱汁浇在米饭上，味道特别棒，不过这家餐厅的猪排也做得非常美味。
