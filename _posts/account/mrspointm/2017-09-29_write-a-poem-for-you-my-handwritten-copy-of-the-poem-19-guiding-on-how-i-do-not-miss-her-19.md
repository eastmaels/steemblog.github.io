#### Hi, steemit friends!

Long time no see! It has been a month since I shared poems. Today, I want to share with you a poem <i>Guiding on How I Do Not Miss Her</i>, written by Liu Bannong. The original text is as follows:
我的手抄诗歌有一个月的时间没有跟大家见面了，有没有想我啊？今天想要和大家分享的一首诗，叫《教我如何不想她》，作者刘半农。原文如下：

<b>教我如何不想她</b>
天上飘着些微云，
地上吹着些微风。
啊！
微风吹动了我头发，
教我如何不想她？

月光恋爱着海洋，
海洋恋爱着月光。
啊！
这般蜜也似的银夜，
教我如何不想她？

水面落花慢慢流，
水底鱼儿慢慢游。
啊！
燕子你说些什么话？
教我如何不想她？

枯树在冷风里摇。
野火在暮色中烧。
啊！
西天还有些儿残霞，
教我如何不想她？
****
<b><code>Translation version 译文</code></b>

<b>Guiding on How I Do Not Miss Her</b>
Some thin cloud floating in the sky and,
Some breeze blowing on the ground.
Oh!
Breeze blowing my hair,
Guiding on how I do not want her?

Moonlight loving the sea here,
Ocean and moonlight.
Oh!
So honey also like - a silver night,
Guiding on how I do not want her?

Flowers falling on the water flow in slowly,
Underwater fish swimming slowly.
Oh!
Swallow you what to say here?
Guiding on how I do not want her?

Withered tree shaking in the cold wind,
Wildfires burn in the twilight.
Oh!
Some children remaining western sky afterglow,
Guiding on how I don't miss her?
****
The following is part of my handwritten copy for this poem.
以下是我的手抄本（部分）。
![IMG_4489.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmXXir1PCbmwNG4SQF7imTX97SYwxmvg2Cn6cMaRb6F5QD/IMG_4489.JPG)
In fact, most of the poems are written to express the authors' emotions or feelings, such as being disappointed by officialdom, missing his/her lover, and missing the motherland etc. This poem is no exception, as can be seen from  the last paragraph of the poem——<b> Withered tree shaking in the cold wind, wildfires burn in the twilight. Oh! Some children remaining western sky afterglow, guiding on how I don't miss her?</b> There is a complete contrast between the cold wind and the rest of  the sunset glow, which shows the author's loneliness and loss.  At the same time, we can read he has motherland in mind.  Have you ever noticed that  each part of the poem includes the words ——<b>guiding on how I don't miss her</b>, which repeatedly deepen the emotion and reflect the title.
>其实大部分的作者都是借诗来表达自己的情感，有官场失意的，有表达对爱人的思念的，也有对祖国母亲的想念的。这首诗也不例外，诗中最后一段“枯树在冷风中摇动，野火在暮色中烧。啊！西天还有些儿残霞，教我如何不想她？” 这冷风和天边出现的残霞形成强烈的对比，映衬出诗人远在异乡的孤独失落感和对祖国的思念之情。尤其是暮色出现的残霞，便是最好的寄托。同时，诗歌的每一段都以教我如何不想她结尾，照应标题，也在反复加深情感。
