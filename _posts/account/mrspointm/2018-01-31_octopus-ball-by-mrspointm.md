#### Hi, steemit friends!💙
This is one of my favorite snacks, and every time I go to a Japaneses restaurant, I always order it. Octopus ball, also known as tokoyaki, originated in Japan and mainly consists of octopus ingredients, octopus burning powder, seaweed and octopus sauce, etc. (Actually, the way to make it varies from restaurant to restaurant).
>章鱼小丸子是我最喜爱的小吃之一，也几乎是我去寿司店必点的一道美食。章鱼小丸子又名章鱼烧，起源于日本，成份主要是章鱼，章鱼烧粉，海苔，章鱼烧酱等（不过每家餐厅的做法稍有不同）。
