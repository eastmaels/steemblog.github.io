Since the new semester began, I've been busy with my work. Seldom do I have time to see films in the cinema. It was not until the video platform iQiyi uploaded <b>_The Island_</b> that I had a chance to enjoy it.

Compared with other similar films or TV plays, this is a less bloody film on how to survive on a deserted island. The most terrible part of the whole play is the mass brawl. During the hundreds of days of fighting survival on this island, no one died because of the struggle for interests. It shows this scale is well grasped. At this scale, <b>_The Island_</b> can still stimulate my reflection.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmNXsBp5qK9g9VNASCTJ5EtHRZm4tkgPtY9TExhzXyhHen/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87_20180924211911.jpg" alt="å¾®ä¿¡å›¾ç‰‡_20180924211911.jpg" /><br/>

A group of people in different positions work in the same company. One day, a huge wave brought them to an uninhabited island, where all of their positions would be cleared. Thus, the nature of human being was exposed at this time. The film is well mastered in character characterization. People in different positions all exposed their selfish humanities at different moments.

For example, the security guard Zhao Tianlong was originally honest,  and even didn't know what the word "league building" meant, but he was the first person to break out. People who would flatter the leader also revealed their characters.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmXEpj8XFXt3L6u4x9dfRwAdgTB7TmS5zudZRb8FPLtPtf/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87_20180923211520.jpg" alt="å¾®ä¿¡å›¾ç‰‡_20180923211520.jpg" /><br/>

I have to say that the starring Huang Bo is the highlight of the whole play. The hero Ma Jin(played by Huang Bo) knew that he had won a 60-million-prize in a lottery on the first day of this desert island, and that he was required to cash the winning lottery ticket in 90 days made him crazy.

Compared with his life, to own 60 million counted more. Approaching the deadline, Ma Jin almost broke down and cried. Dramatically, at this time, a heavy rain brought countless marine fish to Ma Jin and Xiao Xing (played by Zhang Yixing). Ma Jin said to himself, "This is absolutely a reward for me."

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmPMoDkxkKcdWSrxNZjzwnexLoEB3fuLjYVM92cidPNH9T/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87_20180923211528.jpg" alt="å¾®ä¿¡å›¾ç‰‡_20180923211528.jpg" /><br/>

On this deserted island, whoever owns food is the King. What this film expresses most is the reality and horrible humanity. Most of the 30 people on this island were mediocre. In times of distress, there was only one thing in their mindâ€”to live on.

Without morality and bottom line, if anyone was egged on, they would do whatever they were asked to do. At that moment, I realized that most people in this world are like this, perhaps including you and me, because we are used to disguising ourselves in such a materially rich world.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmXywdgcQBP7YopxjNtpgPwwNaa1W6D5KxrNir7kbeQizt/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87_20180923211536.jpg" alt="å¾®ä¿¡å›¾ç‰‡_20180923211536.jpg" /><br/>

The so-called honest Xiao Xing also became a bad guy. Because of his powerâ€”the ability to control others, he began to become so crazy that later on he destroyed othersâ€™ hope of returning home for the sake of his own interests. Someone thinks that Xiao Xing is actually the gloomy side of Ma Jin, but I think this is everyone's gloomy side. Ma Jinâ€™s kindness was more than desire. The ending of the film is almost perfect. Thirty people have returned to their homes, safe and sound. I believe this ending totally caters to the expectations of most audiences.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmaZyKtF7S2T9aA2ReKpUoNtSwGZCtEVJszX3cMneBBsnz/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87_20180923211546.jpg" alt="å¾®ä¿¡å›¾ç‰‡_20180923211546.jpg" /><br/>

However, from the perspective of the authenticity of the film, there still exist some flaws in it. It reminds me of the movie named <b>_Cast Away_</b> starring Tom Hanks in 2000, which does better in dealing with details. On this abandoned  island, it is hard to find food.

The chronically underfed won't be gentle and friendly. The most important thing is that in that case there must be some people who get sick. Another American play , <b>_Lost_</b>,  tells of dozens of people struggling to live on a small island. The details are also handled very well. Of course, TV plays and movies are incomparable.

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmNU2dNziDnq7UBc1ZNck8KEpFVtMVE8rjfCCVAD3X64nK/%E5%BE%AE%E4%BF%A1%E5%9B%BE%E7%89%87_20180923211552.jpg" alt="å¾®ä¿¡å›¾ç‰‡_20180923211552.jpg" /><br/>

To be frank, <b>_The Island_</b> is a wonderful domestic movie that I have seen in recent years, for its success doesn't lie in nostalgia. If you have an interest, you may as well see this film.
