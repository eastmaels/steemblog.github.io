#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

Today is the last Sunday of 2017. I want to record this special day. As can be seen from the title, the exam is not about me but about my senior three students, for it's the final exam in 2017. It won't be half a year before they graduate.
>今天是2017年的最后一个星期天，所以我想记录下这特别的一天。如题，所谓的“考”并不是我的考试，而是学生们的考试，这是2017年最后一次年级统考。距离他们毕业仅有不到半年的时间了。

![IMG_6656.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmNeZA35qoqPqu2a6yxPJHd6vHrpnUyKqnRw4VJ7viH1Ms/IMG_6656.JPG)

This New Year's Day, all senior three students and teachers only have one day and a half off. Though short, I'm still happy to have a rest. 
>学校全体高三学生以及教师才开始元旦假期，尽管只有短短的一天半，我仍然很开心能在繁忙的工作中好好地休息一天，至少能睡个懒觉，哈哈。

![IMG_6659.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmTFCqf5k3SKLPmhibjMv5YQQ4PNs12P8AjmRJXVx5W5EJ/IMG_6659.JPG)

After lunch, @mrpointp and I drove home. It was a sunny afternoon.  By the way, I have marked all the test papers. 
>其实挺开心，吃了中午饭就和 @mrpointp开车回了家，天公作美，一路阳光明媚。顺便把刚刚考完的试卷改了，我是不是很敬业呀？♪(^∇^*)

Tomorrow will be the first day of 2018. I wonder if you are watching cross-year party on TV, accompanied by your family. Hope you are happy and blessed! Happy New Year!
>明天就是2018年的第一天了，不知道各位此时此刻是否候在电视剧前看着跨年晚会，希望大家在这辞旧迎新的时刻开心幸福！
