#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

Passiflora edulis, also known as passionfruit, is native to the tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. Its fruit juice is rich in nutrients, and smells particularly aromatic. It has the reputation of "King of Juice".

>百香果，又称为热情果，多自于美洲的热带及亚热带地区。果汁营养丰富，气味特别芳香，有“果汁之王”之美称。
