In 2014, my friend @acactus1013 and I traveled to Xiamen. Gulangyu Island is part of Xiamen, which is a very gorgeous island. It is famous for its architecture and for being home to China's largest piano museum.
>2014年暑假，我和 @acactus1013去厦门旅行。鼓浪屿岛就在厦门市，是一个非常美丽的岛屿。它因许多建筑物而闻名，也是中国最大的钢琴博物馆的故乡。
