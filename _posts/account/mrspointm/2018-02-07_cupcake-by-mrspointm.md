#### Hi, steemit friends!💙

![IMG_0482.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmQTq9xdmxbyoJUDKCfDQnRF5pidw9LSKk3hZTcuEMABXb/IMG_0482.JPG)

This kind of cupcake,I think,  seems not very gorgeous. But its special taste amazes me. Though offered in a hot pot restaurant, it tastes really good. If I could have a cup of green tea, that would be perfect.

>这些杯子蛋糕看起来并不惊艳，至于口感有让我惊讶到。虽然这只是一家火锅店提供的甜品，但是酸甜的奶油搭配松软的蛋糕，甜而不腻。这时候如果再搭配一杯绿茶就完美了！
