This is the second time that I have taken part in Photography Contest held by @juliank. Here are my entries for it. This time, the theme is anything related to landscape.

It was our first trip to Sanya.  Since the fim <i> If You Are The One</i> came out, an increasing number of people have been flooding into Sanya to enjoy its beautiful scenery. Yalong Bay is known for its beaches and this tropical forest park. In the second picture, the house is used as the shooting place for this film.
