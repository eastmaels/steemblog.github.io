<strong>Hi, steemit friends!💙</strong>

In such hot summer, we probably drink some cold drinks to avoid the summer heat. Today, I'd like to introduce some special drinks in Starbucks. Imagine that when you go shopping with your friends for a while, it is a good idea to have one. Starbucks is a suitable place where you can have a rest and relax yourself.

夏日炎炎，难免想喝点冷饮，今天想给大家介绍几款星巴克的特色饮品。可以想象一下，当你和朋友逛街太久口干舌燥时，是不是很想喝一杯冰冰凉凉的饮品，星巴克的环境和味道就很适合。

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmW98L2jzL89yPkEtiaForu7ohxiWUcmoY2hxUHGNwYvzt/w_ed2e3cf66bdc11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="w_ed2e3cf66bdc11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" /><br/>

Since most of you know its surroundings, I won't focus on this part. Its chain stores can be seen in most cities with its same decorative style. I've created a new membership card, for there are three chances to buy a one-for-one. Do you think it is cute?

星巴克的环境就不用过多介绍了，因为许多城市都能看见它的连锁店，装潢都是一个风格。今天还新办了一张会员卡，有三次买一送一的机会。有没有觉得会员卡很可爱？

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmQcJdzVbJyqccoRN63fTLZs71SZjwwyK61waz574haCMs/w_e06168866bdc11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="w_e06168866bdc11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" /><br/>

This one is a new product of Starbucks and I always like the shape of such drinks, thus ordering it. Gently take a sip, and you will feel the aroma of Mocha, which leads to endless aftertaste.

这杯酷黑摩卡是星巴克的新品，一直很喜欢这种形状的饮品，所以首先就点了这杯。轻轻吸一口，浓香的摩卡味就窜入口中，回味无穷。

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmVJKthKYrouS7W496YyTifQk7FCniHKnFq24deQZeuyGF/w_f7e4054d6bdc11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="w_f7e4054d6bdc11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" /><br/>
<center><sub>Mid-night mocha 酷黑摩卡</sub> </center>

Frankly speaking, it tastes just so so. But I think there may be a lot of people ordering this drink, for its name really sounds fresh. In addition to water and ice, some peach sarcocarp is also added to it so that the customer s do not feel it a monotonous drink.

客观地说，这被饮品味道一般。不过我觉得应该会有不少人点这杯饮品，因为它带给我们的感觉就是清新淡雅。这杯绿茶，除了水和冰，还加入了一种蜜桃果肉的东西，让客人喝起来不会感觉太单调。

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmUoHiZEETCzqcguFw1ksM5zFQyd1VZyhfCtm3RcrgbXjL/w_0c1750256bdd11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="w_0c1750256bdd11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" /><br/>
<center><sub>Iced shaken peach green oolong tea 冰摇桃桃绿茶 </sub> </center>

This one is my favorite. For girls, we enjoy a drink just because it's good-looking. The main color is pink. With red and white milk foam, it makes people warm.  As for the taste, I like the whole taste with chocolate, which is very unique.

这杯星冰乐是最令我喜欢的，对于女生来说，喜欢一杯饮品有一半的原因或许是它的外观。它的主要颜色是粉色，红白相间的奶泡，让人感觉很温馨。至于味道，我很喜欢它整体味道中带一丝巧克力味，很独特。

<img src="https://cdn.steemitimages.com/DQmU3gx8Y8bwdu1pjoMiooDR9fsM25wiRh3EEQzpryPcBYt/w_1da86df76bdd11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" alt="w_1da86df76bdd11e882c7066aa14dc6a6.jpg" /><br/>
<center><sub>Strawberry white chocolate flavored frappuccino 草莓白巧克力风味星冰乐</sub></center>

I remember that each time I go to Starbucks, I always order frappuccino, which can be regarded as a typical one in Starbucks. I  think frappuccino is good in all respects except for the fact that it is a bit too sweet. If you drink it before a meal, it will be likely to affect your appetite. In the following ones, I like Matcha,which is less sweet than others.

记得每次去星巴克的时候，几乎每次都点星冰乐，它应该算是星巴克中特别经典的饮品了。但我总认为，星冰乐各方面都还不错，就是甜味有点太多。饭前喝的话会很影响食欲，在几种星冰乐里，我最喜欢喝抹茶味，因为相比其他的口味，它算是不那么甜腻的。
