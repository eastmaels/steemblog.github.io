#### Hi, steemit friends!

Happy Tuesday! Here is my photo for food photography. In the past few months, I have been taking part in the daily food photography contest held by @howtostartablog and here I want to say thanks to him for supporting me all the way. Though this contest is stopped now, I will continue to share my food photography with you. 

>这是我的食物摄影照片，在过去的几个月中，我一直都有参加由 @howtostartablog举办的每日食物摄影比赛，其中也获得了好多次奖励，这里想要谢谢他对我们的支持。尽管比赛已经中止，作为吃货，我还是会一如既往地给大家分享美食。
