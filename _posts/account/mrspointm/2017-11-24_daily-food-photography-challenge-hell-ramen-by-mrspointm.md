Theme: Daily Food

Location: Chengdu, China

Camera used: OPPO R11

Several days before, I have shared a photo of tonkotsu ramen which is the specialty of that Japanese restaurant. Compared with it, hell ramen tastes spicier.  Inside are vegetables, a soft-boiled egg and roast pork. 
>几天之前，我给大家分享过豚骨拉面的照片，它是这家店的招牌啦。跟这个相比，地狱拉面更辣。里面加入了蔬菜，木耳丝，溏心蛋，叉烧肉。
