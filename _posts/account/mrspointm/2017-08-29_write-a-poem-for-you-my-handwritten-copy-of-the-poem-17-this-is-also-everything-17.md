#### Dear steemit friends,

I want to share with you a poem <i>This is also everything </i>, written by Shu Ting. The original text is as follows:
今天想要和大家分享的一首诗，叫《这也是一切》，作者舒婷。原文如下：

<b>这也是一切</b>
不是一切大树， 
都被暴风折断； 
不是一切种子， 
都找不到生根的土壤； 
不是一切真情， 
都流失在人心的沙漠里； 
不是一切梦想， 
都甘愿被折掉翅膀。 
不，不是一切 
都像你说的那样！ 

不是一切火焰， 
都只燃烧自己 
而不把别人照亮； 
不是一切星星， 
都仅指示黑暗 
而不报告曙光； 
不是一切歌声， 
都掠过耳旁 
而不留在心上。 
不，不是一切 
都像你说的那样！ 

不是一切呼吁都没有回响； 
不是一切损失都无法补偿； 
不是一切深渊都是灭亡； 
不是一切灭亡都覆盖在弱者头上； 
不是一切心灵 
都可以踩在脚下，烂在泥里； 
不是一切后果 
都是眼泪血印，而不展现欢容。 

一切的现在都 孕育着未来， 
未来的一切都生长于它的昨天。 
希望，而且为它斗争， 
请把这一切放在你的肩上。 

****
<code><strong>Translation version 译文</strong></code>

<b> This is also everything</b>
Not all trees have been broken off by the storm;
Not all seeds cannot find soil to root;
Not all true feelings lost in deserts of human hearts;
Not all dreams wish to be cut off the wings.
No, not everything is like what you said!

Not all torches are burning themselves but not illuminating others;
Not all stars indicate darkness but not predict dawn;
Not all songs pass through the ears but not stay in the hearts;
No, not everything is like what you said!

Not all appeals have been dismissed;
Not all losses cannot be compensated;
Not all abysses are deaths;
Not all grief lands on the heads of the weak;
Not all hearts and souls to be stepped on and buried in the mud;
Not all consequences are tears and blood, but without mirth.

Everything in nowadays is creating future,
Everything in the future is from it yesterday.
Hope, and fight for it,
Please put all of them on your shoulders!

****
The following is part of my handwritten copy of this poem.
以下是我的手抄本（部分节选）。

![IMG_3214.JPG](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcbrYEw6khf4eyWNRqBKt3T2RXybvJpvWbPP9RhKo9tVz/IMG_3214.JPG)

Compared with the poem <i> All</i> by Bei Dao, <i>This is also everything</i> shows that the author has a positive attitude towards life.  What the poem<i> All</i> has expressed is very negative and  is immersed in the great pain. And Shu Ting's poem is written  in a positive and optimistic expression, which shows she is full of hope , especially the words"not all ..., all ... " What I like best is the sentence parallelism. When reading this poem,  you can deeply feel the author's romantic and passionate heart!

<blockquote>《这也是一切》可以说是舒婷对北岛的《一切》进行了强烈的反驳回应。北岛在诗中表现得十分消极，将整首诗都沉浸在痛苦的笼罩之下。而舒婷的这首诗正是在以积极乐观浪漫的形式表达其实生活充满了希望，尤其“不是一切XX，都……”这样的排比句让我非常喜欢。读《这也是一起》，可以深深地感受到舒婷浪漫且富有激情的内心！
</blockquote>
