生日🎂


世上不必费劲就能得到的是年龄

在岁月中不期而遇

生日就这样如期而过

今天才来把十一生日蛋糕🎂 亮出来

自己选的昂贵蛋糕诶

再次重温了一次少女心

最真的梦✨

 

没鸡汤 … 没语录

深话浅说… 长夜漫漫

未来岁月里

希望尽不将就

也不辜负 ​

因为每个人都有属于自己内心的执着

坚守不变

…

时间是让人猝不及防的东西

晴时有风阴有时雨

岁月是一场有去无回的旅行

好的坏的都是风景

承蒙时光不弃

终究又长了一岁🌵

愿所得皆所求

所遇皆良人


What the world doesn’t need is age

Meet by chance in the years

So the birthday passed

Today came up to the eleventh birthday cake 🎂 bright

Expensive cake of your choice

Once again relived a girlish heart

The most really dream ✨

No chicken soup… No quotation

In simple words… All night long

In the years to come

Hope is not enough

Don’t live up to

Because everyone has their own heart attachment

Stick to the same

Time is something that catches people off guard

Rain and wind on sunny days

Time is a journey that has no return

The good and the bad are landscapes

It is generous of you not to lose time

Again long one year old 🌵 after all

Wish for whatever you get

Every man I meet is a good man

Love what I love,

Do what I like to do,

Listen to the heart

Feel it with love.
