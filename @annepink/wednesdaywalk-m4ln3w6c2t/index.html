WednesdayWalk周三晚步行回家






 

公司每周三晚上7点都有夜校课…所以只要我一有时间就会回公司上夜校学习

那么夜校课上完…就是自己独自走在回家的路上了!街拍了一些路上的照片，透过照片看上去，漫长的路上，显得特别冷清…又特别温暖…

公司在第9层，有时候加班透过玻璃窗，也可以看到对面马路上的车水马龙，不知道他们是不是也有和我一样，会拍下回家路上的照片，记下回家的心情呢!

一般我回家的路上，都是乘坐公交车🚌 我也喜欢在公交车里看看车窗外面的风景～一般回家的时间是晚上九点左右，车上人也不太多，我会选择靠窗的位置坐，看着窗外灯火，也看着窗上若隐若现的自己的脸，思考下人生…我只要静下来就会左思右想…想出一大堆的事出来…有时候就会坐过到终点站了…😅

这些照片都是在步行到公交车站台，路上随拍的夜景!看似简单的高楼大厦🏢 是不是有着熠熠生辉哒感觉呢～

我们公司在南昌红谷滩新区…第一张图的左边是南昌著名的双子塔噢!没有特效拍摄可能无法看出它的靓丽壮观的景象…😊

然而回到家以后感觉总是很累了…这时候想着到家的第一件事不是吃饭，而是睡觉!😂

The company has evening classes every Wednesday at 7:00 PM… So whenever I have time, I will go back to the company to study at night

So after night school… Is walking alone on the way home! Street took some photos of the road, looking through the photos, the long road, seems very lonely… And very warm…

The company is on the 9th floor. Sometimes I can see the traffic on the opposite road through the window when I work overtime.

My way home commonly, it is to ride the bus 🚌 I also like in the bus to see the scenery outside the window to the general home time is about 9 o ‘clock in the evening, the car is not too much, I will choose to sit by the window position, look at the lights outside the window, and looked at the window is like concealed his face, think about life… I just calm down and think… Come up with a bunch of things… Sometimes I miss the terminal… 😅

These photos are walking to the bus stop, along with the shot of the night! Seemingly simple gleaming skyscrapers 🏢 have dalai feeling ~

Our company is in honggutan new district, nanchang… On the left of the first picture is the famous twin towers in nanchang. Without special effects you might not be able to see the spectacular spectacle… 😊

But when I got home, I was always tired… At this time, the first thing to think home is not to eat, but to sleep! 😂
