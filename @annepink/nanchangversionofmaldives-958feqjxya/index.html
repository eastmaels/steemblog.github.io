南昌版“马尔代夫”Nanchang version of Maldives







五一四天假期，天空正好放晴☀️ 气温舒适及不适合宅在家里，不然都对不起愉快地假期了…😅

🌤趁着春光明媚的大早上，洗刷刷整装之后与好盆友相约来到南昌瑶湖森林公园 …大约40分钟左右的地铁行程之后就到了…

进入公园映入眼帘的海阔天空和郁郁葱葱的花木美景瞬间被吸引😍完全把人潮拥挤的路程抛诸脑后了…

闻着春天的气息，吹着小风，躺在柔软的沙滩上…享受这份难得的闲适心情…

当城市的喧嚣成为生活的日常，清风绿水自然的馈赠似乎就成为一种奢望了…

如果你想短暂的逃离嘈杂声的都市和快节奏的压抑生活，不妨可以选择来到这里~~瑶湖郊野🌲森林公园🌸🍀🌷🌹🌻🌺 给自己疲惫的身心放个假吧🤗

这里不仅有美丽的沙滩湖岸🏖 还有澄净的湖光美景🏝 被南昌人称为南昌版的“小马尔代夫”喔~😎

小桥流水鸟语花香，一汪湖水清澈照人，行道长廊满目青翠，秀色可餐的美景简直令人心旷神怡啊…💆

瞧见没…优美宜人的自然生态环境…勾勒出一幅幅靓丽的画面…还有青青湖边草🌱… 清新自然的直觉👃

特别是有着异域风情的茅草屋…烧烤区让慕名而来的游客们欢喜地很呢

随手拍下它们最灿烂的瞬间这便是让我最放松也最满足的时刻了…

这一幕我又想起那首格格的歌《火苗》暖风吹…春来到…欢迎来到瑶湖马尔代夫与阳光沙滩、湖景零距离接触~🤗

During the May 14th holiday, the sky is just fine. The temperature is comfortable and not suitable for the house at home, otherwise I am sorry for a happy holiday…😅

Taking advantage of the bright spring morning, after washing and brushing, I came to Nanchang Yaohu Forest Park with a good friend… It’s about 40 minutes after the subway trip…

The beauty of the open sky and the lush flowers and trees that enter the park are instantly attracted to the crowd and the crowds are completely left behind…

Smell the smell of spring, blowing a small wind, lying on the soft sand… enjoy this rare leisure mood…

When the hustle and bustle of the city becomes the daily life of life, the natural gift of the breeze and green water seems to be a luxury…

If you want to escape the noisy city and the fast-paced depression for a short time, you can choose to come here~~Yaohu Country Forest Park 放 Give yourself a tired body and mind.

There are not only beautiful beaches and lakes, but also the beautiful scenery of the lake. It is known as the “Little Maldives” by Nanchang.

The bridge is full of water and birds, and the lake is clear and clear. The pavilion is full of greenery. The beautiful scenery is delicious and refreshing…💆

See you no… The beautiful and pleasant ecological environment outlines a moving picture… and the Qingqing Riverside Grass… Fresh and natural

Especially the exotic thatched cottages… The barbecue area makes the tourists who come here happy.

Taking pictures of their most brilliant moments is the moment that makes me the most relaxed and satisfying…

In this scene, I remembered the song of the first song, “The Flame”, the warm wind blows… Spring is coming… Welcome to the Yaohu Maldives and the sun and the beach, the lake view is in close contact~🤗
