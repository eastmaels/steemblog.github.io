Then my child and I came here.





熊孩纸看到了佛像继续参拜

他对神马都好奇好玩

The child saw the Buddha statue and continued to pay tribute to him.

He is curious about everything.


这个地方叫许愿林

两边是苍松翠绿深草青藤


We continue to climb along the winding stone steps.

Both sides are green pine green grass

不知不觉我和熊孩纸就走到了素有小南方之城的‘‘长城’’脚下

从山脚下向上仰望

“长城”已赫然眼前

每隔一段路

便设有一个烽火台

两台之间遥遥相对

一台更比一台高

看上去颇为壮观

Unknowingly, I and the bear child went to the foot of the ‘‘Great Wall’’, which is known as the Little Southern City.

Looking up from the foot of the mountain

“Great Wall” is already in front of you

Every other way

There is a beacon

Relatively distant between the two

One is more than one

Looks quite spectacular




我们一下来了兴致

摩拳擦掌

准备登上“长城”的最高烽火台！

As soon as we came, we were ready to go to the highest Beacon Tower of the Great Wall.


延绵整个山顶

有“南方小长城”之美誉

登上峰顶

Extending the entire summit

Has the reputation of “Southern Great Wall”

Mount the summit

Standing on the beacon tower of the Great Wall, you can see it

Unobstructed views of the mountains and the countryside

Under the blue sky, the forest is full of mountains




我们不约而同

Loudly shouting to the valley

Breathing my heart… letting go of the mood

It seems that the canyon echo has been resoundingly so far…

Because the weather is hot

The sun’s strong light can’t open the eyes.

Especially curious




准备往下山路下来

熊孩纸玩的好开心啊

在这里飞奔嘻耍😄

Ready to go down the mountain road, the children are having fun.





下山后 又偶遇他新结识的小伙伴

两个小朋友玩的不亦乐乎

帮他俩一起合影留念

After going down the mountain, I met his new acquaintance partner.

Two children are having fun

Help them take a group photo together

Oh, they are so cute.



诚然怪石岭的“长城”虽然不是八达岭长城

也没有秦始皇统一中国的重大意义及历史故事和孟姜女哭长城的爱情故事

但也体现了江西劳动人民的智慧与力量

也体现了设计师独特的创意

此处山清水秀

崇山峻岭中一条蜿蜒曲折的巨龙为之平添了几分神奇色彩
