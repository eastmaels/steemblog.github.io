今天我分享的这组照片是我和我家熊孩纸去秋水广场玩的自拍秀
还有在那里拍的夜景

The group of photos I shared today is a selfie show that my son and I went to at Qiushui Square.
There is also a night scene taken there.

秋水广场
是位于江西省南昌市红谷滩新区的赣江之滨
紧邻行政中心广场
于2004年1月28日落成
是利用荒滩兴建
既防洪 ～又观景
与中国著名的滕王阁隔江相望
再现了千古名篇《滕王阁序》中的”落霞与孤鹜齐飞 ～秋水共长天一色”的意境
秋水广场正是由此而得名
它是一座以喷泉为主题
集旅游、购物、观光为一体的大型休闲广场

Qiushui Square
It is located on the bank of the Lijiang River in Honggutan New District, Nanchang City, Jiangxi Province.
Close to the administrative center square
Sunset on January 28, 2004
Is to use the wasteland to build
Both flood prevention and viewing
Across the river from the famous Tengwang Pavilion in China
Reappearing the artistic conception of “Luo Xia and Lonely Qi Fei ~ Qiu Shui Gong Chang Tian Yi” in the famous “Teng Wang Pavilion Preface”
Qiu Shui Square is named after this
It is a fountain theme
A large leisure plaza integrating tourism, shopping and sightseeing

我们俩每次出去散步玩
都会玩潮自拍
还会拍些路上所见所闻的风景
这些照片都是秋水广场的新年夜景
这里每年的新年都会布置大型生肖灯展

We both go out for a walk every time
Will play tide self-timer
I will also take some pictures of what I saw on the road.
These photos are the New Year’s night view of Qiushui Square.
The annual Chinese New Year will be equipped with a large zodiac light show.

每当夜幕降临之时
这里将是人们观景游玩的首选地方
当然白天也是非常好看的风景
每次来这都是人山人海
因为这里拥有世界超级炫彩缤纷的大型音乐喷泉⛲️
大部分人都是为了来观摩音乐喷泉⛲️
好的～下次有机会再跟朋友们分享音乐喷泉的盛况😄

这次分享就这些了
希望这些美丽的夜景和有趣的照片会给大家带来快乐的笑容～谢谢欣赏😚
再会😊😊😊

When night falls
This will be the preferred place for people to watch and see.
Of course, the day is also very beautiful scenery.
Every time I come here, it’s a sea of ​​people.
Because there is a large, colorful music fountain in the world.
Most people are here to watch the music fountain⛲️
Ok~ Next time I have the opportunity to share the grandeur of the music fountain with my friends😄

This sharing is all about this.
I hope that these beautiful night scenes and interesting photos will bring you a happy smile~ Thank you for your appreciation.
