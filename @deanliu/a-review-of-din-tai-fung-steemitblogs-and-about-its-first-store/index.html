I tried them several times actually but I did not blog even once. Well, it is common to eat in a DTF here in Taipei (LOL…)  But I have never went to the very FIRST store of DTF in the Yong Kang St…. It is always packed with tourists from all over the world, especially from Japan. And it is a small 3-story store. Cannot accommodate many diners, thus creating a bit of mystery among gourmets… 

We went to Yong Kang St to have a walk last weekend and it is still jammed. Well, that’s why I make this post… :) Just to show you that this sacred store is as always…
