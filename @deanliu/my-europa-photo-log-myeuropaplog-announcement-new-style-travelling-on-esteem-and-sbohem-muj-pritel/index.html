The Trip


I am going to Europe.

I am going to Europe, mainly to four cities including Prague, Vienna, Bratislava and Budapest.

I am going to Europe for the first time in my life so I am excited, and if you have any good suggestions for what to do, to see or to experience in these great cities, please feel free to let me know.

I am going to Europe and I will still miss Steem(it) so I decide to start my little experiment for new style travelling by documenting my trip on eSteem with mainly daily selected photos.

I am going to Europe and I know that Europe is quite advanced in terms of bitcoin adoption and blockchain industries, so if you can recommend any restaurants or cafes that accept cryptocurrencies, or even there is an ATM for bitcoins, do let me know!

I am going to Europe but I will not let my little experiment occupy too much time of my precious travel time, so please forgive me if I do not reply or upvote you …

I am going to Europe and I will say goodbyes to friends here in the following but for now…

I am Sooooooooooooooo going to Europe!!

My Europa Photo Log experiment



  • Duration - From departing from Taiwan until I come back from Europe [2 weeks roughly]

  • Platform - eSteem

  • Posting details

    • content - selected travel photos. texts and interactions will be minimal.

    • frequency - not sure. hopefully at least 1 post per day, no more than 3 posts per day

    • tags - these are the 5 tags I am gonna use for this series of travel photo diary posts: myeuropaplog, travel, photography, europe, and blog. I decide not to use #esteem to avoid tag spamming.
