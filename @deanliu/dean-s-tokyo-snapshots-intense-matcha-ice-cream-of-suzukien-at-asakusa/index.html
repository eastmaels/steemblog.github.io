It is said that here has the most intense Matcha (powdered Japanese green tea) Ice Cream of the world!! So we came here to find out (just excuses… ha)

It is located on a common road in Asakusa (淺草) near the famous Sensoji tourist area.

A small store. Packed with customers from all over the world. Lots of people from Taiwan I think.

Their matcha is from Shizouka (靜岡) I think.

All kinds of matcha merchandise.

WOW! So cool there were some ladies coming in wearing Kimonos (Japanese traditional garment). ❤️～～～

Lots of flavors but matcha is obviously the main appeal.

Try some!? Oh it is indeed yum…. I don’t care how much tea inside, but it’s yummy and that’s enough for me. ^^

Strawberry for my kid and sesame for my wife. Sesame is also very good…
