Steemit is a honest and efficient platform! Thank you for money back and bugs removed!


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/31/logoe4764.png


Two days ago, as the dev team introduced Promoted Content, many Steemians were so excited and tried this new feature including me. When I found out that posts older than one day can also be promoted, I thought this could be a bug as the above post did not mention this. So I thought why not give it a try as I really wanted to give my old posts a second chance. But I knew it was a fluky move. So I did not put serious money into it either - probably $7 for a dozen posts. These old posts all showed up on the promoted pages (mostly under specific tags as my money is small) but soon I saw @jesta ‘s kind reminder warning that this would lead to a one-vote only result. I then knew that my money was gone but felt so funny that I wanted to fool the system but get fooled! It is not big money anyway.


This morning just 2 hours ago, I saw my wallet get refund from the system (actually from @zaebars - why?!) with a memo saying “refund for a post promotion bug”. I do feel a little bit embarrassed and moved as I did know my risk when doing these but the platform is very decent to refund the money. Thank you so much! So I have to let more people know this: Steemit is a honest platform!!


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/31/refundc5071.png


What about efficient?! Aside from “refunding” the moeny so quickly, they have also fixed a bug that I mentioned under the first post about being able to “promote” a “reply”. They have found and fixed it maybe just a little more than 24 hours! That’s very efficient, don’t you think!? 


Lately, I have been too negative about things going on here, but these actions by the platform along with @jamielefay the Steemit novelist’s postive remarks to me has made me positive and optimistic again! Like I once said - “promise a journey, not just a sprint“. Come on! Steem on!


An Update! Could anyone who plans to upvote this post check the following post and upvote there instead of here as that post deserves much much more than mine!

首先是我昨天發的這篇文章：What are the Captains doing during the Steem Price Storm? – My Sunday Investigation  


大家可以稍微看看，主要是因為Steem實在跌得太兇了，讓我不得不有些擔心，與此同時，也想找找高層的dan跟ned都在幹嘛，是否有相關回應等等。畢竟從8/9左右開始就一路大跌到今天都止不住，0.006到0.003到0.0015，腰斬再腰斬，現在已經在0.0013上下了… 所以我就去看看這兩位「船長」到底這段時間，尤其是上一週都在關心什麼了…    簡單總結一下就是…

  • dantheman就比較活躍一點，尤其前一陣子大談voting跟curation好幾次可能大家都知道。比較特別的是他前兩天回應了一篇關於Steem價格被steemit這個帳戶壓低的質疑，dan的簡短回應是這是為了SBD的保值以及Steem重分配

  • 所以我的簡單結論是船長們目前還沒有慌亂的跡象，大家除了緬懷太早下的注之外，就還是抱著希望繼續耕耘吧，等反彈穩定了再考慮投資。 大概就是這些，只是我故意寫得比較輕鬆，畢竟這些事我沒辦法真的很懂，重點在於分享我看到的東西
