Jiufen [九份] is a small once-splendid-gold-mining-but-later-occupied-by-artists-after-deserted town on the mountain near Keelung … blah blah blah …. Just see the previous post(s) here … 


Cat - Jiufen photo series 001


local delicacies 1 - Jiufen photo series 002


This little restaurant is called “Jiufen Old Noodles House“ (九份老麵店). It is almost the oldest local restaurant here so we come here to have meal every time as a support for the original Jiufen. Their dishes are also quite “old” or traditional which also fit my appetite as I am not… well… young. lol…


This time we had (in the order of photo appearance) beef noodle, beef tendon & belly, wonton soup and rice with minced pork and bamboo shoots… oh, some side dishes are boiled vege and dried bean curds … The bill for this dinner is about 11-12 SBDs… actually not cheaper than city prices… damn the tourists other than us… lol


Oh, you can try to guess what it means for that huge Chinese compound word in the last photo! :)
