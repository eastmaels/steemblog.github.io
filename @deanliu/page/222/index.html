My Europa Plog - Neruda on Charles Bridge at Prague

We have lost even this twilight.
No one saw us this evening hand in hand,
While the blue night dropped on the world.

https://i.imgur.com/AgXK2QJ.jpg
https://i.imgur.com/w6lP3tf.jpg
I have seen from my window
The fiesta of sunset in the distant mountain tops.
https://i.imgur.com/B1PEtAR.jpg
Sometimes a piece of sun
Burned like a coin between my hands.
https://i.imgur.com/I9qJB3y.jpg
https://i.imgur.com/l5w8es1.jpg
I remembered you with my soul clenched
In that sadness of mine that you know.
https://i.imgur.com/LTmMXg6.jpg
Where were you then?
Who else was there?
Saying what?
Why will the whole love come on me suddenly
When I am sad and feel you are far away?
https://i.imgur.com/69AIeHF.jpg
https://i.imgur.com/GlsUuur.jpg
The book fell that is always turned to at twilight
And my cape rolled like a hurt dog at my feet.
https://i.imgur.com/usztKmq.jpg
Always, always you recede through the evenings
Towards where the twilight goes erasing statues.
Texts: poem of Pablo Neruda. Photos: mine.
