HUIT, such a lovely French style cafe by the Meguro river. I was told that this place is so very popular during the cherry blossom seasons. I can imagine because en route to this restaurant, we walked along the river and saw many cherry trees on both sides of the river…. Must be so romantic when those beautiful flowers arrive….

Well, it is fall now and the scene is totally different but I love it as well. Not to mention their dishes… really exquisite …. c’est délicieux!!

Bon appetit!!
