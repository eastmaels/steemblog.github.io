Today we also have two themes and they are little guests and good morning breakfast. 


這次主題是小客人與豐盛的早餐。 


Regent Taipei, in our experience in recent years, is adjusting to focus more on family instead of just business customers. But as a five star hotel, accommodating little guests is always essential. For infants, they can provide a bottle sterilizer, a baby basin and a baby cot. Since my boy is already four years old, we just need a small bed. But Regent has given us more this time, which is a surprise for us.


最近到台北晶華酒店的經驗，發現他們逐漸調整營運策略。原先以商務與觀光客為主，現在也開始重視家庭旅客市場。對於五星級飯店來說，提供小客人入住所需物品，是一定必備的，通常對於有嬰兒隨行的客人，飯店會提供奶瓶蒸氣消毒機、小澡盆以及嬰兒床等。我兒子已經超過4歲了，我們只需要一個小床。但這次為小孩準備的東西讓我們有一些驚喜。看下去吧！

Galilei Family Brunch at Taipei Xinyi has a fun painting 台北伽立略家庭餐廳的「萌那麗莎 」


http://i.imgur.com/bK8ne4Wl.jpg


I had some hesitation in sharing this family restaurant with you since although it is good but not really too special. However, when I reviewed those photos I took and saw the one I put above, I couldn’t help sharing it - it is way TOO cute and hilarious!! Hence the title. So, read on.


原本我有點猶豫是否要分享這間親子餐廳「伽立略家庭餐廳」，因為好歸好，但沒太大特色。不過，整理照片看到一張餐廳的畫，太過犯規的可愛，實在讓我不得不分享，所以，就連餐廳一起分享吧。：）


The Restaurant and the Painting


This nice family restaurant, Galilei Family Brunch, is located on the 4F of Shinkong Mitsukoshi A8 Mall (Xinyi Place), at the heart of Taipei’s newest core commercial area. It is really important for parents with little children if they decide to come here for shopping or having meals as this neighborhood is very crowded and good options for parents are really limited. Here they endeavor to provide a relaxing environment for parents as well as their little ones. You can see at the entrance that it is designed to be under a huge tree.


這間「伽立略家庭餐廳」位於台北信義計畫區新光三越百貨A8館4樓，這區是台北市最新最熱鬧的商業核心區域，遊客與當地客都多，雖然基礎設施很不錯，但用餐時間，許多餐廳很容易客滿，帶著小小孩的父母要找好的餐廳好好吃頓飯是不容易的。因此，這間親子餐廳的出現，對於許多父母是一大福音。這間餐廳價位比較高，所以他們對於父母餐點品質一樣很重視。入口可以看到一棵大樹造型。


http://i.imgur.com/PM9OVZPl.jpg


They hang a really funny painting here and literally I find everyone who first sees this painting will at least smile, or often laugh. I saw this painting long time ago when I passed by and immediately I remembered this restaurant. Finally we went two weeks ago when we happened to need both: “family” (i.e. get rid of my kid) and “restaurant” (I need to eat! with dignity!). I really love this “Monalisa baby” but I don’t know if it is original or just a copy. 


一進入餐廳就會看到這幅「萌那麗莎的微笑」，我觀察了一下，沒有人看到會不笑的，實在太可愛了。早在我來消費之前好久，我就已經記得這餐廳，就是因為這幅畫。終於兩週前帶孩子來這裡買東西，中午到了，就來這裡試試看。我非常愛這幅畫，但不知道是複製品還是原作。

嬰兒們的坐騎與當時的萬聖節裝飾。

The Food


Their food is pretty good, considering that most family restaurant in Taipei don’t pay much attention to adults’ menu as they know our weakest point - we will come as long as they can provide good environment and food for kids. So quality food for parents is really just a bonus as well as how it stands out from its competitors. I did not take many photos of our food - too hungry to shot, you know. Here is one and it does look good.


他們的餐點其實很不錯，如果你知道的話，台北近年來開了很多親子餐廳，但是通常父母在乎的是設施以及小孩的餐點（這樣就會乖乖買單了），自己吃得就隨便一點，因為只要能夠短暫擺脫小惡魔，這比什麼都更美味！所以，如果餐廳對於父母餐點有用心的話，那麼就會讓人印象深刻，完美！所以，我們太餓了，我沒多拍大人餐點，只有這個剛上來的附餐拍下來了，看起來不錯吧。

解構區塊鏈之玩弄文字詩 A word playing poem for blockchain in Chinese


This is a playful post. Not technical at all. A poem in Chinese. English explanation and translation is given below the photo.


前兩天在飯店的cafe消磨了半個下午，小酌了一點。看著一些區塊鏈的資料，看多了「區塊鏈」這三個字，不禁習慣性地想把它拆解一番，想著想著，最後寫出這篇小玩意兒。不登大雅之堂，難以稱詩，玩弄文字詩，那就勉強吧。棄之可惜，還是寫出來供大家娛樂一下吧！說明一下，就是把「區塊鏈」這三個字 (當然是繁體，簡體就不能這麼拆了) 逐個拆開看，並將之與區塊鏈性質相關連。


區塊鏈

口為人，三口為眾人

區字 打破人為的國家邊界

眾聲喧嘩，全球意見自由流動

土為地域，鬼為才華

塊字 釋放空間對人類才智的限制

繽紛奪目，八方智慧與藝術迸發激盪

金為價值，連接串流

鏈字 描繪世界多元價值的即時銜接疊加

兩儀萬象，生態系統創造以人為主的時代新紀元

區 塊 鏈

出現於過去，正從未來，走向現在

—————————————————————


note. 還是多此一舉解釋一下：1. 區字右邊是開放的，讓三口可以自由出入；2. 兩儀比喻0跟1

The above is a word playing poem for blockchain in (traditional) Chinese. It was written during days ago when I spent an afternoon in a hotel cafe. Me and my wife had some drinks (for the record, she, not me :), ordered this apple cider and I had beer but her drink looks better so I put it here) and I read some articles on blockchain. After seeing this term in Chinese (區塊鏈，pronounced as Chiu Kuai Lian or Qu Kuai Lian) repetitively, I got naughty and tried to dismantle these three words and combined it with blockchain’s potential impact and influences to write up this ‘poem’ (it is harder to do so in English … what can you dismantle from it other than block and chain?). I quickly revised a Google-translated draft to finish with the following English version. Just to give you some idea of what I write in the Chinese one. 


Blockchain

Mouth (口) for people, three for all

Chiu (區) the word breaks the artificial state boundaries

The sound of public opinion, free flow of global opinion

Soil (土) for the region, ghost (鬼) for talent

Kuai (塊) the word releases human intelligence from space limitation

All so eye-catching, wisdom and art burst and intensify in all dimensions 

Gold (金) for value, connecting (連) streams

Lian (鏈) the word depicts the instantaneous cascading of the world’s multiple values

From binary to omnipresent, ecosystems create a new era of individuality 

Blockchain

Appeared in the past, coming from the future, to the present
