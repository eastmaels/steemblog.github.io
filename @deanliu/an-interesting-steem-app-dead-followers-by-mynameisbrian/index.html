This tool has a formidable name “Dead Followers”. It is created by @mynameisbrian, who is a very famous comics artist and successful blogger on Steemit. I did not know that he can develop APPs! Thanks Brian for all the fun and the convenience you bring us!

https://steemitimages.com/DQmUskxw7eG4EN4WWGpPXUrcqMcKcdsKwj4jQCyVcAePzan/image.png


Click to go to the “Dead Followers” website - http://steemit.deadfollowers.info/ and you are ready to check the following status of any account, not only yours, yeah! It’s a transparent platform remember!? Ok. Now you know that this site gives you a sketch of the status of your followers! By Dead it means being inactive for more than one month. So theoretically you can still save them from death by asking them to come back and post/comment! LOL… You can try your account but I will show you mine and a few users from the cn community.


找到後點擊網址，就可以到達”Dead Followers”的網站 - http://steemit.deadfollowers.info/ 很簡潔。你就可以開始玩了！輸入任何帳戶名稱，就可以知道此帳戶的粉絲們（followers）的活躍情況。Dead的意思是超過一個月以上不活躍，所以，想要你的死亡粉絲們起死回生的話也是有可能的喔，只要你找得到他們，請他們回來發發文章或回回帖，馬上就變成活粉絲了喔！呵呵！在你開始玩之前，我把自己跟幾位CN區的大咖（我只是中咖）的數據列出來瞧瞧！
