DailyDialogue (beta) #0001 Keeping Track of My Crypto New Life


Lately I have been thinking, reading and writing so much about Steemit and all the crypto new world. Too much that I cannot organize them systematically to share with others. Just like the other day when I saw this scene when going to work and I was quite surprised that my first thought on this demolition work was:


Isn’t this what Steemit and other blockchain-based new models are doing to the world? 


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/13/IMG_20160812_08385032ed2.jpg


And today I saw my page reach these comfortable numbers (however, 200 posts are not actually 200 “posts”, see here), an idea presented itself: why not keep track of what I thought, learnt, or even wondered in a personal way without obvious themes so I get to share many random things in my head and also document my crypto-growth every step of the way? (see how no post type distinction drives me so - here)


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/13/2016-08-132.45.339f016.png&nbsp;


A name like Daily Dialogue sounds cool (Deanliu’s Daily Dialogue, even more so!), so I started the beta mode as a trial version like this one. Maybe someday I will do it officially like SteemMag (by @infovore ) but of course not so professional… 


There we go. Some new developmentt I saw just now that I can share with you - introduction of Trending (30 days) - so we can check trending post of every tag within this month. Hence I get the 2nd place currently under #cn tag (thanks to you guys!) with this new feature. What an honor and surly feels great! 


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/13/2016-08-134.23.521c33b.png


Welcome any suggestions and replies re this new experimental series! Just a reminder that it won’t be indeed daily. Welcome to tag alone and grow together!
