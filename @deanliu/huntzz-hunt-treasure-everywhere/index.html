Screenshots

Hunter’s comment

If you are a true hunter… yes, I believe you are, or you won’t be here on Steemhunt.com!!

Well, if you are, then you must love this amazing App that is created for hunters and for hunt designers for various purposes like pure treasure hunting fun, promoting your home town’s tourism, making a big party more fun, incentivizing kids to learn more about history or geography, or, maybe just design a hunt to earn some profit!!

Yes! This Huntzz app allows the users to play public treasure (scavenger) hunts, or they can also create and share own treasure hunts anywhere in the world for free!

Playing Hunts

Existing public hunts are informative as well as fun and currently on locations in the UK and some part of Europe - like the British Museum, tower Bridge, Trafalgar Square and etc in London; Oxford, Cambridge, York, Liverpool, Birmingham and etc in England; Mont Orgueil Castle in Jersey; the Louvre, Champs Élysées & Arc de Triomphe, Eiffel Tower and etc in France!!

Creating Hunts

In my opinion, the best part of this App is the hunt creation tool! Yeah, It’s easy and free to create a hunt of your own and share it with friends, family members or colleagues to play. You can even submit the game to the Huntzz Inc. for them to consider including your hunt to their public hunts (you may get rewarded!).
