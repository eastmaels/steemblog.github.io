Jiufen [九份] is a small once-splendid-gold-mining-but-later-occupied-by-artists-after-deserted town on the mountain near Keelung … blah blah blah …. Just see the previous post(s) here …

It’s quite normal to focus on food so quickly … Asians eat frequently, don’t you know that!? lol … So I will have more to share soon .. Let’s check this one out first. This is a store famous for its Glutinous Rice Cake. We also order some more soups (with tofu and with rice vermicelli). To be honest, they used to taste better… I guess there are too many one-time customers that they start to loose their quality standards… bummer! [you can see how they come up with a SOP for ordering as an evidence of my guess. last photo.] 


But it’s still good. After all, I was in traveler’s mood!
