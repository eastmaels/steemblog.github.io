[D&TT] TT's first NBA collection ... Hoodies!

Last time I shared a drawing of my son, showing his (false?) passion for the game: TT’s NBA drawing

Yesterday, his mother took him downtown and they ran into this NBA store in the Xinyi area. This time, TT is not the him one month ago and he knows what NBA is now. Being a fan and all that, he dragged his mother to the store and they were lucky that the store is having a winter sale at the moment - buy two and get one free!! woohoo~~~

So the question is: which two teams’ hoodies (mother thinks this is the best thing to buy in the store) to buy?

So they called me. I suggest two teams (as well as two players from both teams) that I love and the proposal was accepted.

They are: Stephen Curry from GSW and Kyrie Irving from BOS.

So now TT can wear his Gold/Blue 30 or Green 11 hoodie and go out there showing his love for the game!
