Chicken Attack to start the Year of Chicken 雞年伊始之雞雞攻擊


Normally I do not post on internet memes but I had done once before when PPAP was hot. Today would be my second time doing this. The reason is similar … just can’t resist the desire to share with more people! Besides, this song is about chicken and when is a better time to share this at the beginning of the Year of Chicken!?


http://i.imgur.com/zW2YCbz.png


This is a new song by Takeo Ischi, a 69 years old Japanese singer/yodeler active in Germany. You can check the wiki, but it doesn’t really matter who he is, right… okay, here is the song titled … 


!!!  Chicken Attack  !!!


It is a English song by a Japanese singer in Germany with the theme of Chicken, animal of the year for Chinese. Sounds international enough? Not only so, it has so many things in it: yodeling singing (I learn this term for the first time), environmentalism, animal rights, justice, martial arts, humor … and most important of all … it sounds great and addictive!! 


It is just so inspirational and positive that I decide to Chicken Attack you!! Now, watch out and take it !


https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m8CIZgEppnU


If you really like it like really really like it… then here is a 10 hour version for you :)
