A Walk into Postcards - A Short Trip to Okunikko and Senjogahara (original)


In recent years, Japan has been a top favorite travel destination for my family. We developed this preference accidentally by the low prices of Japanese Yen and later were trapped into Japan’s unique culture and beautiful sceneries, oh, and the cuisines as well!


In this post, I am going to share some photos taken with you from our short 2-day trip during our major Tokyo one week journey. We weren’t expecting much but the sheer beauty of nature made us astonished again and again and we quickly recovered our energy from the tiresome shopping spree in Tokyo before this short trip. 


This short trip was to Okunikko （奧日光）and Senjogahara （戦場ヶ原，せんじょうがはら）.


Let the photos speak for us. Include our neat and simplistic accommodation as well. Hope you enjoy them!
