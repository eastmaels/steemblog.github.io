Really a less than minor bug ... but still a bug.


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/09/12/bug-1296147_640bca31.th.png


Please, don’t bother reading this post as this is really minor. 


I have noticed this problem (bug?) for quite some time but it doesn’t really affect my steemit activities so I never bother telling anyone. Until recently I tried to bookmark some pages and realized that it does cause some inconvenience. But again, not important as it’s too easy to deal with. 


See page titles in this picture: today’s trending page as well as top four pose pages… fantastic jobs, guys!


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/09/12/hpage16e867.png


They won’t be the same after you read them… any scrolling down of the page will turn the title into “Post-Steemit”… Now we have four identical post page titles (you can get them back if you click ‘reply’ or reload the page)… That’s it. The small tiny bug I mentioned. Not really of any importance… Just not neat enough. For me of course.


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/09/12/hpage22f925.png


It causes some tiny problems too. When you want to bookmark it without noticing this, your bookmark will be slightly different in the title part (red in the following). Not a problem, just reload the page and save the bookmark then. I know. That’s why I said less than minor.


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/09/12/hpagebookmark9b500.png


I just post it so if anyone could take care of it sometime maybe in split seconds… after all, this less than tiny bug bugs me a little bit… and, by the way, I don’t know anything technical or Github. 


Happy Steeming, fellow Steemians!
