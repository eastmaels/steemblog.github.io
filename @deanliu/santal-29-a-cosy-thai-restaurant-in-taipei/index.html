Santal 29 - A Cosy Thai Restaurant in Taipei

Last week I went to a Thai restaurant in Taipei with a friend for a weekday lunch. That was my first time to this place and I really enjoyed my dining experience there! I’ve always love the Thai style - from their aesthetics, food, to its people… I love them all! If I can pick the place where my next life is going to be born, Thai might be one of my favorite place on Earth! So the restaurant definitely has a very authentic Thai style - from plants, table and chairs, tableware, … to music selections. The food is of course Thai - you can find those classical dishes here like Thai Basil Pork Rice, Red Curry Noodles, Chicken Soup with Galangal and Coconut Milk, or Tom Yum Soup and more… Follow me to have a look at this serene and elegant Thai restaurant hidden in the alley of busy business district of Taipei. ## Santal 29
………. Make yourself at Thai ………. ## The Food
………. Curry Omelet ………. ………. Thai Fried Chicken Wings ………. ………. Chicken Soup with Galangal and Coconut Milk ………. #### If you are looking for a time-out, missing Thai style relaxation or flavor, or just want to try something unusual, Santal 29 might be your good choice. #### But of course, you have to be here in Taipei first. Address. 台北市大安區和平東路三段119巷16號 Phone. 02 2732 7436

CN區朋友們，這是台北一間風格非常清新的泰式餐廳。隱身在遠企後面，安和路巷弄的住宅區內，風格非常低調，或者說泰式。我有一陣子常去泰國，那裏的餐廳也常常是這樣的低調隱身在深深巷子裡，讓你感覺到進去之後，別有洞天。

這間Santal 29風格也是簡約自然，但簡單中不失優雅與時尚感。店員的穿著也是泰式風格喔！我跟朋友約在這裡吃午餐討論事情，點了綠咖哩蛋捲早午餐、南薑雞湯以及泰式炸雞翅，味道非常正宗泰式，配合店內輕快的音樂與較暗的採光，感覺非常舒適放鬆。

去過之後，我又開始懷念起泰國那種悠閒的生活步調了….
