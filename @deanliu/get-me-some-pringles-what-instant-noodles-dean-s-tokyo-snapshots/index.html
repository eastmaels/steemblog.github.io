Yeah, that’s right…. Pringles NOODLES!!!

We were shocked to see this when we were in Tokyo’s supermarket and then we decided to get it a try…. After all, who doesn’t love Pringles…. even it was disguised as … noodles … LOL

Come take a look!

Unpack the powders…

Pour in hot water…. and ready to serve!

Hmmmm~~~~~ Well~~~~~~~~

It’s ………. not bad actually …. in a way you can say it’s delicious ….

But actually it’s hard to describe exactly how it’s tasted …. You know it’s food, it’s a hot instant noodles…. but that’s it.

Well, I can only say that this is definitely the last cup of Pringles noodles of my life. hahaha~~~~~
