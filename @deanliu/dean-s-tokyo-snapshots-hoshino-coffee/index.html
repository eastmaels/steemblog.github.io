My wife told me that this Hoshino Coffee shop is a famous chain cafe and they have some pretty decent meals. So we went there when we were staying at Kinshicho.

The decoration is very good but since it’s Japan we don’t want to be impolite and take photos at others… So, most photos were on our food…

menus…

Lovely mug and great coffee too!

Iced tea and orange juice.

We ordered three meal sets and they were all good. This one was especially recommended.

We had a cake for the kid. Looks really great!

Look!! We have found the Three-star Dragon Ball!! ^_^
