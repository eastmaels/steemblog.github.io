再看花燈，再猜燈謎！My Lantern Festival Night in Taipei - Part 2

Well, it’s time to continue to share the part 2 to this previous post: 逛花燈，吃東西，你猜燈謎？My Lantern Festival Night in Taipei - Part 1 before it’s too weird to share photos too long ago.

In this lantern section that we bumped into casually, here seemed to show the lantern works from some schools in Taipei. See the themes here were mostly very familiar with kids, but we all know that more likely than not they were made by teachers … lol. Check them out below. Hope you enjoy it!

看倌們，沒想到一下子就三天了（幣圈一天，人間一周？^^），差點忘記還有續集要發。今天就抓緊發了。我知道大家可能對花燈已經沒啥興趣了… 呵呵，說真的我也興趣不大了。現在的花燈爭奇鬥艷，我看其實都非常俗氣，距離古代人那種提燈籠的氣氛，早已是十萬八千里了… 不說這個了，倒是猜燈謎本身，不會差多少的，最後面，就再來一次猜燈謎吧！但你可得先看完這些花燈啊
