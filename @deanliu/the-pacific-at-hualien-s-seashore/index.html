The Pacific at Hualien's seashore / 花蓮的海・日與夜

https://steemitimages.com/0x0/https://steemitimages.com/DQmdM9bGsnqSqrc72N23p1My4YRqXAdZfYwm9vn1PQeaGnh/DSC_4896.JPG

This post is a collection of my seaside videos shot during my latest stay in Hualien, Taiwan. I deliberately keep all original sounds and did not add any music, so the audience can enjoy the sound of the waves and the rolling pebbles.

本集收錄過去兩天在花蓮北濱公園海岸所拍攝的海邊聲與影，未加任何後製音樂或是文字，保留現場的海潮聲，石頭被浪翻動的聲音等等。時間包括日出時刻與日落時刻。
