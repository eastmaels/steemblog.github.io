Something New at Steemit

Just found out something changed slightly when you search for tags. You should already know how to look for tags… like this

https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/14/2016-08-142.52.08e7f81.png

At the end of it you can see now there are show more topics available now

https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/14/2016-08-142.52.333ad02.png

Click it and you shall see the list of different tags and replies and payout amount in a given period of time (I suppose so as there is no explanation). So now you get to know which tag is more popular and earns more if that’s what you are looking for.

https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/08/14/2016-08-142.51.405dc8c.png

Obviously steemit is the most active tag. And life, photography and etc are also popular soft topics at Steemit (a reflection of whales’ preferences perhaps?). The most inactive tag shown is marketcap with 2 replies and our familiar 0.00 friend payout. Browse through the tags, there is a lot of information in it. A secret to you is that there is a hidden tag called NSFW which is sometimes very hot. 

A piece of advice from me. Use the tags wisely (see @berniesanders on steemit tag abuse). Do not abuse them or it may harm somehow. See? I use only one tag here and it is used only by me so far.

In addition, an even smaller change: we no longer have “pending payout” but now “potential payout” (as this amount might go down before confirmation). It is very subtle but yet signaling that the dev team is improving everyday!
