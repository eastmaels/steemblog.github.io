Weird Blockchain Engineer Job Opening at bitFlyer Japan


https://steemitimages.com/DQmPRDXN3kJKJGkieQxZRQvugeFShKGFTKQ77LA7yw3nBZS/image.png


Recently I am browsing around to look for job offerings in the blockchain related area. I am just curious because as I don’t have coding expertise but wish to participate in this growing new business (it is much appreciated if anyone can give me any suggestions), still I want to know what others are doing. Today I found a very weird job opening on the website of Japan’s number one bitcoin exchange, bitFlyer. It is a “Blockchain Engineer“ offering so I am very interested in what qualifications this position requires (not like I can do it any time soon, but am curious about what this term means only).


But I cannot understand at all as it is not in English.


Follow me. I will show you and hopefully someone can tell me if this is simply an error or actually a test to a potential applicant. If it is the latter, then it is really cool! It will at least be funny if the former is the case.
