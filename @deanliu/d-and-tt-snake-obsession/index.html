[D&TT] Snake Obsession 迷戀蛇

My son TT has recently been so obsessed with … snakes.

Yeah, that’s right, snakes. I really don’t know why. He asks us to buy him books about snakes, keeps asking questions about snakes, especially poisonous snakes like black mamba, king cobra… etc. If I have to guess why, I think maybe because snakes look every dangerous and thus very cool to like.

Well, little boys… whatever. ^_^

So, these photos are taken right after we bought him a small plastic snake toy… It goes with everything, apparently.

TT近來迷上一種動物….. 蛇🐍。

是的，沒錯。冷血，危險，陰森…. 的蛇。

真要問我為什麼的話，我猜可能是因為蛇上面說的這些特質，很“酷”吧！小男生，可能就喜歡酷的東西，危險，厲害的東西…

隨他去吧，反正很快就會換東西迷了，呵呵～～

這些照片是我們買一隻小塑膠蛇給他之後，他到處要求幫蛇照相而來的～～～ 哈哈哈～～～
