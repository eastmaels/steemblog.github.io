What will happen after you Reblog/Resteem a Comment ? Probably not a bug...


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/09/12/bug-1296147_640bca31.th.png


Please, don’t bother reading this post if you are not extremely bored.  


Two weeks ago, Steemit introduced the Promotion function. Somehow I found out that you could actually ‘promote’ a ‘comment’ (which is pointless obviously) and the team solved it very quickly along with the problem of promoting posts after first payout… Good. Great!


Days ago, Steemit introduced another new function - “Reblog” (or “Resteem” ?? I am confused…). Which is also interesting and great… Good! Well, this time again I found that there is a button for Reblog/Resteem when you have the comment shown in a single page. See here - comment to my linguistic coffee from the poet @richardjuckes … 


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/09/22/commentre3650a.png


I have a such a strong desire to just click it and see what happens and I think Richard’s comment looks very good to appear on my blog page so I will give it a go. Although, it is also quite pointless to reblog a comment (which is processed similar to posts in many ways inside the Steemit system). Anyway I clicked it and nothing happened. Nothing new showed up on my blog page. There is one error message though. Saying… (sorry! Richard! your comment is not Top level enough… :p)


https://www.steemimg.com/images/2016/09/22/commentre2b6cf6.png


So… Conclusion: You CAN click the reblog button at the comment page but nothing will happen (have fun clicking though… ). And, this is not a bug as the team has obviously cut off this possibility (they just haven’t found time to remove the button I guess… ). If you really want to see bugs… check here: my bugs or @pfunk ‘s more beautiful(?) bugs…  


Have a great day!!
