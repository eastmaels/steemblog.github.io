A Trip to Taipei Main Station 周末去趟台北車站

We need to go to Taipei Main Station for doing something, so we took our risk (of getting infected) and went there today with masks. We first arrived at the Beimen MRT Station which is the connecting station for Airport Metro (Danger!!)

我們周末有事得去台北車站一趟，於是帶著戒慎恐懼的心情，做好準備，戴上口罩，一家人就出門去台北車站了。先到了北門站，北門站是桃園機場捷運的連接點，怕怕

Good health to everyone reading this post and keep safe!

希望大家都能保持健康，保持警覺，早日能找回往日生活！
