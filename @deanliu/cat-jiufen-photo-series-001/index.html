Cat - Jiufen photo series 001


Jiufen [九份] is a small once-splendid-gold-mining-but-later-occupied-by-artists-after-deserted town on the mountain near Keelung (also not too far from Taipei city). It is one of my favorite place to relax for a day or two despite the ever growing tourists from around Asia are really annoying … (okay okay I am also a tourist, but somewhat local and early so I decide to claim this place my backyard)


I had a trip not long ago and since at that time I have upgraded my phone to be smart - a Sony phone, so I think now I can share more of my artworks here so as to slowly grow my fan base. If you are lucky enough to see this… yeah, why not? follow me… more photos from my Sony phone will entertain you … :)
