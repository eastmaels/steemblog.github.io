Now Profit By Delegating To Open Source Projects | In Depth Analysis and Utopian Vision

In Utopian.io you can profit by delegating your voting power to @utopian-io. 20% of all the author rewards generated on the platform are dedicated to the delegators, as we call them Utopian Sponsors.

We just made another ground-breaking change and in this video we are going to show how you can have the very same profit as if you were delegating to @utopian-io by delegating to another specific Open Source project you love.

Utopian Community-Driven Witness
