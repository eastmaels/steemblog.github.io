Delegating To Projects

By delegating to projects you participate and help their growth - Project owners can only use the delegated voting power to upvote ONLY contributions for their project. - The account is managed by Utopian. - By delegating you will show up in the project dedicated page.

Delegating To Utopian

By delegating directly to Utopian.io you will receive 20% of all the author rewards generated on the platform based on the amount you have delegated. The voting power is used by Utopian to upvote every contribution submitted on the platform for every Open Source project.
