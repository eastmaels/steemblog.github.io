GAGA light meal Restaurant




Hello, everyone, I would like to recommend this restaurant today. It is the place where I often come! Because it’s near home.

If you are in Luohu district of Shenzhen and want to get together with friends, or if you like healthy food, recommend this one: gaga.

Come here and order some snacks, and have a cup of fresh fruit juice, you can chat with friends for an afternoon.

This place is located on the first floor of King Guang Hua Digital Plaza, Luohu District. After shopping here or taking the children to play in the amusement park, you can also come here to have some delicious snacks.
如果你在深圳罗湖区，想和朋友聚一聚，或者你喜欢健康食品，推荐这个:gaga。

过来点些小吃，喝一杯新鲜果汁，你可以和朋友聊一下午。

这个地方位于罗湖区光华商场国王一楼。在这里购物或者带孩子们去游乐园玩之后，你也可以来这里吃一些美味的小吃。

There are a lot of people here at weekends, and new products and preferential activities have been launched this month.
周末这里人很多，本月推出了新产品和优惠活动。

The following is the menu here, the picture is also very attractive, there are salad, toast, hamburger, bread snacks, ice cream and so on.
下面是这里的菜单，图片也很吸引人，有沙拉、烤面包、汉堡、面包点心、冰淇淋等等。

Because we came here after lunch, we finally chose the hamburger set meal and a cup of fruit tea.

He finished a job here, and then we had a pleasant chat here. Very nice place. You can also order a cup of coffee or tea and a cake and spend the afternoon here.
因为我们在午餐后来到这里，我们选择了汉堡套餐和一杯水果茶。
他在这里完成了一项工作，然后我们在这里愉快的聊天。非常不错的地方。你也可以点一杯咖啡或者茶，一份蛋糕，就可以在这里度过一下午了。

This is a very popular restaurant, here is very spacious, you can choose to sit indoors or outdoors!
这是一家非常受欢迎的餐馆，这里很宽敞，你可以选择坐在室内或室外！

On this day, we spent a total of about US$ 17 here, which is also a very reasonable price!We recommend you to come here.
在这里我们花费了111元人民币，非常愉快的下午！
