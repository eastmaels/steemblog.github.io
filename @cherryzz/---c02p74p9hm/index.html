上周我找出来这个做了一样东西，这个是原材料，我切了一小块一小块出来，然后加上橄榄油，和橙子精油，加热然后让它和油融化在一起。Last week I found this one and made something. This one is a raw material. I cut small pieces and then add olive oil and orange essential oil, heat them and let them melt together.



融化后就基本完成了，现在只需要装进润唇膏的管子里面就可以了。After melting, it is basically finished, and now it only needs to be put into a lipstick tube.

最后放 冰箱一晚上润唇膏就可以用了，哈哈，没错，我在做润唇膏，因为冬天快来了天气开始干燥，这些是我为家人准备的。The last night in the refrigerator, lip balm can be used. Ha ha, that’s right. I’m making lip balm because winter is coming soon and the weather is beginning to dry. These are for my family. .
