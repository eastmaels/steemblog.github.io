颐和园万寿山

颐和园，中国清朝时期皇家园林，前身为清漪园，坐落在北京西郊，距城区十五公里，占地约二百九十公顷，与圆明园毗邻。它是以昆明湖、万寿山为基址，以杭州西湖为蓝本，汲取江南园林的设计手法而建成的一座大型山水园林。 The summer palace, the imperial garden of the Qing dynasty in China, formerly known as Qingyi garden, is located in the western suburbs of Beijing, 15 kilometers away from the urban area and covers an area of about 290 hectares. it is adjacent to Yuanming garden. It is a large-scale landscape garden based on Kunming lake and Wanshou mountain, based on Hangzhou west lake and drawing on the design methods of Jiangnan gardens.
