Pepper and chicken casserole 胡椒猪肚鸡




Hello everyone, I would like to recommend this place this week. This shop has been in Shenzhen for many years. It specializes in cooking chicken.
大家好，这周我要推荐这里，这家店在深圳很多年了，一家专门做鸡肉煲的地方！

When you come to the door of the store, you need to go to the next staircase. The wall of the staircase depicts the history of the store.
来到店门口要下一层楼梯，楼梯的墙上画了这家店铺的历史。

This day we came at noon. We came earlier and there were not many guests. The environment here is comfortable. I came here with my friends.

The two of us ordered a pot of chicken and a pot of rice.The two of us ordered a pot of chicken with pot belly and a pot of rice. Soon we had a pot of pepper soup.
这天我们是中午来的，来的比较早，客人还不多。这里环境舒适，我和朋友一起来到这里。

The waiter poured all the chicken into the pan, while it was still cooking, we went to mix some sauce.
服务员把鸡肉全部倒了进去锅里，趁还在煮的时候，我们去调了一些酱料。

The pepper chicken soup here is made into milky white, especially delicious soup. We drank several bowls.
这里的胡椒鸡汤，煮成奶白色的，特别好喝的汤。我们喝了好几碗。

Here comes our bacon and hot pot rice, which contains sausage, bacon and spareribs. Cook chu in casserole

我们的腊肉煲仔饭来了，里面有腊肠，腊肉，排骨。煲仔煮出来的饭特别好吃。

There is a discount for the two of us for this meal, which cost us 14 dollars in total. The price here is well worth it.
我们这一餐还有折扣，一共就花了14美金。在这里的价格是优惠了。
