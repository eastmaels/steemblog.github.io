Growth Diary (3)成长日记（三）3月17日

The diary of the child’s growth came. I was busy yesterday and did not upload it. This is a video of Sunday. The family had morning tea on Sunday and went to our mother’s house in the afternoon. The children said they wanted to see the hamster and played card games. When they came back, they passed by the nearby antique city. Happy weekend.
