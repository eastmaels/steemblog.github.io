旅游回来老家的第一天，带给steem 朋友们我们这里的美食On the first day of traveling back to my hometown, I brought steem friends our delicious food here.

今天和孩子们旅游完回老家了，在北京天津游玩了10天，拍了很多风景照片，之后慢慢分享给大家看。Today, I went back to my hometown after traveling with my children. I spent 10 days in Tianjin, Beijing, and took many scenery photos. then I slowly shared them with everyone.

北京回到来，龙眼也成熟了，早上买的无花果，很新鲜，很好吃呢。When Beijing came back, longan became mature. the figs bought in the morning were very fresh and delicious.

回到南方，我们吃的菜就不同了，客家菜我觉得是非常好吃的，菜品丰富，今天中午煮的，图片发给打家看。Returning to the south, the food we eat is different. I think the Hakka food is very delicious. the food is rich. it was cooked at noon today. the picture was sent to the chef. 白切鸡Boiled Chicken with Sauce我们这里最喜欢的吃法。 红烧猪蹄Braised pork trotters 辣椒炒竹笋Fried bamboo shoots with Chili 猪肉酿苦瓜Pork makes balsam pear 焖鱼肉Braised fish 白灼虾Boiled Prawns放进开水里面煮一下就可以了 凉拌牛肉Beef salad

下午来到这家农家菜吃鱼，旁边就是一大片荷花池，我们坐在池塘边，闻着荷花荷叶的清香，令人神清气爽，今天吃这里招牌，一鱼三食，就是一条鱼有3种吃法。I came to this farmhouse this afternoon to eat fish. next to it was a large lotus pond. we were sitting by the pond, smelling the faint scent of lotus leaves. it was refreshing and refreshing. today I ate the sign here. one fish and three food, that is, one fish has three ways of eating. 周围种了很多的菜There are a lot of vegetables planted around. 先上来的是酸菜水煮鱼First came sauerkraut and boiled fish. 炸鱼骨Fried fishbone 鸡蛋蒸鱼皮Steamed fish skin with eggs

美好的食物总是让人开心，明天向大家分享更多。Good food always makes people happy, and I will share more with you tomorrow.
