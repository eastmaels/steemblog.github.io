艺术雕塑--深圳人的一天 Art Sculpture--A Day of Shenzhen People

周末和孩子去图书馆，经过这里，一个城市公共艺术雕塑：“深圳人的一天”，在1999年，随机选择了在深圳的18位不同行业的普通人做为模特进行翻模，然后做在雕塑，反映深圳人一天的不同生活状态。 At the weekend, I went to the library with my children. After that, a city public art sculpture named “Shenzhen People’s Day”, in 1999, I randomly selected 18 ordinary people from different industries in Shenzhen as models, and then made sculptures to reflect the different living conditions of Shenzhen people in one day.

这些青铜人物雕像，是根据11月29日那一天，在深圳大街小巷中活动的普通人物而随机选定，其中有外来求职者、打工妹、中学生、银行职员、外来退休老人、儿童、咨客(服务员)、医生、工程承包人、股民、保险业务员、抱狗的女人、政府公务员、港商、清洁工人、设计师等，雕像与真人的高矮、大小、外貌、衣着、装扮等几乎完全一致. These bronze statues were randomly selected according to the ordinary people who were active in the streets and alleys of Shenzhen on November 29. Among them were foreign job seekers, middle school students, bank staff, foreign retired elderly people, children, consultants (waiters), doctors, engineering contractors, shareholders, insurance salesmen, women with dogs, Government Civil servants, Hong Kong businessmen, cleaners and designers. And so on, the statue is almost identical with the real person’s height, size, appearance, clothing, dress and so on.

带着小狗散步的女人。 A woman walking with a puppy.
