Anime Character Drawing Challenge # 2 Design your own Steemit manga hero| 50 SBD Prize Pool 動漫人物繪畫比賽 #2 設計你的Steemit漫畫英雄| 50 SBD奬池


Time flies, it has already been a month since I held the first Anime Character Drawing Challenge. Last time we have received so many fantastic entries from different countries and I was amazed by the talents of my fellow Steemians. You can take a look at them at the post - Anime Character Drawing Challenge - Announcing the Winners


Source

Inspired by @jnart, the Second Prize winner of the challenge, I decided to hold a new Anime Character Drawing Challenge with a new theme - “Design your own Steemit manga hero”. I think this theme allows more space for creativity and imagination, and I can’t wait to see the entries submitted by YOU! Yes, YOU, the one who is reading this post! Stop hesitating and submit your entry! It’s time to show your talent and let the other Steemians notice you!

Let me announce the details about the prizes and rules here:

1st Prize：15 SBD
2nd Prize：10 SBD
3rd Prize：5 SBD
Merit Prizes：4 SBD x 5

Rules

  • Write a new post about your entry and the drawing process
  • Reply this post(under the first comment) to submit your entry
  • the post must include an #animedrawingchallenge tag(Don’t have to be the first tag)
  • Your drawing must include your own Steemit id to prove that it is your original work
  • Drawings are not limited to: sketch, color pencil, watercolor, oil painting, ink painting, computer drawing
  • The deadline for submission is 16th October.

Look forward to your entries and make use of this opportunity to make friends with other anime lovers.

上次的動漫人物繪畫比賽舉辦得十分成功，有來自世界各地的動漫愛好者聚首一堂，提交了不少美麗的作品。受到上次比賽亞軍 @jnart的啟發，這次比賽的主題是「設計你的Steemit漫畫英雄」，給與參賽者更多發揮他們想像力和創意的空間。不要猶疑了，馬上開始設計你的作品參賽吧！

奬項：

一等奬：15 SBD
二等奬：10 SBD
三等奬：5 SBD
優異奬：4 SBD x 5

規則如下：

  • 寫一個新的帖子描述繪畫過程
  • 回覆這帖子來提交你的參賽作品
  • 參賽帖子需要使用 #animedrawingchallenge標簽（該標簽不需要放在第一位）
  • 參賽作品上需要清楚顯示你的Steemit id
  • 繪畫形式不限於：素描，彩色鉛筆，水彩畫，油畫，水墨畫，電腦繪畫。
  • 提交作品的截止日期為十月十六日。

期待你們的畫作。
