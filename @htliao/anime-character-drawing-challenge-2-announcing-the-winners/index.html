Anime Character Drawing Challenge #2 - Announcing the Winners


Source

Anime Character Drawing Challenge #2 is over! I am so happy to see that there are still many talented Steemians from different countries joining the challenge! Since the theme this time was “Design your own Steemit mango hero”, originality and creativity will be the major criteria for selection of the final prize winners. I was amazed by the Steemit heroes designed by the participants and some have interesting settings too!

Once again, thanks for those who have joined the challenge, and many of you showed your creativity and extraordinary talent in drawing. Hope that you have enjoyed the challenge and know more about your fellow Steemians who share similar interests. So, let’s announce the winners!

1st Prize:

Congratulations to all the winners. All the SBD rewards have been sent directly to your wallets, cheers and enjoy your day.

At last, if you like their drawings, don’t forget to support and follow them :)

第二屆的動漫人物繪畫大賽完滿結束了，這次也有不少人參加呢，比賽的主題還能讓參賽者充份發揮他們的創意和想像力。很高興能看到這麽多有著高超繪畫技巧的動漫愛好者聚首一堂，一起參加這個比賽！

由於這次的主題是「設計你的Steemit漫畫英雄」，所以原創性和創意會是評審的關鍵要素，我也留意到有很多參賽者的繪畫技巧令人嘆為觀止，但有些實在和我認識的動漫角色太像了，所以沒有被選上，真可惜呢。

再次多謝參加的各位，你們都展示了非常捧的繪畫技巧呢！有興趣的話可以看一下上面的得奬作品。

恭喜得奬的參賽者，沒得奬的也不用灰心，繼續期待下一次的活動吧。 :)
