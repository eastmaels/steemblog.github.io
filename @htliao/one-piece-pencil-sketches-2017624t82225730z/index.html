鉛筆素描- 海賊王 (One Piece Pencil Sketches)

星期六了，就出篇休閒點的文章吧，大家早安。

Cn區有喜歡畫畫的同好嗎？雖然小弟喜歡畫的都是漫畫，沒有經過系統性的學習，也只是照著漫畫書上的臨摹，沒多少藝術細胞、創作天份，卻自得其樂，也能使自己安靜下來。

這帖的畫都是海賊王中的角色，有人愛看嗎？這是一部關於海賊的冒險故事，是日本最出名的周刊少年Jump的王牌漫畫之一。

主角路飛是我最愛的角色，但我不太喜歡4檔的樣子，太醜了，只找到以前差一點完成的照片。

這是剛開始畫時拍的，只是跟著某一格漫畫畫的

快完成的樣子

魯賓，美女哦，也只找到還沒完成的照片，很可惜。（相信我，我都畫完了的！ T^T）

卓洛，酷酷的大劍客，這可是完成品哦！

可愛的Chopper， 攝於剛開始畫時。

這些畫每一張都畫了我數個小時哦。好後悔當初沒好好把完成品每張拍個照，現在整本畫冊也不見了。 T^T

這些都畫了有一段日子了，出來社會工作後都難得有機會動筆畫，近來因為Steemit又有機會動筆，畫了一隻比卡超，感謝一下followers，但只是用原子筆畫的（挺難的，不能有任何差錯，所以就比較醜一點啦），畫了五分鐘左右，只能算是証明一下我的畫畫功力吧。

歡迎在下面留下你的感受或建議，cn區裏有人感興趣的話下一次辦個畫畫比賽也可以。

English Version

Does anyone love drawing and pencil sketches? I am just an amateur in drawing and sketches, but I used to love comics, so I tried to use a pencil to imitate the drawings in comics and follow the lines. I am not an expert, just a comics lover who tries to imitate, but I have enjoyed the process.

The posts shows some drawings of the characters in One Piece, the famous Japanese anime.

Actually these are drawn years ago, so I am sorry that I cannot find the finished version. They were lost and I had spent hours to draw them T^T. Now after I entered the workplace, I don’t have the opportunity to pick up this hobby again.

Luffy(just started)

Luffy(nearly finished)

Robin(nearly finished)

Zolo(This is a FINISHED version)

Chopper(Just started)

I have drawn for several hours for each of them.

That’s quite a pity to lose the finished version of the pictures, but at least these photos recorded the process and it means something to me. Thanks to Steemit, I was able to pick up my drawing skills recently, to thank my followers. This is just a pen sketch and I just used around 5 hrs to draw them. This is the pikachu I drew with my username on it, kind of proving that I have some drawing skills, even now. It’s harder to use a pen since you can’t make any mistake.

Thank you for your reading, hope that you have enjoyed my drawings, but leave a comment below to let me know your feelings.
