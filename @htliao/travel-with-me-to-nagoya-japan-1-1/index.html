Travel with me to Nagoya, Japan (1) 我的名古屋之旅（1）

我的Steemit好友你好，這是我新的名古屋遊記系列，希望你們喜歡。

Hello my Steemit friends, this is my new travel blog series about my trip to Nagoya, hope you will enjoy this.

提起日本，大概很多人會馬上想起東京、大阪、北海道等地方。其實日本也有很多其他值得一去的地方的，而名古屋絕對是其中之一。

Speaking of Japan, many people will have places like Tokyo, Osaka or Hokkaido popped up in their mind. In fact, there are also other awesome places to visit and Nagoya is absolutely one of them.

剛到埗的名古屋市美麗夜景。

The beautiful night view of Nagoya City upon my arrival.

餓翻了呢，先到エスカ地下街的吉田きしめん 品嘗名古屋著名的美食-碁子麵（きしめん）。

As I was starving, we went to Kishinev Yoshida in the underground mall to try Kishimen, a special kind of Japanese udon in Nagoya.

看上去跟一般的日式烏冬沒兩樣吧？但吃上去麵條入口滑溜而不爛，湯頭十分甘甜，這大概就是平凡中的不平凡吧，和普通烏冬不同的是這烏冬扁扁的，十分有趣。

Do you think it looks no different from the ordinary Japanese udon? It is really tantalising, especially the soup. The noodle is flat in shape, so cute!

我們回到酒店放下東西後，在酒店附近的超級市場逛逛就到了一間地道的居酒屋用餐。（真會吃呢！）

Then we went to a supermarket near our hotel. After that, we had our dinner in a local izakaya.

乾杯！

Cheers

雞肉串燒盛合

Assorted Japanese Chicken Yakitori Kebabs Set

這個親子丼（滑蛋雞肉飯）超級好吃，滑蛋的熟度剛剛好，沒有過熟或太生。

This Oyako-don is super delicious!!!

這個蕃茹切片很好吃呢。

These tomato slices are very fresh and awesome.

翌日我們早上就出發到名古屋的名勝-白川鄉。白川鄉被稱為此生必到訪一次的童話村落，它的「合掌造」式的民宅還於1995 年被指定爲聯合國教科文組織的世界文化遺產呢。

In the next morning we went to the famous tourist spot- Shirakara-go. The village is known as the must-visit place in a lifetime. The Historic Villages of Shirakawa-go and Gokayama is one of Japan’s UNESCORTED World Heritage Sites.

櫻花樹和背後的雪山形成一幅絕美的圖畫。

The sakura trees formed a splendid picture with the snow mountains behind.

多麽美的村子。

How a beautiful village.

村裡到處都是賣紀念品的商舖，它們全都是「合掌造」式的建築。

There are many souvenir shops in the village, they are all gassho-style farmhouses.

來到飛驒高山，當然要嚐一下飛驒牛。飛驒牛在日本可是與松板牛、神戶牛、佐賀牛等頂級和牛齊名的呢。

When you come to Takayama, you must try the Hida beef. Hida beef is a well-known brand of wagyu in Japan, and it is as delicious as Kobe beef, Saga beef and Matsusaka beef.

我們這晚在平湯的一間溫泉旅館住宿，感受一下睡在日式榻榻米卧室的感覺，在這裡你還可以付多點錢享用露天私家溫泉呢！這是在晚餐時拍的，大家都換上了傳統的日式和服，食物也非常豐富呢！

We were staying in a decent onsen ryokan(hot spring hostel) in Hirayu, having a taste of the traditional Japanese bedroom. You can also pay some extra money here to have your own private hot spring. That’s awesome!! This photo was shot when we were about to have our dinner, we were all wearing the traditional Japanese kimono.
