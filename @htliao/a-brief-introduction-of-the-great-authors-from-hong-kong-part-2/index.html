A brief introduction of the great authors from Hong Kong(Part 2)

Hi my Steemit friends,

The number of Hong Kong Steemians has been growing at a steady pace and we can see new people moving to Steemit almost every day.

Last time I have introduced some of the great authors in Hong Kong, and you can view the post here. Today I am going to introduce more great HK authors and their post series to you. We will try our best to keep producing diversified, original and high quality content. Please follow them and check out their posts if you think their contents are interesting.

1) @krischy

@krischy is a pretty girl who loves travelling, photography, technology and hiking. She enjoys cooking and drawing in her leisure time and she shares the beauty of her hometown. Her posts series include:

  • Learn to cook with Kris

  • Travel with Kris to the United States

  • Learn to draw with Kris

  • Explore Hong Kong with me

Know more about her by reading her self introduction here

2) @biuiam

@biuiam is a science/biology enthusiast who has just finished his PhD degree. He is actively writing scientific posts related to daily life. He also took part in the @steemSTEM management team, helping to promote good Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics posts on Steemit. He and @steemSTEM are currently hosting the 1st sciencefair on the Steemit platform, be sure to visit them and check the 1st Science Fair out here

His recent science posts are mostly in bilingual to help support the spread of science to different communities.

Check out his Bilingual Science posts here:

  • Goldilocks and the antibiotics, are we just right??

  • Let’s do some debugging, pest control with bacteria

  • Negative Calorie Drinks? Anti-matters? How to make your own~

Know more about him by reading his self introduction here

3) @carinewhy

@carinewhy is a HK girl who loves singing and food. She likes Japanese culture and she is so fluent in Japanese that she can write her posts in Chinese, English and Japanese. You can read some of her posts here:

  • Ramen shop recommended by my JPN friend in HK - Tsukemen Mitaseimenjo

  • Drawing Challenge#2- How I did I draw hydrangeas even I can’t draw

  • How to make a hydrangea decorarion

  • I went karaoke alone

Know more about her by reading her self introduction here

4) @aaronli

@aaronli is an amateur photographer in Hong Kong. He is currently working in the advertisement and dance industry. His post series Hong Kong Snapshot are written in great details with awesome photos shot by himself. It is a very good way to know more about Hong Kong and its history if you are interested.

His posts series include:

  • Hong Kong Snapshot

  • My Postcrossing

  • My work

Know more about him by reading his self introduction here

I think I would just stop here and next time I will introduce more great authors from Hong Kong to you, please stay tuned and support the younger Steemians. We will definitely need your support and encouragement. The SBD rewards from the last post have already been distributed to the authors’ wallets.

致中文區的朋友：

上次和大家介紹了幾個香港優秀作者，相信這裡應該很少人不認識他們了，有新來中文區的朋友也可以follow一下他們，看看他們十分用心寫的帖子。這次為大家多介紹幾位來自香港的作者，他們的帖子都寫得很詳細，也有著不同的題材，例如@biuiam就主要是寫生物學的雙語科普文章，他們都是十分優秀的作者呢。

這帖子的主要目的是向外國朋友介紹一下香港區的優秀雙語作者，為他們多增加一些追隨者人數，特別是美女，應該很容易就能引來一大堆外國來的狂蜂浪蝶了。

希望大家繼續支持香港區的優秀作者，一起努力狀大中文區。

至於上次的帖子奬勵已經分發給各位參與的作者了，這次也是一樣，此帖子的SBD收入將會平分給所有在此帖介紹的作者。
