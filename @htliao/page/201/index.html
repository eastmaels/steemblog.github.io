Sweatcoin App - An app that pays you cryptocurrency for every step you take

Sweatcoin App

An app that pays you cryptocurrency for every step you take

Screenshots

Hunter’s comment

Sweatcoin is founded by a team based in London. If Bitcoin is mined using your computer’s hashing power and Steem is mined through your social networking activities, then Sweatcoin is mined using your outdoor steps. The app uses the accelerometer and GPS in your smartphone to accurately calculate your outdoor steps and avoid cheating. Every 1000 steps can be converted to 0.95 Sweatcoin. Now you can even watch a short video ad to claim your daily bonus.

So what can the coins used for? Sweatcoin is currently not available for trading on cryptocurrency exchanges, but you can use the coins to buys some products and services from the store, including curved Samsung TV, PayPal Cash, and even iPhone 8! You can also send the coins to your friends.

Isn’t it another good motivation to spend more times outdoor walking and jogging, and build a fit body? The app also provides a neat interface to let you check your daily progress.

StakeNet的基本介紹及背景資料整理

Source

總量：約七千七百萬
代幣符號：XSN

StakeNet是一隻較近期推出的幣種，是PIVX的分叉。StakeNet採用了一種全新的共識算法，也就是TPoS機制，是一種無需信任的PoS算法，能在線下做staking，隱私度和安全性較高。除此之外，它還有masternode，適合像 @wilkinshui這種喜歡收利息的人。

Source

共識算法: TPoS算法
