Anime Character Drawing Challenge| 50 SBD Prize Pool 動漫人物繪畫比賽| 50 SBD奬池

Hello my Steemit friends,

Source

I love watching anime and I used to sketch anime characters when I was young. Therefore I would like to organize an anime drawing challenge and you can feel free to participate in it if you are interested. Just choose one anime character and draw it in the way you like.

之前發過一個自關於我大學生活的帖子，發覺中文區裡有不少人都有看海賊王呢。這時我就在想應該不少人也有看動漫吧，就算現在長大了沒再看小時候也應該看過吧 LOL 現在就舉辦一個動漫一物繪畫比賽讓喜歡看動漫的朋友能一展所長，把你最愛的動漫／卡通角色畫出來吧，男生不太會繪畫的話畫Q版人物也可以哦。

I used to love drawing anime characters, but I forget this hobby after I grew up.

我以前很愛畫動漫人物的，但長大後就把這嚐好丟在一旁了，你能看得出來這是誰嗎？

Let me announce the details about the prizes and rules here:

好了，說完廢話了，讓我宣佈一下比賽的奬勵和詳情吧：

1st Prize(一等奬)：15 SBD
2nd Prize(二等奬)：10 SBD
3rd Prize(三等奬)：5 SBD
Merit Prizes(優異奬)：4 SBD x 5

Rules

  • Write a new post about your entry and the drawing process
  • Reply this post to submit your entry
  • the post must include an #animedrawingchallenge tag(Don’t have to be the first tag)
  • Your drawing must include your own Steemit id to prove that it is your original work
  • Drawings are not limited to: sketch, color pencil,watercolor, oil painting, ink painting, computer drawing
  • The deadline for submission is September 10

規則如下：

  • 寫一個新的帖子描述繪畫過程
  • 回覆這帖子來提交你的參賽作品
  • 參賽帖子需要使用 #animedrawingchallenge標簽（該標簽不需要放在第一位）
  • 參賽作品上需要清楚顯示你的Steemit id
  • 繪畫形式不限於：素描，彩色鉛筆，水彩畫，油畫，油畫，電腦繪畫。
  • 提交作品的截止日期為九月十日。

Look forward to your drawings~

期待你們的畫作。
