鉛筆素描 #2- 梵哥利一世 Pencil Sketch Vongola I

在這個美麗星期天就分享一下我以前的畫作吧。動漫迷應該會認識吧。他就是家庭教師HITMAN REBORN! 中的梵哥利一世。

Hello my Steemit friends,
I would like to share my pencil sketch on this wonderful Sunday and I hope you would enjoy it. If you are an anime fan, you may know him. He is Vongola I in the Japanese anime Katekyo Hitman Reborn! He is the boss of a mafia family in Italy.

我不懂畫畫，只是照著漫畫裏的樣子一筆一筆地臨摹。這可費了我不少功夫呢！希望你們喜歡 :)

I am not an expert in drawings, I had to follow the drawings in the comics line by line to finish it. It has spent me several hours to draw this. Hope you have enjoyed it :) Thank you for reading this.
