Drawing Challenge #3 Strawberries Pencil Sketch 草苺鉛筆素描

Hello my Steemit friends,

I have joined this drawing challenge organised by @helene and this time one of the greatest author on Steemit, @sweetsssj will be the judge! How a great opportunity! So let’s go for drawing and submit your entry, the theme of this challenge is fruit and I have chosen strawberries to draw as strawberry is my favourite fruit!

上次參加了 @helene的drawing challenge後，目睹了cn區眾美女的佳作，不禁自慚形穢，難道美女天生就有繪畫細胞嗎？強烈要求以後分男女子組好了，不然我們男生都被比下去了，哭這次的評審還是甜心大神呢，Steemit上公認的超級網紅作者，機會難得，大家快去參賽吧。這次的主題是水果，我選了草莓來參賽，因為這是我最愛的水果！充滿少女風情吧？

Strawberry smoothies is one of my favourite drinks

草莓沙冰是其中一種我最喜愛的飲品

Source

The Process:

素描過程:

Step 1: Line work of the leaves

先畫出葉子的鉛筆線稿

Step 2: Sketch the first strawberry

然後把旁邊的第一顆草莓的外形畫出來

Step 3: Draw the seeds

把上面的種子畫出來

Step 4: Finish the linework

完成整個鉛筆線稿

Step 5: Add light and shadow to the strawberries pencil sketch and finally, my Steemit id.

為草莓底部和葉子補上陰影，令它看上去更真實。最後加上自己的id。

The pencil sketch is finished.

這樣一幅草莓的鉛筆素描就完成了，大家也趕快去參賽吧。

Hope you have enjoyed my drawing. Have a nice day! Thank you.
